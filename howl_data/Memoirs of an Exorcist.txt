Title: Memoirs of an Exorcist
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Memoirs_of_an_Exorcist
--------------------------------------------------
Memoirs of an Exorcist is a book located in the library of William Norland's House. It appears to be a factual account of its author's observation into necromancy or demon summoning. What is known is that the book's content deeply disturbed Charmain Baker, to the extent that she was deprived of sleep soon after reading it.[1]
