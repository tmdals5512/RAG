Title: Hans the Cruel
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Hans_the_Cruel
--------------------------------------------------
Hans the Cruel was one of the Nine Bad Cousins of Prince Ludovic and King Adolphus X. At some point, he was poisoned by his cousin, Isola the Murderess, for reasons unknown.[1] The dreadful behaviour of these cousins was suppressed by the royals, but remained a topic of study for schools in Montalbino, as evidenced by the fact that Peter Regis knew about them, while Charmain was utterly oblivious to their existence.[2]
