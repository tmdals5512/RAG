Title: Flying Kayak
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Flying_Kayak
--------------------------------------------------
Flying Kayaks are a type of airship seen in Howl's Moving Castle (Movie).  It gains its name from its appearance, shaped like a silver kayak with dragonfly-like wings.  It has two seats, though seems perfectly capable of having three passengers, and a steering wheel.  They are used by the Ingarian army.
