Title: Hakim
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Hakim
--------------------------------------------------
Hakim was one of Abdullah's relatives, who the latter despised, who would often chide the latter for his refusal to save any of the profits he made in the carpet trade. Abdullah would often have to explain to him how his approach to money worked.[1] Following Flower-in-the-Night's abduction by the djinn Hasruel, Hakim sold Abdullah out to the Sultan's guards.[2]
