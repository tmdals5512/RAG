Title: Timminz
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Timminz
--------------------------------------------------
Timminz is a kobold who operates within the vicinity of Norland's House. He is the de facto leader of the local kobold tribe.[1] At some point, he and William Norland came to a disagreement on account of the fact that the kobold took offence to the colour of the flowers within his garden, with the wizard apparently laughing it off when the kobold had the audacity to complain.[2] Following Norland's illness, Timminz then passed on the complaint to Charmain Baker, Norland's niece, who adamantly refused to remove the bushes on account of the fact that her responsibility was to look after the house and leave everything as she had left it. This led all the kobolds to retreat in disgust from the property.[3] After Charmain captured Rollo, another kobold who had worked in collaboration with a lubbock, Timminz, who was repairing a cuckoo-clock at the time, encountered them and interrogated Rollo concerning this partnership, before revealing his own people's collaboration with Prince Ludovic.[4] Following Ludovic's defeat, Timminz volunteered to continue the time-honoured tradition among the kobolds of looking after Norland's home, to Charmain's considerable relief.[5]
