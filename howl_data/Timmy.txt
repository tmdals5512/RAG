Title: Timmy
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Timmy
--------------------------------------------------
Timmy was a junior apprentice at the bakery run by Charmain Baker's father. During the plot orchestrated by the lubbock to conquer High Norland, Timmy, having failed to recognise her, rudely attempted to dismiss Charmain from the bakery, and would have been successful were it not for the intervention of Belle, who did recognise Charmain. Timmy glowered at Charmain as she entered the bakery despite this.[1]
