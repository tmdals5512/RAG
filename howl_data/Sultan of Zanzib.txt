The Sultan of Zanzib is the incumbent ruler of Zanzib, one of the Sultanates of Rashpuht. Some time prior to Abdullah's adventure, he deliberately kept his only daughter, Flower-in-the-Night, isolated to the extent that she would not behold any man other than himself, because he planned to forge a marriage alliance with the countries around Ingary on account of their magical and technological prowess. However, the young carpet merchant, as he explained to him, proved a hindrance to his plans.[1] Therefore, it was the Sultan's intention to kill him by impalement, and even following Abdullah's rescue of his daughter, he remained ungrateful towards him and still threatened to impale him if he ever returned home.
