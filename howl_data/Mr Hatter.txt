Title: Mr Hatter
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Mr_Hatter
--------------------------------------------------
Mr Hatter was the father of Sophie Hatter and her sisters. All that is known about him is that he was so proud of his daughters that he ensured that they were as well-educated as possible,[1] to such an extent that he blew all the family's money on trying to pay their tuition fees.[2] He died suddenly of an unknown cause (likely a heart attack), and, upon his death, Sophie and her sisters were forced to leave school and find apprenticeships, which had been arranged by Fanny Hatter, Mr Hatter's second wife.
