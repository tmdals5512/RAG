Princess Valeria is the child princess of Ingary, daughter of King Rolland and niece to Prince Justin.
She appears briefly in Howl's Moving Castle as an infant, but has a much more prominent role in Castle in the Air, where she is one of the princesses kidnapped by the djinn Hasruel. While she is originally guarded by Wizard Suliman's magic, his spells are weakened when he transforms Midnight back into her human form, allowing Hasruel to kidnap Valeria. She is critical to the princesses' escape plan, throwing a tantrum to distract the djinn while Abdullah, the soldier, and the princesses overtake him.
