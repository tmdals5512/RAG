Title: Royal Wizard of High Norland
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Royal_Wizard_of_High_Norland
--------------------------------------------------
William Norland is the Royal Wizard of High Norland.  A master of dimensional magic, he owns a house with a single doorway that leads to many different places all across the country simply by turning different way when walking through it.  He only appears in House of Many Ways.
