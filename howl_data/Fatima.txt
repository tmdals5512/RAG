Title: Fatima
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Fatima
--------------------------------------------------
Fatima was one of Abdullah's relatives who would chastise him on account of his refusal to take any woman to wife and therefore resolved to find wives on his behalf.[1] Later, she succeeded in her search and found two obese nieces of her own niece so that Abdullah could marry them. She also gave him a copy of the prophecy which had been issued to his father.[2] When first encountering Sophie Hatter, Abdullah was greatly turned-off by her strong-willed personality, believing that it reminded him of Fatima.[3]
