Title: Wizard Melicot
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Wizard_Melicot
--------------------------------------------------
Melicot was the creator and owner of the House Of Many Ways, before William Norland. He was a great wizard, and certainly the Royal Wizard of High Norland of his time. Moreover, he was the one who enchanted the tin tiles of the Royal Mansion's roof in order to make them look like gold. Below, you will find the list of the different works of Wizard Melicot : 


Creating of the House of Many Ways
Enchanting of the Royal Mansion's roof
Hiding the Royal Treasury under the Royal Mansion's roof
