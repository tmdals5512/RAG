Title: Howl's Moving Castle (2017 stage production)

--------------------------------------------------
Howl's Moving Castle is a theatrical stage adaptation of the novel by Diana Wynne Jones. The musical premiered at the Book-It Repertory Theatre in Seattle, Washington and had a two-hour, twenty-five minute runtime.

Cast[]
The cast list, as noted upon the website for the theatre company, is as follows:[1]

Michael Feldman - Howl
Sara Porkalob - Sophie Hatter
Kate Jaegar - Witch of the Waste/Annabel Fairfax
Katherine Jett - Lily Angorian
Lamar Legend - Calcifer
Randall Scott Carpenter - Michael Fisher
Opal Peachey - Fanny Hatter
Sarah Russell - Lettie Hatter
Reception[]
The musical received a mixed reception when it premiered, where its production values were praised, but not without criticism directed at the forgettable melodies, convoluted narrative, overlong runtime and lack of intricate special effects.[2][3]
