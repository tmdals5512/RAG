Title: Advanced Seminal Sorcery
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Advanced_Seminal_Sorcery
--------------------------------------------------
Advanced Seminal Sorcery is a book located within the library of William Norland's House. Based on its title and the only portion of the text which is explicitly quoted, it seems to be a text examining magic from a strictly academic perspective, to the extent that Charmain Baker found it intolerable to read and hastily closed it.

Contents[]
The only text of the book explicitly quoted is:
"If we extrapolate from our findings in my earlier work, we find ourselves ready to approach an extension of the paratypical phenomenology."[1]
