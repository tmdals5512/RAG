Title: Mermaid
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Mermaid
--------------------------------------------------
Mermaids are a species of fantastical creature which reside within the world in which Ingary and its surrounding environs are located. While very little is actually known about them, a party of them manifested in Porthaven during the duel between Howl Jenkins Pendragon and the Witch of the Waste.[1]
