Title: Unidentified nieces of Fatima's niece
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Unidentified_nieces_of_Fatima%27s_niece
--------------------------------------------------
These two relatives of Fatima's niece were scouted by her as potential spouses for Abdullah. Even though they complimented his looks, theirs greatly revulsed the merchant.[1] Following Abdullah's escape from Zanzib, the two nieces were held captive by the Sultan in his stead.[2] Abdullah ordered Hasruel, from whom he had extracted such information, to have them magically transported to Howl's Castle, where they encountered the djinn Dalzel and complimented his looks, to the delight of the djinn who decided that he greatly preferred them over any princess in the world.[3]
