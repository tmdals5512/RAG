Title: Seven-league boots
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Seven-league_boots
--------------------------------------------------
Seven-League Boots are a type of magical item used by the soldiers of Ingary. Made, and possibly designed, by Wizard Howl, they are capable of transporting their wearers seven-leagues with every two strides.
These "boots" are described as boxy, leather buckets, the finished model only vaguely resembling real boots. They are meant to be worn over the wearer's real shoes or boots. Howl's prototype versions do not travel as far, and look even more bucket-like.
