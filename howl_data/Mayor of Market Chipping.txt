Title: Mayor of Market Chipping
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Mayor_of_Market_Chipping
--------------------------------------------------
The Mayor of Market Chipping was the elected politician who provided chief administration for the town. When Howl's Castle was seen on the hills above the town, he considered contacting the King of Ingary in order to request backup.[1] He also had a son, who proposed marriage to Lettie Hatter, who rejected him on the grounds that she was unprepared for such a commitment.[2]
