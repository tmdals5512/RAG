Title: Neil Jenkins
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Neil_Jenkins
--------------------------------------------------
Neil Jenkins is Howl's adolescent nephew, who resides with his mother Megan Jenkins and sister Mari in the latter's home of Wales. He appears to be fond of playing video games, as, during Howl and Sophie's visit to Wales, he was enthusiastically enjoying a video game with friends, until Howl forcibly unplugged the console and presented him with a piece of his English homework, containing the first verse of John Donne's Song, which had somehow ended up in Ingary.

Behind the scenes[]
Neil is voiced by Gerran Howell in the BBC audio drama,[1] and is the only member of his family to make an appearance in that particular adaptation; with Megan and Mari instead being omitted although Megan is mentioned, but not by name.
