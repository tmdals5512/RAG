Thayack is a country located in the world in which Ingary exists. Very little is known about it, other than that its princess, the Lady Heiress, was abducted by Hasruel on Dalzel's orders.[1]

Behind the scenes[]
This country may be a reference to one of Diana Wynne Jones's prior works, The Homeward Bounders, where one of the protagonists originates from a demon-hunting world named Kathayack.
