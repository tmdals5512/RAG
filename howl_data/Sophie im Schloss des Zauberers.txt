Sophie im Schloss des Zauberers is a 2018 musical adapted from Howl's Moving Castle. It was adapted from the novel by Katrin Brescke and Mina Salehpour and premiered at the Schtathausspiel Theatre Company in Dresden.[1]

Cast[]

David Kosel - Howl
Sven Honig - Michael Fisher
Denis Geyersbach - Calcifer
Tammy Girke - Lettie/Witch of the Waste
