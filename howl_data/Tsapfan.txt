Title: Tsapfan
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Tsapfan
--------------------------------------------------
Tsapfan is a country somewhere away from Ingary. It is clear that Tsapfan is not its actual name, as it is a mispronunciation by its high princess, who was just a child when she was abducted by Hasruel on Dalzel's orders.[1] Its actual name is unknown, and it appears, prior to the capture of its princess, that the region was virtually unknown to anyone in the part of the world in which Ingary is located on account of how distant it actually was.[2] Following the release of the princess from the clutches of Dalzel, Abdullah and Flower-in-the-Night travelled to the region on a diplomatic mission, and returned with boxes full of confectionaries from the region.[3]

Behind the scenes[]
Based on its name, and some of its customs, not to mention the geographical distance from Ingary, this country appears to be the equivalent of China, since China was not visited by Europeans before the twentieth century, save for the merchants who travelled up and down the Silk Road.
