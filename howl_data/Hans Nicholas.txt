Title: Hans Nicholas
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Hans_Nicholas
--------------------------------------------------
Hans Nicholas was the cousin of Matilda Regis and was third in the line of succession to the throne of High Norland. Since he was no more interested in statecraft than her, he eventually married into wedlock with her and sired her son Peter. An adventurous mountaineer and adventurer by nature, Hans emigrated to Montalbino with Matilda, where he became an accomplished mountaineer and guide to travellers.[1] Unfortunately, Hans would eventually perish in an avalanche at Transmontain shortly after his son's birth.
