Title: The Twelve-Branched Wand
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/The_Twelve-Branched_Wand
--------------------------------------------------
The Twelve-Branched Wand is a book in the library of William Norland's house. It was one of the books studied by Charmain Baker, who found it something of a confusing read due to the many branches growing out of the wand itself. Based on its title, it appears to be a book designed to confuse anyone who reads it unless they are explicitly trained in the magical arts.[1] Ironically, when Charmain first found this book, she considered it "promising".[2]
