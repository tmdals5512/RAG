Title: Kingsbury
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Kingsbury
--------------------------------------------------
Kingsbury is the capital city of Ingary.  It is here that the Royal Palace sits, and the King of Ingary and his Royal Wizards reside.  It is the richest setting touched upon in the story.  Its residents are all wealthy and finely-dressed.  The buildings themselves are even topped in gold.

Districts[]
Old Quarter[]
This precinct of the metropolis is comprised of narrow alleyways and courtyards. Ben Sullivan's House is located within this district.[1]
