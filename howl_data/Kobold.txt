Title: Kobold
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Kobold
--------------------------------------------------
Kobolds are short, blue, human-like creatures who live in the underground of High Norland. They have very large noses, wear clothes of all blue, dislike sunlight, and are very crafty and impressive workers.

Behind the scenes[]
Kobolds originate from Germanic mythology, where they appear to be similar to the Brownies of Scottish folklore and household spirits from European folklore.[1]
