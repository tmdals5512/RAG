Title: Jane Farrier
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Jane_Farrier
--------------------------------------------------
Jane Farrier was a girl from Market Chipping who apparently eloped with the Count of Catterack, a prominent nobleman within the King of Ingary's court. The circumstances of their elopement provided gossip for the women of the town and Sophie attempted to capitalise upon this by making a false claim to a customer that one of the hats she was working on was the hat Farrier had worn when she ran off with the Count.
Farrier's fate is unknown although presumably, the Count led her to the altar.
