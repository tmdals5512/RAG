Title: Aunt Sempronia
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Aunt_Sempronia
--------------------------------------------------
Aunt Sempronia is a relative of Charmain Baker and a great-niece of the Norlandese Royal Wizard William Norland. What is known about her is that she married a man named Ned, who died some time afterwards.[1]
