Title: Adolphus II
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Adolphus_II

Adolphus II was a previous king of High Norland, son to "elf-child" Hans Nicholas and ancestor to King Adolphus X. According to the research conducted by the royals into their heritage, he appears not to have done very much in the grand scheme of things.[1]
