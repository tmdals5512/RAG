Title: Sosban Fach
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Sosban_Fach
--------------------------------------------------
Sosban Fach is a famous Welsh folk song which is often sung at rugby matches. Calcifer was known to sing it to Sophie on her first night in the castle, although, oblivious to its actual name, she would refer to it as "Calcifer's silly saucepan song" on account of the number of times the word "saucepan" appeared within its lyrics.
