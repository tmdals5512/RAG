Julia McKenzie CBE (born 17 February 1941) is a British actress most notable for portraying Hester Fields in the 1980s sitcom Fresh Fields and its sequel French Fields and for her portrayal of Agatha Christie's Miss Marple. She portrayed the elderly Sophie Hatter in the audio drama adaptation of Howl's Moving Castle.[1]
