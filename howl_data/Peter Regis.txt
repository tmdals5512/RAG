Peter Regis is the son of Matilda of Montalbino and an unknown father, a friend of the Norlandese wizard William Norland. Some time prior to Charmain Baker taking up residence within Norland's House, his mother sent him to study with Norland as his apprentice, apparently oblivious to the fact that Norland had already been taken for healing by the elves. Thus, when Peter set out, he arrived at the house some days after Charmain did.[1] Although Charmain was sceptical of his claims, she was able to locate a letter written by him confirming his intentions.[2]

Biography[]
Early life[]
Peter Regis was born to the Witch of Montalbino and an unknown man, who was killed in an avalanche shortly after his birth.[3] Unbeknownst to Peter at the time, his father was Hans Nicholas, heir to the throne of High Norland, who had emigrated to Montalbino with his mother simply because he had no interest in kingship and wished to indulge his passion for mountaineering.[4]

Physical description[]
When he makes his first appearance, having come out of the rain, Peter is described as brown, curly hair and primarily wearing a sensible black colour scheme in regards to his attire.[5]

Personality[]
Behind the scenes[]
The word "regis" is the singular genitive in Latin of the word rex, which translates to "King".[6] This is a reference to his being descended from the royal lineage of High Norland.
