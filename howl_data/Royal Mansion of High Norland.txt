Title: Royal Mansion of High Norland

--------------------------------------------------
The Royal Mansion of High Norland is located within High Norland City, the capital of the country, and is where the royal family of the country reside. 

Physical description[]
While the Mansion itself is not described in detail, the interior of the mansion is described as rather humble, with no fancy decorations anywhere to be seen, no doubt affected by the royals' poverty.[1] The Mansion is also described as having a large front door, with a door knocker which is apparently famous for bearing a door knocker which is enchanted to bear the appearance of it being constructed entirely of gold when it is not the case in reality.[2] It is also mentioned as having golden tiles upon its roof.
