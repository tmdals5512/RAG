Title: Manfred
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Manfred
--------------------------------------------------
Manfred was a manservant who worked for Ben Sullivan as his valet. When Abdullah and a magically-disguised Prince Justin (then believing himself to be a soldier) visited Sullivan's house in order to warn him about Hasruel's plot to abduct Princess Valeria, the butler rudely attempted to dismiss them, only for the wizard's wife Lettie to intervene on their behalf.[1]
