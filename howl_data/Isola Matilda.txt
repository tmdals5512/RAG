Title: Isola Matilda
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Isola_Matilda
--------------------------------------------------
Isola the Murderess was one of the Nine Bad Cousins of Prince Ludovic and King Adolphus X. At some point, she poisoned Hans the Cruel for some unspecified reason, only to later be killed by Hans the Drunkard while the latter was inebriated.[1] According to the account given by Matilda Regis, Isola was not, in fact, evil, but was instead kind and gentle, but apparently framed for attempted murder by Ludovic simply so he could eliminate her as a potential obstacle to the throne.[2]
