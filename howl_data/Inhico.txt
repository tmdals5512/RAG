Inhico is a country located in the world where Ingary exists. Very little is known about it.[1] During the time in her life where Matilda Regis consulted Mrs Pentstemmon about where she could take her family, including her husband Hans Nicholas and son Peter to protect them from the wrath of Prince Ludovic, Pentstemmon suggested Inhico as a possible place to hide.[2]

Behind the scenes[]
Based on its name, Inhico may be Ingary's world's equivalent of Brazil, however this is unconfirmed.
