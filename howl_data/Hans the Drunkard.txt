Title: Hans the Drunkard
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Hans_the_Drunkard
--------------------------------------------------
Hans the Drunkard was one of the Nine Bad Cousins of Prince Ludovic and King Adolphus X. At some point in the past, he murdered Isola the Murderess in the midst of his inebriation and later died by falling down a flight of stairs and breaking his neck.[1] While his misdeeds were suppressed within High Norland itself, it became a common topic for study in Montalbino, as evidenced by the fact that Peter Regis knew about the Cousins. It is indeed later revealed that he was likely murdered by Ludovic in order to eliminate him as an obstacle to the throne and to ensure that the royals of High Norland would be portrayed in a bad light.[2]
