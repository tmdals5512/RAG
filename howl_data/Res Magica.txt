Title: Res Magica
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Res_Magica
--------------------------------------------------
Res Magica is a magical encyclopedia, a distinctive set of which is owned by William Norland. Soon after Peter Regis arrived at Norland's House, he studied these books from cover-to-cover and in order, although he notably struggled to read volume five, failing to understand the contents of it.[1] He recommended that Charmain Baker study it, and from the book they discovered the concepts of Lubbocks and lubbockin.

Volumes[]
The encyclopedia contained at least twenty-eight volumes, including the following:

Volume 3: Giroloptica
Volume 5: Panacticon
Volume 6: Hex
Volume 19: Advanced Seminal
Volume 27: Terrestrial Oneiromancy
Volume 28: Cosmic Oneiromancy
Behind the scenes[]
The title is of Latin origin and translates as "the matter of magic".
