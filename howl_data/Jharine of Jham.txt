Title: Jharine of Jham
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Jharine_of_Jham
--------------------------------------------------
The Jharine of Jham is the child princess of the kingdom of Jham, who was abducted by Hasruel on the orders of Dalzel.[1] She is described as "lumpish" and "swaddled in a coat", suggesting that she is not used to the temperate climate of Ingary.[2]
