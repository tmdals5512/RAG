Title: Transmontain
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Transmontain
--------------------------------------------------
Transmontain is a location adjacent to High Norland. Little is known about it, save for the fact that it was where Hans Nicholas, the father of Peter Regis and distant relative to the royal family of High Norland, was assassinated in an avalanche orchestrated by the lubbock so that its child, Prince Ludovic, could become the sole heir to the throne.[1] The Gretterhorns may be located in this region.
