Title: Mayor of Porthaven
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Mayor_of_Porthaven
--------------------------------------------------
Mayor of Porthaven is a minor character from Howl's Moving Castle directed by Hayao Miyazaki of Studio Ghibli.
The mayor is a patriot distributing leaflets in his beloved country Ingary. He visits Howl to convince him to join the army.
