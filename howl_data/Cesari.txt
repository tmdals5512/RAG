Title: Cesari
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Cesari
--------------------------------------------------
Mrs. Cesari is the owner of the pastry shop in Market Chipping. A good friend of Fanny Hatter, Cesari agreed to take Lettie Hatter on as an apprentice after Mr. Hatter died.
Sometimes written as "Chezarys" in Japanese merchandise.[1]
