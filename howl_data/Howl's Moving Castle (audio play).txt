Howl's Moving Castle is an audio play produced by Bafflegab, adapted from the novel of the same name. The play was broadcast on BBC Radio 4 as part of the BBC's Drama on 4 slot.[1]

Plot[]
The plot remains true to the plot of the novel, but abbreviates and shortens much of its content in order to fit within a one-hour timeslot. For example, the subplot involving Jane Farrier and the Count of Catterack is omitted, as is the subplot concerning Martha and Lettie swapping places using an illusion spell.
