Ben Sullivan's House is the residence of Royal Wizard Ben Sullivan and his wife Lettie Hatter.

Physical description[]
Located within the Old Quarter of Kingsbury, the capital city of Ingary, the house is described as "a handsome narrow house with diamond-paned windows and interwoven magic signs painted on its old irregular walls" and "tall spires of yellow flame burning in brass stands on either side of the front door."[1]
