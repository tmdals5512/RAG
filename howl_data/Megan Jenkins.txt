Title: Megan Jenkins
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Megan_Jenkins
--------------------------------------------------
Megan Jenkins is the sister of Howl Jenkins Pendragon and the mother of Neil and Mari Jenkins. She, for her part, treats Howl with disdain, and looks down on him as a vagabond layabout, unaware of his status as a wizard in Ingary.
