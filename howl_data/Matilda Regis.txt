Matilda of Montalbino is the incumbent Witch of Montalbino and Peter Regis' mother.

Biography[]
Matilda was born as a cousin to the royal family of High Norland, and the fifth in the line of succession for the kingdom's throne, which she was not interested, being more interested in witchcraft than anything to do with statecraft. At some point she married her cousin Hans Nicholas and gave birth to Peter.[1] They moved to Montalbino where Matilda became a witch and Hans a mountaineer and adventurer. Matilda would later travel to Ingary to study magic under the distinguished sorceress Mrs Pentstemmon.[2] She would later consult Pentstemmon about how to keep her husband and son safe, and would later discover from her Ludovic's true identity as a lubbockin.[3]

Physical description[]
Matilda is described as having a "sun-weathered" countenance, "narrow" hands and being dressed in attire less evocative of a typical witch in fantasy fiction and more akin to a Romani, with "bright" albeit faded colours.[4]
