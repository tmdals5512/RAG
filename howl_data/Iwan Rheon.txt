Title: Iwan Rheon
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Iwan_Rheon
--------------------------------------------------
Iwan Rheon (born 13 May 1985)[1] is a Welsh actor most notable for his role as Ramsay Bolton in Game of Thrones. In addition to providing the voice for Remus Lupin in the full-cast audiobooks for the Harry Potter series, he also provided the voice for Howl Jenkins Pendragon in the 2021 BBC radio adaptation of Howl's Moving Castle.[2]
