Title: Gretterhorns
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Gretterhorns
--------------------------------------------------
The Gretterhorns are a perilous mountain range, located presumably in Transmontain. While not much is known about the region, Peter Regis' father, Hans Nicholas, organised a mountain rescue team up there at some point, only to be later killed in an avalanche which was caused by a Lubbock.[1]
