Not to be confused with Matilda Regis.
Matilda II was one of the Nine Bad Cousins of High Norland. According to Peter Regis, she became a witch so absolutely evil that Prince Ludovic, her second cousin, had her incarcerated within his home of Castel Joie, with him largely keeping a safe distance from her, with rumours circulating that she would kill people simply by gazing at them.[1]
