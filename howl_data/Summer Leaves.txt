Title: Summer Leaves
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Summer_Leaves
--------------------------------------------------
Summer Leaves are some type of confectionery which seem to be popular in Tsapfan. Years after their liberation from the djinn Dalzel's clutches, the princess of that country gifted a box full of them to Princess Valeria, in a box whose beauty was to such an extent that the princess repurposed it for use as a jewellery box, which were presented by Abdullah and Flower-in-the-Night following a diplomatic mission in the region.[1]
