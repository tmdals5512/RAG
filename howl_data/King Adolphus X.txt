King Adolphus X is the elderly king of High Norland and the father of Princess Hilda.

Physical description[]
The King is described as a large, elderly man with a neatly-trimmed marmoreal-coloured beard and kindly eyes of blue, and dressed in far more humble attire than his daughter.[1]

Personality[]
The King is described by Sim as an apparent canine aficionado having attempted to befriend them and having been bitten by them several times.[2] He appears to be entirely dependent on the aid of his daughter, as Hasruel mocked him behind his back for relying on his daughter to help him organise his library, and stating that, by comparison, Abdullah was far more proactive in attempting to rescue anyone than Adolphus ever was.[3]
