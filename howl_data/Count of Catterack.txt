The Count of Catterack is a nobleman who is a high-ranking member of the King of Ingary's court.

Biography[]
The Count at some point eloped with a girl known as Jane Farrier, which later proved to be a point of gossip for the women of Folding Valley. Because of this, Sophie Hatter took advantage of a customer by claiming that one of her hat shop's products was the hat Farrier wore when she eloped with the count.
During Sophie's stay at the castle, following her transformation into an elderly crone, the Count was challenged to a duel by some opponent due to some unspecified offence, and so came to Sophie for assistance in the wizard's absence. Sophie was extremely reluctant to lend him aid, due to her misgivings concerning duels to the death, but nevertheless gifted him with a spell, informing him that once he threw it into the air, either he or his opponent would possess the chance of victory, but that victory would be entirely dependent upon him.
Following Sophie's visit to the King in an attempt to blacken Howl's name, she later encountered the Count, who thanked her for her assistance and informed her that he had won the duel.
