Title: Belle
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Belle
--------------------------------------------------
Belle is one of the members of the faculty within Mr Baker's bakery in High Norland City. She appears to be friends with Charmain, and her connection with her proved to be beneficial for the latter when the latter was about to be dismissed by the junior apprentice Timmy on the grounds that he was unable to prove her true identity and did not believe her. She also informed Charmain to leave Waif by the door to the bakery, since its staff were industriously occupied at the time.[1]
