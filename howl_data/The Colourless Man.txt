Title: The Colourless Man
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/The_Colourless_Man
--------------------------------------------------
The Colourless Man was an accomplice of Prince Ludovic and the Lubbock. While not much is known about the man's origins, it is discovered that he is a lubbockin.[1] During the Norlandese Royal Family's search for their lost money, the Colourless Man worked openly as a steward for the family, and apparently had worked at the Royal Mansion, for a long time, to the extent that his actual name had been forgotten even by King Adolphus X, who also reckoned that his daughter, the Princess Hilda, might be able to know.[2] The man also appears to have been a magic user as well, as evidenced when he tried to cast a spell to get Ludovic to safety, only for the family's aging butler, Sim, to drop a gold brick on his toe.[3] The gentlemen then shifted into the form of a white rabbit, before Waif ambushed and killed him.[4]
