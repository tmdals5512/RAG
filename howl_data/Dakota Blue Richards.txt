Dakota Blue Richards (born 1994) is an English actress, and former child actress. At the age of thirteen, she made her film debut in The Golden Compass, where she portrayed the film's heroine, Lyra Belacqua. She portrayed the younger Sophie Hatter in the BBC radio adaptation of Howl's Moving Castle.[1]
