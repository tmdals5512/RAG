Dorimynde is a country located in the world in which Ingary exists. Very little is known about it other than the fact that its princess, the Damoiselle, was abducted by Hasruel on Dalzel's orders.[1]

Behind the scenes[]
Based upon the title of the Damoiselle, this country may be the world's equivalent to France. However, this is unconfirmed.
