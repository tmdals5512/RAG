Dan Starkey (born 27 September 1977)[1] is an English actor most notable for various appearances in the BBC science fiction television series Doctor Who. He provided the voice for Calcifer in the 2021 BBC Radio adaptation of Howl's Moving Castle produced by Bafflegab.[2]
