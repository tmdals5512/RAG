Title: Dazrah
URL: https://howlscastle.fandom.com//wiki/Dazrah
--------------------------------------------------
Dazrah was a Great Spirit who was the mother of Dalzel and Hasruel. At some point in her life, she was defiled by an unidentified evil djinn and therefore disowned Dalzel on account of the fact that he was both born hideously ugly and because of the fact that the respective natures of her and the djinn were incompatible.[1]
