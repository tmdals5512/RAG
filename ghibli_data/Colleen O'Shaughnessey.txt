Colleen Ann Villard  (sometimes credited as Colleen O'Shaughnessey) is an American voice actress who is best known for her roles as Jody Irwin in The Life and Times of Juniper Lee, Jazz Fenton in Danny Phantom, Sora Takenouchi from Digimon, Jenny from Transformers: Robots in Disguise (2001 cartoon), Charmy Bee and Miles "Tails" Prower in the Sonic the Hedgehog video game series, among others. She voiced the character of Karen, one of Sōsuke's best friend, in the English dub of Ponyo.
