Kabo is a city in the northwestern part of the Dorok Principalities [1]. It is on the lands of the Kabo Tribe. According to Yupa's map, it is on the road between Sapata and Semi, near theNaulim River.
When Yupa and the other people on the Valley Gunship reach Kabo, they find it overtaken by the fungus of the Daikaisho [2].
