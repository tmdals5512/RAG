Title: Beicho Katsura
URL: https://ghibli.fandom.com//wiki/Beicho_Katsura
--------------------------------------------------
Beichô Katsura was born on November 6, 1925, in Dalian, Liaoning, China as Kiyoshi Nakagawa. He was an actor, known for Pom Poko (1994) and Takajin one man (1998). He died on March 19, 2015, in Hyogo, Japan.
