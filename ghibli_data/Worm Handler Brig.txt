The Worm Handler Brig[1] is a old and much-used airship used by the Worm Handlers. It's a patchwork of old repairs and looks on it's last legs.
Yupa "hitches a ride" (aka stows away) on it from the Mining Town to the Worm Handler Enclave and later steals it to escape with Asbel and Ketcha[2]. They encounter a Dorok Air Monitor which shoots them down[3].
The brig's crash site is found by a Dorok Air Monitor and investigated[4] and the Worm Handlers attempt to salvage the brig's engines but are ordered to search of Yupa, Asbel and Ketcha, instead.
