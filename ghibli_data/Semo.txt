Semo is an autonomous state in the Periphery [1]. It is along the Ocean of Salt, between the Toxic Jungle and Epo, the Westernmost city of Torumekia. The Valley of the Wind is a close neighbor to the North.
Semo is part of the treaty with the Kingdom of Torumekia and required to bring a gunship and contingent when called on by the Vai Emperor. No specific gunship or chieftain is identified in the narrative.
