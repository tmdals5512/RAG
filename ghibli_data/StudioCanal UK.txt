Title: StudioCanal UK
URL: https://ghibli.fandom.com//wiki/StudioCanal_UK
--------------------------------------------------
StudioCanal UK (Formerly Optimum Releasing) is a British subsidiary of StudioCanal, and were the main distributors of Studio Ghibli movies in the United Kingdom from December 2003 until December 2022, following a lawsuit between the company and Wild Bunch International over the Netflix streaming deal.
The company released DVDs and Blu-Rays under the "The Studio Ghibli Collection" brand. The company also has the rights to several other Ghibli-related movies, including The Castle of Cagliostro and The Little Norse Prince.
