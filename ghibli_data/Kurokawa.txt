Kurokawa (黒川) is a character in The Wind Rises. 

About[]
He is the leader of a group of airplane designers at Mitsubishi Aircraft Company.
Kurokawa is first seen when Jiro is working at the designs of the plane and sketching.

Etymology[]
The surname Kurokawa means "black" (黒) (kuro) and "river, stream" (川) (kawa).
