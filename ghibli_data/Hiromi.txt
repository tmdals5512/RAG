Hiromi (ひろみ) is the best friend of Haru Yoshioka in The Cat Returns, directed by Hiroyuki Morita of Studio Ghibli. She was voiced by Hitomi Sato in the Original Japanese and Kristen Bell in the English Dub.

Story[]
     
Hiromi goes to class on Haru's school alongside Haru and Chika. She lives in an apartment. Her parents never appear neither mention in the film. In Aoi Hiragi's manga Kikyou no Sakukoro (桔梗の咲く頃) Hiromi is a protagonist.
There isn't much to mention in terms of her personality, only that she seems to be slightly opposite of Haru. Where Haru is more awkward and clumsy, she seems more outspoken, confident and outgoing, and has no qualms in voicing her opinion when it comes to the people she likes and dislikes, such as calling Prince Lune dumb for walking into the street, and the truck driver stupid for almost running the  cat over.
Hiromi also has a crush on Tsuge. She asked Haru to take over for cleaning the erasers after school, so she could attend his table tennis game, and was cheerful when he won.

Etymology[]
The name Hiromi is written in hiragana (ひろみ), which has no special meaning. However, when written in kanji (寛美, 裕美, 博美 or 弘美), it could possibly mean:
寛美 - "tolerant, generous" (寛) (hiro) and "beautiful" (美) (mi).
裕美 - "abundant" (裕) (hiro) and "beautiful" (美) (mi).
博美 - "command, esteem" (博) (hiro) and "beautiful" (美) (mi).
弘美 - "vast, broad, wide" (弘) (hiro) and "beautiful" (美) (mi).
