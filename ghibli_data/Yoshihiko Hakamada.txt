Yoshihiko Hakamada (袴田 吉彦 Hakamada Yoshihiko, born 16 July 1973, in Hamamatsu, Shizuoka Prefecture) is a Japanese actor who provided the voice of Baron Humbert von Gikkingen in The Cat Returns.
