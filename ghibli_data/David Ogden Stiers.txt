David Ogden Stiers (born 31 October 1942 in Peoria, Illinois, USA and died 3 March 2018) was an American actor, voice actor, and musician, noted for his roles in Disney films, the television series M*A*S*H as Major Charles Emerson Winchester III, and the science fiction drama The Dead Zone as Reverend Gene Purdy. He is also known for the role of District Attorney Michael Reston in the Perry Mason TV movies.
