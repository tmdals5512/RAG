Title: Bridget Hoffman
URL: https://ghibli.fandom.com//wiki/Bridget_Hoffman
--------------------------------------------------
Bridget Hoffman (born: March 5, 1957 (age 61 years), Los Angeles, CA) is an American actress and writer who has provided voices for a number of English-language versions of Japanese anime films and television series, usually under an alias such as Ruby Marlowe.

Filmogrpahy[]
The Castle of Cagliostro - Clarisse d'Cagliostro
Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind (2005 Disney version) - Additional Voices
My Neighbor Totoro (2005 Disney version) - Ryōko
Howl's Moving Castle - Additional Voices
From Up on Poppy Hill - Additional Voices
