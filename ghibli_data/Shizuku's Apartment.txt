Shizuku's Apartment is the home of the Tsukishima Family in the Studio Ghibli film Whisper of the Heart.
Shizuku's room is the most featured room, which she shares with her sister, Shiho. Shizuku is mainly seen writing in her room, so she spends the most time at her desk, sometimes even sleeping at it.
The kitchen is also featured as a small and fairly dark room where the family eat. Leading off from the kitchen is a workroom where Shizuku's parents work.
