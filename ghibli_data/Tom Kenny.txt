Thomas James "Tom" Kenny (born July 13, 1962) is an American actor, voice actor, and comedian who is best known for voicing SpongeBob SquarePants on the Nickelodeon animated series of the same name. His other known roles include Eduardo from Fosters Home for Imaginary Friends, Lumpus, and Slinkman from Camp Lazlo, Heffer from Rocko's Modern Life, Rabbit in Winnie the Pooh after Ken Sansom's death, and the Ice King in Adventure Time.
