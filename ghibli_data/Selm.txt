Selm






Gender
Male





Selm [1] is one of the People of the Forest. He helps Asbel, Yupa and Ketcha. He later turns out to be Ceraine's brother.

Biography[]
When Yupa, Abel and Ketcha became stranded in the Toxic Jungle escaping the Doroks [2], they are rescued by several Forest People, including Selm. Selm gives them shelter and helps them recover from their wounds. Then he gives them the means to depart the forest.
