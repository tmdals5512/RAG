The Angry Farmer is a minor character who appears in Grave of the Fireflies and My Neighbor Totoro.

In film[]
When Seita steals food from him in order to feed Setsuko, the man fires up into yelling and chases Seita. He then beats him and send him to the police. The policeman realized Seita is hungry and protect Seita claiming the farmer also committed a crime of assault. The farmer is shock and go back in his propriety.
In the live-action version his son is an officer of the Imperial Navy who died in combat off the coast of Malesia.
