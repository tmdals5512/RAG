Yuji Nomi (Nomi Yuji born July 19, 1958,Tokyo,Japan) is Japanese composer. His work includes the Studio Ghibli films Whisper of the Heart and The Cat Returns .His mentor was Ryuichi Sakamoto, where he helped him with The Last Emperor and The Wings of Honneamise.
