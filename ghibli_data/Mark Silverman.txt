Mark Silverman has played characters in several Studio Ghibli films including Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind  where he plays Jhil and Howl's Moving Castle where he plays the king.  Mark is also the voice of the scroungy little bearded person who sells the Sword in "Tales from Earthsea."

Filmography[]
Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind - Jhil, Niga (2005 Disney version)
Howl's Moving Castle - King of Ingary
Pom Poko - Additional Voices
Tales from Earthsea - Additional Voices
