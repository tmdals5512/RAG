Carlos Jaime Alazraqui (pronounced "Alla-zer-kee") is an American stand-up comedian, actor, voice actor, singer, impressionist, producer, and screenwriter best known for voicing Taco Bell Chihuahua in the Taco Bell commercials, Denzel Crocker and Juandissimo Magnifico on The Fairly OddParents, Rocko and Spunky on Rocko's Modern Life, Lazlo and Clam in Camp Lazlo, Granpapi Rivera in El Tigre: The Adventures of Manny Rivera, and as Mr. Weed in Family Guy.
