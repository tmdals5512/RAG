Daran Morrison Nordland, better known as Daran Norris, is an American actor, voice actor, and comedian who is best known for voicing Cosmo, Jorgen Von Strangle, and Mr. Turner in the Nickelodeon animated series The Fairly OddParents, as well as Ralph Runner in Loonatics Unleashed. He also starred as Bhudda Bob in Big Time Rush.
