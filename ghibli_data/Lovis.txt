Lovis is a supporting character who appears in Ronja, the Robber's Daughter. She is the mother of Ronja

Appearance[]
Lovis has brown eyes and reddish-brown hair tied into two braids with red ties. On either side of her head she wears two large medallion-like hair clips. She has two strands of hair hanging off her head, with red and white ribbons twisting around them.

Biography[]
Beyond taking care of Ronja, she holds a firm and well-established authority on all domestic matters in the fort: cooking, cleaning, but not all thanklessly; a mother-like figure to all the menfolk, save maybe Noddle-Pete, she will withdraw meals if chores aren't done first. Even her husband Mattis, a fierce and mercurial chief, recoils helplessly under her command in simple matters such as combing lice out of his hair.

Personality[]
Lovis is serene and rational.
