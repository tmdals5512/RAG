Torumekian Shield




Nausicaä fights the Torumekian Duelist, who uses a Torumekian Shield


Equipment

Type
shield




A Torumekian Shield, also known as a Torumekian Cavalry Shield, is a segmented shield used by Torumekian cavalry [1] and soldiers. The shield attaches to the off-weapon arm.
