Miki Hokuto (北斗 美樹, Hokuto Miki) is a character that appears in the Japanese film--From Up on Poppy Hill. She is a doctor-in-training staying at Coquelicot's apartment.

Appearance[]
Miki is shown to have brown hair that is usually tied back into a ponytail. 
She is seen wearing a yellow cardigan throughout the film.

Personality[]
There's nothing much to say about Miki Hokuto except that she is a laid-back and calm person who spends most of her time doing whatever she wants in the Coquelicot Manor. 

Voice actor[]
Miki Hokuto was voiced by Yuriko Ishida as San in Princess Mononoke and Okiyo in Pom Poko and Gillian Anderson in English.

Etymology[]
The name Miki means "beautiful" (美) (mi) and "tree" (樹) (ki).
Miki's surname Hokuto means "north" (北) (hoku) and "Dipper" (斗) (to).
