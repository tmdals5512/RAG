Taku Morisaki (杜崎 拓, Morisaki Taku) is the eighteen-years protagonist of Ocean Waves and he is voiced by Nobuo Tobita in Japanese.

Appearance & Personality[]
Taku has dark brown hair and brown eyes. He wears a white shirt and green tie as part of the Japanese school uniform. During the school year, he wears a light pink shirt and white T-shirt and blue 
The positives about Taku has shown to be a type of boy to be caring & brave enough to express anything. He shown to care a lot about Rikako & Yutaka in Highschool. Although he has shown how impulsive he can be. He does & says things all of a sudden without seeing the big elephant in the room. He’s expressed it many times by slapping Rikako in the face, and telling his Best-Friend Yutaka how she was getting bullied & how “brave” she was for standing up for herself & doing absolutely nothing about it. He’s shown to be the same person after 2 years & going to college but slightly changing that behavior. He has shown to be very well in socializing & keeping a conversation outgoing.

Etymology[]
The name Taku means "expand, open, support" (拓).
Taku's surname Morisaki means "forest, woods, grove" (杜) (mori) and "cape, peninsula" (崎) (saki).
