John DeMita ​(Born 6 January 1959 in New York, New York City) is an English voice actor for three Studio Ghibli films.

Filmography[]
Princess Mononoke - Kohroku
Castle in the Sky - Additional Voices (2003 Disney version)
Kiki's Delivery Service - Ket's Father, Additional Voices (1998 Disney version)
