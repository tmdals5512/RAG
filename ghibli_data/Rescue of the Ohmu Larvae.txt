Rescue of the Ohmu Larvae

Dorok is ruled by a Holy Emperor and a Council of Monks, so religion is an important part of Dorok life.
Each tribe is headed by a priest. The Mani Tribe is lead by the Holy One.
Dorok priests are bald or have shaved heads [1] and wear special face masks.
Priests sing songs, play instruments and dance to incite Dorok forces to "slay the heretics" prior to an attack on the Torumekian-held City-Fortress of Sapata [2].
Kushana reveals at the siege of Sapata that she expects the final assault to occur at noon [3] due to the Dorok religion. It is unknown what religious significance noon holds.
