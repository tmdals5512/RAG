Yuko Maruyama (born 22 October 1946 in Tokyo Japan) is an Japanese voice actress of two Studio Ghibli films.


Filmography[]
My Neighbor Totoro - Kanta's Mother (1988)
Kiki's Delivery Service - Additional Voices (1989)
