Georgina Gordon, simply known as Nanny is the main antagonist in When Marnie Was There and after The Tale of the Princess Kaguya. She's the abusive caretaker and guardian of Marnie and lives in the mansion.

Voices[]
Nanny was voiced by Kazuko Yoshiyuki as previous films such as Toki from Ponyo.
