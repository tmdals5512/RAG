Dorok Village Temple




Nausicaä flies towards the Dorok temple in the Nameless Dorok Village



The Dorok Village Temple [1] is a temple found in the Nameless Dorok Village.
The temple is a large three story building with a dome and four spires. Five semi-circular steps lead up to a large double door. The doors each have an open-mouthed fish on them, facing each other. The interior of the temple is one giant room.
In the center of the temple is a large, egg shaped statue that has a bare-breasted mermaid clutching a large pearl in front of fronds on her left and a trio of fish on her right. The mermaid is facing forward with her head turned to her right. The sides of the statue has a giant turtle carved in it. The other side of the statue is not visible.
