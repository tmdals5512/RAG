Title: Hitoshi Takagi
URL: https://ghibli.fandom.com//wiki/Hitoshi_Takagi
--------------------------------------------------
Hitoshi Takagi (born 26 February 1925 and died 11 February 2004 with heart failure) was a Japanese actor who performed Totoro in the film My Neighbor Totoro. His last film is Mei and the Kittenbus.

Roles[]
Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind - God Warrior (possibly voice)
My Neighbor Totoro - Totoro and animal voices
Mei and the Kittenbus - Totoro (uncredited)
