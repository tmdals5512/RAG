Tarako (December 17, 1960 in Ota Gunma, Prefecture, Japan) is a Japanese actress and songwriter. Her most notable role was as Madge in Studio Ghibli's film Castle in the Sky.

Role[]
Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind (boy) (1984)
Castle in the Sky (Madge) (1986)
My Neighbor Totoro (friend b) (1988)
