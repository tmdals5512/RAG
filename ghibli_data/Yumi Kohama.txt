Yumi Kohama (小浜 裕実, Kohama Yumi) is a minor character of Ocean Waves film. She is a closest friend of Rikako Muto.

Etymology[]
The name Yumi means "abundant" (裕) (yu) and "fruit, good result, truth" (実) (mi).
Yumi's surname Kohama means "small" (小) (ko) and "beach, seashore" (浜) (hama).
