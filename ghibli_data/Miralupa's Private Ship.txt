Miralupa's Private Ship




Miralupa's private ship arrives at the siege of Sapata


Vehicle Details

Type
private yacht



Miralupa's private ship [1] arrived at the siege of Sapata to oversee the final push to oust the Torumekian forces.
The ship looks like a oblong sphere with stubby wings. The wings are forward on the fuselage, leaving only a slight dome at the front. The cockpit has two large bulbus windows and the landing gear looks like two half-spheres coming out of the fuselage. The wings have no visible leading edge slats or trailing edge flaps. Each wing has one engine. There is no distinct empennage.
