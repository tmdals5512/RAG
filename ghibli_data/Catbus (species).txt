Abilities[]
Catbuses are individuals of bakeneko that transformed to mimic human vehicles for personal interests. 
As being bakeneko, they can perform variety of supernatural abilities aside from transformation. As seen in My Neighbor Totoro, Catbuses apparently can either control or command trees to make ways. They can also fly, run vertically, walk on unstable objects such as electric wires, turn invisible, emit lights, sense calls from a distance, and detect lost human. They are also apparently immune to electricity.
They also run extremely fast for very long and don't show any signs of weariness.
They also possess extremely long life spans; the Catbus appeared in My Neighbor Totoro is alive for centuries, and he was mimicing a kago in Edo period.
