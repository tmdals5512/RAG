Dyanne DiRosario is an American voice actress, actress and make-up artist. She has provided voices for a number of English-language versions of Japanese anime films and television series, usually under the alias Bambi Darro, but sometimes under the alias Bambi Darrow, Bambi Preston or Dyanne de Rosario.
