Bradley Michael Pierce (born October 23, 1982) is an American actor and voice actor with numerous roles and bit parts in television, movies, direct-to-video animation, advertising, and video games. He is best known for playing Peter Shepherd in the 1995 TriStar film Jumanji, and voicing Miles "Tails" Prower in the SatAM version of Sonic the Hedgehog.
