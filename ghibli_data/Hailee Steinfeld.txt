Title: Hailee Steinfeld

--------------------------------------------------
Hailee Steinfeld is an American actress and singer who voiced Anna Sasaki in the GKids English Dub for When Marnie Was There. She is known for her role in multiple films such as True Grit, Pitch Perfect 2 & 3, BumbleBee and Spider Verse. When she saw When Marnie Was There in Japanese, she enjoyed it and wanted to be a part of it.
In an interview, she said she admires Anna and felt connected to the role. She felt that she is similar to other girls in the world. Hailee admits she admires Anna as being a strong person.
