Cloris Leachman (April 30, 1926-January 26, 2021) is an American television actress who played the role of Aunt Louise Trends in Disney's Herbie Goes Bananas, Doc Betty in Miracle Child, Nurse Spex in Sky High, and Lew Lord's secretary Miss Tracy in The Muppet Movie.
She also guest-starred in Episode 224 of The Muppet Show and played Maggie the zombie expert in My Boyfriend's Back. She is also as Maw Maw on FOX sitcom series Raising Hope from 2010 to 2014.
