Kiyoshi Yokokawa (横川 清 Yokokawa Kiyoshi) is a Lieutenant of the Imperial Japanese Navy who served on the IJN Heavy Cruiser Maya , the husband of Mrs. Yokokawa and the father of Seita Yokokawa and Setsuko Yokokawa.

Appearance[]
Kiyoshi has brown hair and dark eyes. 
In a flashback, he wears a Class-I Officers Uniform and a  Class-I Officers Service Cap but a photograph shows that he is seen wearing Class-II Officers Uniform.

Plot[]
Kiyoshi was supposedly in the Navy during World War II where Seita Yokokawa and Setsuko Yokokawa attempt to write to him several times, but they never received any reply from him.  
Much later at the bank, Seita overhears that the Japanese has lost the war and the entire fleet of ships has been sunk, which causes Seita to become astonished that he finally realizes why his father hasn't written back to them.
