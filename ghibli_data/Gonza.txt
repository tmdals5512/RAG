Gonza is the henchman of Lady Eboshi from Princess Mononoke directed by Hayao Miyazaki of Studio Ghibli. He was voiced by Tsunehiko Kamijo in the Japanese version and John DiMaggio in the English version.

Personality[]
He commands the soldiers in Irontown. He is a loyal henchman to Eboshi and is quick to draw his sword to defend her honor. Beside Eboshi, he distrusts all people, because many people like Asano try to occupy Irontown.
Despite being a former pirate, he can't swim.

History[]
He was once a pirate. After his leader was murdered, he follows Eboshi.[1]
