Genesis Machine

Equipment

Type
ancient device




A Genesis Machine [1] is a machine that "starts up" a God Warrior. One was discovered with the God Warrior in the Pit at Pejitei and the Pejitei engineers activated it, causing the God Warrior to start growing.
It appears as a large pitch-black cube. A panel opens revealing two depressions with a Control Stone in one of them. Placing the Control Stone into the other depression starts the genesis process to revive the God Warrior.
