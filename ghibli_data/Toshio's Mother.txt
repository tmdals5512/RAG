Toshio's Mother is a character in Studio Ghibli's film, Only Yesterday. She is Toshio's mother and she lives in a house in Yamagata.

Appearance[]
Toshio's Mother wears white hood and blue dress with a green undershirt and collar.

Voice Acting[]
Toshio's Mother was voiced by Takako Sendou and in English version is Tara Sands.
