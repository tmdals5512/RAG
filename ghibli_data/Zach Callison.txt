Zach Callison (born October 23, 1997) is an American voice, television and film actor. He is known for his roles in Disney shows including Prince James from Sofia the First, Billy from I'm in the Band and additional voices from Kinect Disneyland Adventure. He is also noted for being the voice of Steven from Steven Universe, King Tut in the film, Mr. Peabody and Sherman, Young Jiro in The Wind Rises and Billy Baston in the direct-to-video movies Superman/Shazam!: The Return of Black Adam (2010) and Justice League: War (2014).
