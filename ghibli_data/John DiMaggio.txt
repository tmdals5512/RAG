Title: John DiMaggio
URL: https://ghibli.fandom.com//wiki/John_DiMaggio
--------------------------------------------------
John DiMaggio is an American voice actor and comedian who is best known for his work as Bender from the Emmy award-winning cartoon Futurama, Jake the Dog on Adventure Time, Marcus Fenix from the Gears of War video game series, and Aquaman in Batman: The Brave and the Bold.
