Masaki Suda ((菅田 将暉,  birth name Taishō Sugō 菅生 大将) is a Japanese actor and singer. He was chosen as a voice actor for the The Gray Heron in The Boy and the Heron in 2023.  He has worked in music industry with Aimyon.
