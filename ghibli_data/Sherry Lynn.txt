Sherry Lynn is a veteran American voice actress, known for young girl roles in anime, cartoons, and video games.
Her Disney roles included Adella in The Little Mermaid, Marilyn Piquel and Katya Leggsgowannnalot in Bonkers and various others listed below.

Filmogrpahy[]
Kiki's Delivery Service - Madame's Granddaughter, Additional Voices (1998 Disney version)
Porco Rosso - Additional Voices (2005 Disney version)
Princess Mononoke - Emishi Village Girl, Iron Town Woman
Spirited Away - Additional Voices
Ponyo - Additional Voices
