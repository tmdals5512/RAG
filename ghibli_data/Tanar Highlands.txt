The Tanar Highlands [1] are highlands in the very north of the Dorok Principalities. It is located near the cities of Nomisto and Mitosu.
Since Nomitsu is a Torumekian colony and the highlands are located north of the Shil "Border" river, it is unclear whether it is under Torumkeian or Dorok control.
