Torumekian Second Army




Strike bombers from the Second Army attack a Dorok village


The Torumekian Second Army [1] is a Torumekian army involved in the the main Torumekian military campaign. It is presumably lead by one of the Three Princes.
Kushana's reduced force encounters strike bombers from the Second Army attacking a small Dorok village [2]. Kushana's Armored Corvette shoots down the strike bombers.
