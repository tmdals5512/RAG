Earwig's mother is the overarching protagonist of the film Earwig and the Witch.

Plot[]
Earwig's mother is the first character seen in the film, running from a yellow vehicle in a motorcycle with Earwig swaddled to her chest. She disobeyed Witch Code and is running from 12 crones. She leaves Earwig at an orphanage to protect her. She was the lead singer of the band Earwig before falling off the map.

Appearance[]
Earwig's mother is extremely beautiful with thick red hair and a lovely voice. She seems to not age at all.

Personality[]
Earwig's mother is shrouded in mystery, it is unknown what provoked her to break Witch Code, if it was her becoming a mother or something else. Not much is known about her other than that she is willful and does eventually come back for Earwig.

Trivia[]
Her name are unknown.
