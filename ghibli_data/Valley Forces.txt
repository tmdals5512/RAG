Valley Forces




The four old warriors sing a song while flying in the Valley Barge



The Valley Forces are the military of the Valley of the Wind. They include, among others, Nausicaä, Mito and four more men from the Castle.
A bone of contention with some of the younger people of the Valley is the fact that all five warriors that Nausicaä brings on the Periphery Campaign are old men but Nausicaä chose them because the youth are too valuable to risk in battle [1].
Nausicaä and Mito pilot the Valley Gunship while the other four (as well as Kai) are towed in the Valley Barge.
