Ettie and Lilly DeVille, simply known as The Maids are the secondary antagonists in When Marnie Was There. They are resident maids who stay with Marnie in the mansion, and they bully her while her parents were traveling.

Trivia[]
In the original book, they were not twins and and their personalities are different. In the movie, they have changed into twins with similar appearances.
In the novel, Lilly is really nice. She makes fries for Marnie in bed. Ettie is nasty and likes to scare people. The maids argued about stealing Ettie's boyfriend. Ettie drags Marnie to the silo to scare her. In the anime, Ettie and Lilly were bullying Marnie. The anime Lilly is not like the book Lilly.
They were fan-named Ursula and Ella. And their fan-names shared and similar to Ursula from Disney's The Little Mermaid and Cruella De Vil from 101 Dalmatians as the twin maids with their last names like sisters.
