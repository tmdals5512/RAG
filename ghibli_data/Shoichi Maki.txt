Shoichi Maki (牧勝一, Maki Shoichi) is a supporting protagonist from of the film The Boy and the Heron. 
He is the father of Mahito Maki.

Trivia[]
Shoichi's voice actors in both the Japanese and English dubs (Takuya Kimura and Christian Bale) provided the voice of Howl in the same dubs for Howl's Moving Castle. The casting of Kimura in another Ghibli film was intentional on the part of the studio, and the producers of the English dub, GKIDS, were encouraged to pull the same casting stunt.[1]
