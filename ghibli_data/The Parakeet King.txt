The Parakeet King (インコ大王, Inko-Daiō, lit. parrot great king) is an antagonist in the film The Boy and the Heron.

Description[]
The Parakeet King is a humanoid parakeet who looks less animalistic than the other parakeet persons. On his head is a crown, and he carries a sword. Unlike his subjects, he also sports fangs in his beak.
