Three Princes






Gender
Male




The Three Princes [1] were the three oldest children of the Vai Emperor of Kingdom of Torumekia. Their younger sibling was Kushana. There was no love lost between them and Kushana.
They were granted command of the Main Campaign of the Dorok Campaign by their father.
