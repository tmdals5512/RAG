Tomoko Tabata (田畑 智子 Born: December 26, 1980 in Higashiyama-ku, Kyoto, Kyoto Prefecture, Japan) is a Japanese actress. She won the best supporting actress award from the Mainichi in 2004 for The Hidden Blade and Blood and Bones, and the best actress award at the Mainichi Film Awards for Fugainai Boku wa Sora o Mita in 2012.
