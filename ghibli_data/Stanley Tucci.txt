Stanley Tucci (born November 11, 1960) is an American actor, writer, film producer, and film director known for his roles as George Harvey in Peter Jackson's The Lovely Bones, in which he was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor, as Caesar Flickerman in The Hunger Games franchise, and as Mitchell Garabedian in the critically acclaimed biographical drama, Spotlight.
