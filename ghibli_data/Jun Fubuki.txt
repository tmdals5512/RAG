Jun Fubuki (born May 12, 1952 in Toyama City, Japan) is a Japanese actress and voice actress of Tenar in Tales from Earthsea (2006), Ryoko Matsuzaki in From Up on Poppy Hill (2011) and Utako in The Boy and the Heron (2023). She has appeared in Kiyoshi Kurosawa's films such as Seance, Charisma and Pulse.

Filmography[]
Tales from Earthsea - Tenar
From Up on Poppy Hill - Ryoko Matsuzaki
The Boy and the Heron - Utako
