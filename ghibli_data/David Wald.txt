David Wald (born May 11) is an American actor who voices in English-language dubs of Japanese anime. Some of his major roles include: Gajeel Redfox in Fairy Tail, Bulat in Akame ga Kill!, and Master Chief in Halo Legends. He is involved in productions for Funimation and ADV Films (now Seraphim Digital and Sentai Filmworks) in Texas.
