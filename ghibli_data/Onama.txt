Onama[1] are spirits in the film Spirited Away directed by Hayao Miyazaki of Studio Ghibli.

Physical Appearance[]
They have horns on their head and long teeth. They wear cloak out of leaves.

Plot[]
They are first seen at the entrance bridge, when Chihiro Ogino try to get into the Bathhouse by Haku's help. Later on, Onama are seen on different occasion in the Bathhouse.
