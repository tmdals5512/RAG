The Neglected Garden ( 荒れた庭 Areta Niwa) is the opening insert song for the film The Secret World of Arrietty.

         Arriety OST - The Neglected Garden - Cecile Corbel The Neglected Garden  
It was written by Simon Caby / Cecile Corbel originally in French and English
Lyrics: Cecile Corbel
Translation: Mikiko Takeda, Nao Amisaki
Vocal: Cecile Corbel
