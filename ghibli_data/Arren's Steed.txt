Arren's Steed is a steed used by Arren and Ged appears in Tales from Earthsea.
He was first seen in the desert of Earthsea when Arren left his home. 

Trivia[]
Although he was refereed as a "horse" in the film, he anatomically differs from modern equidae. He resembles several clades of herbivores such as camelidae and equidae and cervidae.
Both Arren's Steed and Yakul from Princess Mononoke were based on Yakul appeared in the The Journey of Shuna.
