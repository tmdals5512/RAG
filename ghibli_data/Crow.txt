Crow






Japanese name
カラス科 (Karasu-ka)


Species
Bird


First Appearance
The Secret World of Arrietty


Role
Supporting character





The crow is a minor enemy in the movie The Secret World of Arrietty, directed by Hiromasa Yonebayashi of Studio Ghibli.
The crow is aggressive and doesn't hold back its anger while attacking a cat. It attempts to go after Arrietty, who is saved by Sho.
