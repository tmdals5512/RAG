Title: Chika Sakamoto
URL: https://ghibli.fandom.com//wiki/Chika_Sakamoto
--------------------------------------------------
Chika Sakamoto (born August 17, 1959) voiced Mei Kusakabe in My Neighbor Totoro and Boy A in Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind, which are both films by Studio Ghibli.

Filmography[]
Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind - Boy A
My Neighbor Totoro - Mei Kusakabe
Kiki's Delivery Service - Baby
Mei and the Kittenbus - Mei Kusakabe
