Pagase is an autonomous state in the Periphery [1]. It is North of Pejitei and Northwest of Valley of Sand.
Pagase is part of the treaty with the Kingdom of Torumekia and required to bring a gunship and contingent when called on by the Vai Emperor.
Pagase's gunship is the "Old Soldier," the oldest ship in the Periphery. Nausicaä and the Valley Forces encountered the Pagase gunship when rendezvousing with the other Periphery forces for the Periphery Campaign [2].
