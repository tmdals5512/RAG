King Jhil's Private Room




Nausicaä talks with her father in his room


King Jhil's Private Room [1] in the Castle contains a large bed, his armor on a stand next to it and a glider (probably his glider when he flew) suspended above the bed. The room has at least two large windows and is assumed to be on the upper floors of the Castle.
