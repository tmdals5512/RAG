Non-Muggles are a fictional subspecies of humans, often depicted to be born with magical abilities and have extraordinarily long lives, that appear in many Ghibli movies. Males are known as "wizards" or "warlocks", while females are known as "witches".
It is a common misconception that the word "mage" refers specifically to Non-Muggle. In fact, the word is a broader term for any user of magic, and can refer to a sorcerer or sorceress, an ordinary human (also known as a Muggle) who acquired magical skills through self-learning.
