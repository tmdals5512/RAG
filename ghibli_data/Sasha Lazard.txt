Dominique "Sasha" Lazard is an American classical crossover singer. Lazard was born in New York City and grew up in Paris. She attended Bennington College and the San Francisco Conservatory of Music, studying opera, but after graduation pursued a more electronic-influenced sound inspired by the local nightclub scene.[1]
She sang the song Mononoke Hime for the English dub of Princess Mononoke.
