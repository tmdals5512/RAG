Toho Co., Ltd （東宝株式会社, Toho kabushiki gaisha) is a Japanese film production, distribution and entertainment company and is one of the largest Japanese film companies in Japan. It is one of the four members of the Motion Picture Producers Association of Japan (MPPAJ) alongside Toei Company, Shochiku and the Kadokawa Corporation. Best known for their kaiju movies, Toho is also the Japanese theatrical distributor of most of the films from Studio Ghibli and the distributor of the films from Studio Ponoc.
