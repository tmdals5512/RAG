Swaine






Gender
Male


First Appearance
Ni no Kuni




Swaine is the third party member in Ni no Kuni. He is ragged looking with unkempt clothes, uses a pistol as his weapon, and has a rather negative outlook. He was introduced as a thief, but is later revealed to be the older brother of one of the Great Sages.
