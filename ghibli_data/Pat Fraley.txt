Title: Pat Fraley

Patrick Howard "Pat" Fraley is an American voice actor and voice-over teacher, who is best known voicing Krang, Baxter Stockman, Casey Jones, Burne Thompson, and other characters in the 1987 animated television series Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles.

     
Filmography[]
Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind - Teto (1985 dub)
Laputa: Castle in the Sky - Animal Vocal Effects (2003 Disney dub)
Porco Rosso - Additional Voices (2005 Disney dub)
Princess Mononoke - Additional Voices
Tales from Earthsea - Weapon Salesman, Advisor #2
