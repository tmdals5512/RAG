The Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind is a manga series. 
Originally individual comic books published in Japan, the series has been translated into multiple languages. The English translations have been combined into several editions of graphic novels, some two volumes and some seven volumes. On July 19th, 2018, Viz Media announced that they were to release Hayao Miyazaki's The Art of Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind book and Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind Picture Book in spring 2019.

"In a long-ago war, humankind set off a
devastating ecological disaster.
Thriving industrial societies disappeared.
The earth is slowly submerging beneath 
the expanding Sea of Corruption,
an enormous toxic forest that creates 
mutant insects and releases a miasma
of poisonous spores..." [1]
