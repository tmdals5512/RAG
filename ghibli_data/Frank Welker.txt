Frank Welker






Characters
Gol, Totoro, Catbus, Mamma Aiuto Gang


Frank Welker (born 12 March, 1946) is an American voice actor. He has voiced numerous roles for Studio Ghibli. 

Roles[]
Gol - Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind
Animal vocal effects - Castle in the Sky
Totoro and Catbus - My Neighbor Totoro
Mamma Aiuto Gang - Porco Rosso
