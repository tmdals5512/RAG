Ocean of Salt







Type
Ocean


Location
North of Torumekia



The Ocean of Salt [1] is an ocean to the North of Torumekia. Several Periphery states are along it, including the Valley of the Wind, Semo and Waji Valley.
"In those lost days, man had been abandoned even by munificence of the sea. The ocean had become the final resting place for all the poisonous substances scattered across the face of the planet..." [2].
