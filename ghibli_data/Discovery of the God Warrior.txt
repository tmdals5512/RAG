Discovering the God Warrior [1] in Pejitei.
Asbel explains to Yupa the discovery of the God Warrior in the Pit at Pejitei.
Finding a God Warrior isn't rare but this one was just the ultra hard ceramic skeleton/shell with a large black cube attached with cables. A panel opened in the cube revealing a space with two small depressions, one with the Control Stone in it.
The Pejitei engineers moved the stone to the other depression but nothing happened for several days until they discovered that the God Warrior was growing.
The Control Stone was removed and the engineers at Pejitei tried to destroy the God Warrior with explosives and fire but it had no effect.
Soon thereafter, the Torumekian's invaded after learning of the God Warrior from their spies.
