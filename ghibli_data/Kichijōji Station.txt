Kichijōji Station is a station in Tokyo depicted in Ocean Waves film.
When Taku Morisaki is in railway platform and waiting train and at the end of the film Taku reunited with Rikako Muto. The first and last scene of the film take place at the station.
There is also a film poster of Porco Rosso to be found at the end of the film.
