Title: Pamela Adlon
URL: https://ghibli.fandom.com//wiki/Pamela_Adlon
--------------------------------------------------
Pamela Adlon (born July 9, 1966) is an American voice actress of Studio Ghibli's English dub. She was the voice actor of characters such as Ket in Kiki's Delivery Service and did various voices in Princess Mononoke.

     
     
     
Filmography[]
Kiki's Delivery Service - Ket
Princess Mononoke - various voices
