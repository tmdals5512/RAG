Yōko Honna (本名 陽子, Honna Youko, born January 1, 1979 in Soka, Saitama, Japan)[1] is a Japanese actress. She has provided the voices for Taeko Okajima in Only Yesterday, Shizuku Tsukishima in Whisper of the Heart, and and additional voice in The Cat Returns, all films by Studio Ghibli.
