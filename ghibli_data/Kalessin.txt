About[]
He is an ancient and powerful dragon, and is the eldest dragon who leads the species. 
Similar to some of other dragons appeared in the film, Kalessin is a white-colored dragon with red-eyes.

Relationships[]
Tehanu and Unnamed Son[]
They are two of children of Kalessin.

Ged[]
In the books, Kalessin supports Ged to restore the world.

Trivia[]
In the books, it is implied that Kalessin may actually be Segoy, the creator of all the lands in  Earthsea, or some guise of Segoy.
