Betty Marion White (born January 17, 1922) is an American actress, comedienne, singer, author and television personality who was best known for her roles as Sue Ann Nivens on the CBS sitcom The Mary Tyler Moore Show from 1973 to 1977, Rose Nylund (her most notable Disney contribution) on the NBC sitcom The Golden Girls from 1985 to 1992 and starred as Elka Ostrovsky on the TV Land sitcom Hot in Cleveland from 2010 to 2015.
