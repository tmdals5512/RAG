Shuwa [1] is the capital of the Dorok Principalities and is often referred to as the Holy City of Shuwa [2]. It is located in the southwest of the Principalities in the Holy Valley.
The city contains the Great Monastery at Shuwa and the Crypt of the Holy Emperor [3].
