Title: Brian Posehn
URL: https://ghibli.fandom.com//wiki/Brian_Posehn
--------------------------------------------------
Brian Edmund Posehn is an American actor, voice actor, musician, writer, and comedian who is best known for voicing Jim Kuback on The WB animated series Mission Hill from 1999 to 2002 and for his role as Brian Damien Spukowski on the Comedy Central comedy series The Sarah Silverman Program. For Disney, he voices Hayashi in Pom Poko.
