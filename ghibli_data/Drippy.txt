Drippy is a character as seen in the game Ni no Kuni. Mr. Drippy is Oliver's companion who starts the game as a doll given to Oliver by his mom, but comes to life when struck by a tear. Mr. Drippy is a fairy, and is quite vocal with his opinions.

Appearance[]
Drippy has beige skin with spiky nose holding lantern. He wears a blue pants
