Doctor  Yamashita is a character as seen in the Studio Ghibli film When Marnie Was There.

Story[]
He is the family doctor of Anna Sasaki. He discusses with her mother Yoriko Sasaki about many things. For example, he wonders about Anna's vacation in Kissakibetsu with her relatives Mr. and Mrs. Ouiwa.

Etymology[]
The surname Yamashita means "mountain, hill" (山) (yama) and "under, below" (下) (shita).
