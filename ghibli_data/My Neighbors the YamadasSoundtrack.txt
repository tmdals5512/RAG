Title: My Neighbors the Yamadas/Soundtrack
URL: https://ghibli.fandom.com//wiki/My_Neighbors_the_Yamadas/Soundtrack
--------------------------------------------------
The soundtrack for My Neighbors the Yamadas was composed by Akiko Yano and is characterized by very short piano themes. Classical pieces were played by the Czech Pilharmonic Orchestra and were conducted by Mario Klemens. The music was released by Tokuma on July 1, 1999.

    My Neighbors the Yamadas O.S.T.
