Ōna is the wife of Miyatsuko in the film of The Tale of the Princess Kaguya. She is helpful by raising up Kaguya and initially in the beginning, she insists to her husband that she must be raised properly and after moving to the capital to understand how her daughter feels about being a noble princess.
