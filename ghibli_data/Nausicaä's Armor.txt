Nausicaä's Armor






Equipment

armor



Nausicaä's Armor [1] is a scale armor made by the Wise Women of the Valley of the Wind, charmed "every scale, spells to protect you from arrow and shell...."
Nausicaä wears it on the Periphery Campaign but abandons it during the escape from the Toxic Jungle with Asbel after Asbel's Attack.
