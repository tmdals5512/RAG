Bida Tribe




The Holy One witnesses the destruction of the Bida Tribe troops at the Battle of the Acid Lakes



Type
tribe


Location
Dorok Principalities


Capital
Bida



The Bida Tribe field the Dorok Suicide Troops during the Battle of the Acid Lakes [1]. Their defense, an Ohmu Shell pillbox, is destroyed by the rampaging Ohmu [2].
The Holy One reports the warriors' destruction to a representative of the Council of Monks in the Worm Handler Enclave [3].
