Yoshikazu Mera - Mononoke Hime (Princess Mononoke) - Performance  
Yoshikazu Mera (米良 美一 Mera Yoshikazu born May 21, 1971, in Miyazaki, Japan) is a Japanese countertenor. His range is three and a half octaves. He performed the song Mononoke Hime for the film Princess Mononoke
