Chieftain of Pagase






Gender
Male




The Chieftain of Pagase [1] leads the Periphery city-state of Pagase. He captains the "Old Soldier", Pagase's gunship and the oldest ship in the Periphery, during the Periphery Campaign.
It is unclear if he survives the defeat of Kushana's joint Torumekian and Periphery Forces after the disastrous Battle of the Acid Lakes nor is it known if he makes it to the Valley of the Wind to join with the other Periphery chieftains in the decision leading to the Breaking of the Periphery Treaty.
