Richard Epcar is an American voice actor. He appeared in the Manga version of the English dub. 

Biography[]
He was born on 4 April 1955 in Denver Colorado.

Filmography[]
The Castle of Cagliostro - Goemon Ishikawa XIII,  Daisuke Jigen (2025 dub)
