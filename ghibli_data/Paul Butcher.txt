Paul Matthew Hawke Butcher Jr. is an American actor, voice actor and singer. He is best known for his role as Dustin Brooks in the Nickelodeon comedy sitcom Zoey 101.

Filmography[]
Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind - Guard Boy (2005 Disney dub)
My Neighbor Totoro - Kanta Ōgaki (2005 Disney dub)
