Richard Anthony "Cheech" Marin (born July 13, 1946) is an American stand-up comedian, actor, voice actor, writer, and activist known for voicing Banzai in The Lion King media. He provided the English voice for Hare in the Disney dub of the film Tales from Earthsea.
