Torumekia's airships.  
An Airship is an armoured flying vehicle which was used in Studio Ghibli's film Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind.
The design of Torumekia's battleship is inspired by USS Enterprise from Star Trek.

Airships[]
Mehve is a glider ride by Nausicaä.
Beside Mehve, Valley of the Wind's Army has a bigger aircraft with guns.
Pejite's Army has their own battleships.
Torumekia's Army has big airships and small ones. The big ones are used to attack Valley of the Wind.
