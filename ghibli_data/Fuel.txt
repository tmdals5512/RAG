Fuel




The Torumekian Armored Corvette is hit by fire from attacking Dorok Air Monitors, damaging their fuel tanks


Equipment

Type
fuel



Fuel [1] is a form of energy used by airships to power them for a periodical time. The Torumekian Armored Corvette took damage to its starboard fuel and water tanks while fleeing some Dorok Air Monitors.
