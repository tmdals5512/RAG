Confucius (コンフューシャス, Konfūshasu) is a minor character appeared in Mary and the Witch's Flower. He is an old dog lives in the Red Manor.

Trivia[]
His name means Confucius or the "Master Kǒng", a Chinese philosopher and politician of the Spring and Autumn period.[1]
