Etymology[]
The name Nao is written in katakana (ナオ), which has no special meaning. However, when written in kanji (直, 奈央 or 奈緒), it could possibly mean:
直 - "straight, direct".
奈央 - "apple tree" (奈) (na) and "center" (央) (o).
奈緒 - "apple tree" (奈) (na) and "thread" (緒) (o).
