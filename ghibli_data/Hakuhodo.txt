Hakuhodo (株式会社博報堂 Kabushiki-gaisha Hakuhōdō) is a Japanese advertising and public relations company. It is headquartered at the Akasaka Biz Tower in Akasaka, Minato, Tokyo. They've assisted in the production of almost all of the Studio Ghibli movies. Starting with Tales from Earthsea, they've been credited as Hakuhodo Dymp.
