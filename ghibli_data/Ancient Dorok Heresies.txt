Ancient Dorok Heresies


The Ancient Dorok Heresies [1] are a group of beiefs held by some factions within the Dorok Principalities.
The ancient heresies include the belief that the Ohmu are sacred. These heresies were purged, presumably by the Council of Monks and the Holy Emperor's Younger Brother, except it still is held by the Holy One of the Mani Tribe.
