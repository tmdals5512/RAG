Akemi Ota (referred into in other sources as Akemi Miyazaki) is an animator. For Studio Ghibli, she worked on Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind and My Neighbor Totoro.

Personal life[]
She has been married to Hayao Miyazaki since 1965. Her sons are Goro Miyazaki and Keisuke Miyazaki.
