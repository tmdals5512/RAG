Astronomical Event[]
A Blue Moon occurs when a month has two full moons in it or four full moons within three months.

Colloquialism[]
Traditionally, the colloquialism Blue Moon means an unusual and not very frequent occurence.

In the World of Nausicaä[]
A Blue Moon seems to be a colloquialism, based upon the sentence structure used [1].
