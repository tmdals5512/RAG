Type
rifle


Appearance(s)

Nausicaä's rifle is a single-shot, bolt action long rifle owned by Nausicaä [1]. It has a wide butt-stock, a pistol-grip and a section removed from the trigger guard to allow shooting with gloves on.
It fires a variety of shells, including siren shells and signal flare shells.
