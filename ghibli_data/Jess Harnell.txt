Jess Harnell is an American voice actor, comedian, singer, and musician who is best known for his voice roles as Wakko Warner on the Warner Brothers animated series Animaniacs, Ironhide in the Transformers film franchise, and Crash Bandicoot in the Crash Bandicoot video game franchise. He is also the current announcer for the ABC reality television program America's Funniest Home Videos since 1998. He is also known for being the lead singer and musician of his rock band, Rock Sugar.
