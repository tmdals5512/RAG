Toshio (トシオ) is the main deuteragonist of Only Yesterday film. He is an organic farmer and a friend of Taeko Okajima who often drives her around.

Story[]
When Taeko arrives to his family's farm, Toshio is nice to her and drives her around and listens to her stories about her childhood. When his relatives noticed how well they got along, they started hinting that Taeko and Toshio would make a good couple but Taeko doesn't like this and walks away until Toshio picks her up and drives her back. He also helps her come to terms with some stories about her classmates including a bully who apparently liked ten year old Taeko.
By the end of the movie, Taeko decides to stay in the countryside and presumably starts a relationship with Toshio.
