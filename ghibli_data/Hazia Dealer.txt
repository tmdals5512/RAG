The Hazia Dealer is a minor character who appears in the film Tales from Earthsea directed by Goro Miyazaki of Studio Ghibli.

     
He sells the drug Hazia that makes you hallucinate for a short time. He waits in dark alleys for easy customers. Then he gives free samples to make his customers addicted to it. Arren meets him when he is new in Hort Town. But fortunately for Arren, Ged comes to his aid.
