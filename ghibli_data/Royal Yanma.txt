Royal Yanma are a type of large flying insects [1] native to the Toxic Jungle. They have four dragonfly-like wings and mandibles and can get up to 2 meters long. They serve as sentinels of the forest.
The Pejitei Brig was attacked by Royal Yanma [2], along with Landgrubs and Wing-worms.
Yanma, from Japanese, means "(large) hawker dragonfly".
