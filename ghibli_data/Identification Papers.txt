Torumekia[]
Kurotowa wears a Headstone of Authority [1], which looks like a small jewel that is held to the forehead with a slim band. Staff officers of the Torumekian military wear them.


Dorok[]
A type of identification paper is a Naming Tablet [2]. They seem to be some sort of travel permit. Charuka writes them for the Dorok Orphans. Whether free travel is permitted automatically with the tablets or if Charuka added that feature is unknown.
