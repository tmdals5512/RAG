Tib (ティブ, Tibu) is one of pet cats of Peter appeared in Mary and the Witch's Flower. He is also a companion of Mary. He and Gib are very close, and Zebedee suspects that they are mates.

Appearance[]
Tibs has slate blue fur with turquoise eyes.
