Horseclaw barding is armor specially designed for cavalry horseclaws.
Torumekian horseclaw barding [1] consists of a helmet, including a beak guard, scale body armor very similar to Roman Lorica Squamata, cavalry saddle and greaves for the horseclaw's legs [2].
