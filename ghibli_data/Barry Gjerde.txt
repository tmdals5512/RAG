Title: Barry Gjerde

Barry Gjerde was born Oddbjørn Egil Gjerde on August 13, 1951, in Vestnes, Norway. He has worked many years in Japan as a translator, mostly narrating various corporate projects and providing voice acting for video games.

Filmography[]
Porco Rosso - Radio Announcer, Telephone Voice, Munitions Engineer, Watchtower Announcer (JAL dub)
