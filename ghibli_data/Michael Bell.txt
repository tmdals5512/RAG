Michael Patrick Bell (born July 30, 1938) is an American actor, voice actor, voice director, and animal-rights activist who is most active in voice over roles. He is best known for doing Chaz Finster and Drew Pickles in Rugrats and All Grown Up. He is the husband of actress Victoria Carroll.
