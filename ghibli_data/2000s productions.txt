2001[]
    Spirited Away theatrical release poster  
July 20 - Spirited Away released in cinemas in Japan.
2002[]
July 19 - Spirited Away released on VHS & DVD in Japan.
July 20 - The Cat Returns released in cinemas in Japan.
September 7 - English Dub of Spirited Away by Disney premieres at the Toronto International Film Festival.
2003[]
April 15 - Spirited Away English Dub released on VHS & DVD.
2004[]
     
November 20 - Howl's Moving Castle released in cinemas in Japan.
2005[]
June - Howl's Moving Castle English Dub by Pixar released in North America.
2006[]
March 10 - Howl's Moving Castle released on DVD.
July 29 - Tales from Earthsea released in cinemas in Japan.
2008[]
July 19 - Template:Pon released in cinemas in Japan.
2009[]
January 1 - Ponyo released in cinemas in Southeast Asia.
August 14 - Ponyo English Dub released in cinemas in the USA & Canada.
