Shiori Fujita (born 22 December 1974 in Tokyo, Japan) is a Japanese voice actor who portrayed young Sheeta in Castle in the Sky.

Filmography[]
Castle in the Sky as Sheeta (child)
My Neighbor Totoro as School Kids
