Not to be confused with LastelleThe Pejite Girl is an unnamed minor character in the Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind film. She helps Nausicaä escape from captivity aboard the Pejite airship by temporarily swapping clothes with Nausicaä and taking her place in the holding cell, which later results in her pink dress getting dyed blue by the blood of the Ohmu larvae.
Plot[]
She is a Pejite citizen who helps Nausicaä escape from captivity aboard the Pejite airship, with the help of Lastelle's Mother, by temporarily swapping clothes with Nausicaä and taking her place in the holding cell, which, due to their close resemblance, allows Nausicaä to escape with her Möwe. She later leaves the cell and helps Asbel and Yupa protect the other Pejite citizens on-board from the Torumekians.
While Nausicaä is wearing her clothes, the Pejite Girl's pink dress gets dyed blue by the blood of the Ohmu larvae. When the Ohmu bear Nausicaä up above them, Obaba believes her to be "the blue-clad one" of prophecy. Nausicaä and the girl later return their original clothes to one another at the end of the film, as Nausicaä is depicted wearing her flight suit in the credits.

Appearance[]
 File:Pejite Girl.jpg   
She is a young woman around Nausicaä's age. Their close resemblance is what allows their temporary clothes swapping plan to be successful. She has black hair and brown eyes. Her own clothes consist of a pink dress (that is later dyed blue) and brown boots. She temporally wears Nausicaä's clothes, consisting of a dress-like military style shirt, beige trousers, and long leather boots.

Trivia[]
She is a character unique to the film and does not appear in the original manga, where Nausicaä instead receives a similar dress from a Dorok woman whose daughter was killed by Torumekians. She is told that she reminds the woman of her daughter. Her Dorok guide wanted the dress, which Nausicaä later keeps after it is dyed blue and converts it into a flight suit.[1]
