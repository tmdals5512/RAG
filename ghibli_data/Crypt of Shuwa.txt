The Crypt of Shuwa [1], also known as the Crypt of the Holy Emperor, is located at Shuwa.
The Crypt contains knowledge of the ancient technologies to "control the wellsprings of life" and create new species [2], a knowledge lost since the Seven Days Of Fire and the fall of the Kingdom of Eftal. Miralupa is believed to have revived these technologies to create an Ohmu from a piece of an Ohmu [3], but the real power in the Crypt is the Master of the Crypt.
