J. Robert Spencer (born 12 February 1969) is an American Actor who provided the voice Seita Yokokawa in Isao Takahata's film Grave of the Fireflies.
He graduated cum laude in 1991 from the Shenandoah University musical theatre program, and received an Honorary Doctorate of Fine Arts in 2009.
