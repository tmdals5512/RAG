Danny Flanagan (ダニー・フラナガン, Danī Furanagan) is a character in the film Mary and the Witch's Flower. He is retired a Scottish mouse who previously was broomstick riding teacher. Overall, he is spastic guy, who now watches over the brooms. He helps Mary Smith multiple times with her broomstick during the movie, and even fixes her broken broom for her.

Personality[]
Mr. Flanagan is a rough but kind guy who looks out for the brooms. During the movie, he appears several times at perfect moments like:
- helping control Mary's broom
- delivering the broom to Mary and Peter to escape
- fixing the broom with a bow
