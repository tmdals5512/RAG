Joe Romersa is an American musician, composer, voice actor, and music producer. 

     
He was born in 1956 in California. His geners of music include Ambient, Electronica, Folk, Rock, Trance.
His main instruments include Drums, Percussion, Guitar, Vocals, having been active since 1978. His label is Shadow Box Studio.
