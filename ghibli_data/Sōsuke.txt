Sōsuke (宗介) is the five-year-old protagonist of the film, Ponyo.

Appearance[]
Sōsuke is a 5-year-old boy who wears a sunshine yellow t-shirt and chocolate brown shorts, and white slip-on shoes. His hair is walnut brown with his hair shaven underneath. Also, he sometimes wears a sailor hat and binoculars.

Personality[]
Sōsuke is intelligent, smart, loyal, cute and heroic. He is willing to protect his friends (including Ponyo, starting from when she washes up on the shore near the house, stuck in a glass jar), even if doing so gets him in trouble with his mother or Ponyo's father (whom they hate).
He is interested in the sea, especially since his father is the captain of a ship. He also knows that Kōichi does his best for the family, as seen when Lisa gets angry at Kōichi and chides him for abandoning her family on a cliff.

​History[]
Sōsuke is first seen when Lisa calls Sōsuke to come back home and he discovers Ponyo. He uses a rock to break her free from the jar she's stuck in, but cuts his thumb in the process. She licks the cut, shocking him as he runs up the hill to Lisa.
After getting in his mom's car to drive to school, Sōsuke offers a piece of bread to Ponyo, but she refuses to have any. So, he tries to offer her some ham, but she takes the entire piece. Sōsuke decides to name the fish Ponyo, and promises to take care of her. After getting to school, he hides Ponyo in his bucket into the bushes, then goes to see her again when school is over. Relieved to see Ponyo awake, he shows his pet to Kumiko, who insults the fish. She responds by spraying her, and Sōsuke runs off.

Trivia[]
The "U" in his name is not pronounced.
He appears similar to Kanta from My Neighbor Totoro when he wears a sailor hat.
After Ponyo is taken from him, when Sosuke cries in the US dub, his voice is the same as in the original Japanese version.
