The Kabo Tribe is one of the tribes of the Dorok Principalities [1].
The Naulim River flows through Kabo lands [2].
Yupa discoveres the insects of the Daikaisho are headig towards Kabo tribe lands [3]. The Valley Gunship then flies over Kabo lands. A lot of the land has been taken over by fungus. They find the Kabo city fortress, which has also been taken over by the corruption [4].
