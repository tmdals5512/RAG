Barbara Goodson (born August 16, 1949) is an English actress who provided multiple voices in Studio Ghibli films.

Filmography[]
Panda! Go, Panda! - Additional Voices
The Castle of Cagliostro - Clarisse d'Cagliostro (young), Woman Playing Cards (1991 dub)
Castle in the Sky - Pazu and Madge (1988 dub)
Kiki's Delivery Service - Kokiri and additional voices (Streamline dub)
The Boy and the Heron - Aiko (English dub)
