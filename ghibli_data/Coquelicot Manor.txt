Coquelicot Manor is a fictional location in Yokohama shown in From Up on Poppy Hill film. It's a boarding house belonging to Matsuzaki family.

Garden[]
The Garden consists of a large backyard where many flower beds are grown, and the flagpole that Umi always use whenever she hoist up her flag signals in honor of her father. It is also the place where the Matsuzaki family hold the farewell lunch party.

Small house[]
The small house is usually where Hana Matsuzaki resides. It consists of a small television, a sliding door, a small table, and seat cushions.

The Main house[]
The main house or the Coquelicot Manor itself consists of two floors: the first floor and the second floor. In the first floor, it has a small kitchen with a refrigerator and vegetable supplies, a dining room that doubles as a living room, and the entrance to the garden. On the second floor, it consists of Umi and Sora's bedroom, Miki's room, Sachiko's room, and Riku's room.
