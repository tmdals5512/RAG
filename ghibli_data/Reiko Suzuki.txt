Reiko Suzuki (鈴木れい子 Suzuki Reiko, born 15 August 1944) is a Japanese voice actress. She started in 1973 and is still active with representation by agency Arts Vision.

Filmography[]
Castle in the Sky - Minor voices
My Neighbor Totoro - Kanta's Aunt
