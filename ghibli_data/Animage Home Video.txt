Title: Animage Home Video
URL: https://ghibli.fandom.com//wiki/Animage_Home_Video
--------------------------------------------------
Animage Home Video was a VHS home video distribution label of Tokuma Shoten. The purpose of the label was to release Studio Ghibli's movies on VHS and Laserdisc in Japan.
Following the Disney deal in 1996, the label was discontinued, with Buena Vista Home Entertainment Japan/Pony Canyon taking over afterwards. The Laserdisc version of Princess Mononoke was released under the "Tokuma Japan Communications" banner.
