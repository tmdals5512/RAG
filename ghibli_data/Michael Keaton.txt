Michael Keaton is an American actor, producer, and director known for his roles in Tim Burton's Beetlejuice, Batman, and Batman Returns, Riggan Thomson in the black comedy film Birdman for which he earned an nomination for Academy Award for Best Actor and as Walter V. Robinson in the critically acclaimed biographical drama Spotlight.
