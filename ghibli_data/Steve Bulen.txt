James Steven Bulen (born August 1, 1949), better known as Steve Bulen, is an American voice actor. He has been doing voices for many animated films and television shows for Walt Disney Animation Studios and Hanna-Barbera.

Filmography[]
The Castle of Cagliostro - Jigen, Italian Councilman, Mustached Councilman, Additional Voices (Streamline dub)
