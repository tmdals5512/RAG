Ghibli Wiki is an encyclopedia of all the works of Studio Ghibli's past, present and future. Films, documentaries, books and games. Characters and shorts can also be found here.

The studio is best known for its animated feature films, such as 'Princess Mononoke, Spirited Away' and Howl's Moving Castle. These films exemplify the master story telling of environmentalism, friendship, love, family, culture and war.
