Louise (ルイーゼ, Ruīze) is a puppet featured in the film Whisper of the Heart.

About[]
    Louise's portrait in The Cat Returns, based on her portrait mentioned in the book series Cat Baron.  
She is mentioned by Shirō Nishi. She was whittled alongside Baron Humbert von Gikkingen, because they made a couple. Shiro brought the couple in Germany, but he didn't take Louise, because she is on repairing. He wanted to take her later on, but the world war broke out. He couldn't find the store, where he brought the Baron. So Louise is separated from the Baron forever.
