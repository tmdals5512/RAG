Peter Boyle (October 18, 1935 - December 12, 2006) was an American actor. One of his most well known roles was Frank Barone in the sitcom Everybody Loves Raymond.

    Peter Boyle  
For Disney, he played Scott Calvin's boss, Mr. Whittle and Father Time in The Santa Clause series. He also starred in the remake of That Darn Cat as Pa and While You Were Sleeping as Ox Callaghan. He also voiced the character Muta in the film, The Cat Returns.
