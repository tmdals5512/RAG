Studio Ghibli's Trajectory as Seen on Animage (1984-2011) (『月刊アニメージュ』の特集記事で見るスタジオジブリの軌跡 , “Gekkan Animēju” no Tokushū Kiji de Miru Sutajiojiburi no Kiseki) is a book that compiled every Animage feature article and cover art from the last 28 years that featured films by Studio Ghibli , published by Tokuma Shoten on March 12, 2011. 
The book includes interviews with Ghibli's directors, animation team, and other staff, along with analysis articles from various perspectives within the industry.
