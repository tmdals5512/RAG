Trivia[]
In the book, it is implied that Cat Kingdom is a sort of an afterlife for cats who died in living world. And all known residents of the kingdom differs from normal cats in the living world; they are bipedal, can perform deeds akin to humans, speak human speeches, and performs magics for some extent. Yuki, a cat who died in the living world and now resides in the kingdom, cannot travel to the living world while other residents including Natori can travel in the living world. This indicates that these residents are either spirits or half-dead beings.
