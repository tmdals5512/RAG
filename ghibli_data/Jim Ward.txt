Jim Ward (born May 19, 1959 - died December 10, 2025) was an American veteran voice actor who provided the voice of the narrator in Playhouse Disney's show short The Adventures in Nutrition with Captain Carlos. He also voiced the additional voices of the English dub of Spirited Away and Porco Rosso.
