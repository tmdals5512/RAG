The Manpuku Temple (菩提餅山万福寺, Botamochi-Yama Manpukuji) is an abandoned temple seen in Pom Poko. It was an assembly house and a headqurter of Raccoons Dogs of Tama Hills.

Trivia[]
It was based on RyūShōji Amidadō (龍生寺阿弥陀堂) in Horinouchi, Hachiōji-city.
Its name is a pun to several Japanese words; "botamochi" is a type of wagashi sweets, "yama" means mountain, "manpuku" means satiety, and "ji" means temple.
