Mitsubishi A5M is a plane designed by Japanese engineers. It is seen in the movie The Wind Rises.
The engineering team is led by Jiro Horikoshi, who also designed Mitsubishi A6M Zero. His company Mitsubishi Aircraft Company pays his study in German aircraft and aircraft engine manufacturer Junkers. For the design he takes inspirations of Junkers's Junkers F.13 and Caproni's planes. He also take his own ideas into his work. The use of rivet make the plane ligther and it is ahead of one's time.
