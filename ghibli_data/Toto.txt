Toto (トト) is the tetartagonist of The Cat Returns.

Info[]
In "reality", he is only a stone statue of a crow who comes to life when the sun sets, while at the Cat Bureau, he has a soul. 
He's very friendly and nice to Haru Yoshioka. He often fights with Muta, like when he taunts Haru for having saved the cat prince from getting run over, which was resulting in the Cat Kingdom trying to get her to marry the prince, much to her dismay. He helps the Baron to catch up to her when she's taken to the Cat Kingdom, and also helps them when they get back.
