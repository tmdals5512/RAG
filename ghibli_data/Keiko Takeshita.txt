Keiko Takeshita (born September 15, 1953 in Nagoya, Japan) is a Japanese actress and voice actress of Sadako Maki in Arrietty (2010), Hana Matsuzaki in From Up on Poppy Hill (2011), Jiro's mother in The Wind Rises (2013) and Izumi in The Boy and the Heron (2023).

Filmography[]
Arrietty - Sadako Maki
From Up on Poppy Hill - Hana Matsuzaki
The Wind Rises - Jiro's mother
The Boy and the Heron - Izumi
