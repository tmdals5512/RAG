Stephanie Ru-Phan Sheh (born April 10, 1977), who also goes by the alias Jennifer Sekiguchi, is an American voice actress, ADR writer, director, producer, and singer. She has worked for several major companies, including Cartoon Network and Sony. She is often involved with doing voice over work in anime, original animation, video games, dubbing, commercials, and TV shows. Her notable voice roles include Orihime Inoue in Bleach, Usagi Tsukino/the titular character in Sailor Moon, Hinata Hyuga in Naruto, Yui in Sword Art Online, Illyasviel von Einzbern in Fate, Nui Harime in Kill la Kill, Audrey Burne/Mineva Lao Zabi in Mobile Suit Gundam: Unicorn, Akira Kogami in Lucky Star, Kuro in Blue Exorcist, Eureka in Eureka Seven, Yui Hirasawa in K-ON!, and Mikuru Asahina in The Melancholy of Haruhi Suzumiya.
