Appearance & Personality[]
Yutaka Matsuno has brown hair & blackish/brown eyes. He wears a white shirt along with red tie and black trousers. 
Yutaka’s personality & humor has expressed that he takes everything serious. He shown his side of maturity into situations & other concerns. Especially around Taku. Although he does have a bit of humor to keep their bond outgoing & strong. He has shown to Taku how great of a friend he is. After 2 years into college he seems to be the same person he was in Highschool. Yutaka has also shown how brave & bold he is to talk about his love feelings if anyone seems to suspect or ask about it.  

Etymology[]
The name Yutaka means "abundant, plenty, lush" (豊).
Yutaka's surname Matsuno means "pine tree, fir tree" (松) (matsu) and "field, wilderness" (野) (no).
