Liliana Berry Davis Mumy (born April 16, 1994) is an American actress who is best known for her role as Jessica Baker in Cheaper by the Dozen.

Filmography[]
My Neighbors the Yamadas - Nonoko Yamada
Howl's Moving Castle - Madge
Personal life[]
Mumy was born in San Marcos, California, the daughter of Eileen Joy, a childbirth educator, and former child star Bill Mumy.
