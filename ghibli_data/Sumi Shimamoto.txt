Sumi Shimamoto (born 8 December 1954) is a Japanese anime voice actress who has had roles in a number of Studio Ghibli films.

Filmography[]
Farewell My Beloved Lupin - Maki Oyamada
The Castle of Cagliostro - Clarisse d'Cagliostro
Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind  - Nausicaä
My Neighbor Totoro - Yasuko Kusakabe
Ocean Waves - Rikako's father lover
Princess Mononoke - Toki
She met Hayao Miyazaki while working on Lupin III. Miyazaki was very impressed by her voice acting.[1]
