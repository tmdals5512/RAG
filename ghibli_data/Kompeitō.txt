Title: Kompeitō
URL: https://ghibli.fandom.com//wiki/Kompeit%C5%8D
--------------------------------------------------
Kompeitō (コンペイトー) are a Japanese hard candy that are fed to the Susuwatari that work in Kamajī's boiler room. They are made by coating a grain of coarse sugar in layers of sugar syrup.
