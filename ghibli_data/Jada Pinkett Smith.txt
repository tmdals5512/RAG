Jada Pinkett Smith (born September 18, 1971, age 54) is an African-American actress who is best-known for voicing Gloria in the Madgascar film series, Fish Mooney in the FOX-TV drama Gotham (2014-2017), and the titular Christina Hawthorne in the TNT drama Hawthorne (2009-2011).
