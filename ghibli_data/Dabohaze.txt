The Dabohaze (ダボハゼ) is a large seaplane owned by the air bandits, the Mamma Aiuto, as seen in the film Porco Rosso.

Technical  Characteristics[]
Capable of flight at 193 kilometers per hour; and armed with a 37mm anti-aircraft gun, a 7.7mm Lewis machine-gun and numerous assault weapons for its crew.[1]

Background[]
A large-scale seaplane owned by the Mamma Aiuto. It's gone through so many overhauls its original shape is unknown. Apparently it was modeled after a German Dornier seaplane.

Trivia[]
"Dabohaze" means Odontobutis obscura.
