Asako Tsukishima (月島 朝子, Tsukishima Asako) is the wife of Seiya Tsukishima and the mother of Shiho Tsukishima and Shizuku Tsukishima in the film Whisper of the Heart.

Etymology[]
The name Asako means "morning" (朝) (asa) and "child" (子) (ko).
Asako's surname Tsukishima means "moon" (月) (tsuki) and "island" (島) (shima).
