Anthony "Tony" Jay (2 February 1933 – 13 August 2006) was an English-American actor, voice actor, and singer who was a former member of the Royal Shakesphere Company, and was also best known for his very low distinctive Baritone voice.

     
He voiced the Narrator in Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind.
