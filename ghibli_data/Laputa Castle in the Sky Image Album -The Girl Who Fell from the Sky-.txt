Laputa: Castle in the Sky Image Album -The Girl Who Fell from the Sky- (天空の城ラピュタ イメージアルバム —空から降ってきた少女—, Tenkuu no Shiro Laputa Image Album -Sora kara Futtekita Shoujo-) is an album released on May 25, 1986. It is the image album of Castle in the Sky, the songs are inspired by "images" from the film's storyboard.
