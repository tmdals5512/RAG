Weapons




Asbel uses a SMG against insects in the Toxic Jungle. A sword in a sheath is on his belt


Equipment

Type
weapons




Most weapons are made from extremely hard ceramics, metals or insect shell, like the Ohmu.
Soldiers and warriors are often armed with a combination of medieval weapons (swords, knives, etc) and semi-automatic pistols and automatic rifles.
Nausicaä uses a single shot, bolt-action long rifle, as well as a ceramic short sword. Her rifle shoots a variety of shells, including siren shells and signal flare shells.
