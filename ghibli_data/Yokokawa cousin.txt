Yokokawa cousin is the paternal cousin of the deceased of Seita Yokokawa and Setsuko Yokokawa, and the daughter of the the aunt. She is a minor character in Grave of the Fireflies.

History[]
Very little is known about her except that she, unlike Seita, went to school and resumed her normal activities during the war, and that she works for Japan's forces, She is seen thrice having dinner with her mother, her cousins and a houseguest. She was last seen having dinner with her mother as she complained to her and the guest about Seita and Setsuko's behavior. She seemingly sides with mother and asks if she scolded her cousins.
It is unknown what happened to the cousin after the war ended, except that she survived.

Trivia[]
Like her mother, her first name was never revealed. She was only called "Young Lady" by her mother.
It's possible that she had a crush on Seita since she blushed and smiled when the two made eye contact during dinner.
In the live version she is a character become elderly lady for telling the tale for young people.
