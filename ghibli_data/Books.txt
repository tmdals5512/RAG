Studio Ghibli Editorial Desk Publisher:Viz Communications

ISBN 1-56931-777-1 Price:$34.95


A large, hardcover coffee-table book featuring artwork from the renowned animated film, Spirited Away, directed by Hayao Miyazaki. Features color stills, sketches, storyboards and illustrations used to envision the rich fantasy world of the film. Also includes extensive production notes and a complete English-language script from the film. Paperback, 240 color pages.
