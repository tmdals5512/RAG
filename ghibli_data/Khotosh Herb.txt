Khotosh Herb




Yupa brings Khotosh Herb to the Valley of the Wind


Khotosh [1] is an herb that prevents infant mortality. The Valley of the Wind does not grow this herb, so it is brought to the community by Yupa, to the joy of the Wise Woman of the Valley.
