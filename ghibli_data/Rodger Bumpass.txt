Title: Rodger Bumpass

--------------------------------------------------
Rodger D. Bumpass is an American actor and voice actor who is best known for voicing Squidward Tentacles on the Nickelodeon animated series SpongeBob SquarePants, Professor Membrane on Invader ZIM and the Chief on the animated series Where on Earth is Carmen Sandiego?
His roles include as Dr. Axolotl in TaleSpin, Chundel in Quack Pack, Savage Lion in Timon and Pumbaa, and various other characters for several Disney projects.
