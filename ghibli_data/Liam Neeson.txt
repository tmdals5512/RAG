Liam John Neeson, OBE is an Irish-American actor and voice actor most famous for his role as Qui-Gon Jinn in the Star Wars franchise and in various action films such as the Taken trilogy, as well as voicing Good Cop/Bad Cop/Scribble Cop in The LEGO Movie. He has played Fujimoto in the Disney English dub of Ponyo.
