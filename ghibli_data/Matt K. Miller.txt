Matthew Kermit Miller (born February 2, 1960), better known as Matt K. Miller, is an American stand-up comedian, actor, playwright and voice artist. He is also known as Kermit Miller and Kermit Beachwood His best-known voice role in anime was Tenchi Masaki in the Pioneer dub of Tenchi Muyo.
