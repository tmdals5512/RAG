Nobody treats my Lady Lady Eboshi like that! Move and I fire!
—Kiyo to Ashitaka, after having watch Lady Eboshi be knocked out by him.

Not to confuse with Okiyo from Pom Poko.
Kiyo is a recurring character in Princess Mononoke.

Physical Appearance[]
Kiyo has brown hair and white kimono with triangular symbols; similar to The Legend of Zelda games and wears pink turban-like and has gun.

Plot[]
She works the bellows in Irontown with the rest of the women. When Ashitaka knocks Lady Eboshi unconscious to stop her fighting with San, Kiyo gets angry and threatens him with a gun. When he starts to walk out, Kiyo unintentionally shoots him through his chest. To her amazement, Ashitaka does not stop and continues to exit the village with San over his shoulder.
