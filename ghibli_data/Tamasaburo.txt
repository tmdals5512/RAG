Tamasaburō (玉三郎), also known as Tamasaburō of the Orge Forest (鬼ヶ森の玉三郎, Onigamori no Tamasaburō), is a character who appeared in Pom Poko. 

Personality[]
Tamasaburo is quiet and sensitive. He does his best to work hard, but his sense of duty is complicated by his love for Koharu. He is one of Shoukichi's closest friends.


Appearance[]
Tamasaburo is a tall-ish, well-groomed tanuki who, when taking the form of a human, appears as a handsome man. He has tufts of white fur on the top of his head. He has black markings underneath his eyes, like eye bags. His default clothing is a long cyan robe.
