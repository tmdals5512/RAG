Esther is the Princess and songstress and friends of Oliver.
She is the daughter of the Great Sage, Rashaad. Esther is known to have a fiery personality, which causes her and Swaine to butt heads frequently. Her real world soulmate is a shy girl named Myrtle, who is nicknamed Starey-Mary by the children because she never leaves her room and always looks out her window.
She initially had part of her heart removed by Shadar but Oliver helps her return to her former self.

Appearance[]
Esther has blonde hair in a ponytail and blue eyes. She a dark indigo crop top with a gold neck collar and light purple short sleeves a matching dark indigo skirt with a gold band near the bottom pink puffy pants with gold bands at the legs and orange dancers' slippers. At the Winter Isles, she wears a pink coat with fluffy white edges. In Castaway Cove she wears a blue bikini with a purple towel skirt around her waist.

Personality[]
Esther is a bright girl with a positive attitude. She is very helpful and quickly feels sorry for others, which is why she wants to help everyone. Precisely for this reason, she often clashes with the pessimistic Sven and also shows her loud, spirited and stubborn side in arguments with him.
