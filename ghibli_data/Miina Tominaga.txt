Yoshiko Masumoto (増本 美子 Masumoto Yoshiko, born April 3, 1966), better known by her stage name of Miina Tominaga (冨永 みーな Tominaga Mīna), is a Japanese voice actress and tarento from Nishi-ku, Hiroshima. She is currently attached to Mint Avenue.


Filmography[]
Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind - Lastelle
