Crispin McDougal Freeman (born February 9, 1972) is an American voice actor, ADR director, and script writer who is best know for his voice work in Japanese anime. His notable voice roles include Alucard in Hellsing, Kyon from The Melancholy of Haruhi Suzumiya, Itachi Uchiha in Naruto, and among others.
His Disney roles include voicing Prince Justin/Turnip Head in Howl's Moving Castle and additional voices in Ponyo.
He is the older brother of actress Cassidy Freeman.
