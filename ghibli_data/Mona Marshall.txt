Mona Marshall (born August 31, 1947) is an American voice actress and writer. She is most well known for voicing many of the female characters (e.g. Sheila Broflovski, Linda Stotch, etc.) on Comedy Central's landmark animated series South Park, replacing the late Mary Kay Bergman after she died after the show's first three years.
