Thomas (トーマス, Tōmasu) is Bella Yaga's cat in the film Earwig and the Witch.

Plot[]
Thomas helps Earwig complete her first spell.

Thomas is an all black cat with green eyes.

Personality[]
Thomas is terrified of Bella Yaga and worms.

Trivia[]
He is based of Jiji from Kiki's Delivery Service.
