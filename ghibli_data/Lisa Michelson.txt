Lisa Michelson (31 March 1958 – 14 September 1991) was an American Voice actress best known for her English roles in the Streamline Pictures dubs. She was sometimes credited as or Lisa Michaelson or Lisa Snegoff. She was married to Gregory Snegoff. Michelson died in a car accident in Los Angeles, California on September 14, 1991. The end credits of the American localization of Robot Carnival are dedicated to her
