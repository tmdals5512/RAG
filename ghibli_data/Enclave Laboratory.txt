Enclave Laboratory




Yupa stumbles upon the laboratory while scouting out the Worm Handler Enclave



The Enclave Laboratory [1] is in the Worm Handler Enclave, where the Dorok scientists are growing Ohmu.
The laboratory contains at least four giant glass tanks filled with a liquid growth culture and attached to machines that have steam lines [2]. One tank has a budding Ohmu.
Yupa discovers the laboratory while evading capture and is corned by the Worm Handlers and Doroks [3].
Asbel, disguised as a Mani warrior, destroyed one of the tanks when fighting Yupa [4] in the laboratory then in the confusion, the pair of them destroy the rest of the tanks, as the Holy One planned.
