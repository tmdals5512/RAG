Granny Cat (ネコばあちゃん, Neko Bāchan) is an extremely old and big catbus. She only appears in the Studio Ghibli's short Mei and the Kittenbus. 

About[]
Granny Cat is much bigger than normal catbuses and can hold many more passengers (totoros). She has a tail that can act as a propeller. She is friendly. She has several decks and has 30 winds per row on each side.
Like other catbuses, she can fly.
At first, Mei was a little scared of her, but they soon became friends. Mei gives her a piece of candy to seal their friendship.
