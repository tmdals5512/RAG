1990[]
     
February 5, My Neighbor Totoro and Kiki's Delivery Service released in cinemas in the USA.
March 15, Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind released on VHS in Japan.
July 24, Daveigh Chase born.
September 21, Kotoe Hatsui dies.
December 21, Kiki's Delivery Service English dub released in cinema from Tri-Star Pictures and Streamline Pictures.
1991[]
July 20 - Only Yesterday released in cinemas
1992[]
July 18 - Porco Rosso released in cinemas
1993[]
March 15 - Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind released as one manga book by Animage.
May 5 - Ocean Waves released as a TV movie.
May 7 - My Neighbor Totoro English dub released in cinemas by Troma Pictures.
June 18 - Ocean Waves released on VHS.
1994[]
January 1 - Only Yesterday released on VHS in Japan
June 27 - My Neighbor Totoro released on VHS in Japan
July - Pom Poko released in cinemas
1995[]
July 9 - My Neighbor Totoro and Porco Rosso released on Swedish TV
July 15 - Whisper of the Heart released in cinemas
1997[]
    Princess Mononoke theatrical release poster  
July 12 - Princess Mononoke released in cinemas
1998[]
May 23 - Kiki's Delivery Service released in Disney in theaters.
September 15 - Kiki's Delivery Service release on VHS from Disney.
1999[]
July 17 - My Neighbors the Yamadas
October 29 - Princess Mononoke release in theaters from Miramax Films.
