Nausicaä's Tiara River Stone earrings that she gave to the Dorok Orphans



Type
River


Location
Toxic Jungle



The Tiara River [1] is a river that flows through the Toxic Jungle.
The stones from this river are given to the maidens of the Valley of the Wind by Master Yupa for their wedding gowns [2]. The stones are considered very beautiful and valuable.
Yupa uses a Tiara River stone to pay for a meal and stabling of his Horseclaw, Kui [3].
In Dorok, the river is known as Taria [4].
