Chikuku is a young boy from theDorok Principalities . His full name is Luwa Chikuku Kulaballuka. He posesses telepathic abilities.

Biography[]
When Nausicaä arrives at the Temple of the Old Religion, she meets Chikuku. When Nausicaä leaves, Chikuku accompanies her. Chikuku telepathically communicates with Nausicaä and translates between Charuka and Nausicaä.
