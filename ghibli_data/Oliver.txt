Appearance[]
Oliver has brown hair and blue eyes. He wears a blue shirt with white collar and a red cloak with green and white trousers and light brown shoes. During his initial appearance in the game, Oliver wore a yellow shirt, blue trousers, and light red socks and blue and white shoes. In Castaway Cove, he obtains red swim trunks with yellow spike designs over the bottom edges. When traveling in The Winter Isles, Oliver wears a thick, blue long coat with white linings, dark gloves, and brown boots.

Personality[]
Oliver is a kind-hearted, nice, and polite young boy. Though a bit naïve and gullible, he's incredibly helpful and helps anyone who asks him to. He does not give up hope and is incredibly brave. Together with his best friend Philip, he pursues the dream of making his own car, one that dwarfs everything Motorville has ever seen. Like most people in Motorville, he is passionate about technology.
Oliver also has a strong sense of justice, believing that while the world may be flawed, it is still a place where many people strive for the greater good. As shown in various points of the game, Oliver is trusting and nice to strangers, even if they had wronged him once before. His good nature completes his great character, and it's what causes many characters in the game to trust and help him.
