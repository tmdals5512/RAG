Ogil [1] was a Dorok soldier who "claimed " Nausicaä as his war prize. 

Biography[]
Ogil was a Dorok soldier who was stationed in a ship together with the Mani Tribe. The ship landed in the Toxic Jungle to investigate a crashed Torumekian vessel, where they happen upon Nausicaä and Asbel. There he tries to claim Nausicaä as his war prize. She defends herself however, resulting in a fight in which Ogil loses his Shohki Mask and dies[2].
Ketcha considers Ogil a bad person and told Nausicaä that "Nobody cry for him" [3].
