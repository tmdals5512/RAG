Kushana's Plan [1], as a riposte to the Three Princes Plot, was to go through with the Periphery Campaign and invade Dorok through the Toxic Jungle and then link up with the Torumekian Third Army, an army she trained and exhibits great loyalty from, overthrow the officers in charge of it and bring the Third Army back to the capital to seize the Torumekian crown.
Things don't go exactly as planned....
