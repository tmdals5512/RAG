Takuya Kimura (born November 13, 1972 in Tokyo) is a pop singer and voice actor of Howl Jenkins Pendragon in Howl's Moving Castle (2004) and Shoichi Maki in The Boy and the Heron (2023). He is known for Bushi no ichibun (2006), Space Battleship Yamato (2010), and others, as well as being a boy band in his youth. . 
When he was cast for Howl's Moving Castle, he showed up to the voice recording session without a script, and did a great performance. His enthusiasm for anime was plainly evident. He was always talking about anime, particularly his favorites Space Sagittarius and The Wonderful Adventures of Nils.[1]
