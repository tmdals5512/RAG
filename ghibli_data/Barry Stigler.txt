Title: Barry Stigler

Barry Stigler (October 31, 1948 – March 1, 2005) was an American voice actor. In his anime voice work, he often used the pseudonym Gil Starberry, which is an anagram of his name. Little is known about his personal life, including his cause of death.

Filmography[]
Castle in the Sky - Louis (1988 dub)
The Castle of Cagliostro - Christopher (Animaze/Manga dub) (as Bob Stuart)
