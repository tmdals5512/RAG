Gas Lamps




Kurotowa lights a torch off of a gas lamp


Equipment

Type
light/heat source



Gas lamps [1] are tall lamps with a bulbous base (presumably holding the gas) with a tall stem and two or three gas mantles at the top to generate the light. Gas lamps are used by nearly everyone as a source of light, both indoors and out.
