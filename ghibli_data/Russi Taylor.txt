Russell "Russi" Taylor (May 4, 1944 - July 27, 2019) was an American voice actress who was best known as the voice of Minnie Mouse since 1986, and several other characters until her death in 2019.

Roles[]
My Neighbor Totoro - Kanta's Aunt (2005 Disney version)
Pom Poko - Otama
Tales from Earthsea - Additional Voices
