The Waji Valley is an autonomy state in the Periphery [1]. It is North of the Valley of the Wind along the Ocean of Salt and Southeast of the Valley of Sand.
The Waji Valley is part of the treaty with the Kingdom of Torumekia and required to bring a gunship and contingent when called on by the Vai Emperor. No specific gunship or chieftain is identified in the narrative.
