Mahito Maki (牧眞人, Next) is the protagonist of the film The Boy and the Heron.

Appearance[]
Mahito Maki, the protagonist of The Boy and the Heron, is depicted as an 11-year-old. His appearance reflects his quiet and introspective nature, often shown with a serious and contemplative expression. He has a youthful appearance, consistent with his age, and his physical presence in the film complements his character's emotional depth and journey. His attire and overall look are indicative of a typical young boy of his era, further grounding him in the film's historical setting.[1]

Trivia[]
Toshio Suzuki believes that he is one of the motifs of The Gray Heron where Hayao Miyazaki is presumed to be the model of Mahito Maki.[2]
His Hair resembles the same as Sosuke's from Ponyo (2008).
