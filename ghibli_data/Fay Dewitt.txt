Title: Fay Dewitt

Fay DeWitt was born on December 4, 1934 in New York City, New York, USA. She is known for her work on Crazy Ex-Girlfriend (2015), Red Dead Redemption (2010) and Circle (2015). She was previously married to Ray Allen.
