Bella Yaga (ベラ・ヤーガ, Bera Yāga) is the main antagonist of the film Earwig and the Witch. She is the Earwig's adoptive mother.

Plot[]
Bella Yaga is the ex drummer of the band Earwig. Later on she adopts Earwig. She brings Earwig to their home so she can have an extra set of hands with spell work.

Appearance[]
Bella Yaga has bright blue, curly hair with mismatch eyeshadow of purple and blue. She is usually well dressed with heavy amounts of makeup and jewelry. When she is working on spells, her hair is tied up and she is in an apron.

Personality[]
Bella Yaga is hard around the edges, coming off as cold, cruel and selfish. She is not heartless, though. It takes Mandrake and her getting in a dispute for her to treat Earwig better. She freezes up when Earwig hugs her or shows any kind of affection, indicating she is used to being solitary and miserable.
