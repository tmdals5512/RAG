Chae Gang-sun (채강선 Chae Gang-seon?), also known as Player 134, was a participant of the 33rd Squid Game. A screen appears showing his name and personal records in the episode "The Man with the Umbrella".

History[]
Chae Gang-sun was born on August 31, 1971 to his parents Chae Bo-seong and Kim Il-ju. He attended Yangju Technical High School and graduated in February 1991. He found work as a transport driver for Haeil Packaging before quitting or being terminated around 1998. He also incurred a significant amount of debt from gambling in March of that year.
He began a second job as a driver for Rainbow Moving but lost it after being arrested for a hit-and-run committed while drunk driving in November 2005. He spent eight years in prison before being released in 2013. He eventually landed another job at Bangi Manpower sometime after. By this time, his debts had increased significantly as he hadn't to pay any of them off while incarcerated, so he took out private loans in an attempt to deal with them in December 2014.
Chae Gang-sun was unable to settle his debts despite these efforts. In 2020, decided to join the Squid Games in an attempt to win money to pay them off.
He survived Red Light, Green Light. He was likely terrified from the deaths of the contestants. A vote was then held to end the games. Despite being terrefied and having begged to go home he voted O because he feared the loan sharks that were after him, he returned for the prize money. In Sugar Honeycombs, he picked an unknown shape. 

Fate[]
In Sugar Honeycombs, he failed to cut his dalgona out and got shot in the head by a guard.

Trivia[]
According to his player file, Chae Gang-sun resides in the neighborhood of Dasan-dong in Namyangju, Gyeonggi Province, and his resident registration number is 710831-1228714.
The photo in Player 134's file and the photo in Player 134's icon are different.
Mistakes[]
Though his birthday is listed to be August 31, 1971, Chae Gang-sun is erroneously shown to be 45 years old on the information sheet displayed in the control room, and his parents are both listed as being 66. This is most likely a mistake on the editors' parts.
Chae Gang-sun seems to have the same player portrait as Player 126, Player 138, and Player 377. This is most likely a mistake on the editors' parts.
Player 134's icon is the same as Player 380's faulty icon.
Behind the scenes[]
Chae Gang-sun was portrayed by Bae Dong-yun, Hyun-ju in his Icon, and two unknown extras.
