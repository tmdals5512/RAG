History[]
Nothing is much known about his past but sometime in the past he shared the same thoughts with Oh Il-nam and then later became one of The VIPs. In 2020 he was invited to attend the 33rd Squid Games hosted by Oh Il-nam then was never seen again after the 33rd Squid Game and left his Golden Eagle Mask behind.

Personality[]
He is portrayed with a French accent. He possesses some culture, as he was able to correct the Lion Mask VIP, who misattributed a quote from William Congreve to William Shakespeare.

Behind the scenes[]
The VIP is portrayed by Stephane Mot. He is credited as "VIP 6".
