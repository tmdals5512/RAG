Personality
Player 197 was a very social Player, and he seemingly formed friendships very easily. He was extremely loyal to Team X, as he not only fought for them during the Bathroom fight but also avenged Player 067, putting his life in major risk just to try to save and avenge a man he had never been shown interacting with. During the Six-Legged Pentathlon, he immediately lets Player 009 onto his team  despite the fact she was an elderly O voter. However, during Hide and Seek, he becomes extremely nervous and terrified of the Seekers.

History
"001"
Player 197 entered the Squid Games due to being in debt like the rest of the players, he woke up in the Dormitory alongside 454 other contestants, signed the player consent form, and took his icon photo. 
At the start of Red Light, Green Light, players were warned by Seong Gi-hun (Player 456 and 2020 winner) that if they lost they would be killed. Player 197 is first clearly visible following the death of Kang Mi-na (Player 196), appearing on the panel to her right. Despite the chaos that followed the death of Lee Eun-ju (Player 283), 197 stayed perfectly still and successfully crossed the finish line, surviving the first game. During the first vote, 197 voted X to end the games, he would be displeased to see that he would have to stay.

"Six Legs" & "One More Game"
In the Six-Legged Pentathlon, when stated they had to form teams of five, Player 197 set off, quickly teaming up with Player 009. The two of them later teamed up with Park Gyeong-seok (Player 246), Sim Jae-seok (Player 343), and Player 140. Player 197 played Jegi for the team and completed the challenge successfully. Player 197 can be seen celebrating at the finish line along with his team.
During the second vote, 197 again voted X, hoping to terminate the games. However, the Os won due to the majority wanting to continue. 

"O X"
Although Player 197 was rarely seen during Mingle, it's confirmed he passed all rounds, as he was present for the third vote, once again voting X. During this time, 197 is seen standing in front of Gi-hun, with Park Jung-bae (Player 390) and Park Min-su (Player 125) next to him, he would be seen cheering with his allies Player 206 and Player 072 when 185 and 015 changed their votes.
After the vote resulted in a tie, Player 197 received and ate his food. Later, in the bathroom, he witnessed Lee Myung-gi confronting Thanos and Nam-gyu, defending Min-su and accusing Team O of forcing Team X to join them in the next vote.
When the bathroom fight began, Player 197 grabbed Player 418 from behind, after Player 418 faced Player 197 directly, Player 197 would be shoved from behind by Player 056 and Player 102 would shove Player 418 against a stall, after Player 102 is taken away from that fight by Player 056, Player 418 and 197 would wrestle and grapple and Player 418 shifted his bodyweight and dropped Player 197. Once they got up, Player 197 grappled and dropped Player 418. As they were struggling to get up, Player 418 would push Player 197 over, pin him to the floor, and attempt to strangle him. Player 197 would shove him off and continue fighting off-screen. At the end of the fight, Player 197 gained the upper hand and attempted to strangle Player 418. Both survived the brawl and were present during their team's headcount afterward. Once it was revealed that the Xs outnumbered the Os, 197 appeared joyful, thinking he would leave the games tomorrow. He then leaves with Player 102 and Player 307 to go to sleep.

"Friend or Foe"
During the Special Game, Player 197 was not seen in the fighting until Player 056 killed 197's teammate (Player 067). 197 ran behind 056 quickly and started strangling him with his tracksuit, snapping his neck and avenging his friend. Player 116 and Player 154 were seen running up behind 197 in order to ambush him, however he survives this encounter. Player 197 would be seen fighting, and grappling with Player 312 at the end of the Special Game, and would be forced to put his hands up after the guards came in.
Player 197 survived the Special Game and did not participate in Gi-hun's Revolt, remaining in the Dormitory. He could be seen being worried after Player 015 decides to join. He later watched Cho Hyun-ju (Player 120) return, searching for Kang Dae-ho (Player 388). Meaning to say, he survived the horrific events of Season 2 and is alive to participate in Season 3.

"Keys and Knives"
Player 197 was seen staring at Gi-hun after he regained consciousness, standing alongside Player 400 and Player 377, as the three of them were all that was left from their friend group. During the re-vote, 197 continued to vote X, despite his team being significantly outnumbered after the Special Game and Revolt, which forced him to remain and play the fourth game.

"The Starry Night"
In Hide and Seek, Player 197 was a member of the Hiders and ran away with Player 134 into the maze. After losing Player 134, he would be chased by Gi-hun, who mistook him for Dae-ho (who he wanted to kill for ruining the revolt and causing Jung-bae's death) since they both had the same hairstyle.Shit...
Player 197 after realizing he was cornered when running from Seong Gi-hun during Hide and Seek in "The Starry Night" Player 197 fled through the maze and was cornered into a dead-end, after realizing a door was there, Player 197 opened the door to escape, but the door led to a large drop and 197 was trapped. Gi-hun grabbed 197, prepared to kill him and 197 begged him not to kill him. But seeing 197's face and number, Gi-hun realized that he went after the wrong player, so he let 197 run away.
Fate
Even though Gi-hun let him go, Player 197 was found and killed by an unknown seeker, puncturing him near the heart or a major artery. Killing him almost instantly. Gi-hun found Player 197's body and verified that he wasn't Dae-ho.

Trivia
He seems to have a hair band on his left wrist, as seen in the Bathroom Fight. This is why he is seen with a pony tail similar to Kang Dae-ho in Season 3.
His player tag, 197, along with his friend who he avenged for, Player 067, is a prime number and a odd number.
His number is part of a prime quadruplet (191, 193, 197 and 199).
He alongside Player 191 were the remaining 2 players with a prime quadruplet who survived 3 games.
His allies Player 377, 307 and 015 are odd numbers just like his number tag, 197.
377 and 197 also have the same digit sum. (1+9+7 = 17 and 3+7+7 = 17).
Notably, his friend, Player 377, who was the eighth player eliminated during Jump Rope, whereas he held the same position of elimination in Hide and Seek.
He’s the only male contestant with the x97s player number, as 097, 297 and 397 are women, they are all X voters too.
He is one of only a few players in the 'X' group known to have killed at least one player in the 'O' group during the Special Game. He shares this feat alongside Players 080, 220 and 102.
Player 197 seems to be one of the stronger X players, being able to snap a neck which has only been seen by Hwang In-ho and Player 224.
Player 197 likely had a higher chance of survival if he was a Seeker, but he most likely chose not to switch with a Seeker due most of his remaining allies (Player 084, Player 232, Player 377 and  Player 400) all also being Hiders.
Player 197's stab wound that we can see was delivered to the upper torso/chest area, slightly left, which is consistent with the heavy blood loss around his chest and the pooling beneath his body. This suggests the blade punctured near the heart or a major artery most likely, killing him almost instantly.
When Gi-hun spots his dead body, Player 394 runs to the room. 394 happens to be twice of 197.
Player 197 seemed to be a very social person, forming many friendships across the games, being 400, 102, 307, 377, and even 084 and 232 were seen talking with him.
It's possible that Player 197 managed to convinced Player 232 to change his to X after Hide and Seek. Before the game began, 197 was seen grouping hands with him and patting him on the back, wishing him good luck which hinted at a small friendship between the two. Unfortunately, both him and Player 232 would later be eliminated during the game.
He is only seen without his jacket on when he uses it to snap Player 056's neck. He somehow found a chance to put it back on and zip it up.
His hair is different in every appearance. His icon, the pentathlon, the vote, the bathroom, the special game, and the fourth game all have him with different hair styles.
Before Hide and Seek, he seemed to form an alliance with 084 and 232, but they didn’t help each other in the game, that ended up costing him and 232 their lives, since all three had the different keys shape and only needed to find the exit, which 084 did.
It's likely that Player 197 befriended most of his allies during mingle, as that game involved the most teamwork, and 197, 102 and 307 were never seen during mingle.
He and Lee Myung-gi were the only X players to fight in the Bathroom Fight, while also making it to season 3, since all the other players died in Special Game and the Rebellion.
If Gi-hun hadn't realized in time that he wasn't Dae-ho, Player 197 would have been the first player eliminated in Hide and Seek.
His number is right next to 2 players presented by the Manager (Kang Mi-na and Jang Do-yeong).
Player 009 was the first confirmed ally he made in the games, since that in the BTS of Six-Legged Pentathlon they were the first of Gyeong-seok's team to team up.
His strategy of running around the maze to find the exit was likely the main cause of his death, since it greatly increased his chance of encountering a roaming seeker, especially as he was making an extreme amount of noise.
His actor also plays Number 006, a soldier for the games.
