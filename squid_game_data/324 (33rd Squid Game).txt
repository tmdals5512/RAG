Personality[]
Player 324 is a laid-back individual. He didn't seem too concerned over waking up in an unknown place, instead choosing to goof around like a person on vacation. He would even make bets with his friend, Player 250, on who would win the first game. This would lead to his death and elimination.
Even if he didn't know he would die if he lost the one of the games, he still didn't take the games very seriously as he should've been because he would have been thrust back into a life of poverty if he lost. Despite the fact that if he won, he could have been freed from his debts.

History[]
Player 324 was in heavy debt and chose to enter the Squid Games in order to win large amounts of money. He was picked up by a van and taken to the island where the games take place. After waking up in the Squid Game lobby, he chose to remain and participate in the games.
While being led through the Squid Game complex, Player 324 remarked to Player 250 about how big the entire place was. While getting his photo taken, he made a silly pose in which he stuck out his tongue and made V-signs with his fingers before the camera went off. He was then pushed out of the way by Player 250 when he tried to have his photo retaken.
Player 324 was then taken to a large open area for the first game, along with all of the other players. He then noticed a large animatronic doll on the other side of the arena and pointed it out to Player 250, remarking that it had a huge head. When the game was revealed to be Red Light, Green Light, the pair expressed disbelief and wondered if they were being serious. He then made a ₩1 million bet with Player 250 for who would make it to the end first.

Fate[]
When the doll turned around for the first Green Light, Player 324 pushed Player 250, and both ran forward as fast as they could. Unfortunately, Player 324 lost his balance on Red Light and was unable to catch himself before the doll detected his movements. He was then shot with a sniper, becoming the first player to be eliminated from the game. He placed 456th, or last place in the 33rd Squid Game overall. Player 250 thought it was an act. Then he came up to him to make sure that he didn't put on a show and get up. Player 324 then coughed up blood onto the ground, making Player 250's expression become serious, realizing elimination means death.

Aftermath[]
Player 250 then begins running towards the exits, only to get shot in the chest. Player 250's blood then splattered onto Player 306's face, who screamed before getting shot in the head. These deaths led to the chain outbreak, where lots of players started running towards the exits trying to escape, only to get shot as well. Those players also shoved or ran into other players who hadn't moved, getting them eliminated as well.

Trivia[]
Player 324 is the first character to die onscreen in the entire show.
He and Thanos shared some similarities, where they both made a pose when taking a picture. They both have dyed hair. They also had partners in the game, where Player 324 paired with Player 250 while Thanos paired with Nam-gyu. Lastly, both of them didn't show much seriousness in the games, where player 324 jokingly pushes player 250 in red light green light while Thanos was more exaggerated, dancing in almost all the games and also doing some bizarre acts like kissing the 'O' button, though he was under the influence of his drugs.
Player 324 is the only eliminated player in RLGL in the 33rd Squid Game whose elimination is announced to the rest of the players, he is also the only player whose elimination is announced before dying and not after.
His season 2 counterpart based on placement is Kang Mi-na, as they were the first to be eliminated during their respective Games.
Coincidentially, both players they shared their numbers with in opposite seasons allied with Gi-Hun at some point during their respective Games before being killed or wounded by a soldier sometime after.
Mistakes[]
Player 324's icon is shown to be in the middle of several other icons even though it should be on the left edge of the portrait grid, given its layout.
A female 324 is seen in the dormitory before Red Light, Green Light.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 324 was portrayed by Lee Han-sol and Lee Eun-sook. Lee Han-sol, who has dark brown hair in real life, got his hair dyed yellow specifically for the part.
