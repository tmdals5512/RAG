This Unnamed Man also known as Bear Mask VIP is one of the fifth of The VIPs attending the Squid Games hosted by Oh Il-nam.

History[]
Nothing is much known about his past but sometime in the past he shared the same thoughts with Oh Il-nam and then later became one of The VIPs. In 2020 he was invited to attend the 33rd Squid Games hosted by Oh Il-nam then was never seen again after the 33rd Squid Game and left his Golden Bear Mask behind.

Behind the scenes[]
The VIP was portrayed by Michael Davis. He is credited as "VIP 5".
