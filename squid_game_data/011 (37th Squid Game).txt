History[]
Player 011 was in heavy debt. As a result, she joined the Squid Games to finish all this money she had to pay off. After she signed a contract and took her photo, she was sent to Red Light Green Light, where she stood still during the mass panic and successfully crossed the red finish line. During the first voting session, she voted to stop the games. However, the majority narrowly voted to continue the games because of one person's ending vote. 
During the Six-Legged Pentathlon, she teamed up with Players 104, 138, 169, and 320. She presumably played Jegi for her team, as seen by their seating arrangement, and they managed to pass the game after successfully completing all five minigames. In the second voting session, she chose to stop the games for the second and final time.

Fate[]
In Mingle, she survived the first round but during the second one, she didn't manage to get into a room in time and thus was eliminated. She could also be seen running with Player 028 in the final round, but that's an error.

Mistakes[]
She appeared running in Mingle's final round, despite her canonically dying in the second.
She can be seen going downstairs after Mingle despite dying in said game.
Trivia[]
She is the player with the lowest number to have voted X in every single voting round before their death.
Player 011 is has fourth lowest number out of every female in the 37th Squid Game behind Player 005, Player 006, and Player 009.
She is the first double digit female player.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 011 was portrayed by Ahn Mi-jin.
