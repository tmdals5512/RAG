Kim Seong-rak (김성락 Gim Seong-rak?) was the actor who portrayed Player 010, Player 040 in Red Light, Green Light, Player 043, Player 082, Player 085, and Player 201 before, during, and after the Special Game in the Netflix TV show Squid Game.
