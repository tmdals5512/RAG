Trivia[]
His first name is shared with Player 400's actress, Nam Ji-hyun.
Kim Ji-hyun stated that he quit on set, presumably prior to the filming of the fourth game.
As he quit, an additional extra had to portray his corpse. Which is why in the show, only his legs were seen.
