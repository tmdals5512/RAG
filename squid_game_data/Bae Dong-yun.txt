Bae Dong-yun (백동윤 Baeg Dong-yun?) is a actor, who portrayed Chae Gang-sun (Player 134) in Red Light, Green Light, Player 312 in Tug of War, Player 151 in the dormitory before Marbles in Season 1 and Player 034 in Mingle in Season 2 of the Netflix TV series Squid Game.
