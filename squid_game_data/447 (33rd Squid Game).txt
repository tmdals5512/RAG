Before "Red Light, Green Light"
We know nothing about Player 447's life before joining the Squid Game. She was likely in debt and joined the games after playing Ddakji with The Recruiter.

"Red Light, Green Light"
Player 447 was taken by a van and transported to the island. After waking up with the other players, she made her first appearance in the dormitory before Red Light, Green Light. She can be seen listening to the guards' speech and signing a contract. Her photo was later taken, and she proceeded to the first game. She can be seen in Red Light, Green Light. She eventually passed the first game after surviving a lot of chaos.

"Hell"
Player 447 participated in the voting. She voted to stop the games. She eventually returned home after 100 more players had voted X, compared to 100 who had voted O.

"The Man with the Umbrella"
She returned to the games and proceeded to Sugar Honeycombs. She chose a triangle dalgona and can be seen waiting in line. She passed the second game just in time and is seen leaving the game area after the game ends.

"Stick to the Team"
After the game, Sugar Honeycombs, she can be seen watching the results of the second game with Player 019 and another player. She later survives the Special Game and a male Player 447 makes the next appearance in the white lobby. He can be seen sitting between Player 403 and Player 088 in the game area. Although He is then seen in Team Five, which was consisted entirely of men. His team was announced to play against Team 4, which was weaker in appearance, but managed to gain an advantage with strategic advice from Oh Il-nam.  They were struggling to beat them, but the team leader Player 194 was able to rally their team, giving them enough strength to begin overpowering Team 4.

"A Fair World and Fate"
Just as Team 5 was on the verge of pulling Team 4 off the platform, Cho Sang-woo's quick thinking led to a last-minute plan of stepping forward. This move caused Player 447 and his teammates to fall from the platform, causing all of them to be eliminated. All their icons disappear off-screen.

Trivia
Both He and His counterpart from 37th Squid Game died being part of a team.
They usually tie their hair up during games and lets it down again in the dormitory, unlike other players who leave it up once they tie it up.
Mistakes
Player 447 was portrayed by four different extras.
The first Player 447 is seen in a majority of his scenes.
The second one is seen in Red Light, Green Light. He is seen running to the doors.
The third one is seen in the Tug of War game area before the game begins.
And the fourth actor plays Player 447, when He competes against Team Four.
Player 447 shares His icon with Player 070.
Their icon is also reused in Player 005's player file.
Behind the scenes
Player 447 was portrayed by Kim Ran and three unknown extras.
