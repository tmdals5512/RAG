History
Park Yeong-gil's dog was first seen when Choi Woo-seok was breaking into Yeong-gil's house. He then left the doghouse and encountered Woo-seok again, attacking him.

Fate
The dog was shot dead by Woo-seok with a revolver after attacking him. However, afterwards, the police showed up and arrested Woo-seok after the neighbors heard the gunshot.
