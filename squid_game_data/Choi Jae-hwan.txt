Choi Jae-hwan (최재환 Choe Jae-hwan?) is a South Korea actor who played Kim Mu-dong in the Season 3 of the Netflix TV series Squid Game. He's known for his roles in TV show like Warrior Baek Dong Soo (2011) and Fate (2008).
