History[]
Player 018 was in tremendous debt, so he decided to enter the Squid Games. He entered a van, got knocked out by gas, and woke up in a room with 455 other players. He signed his contract, got his photo taken, and was taken to the 1st game, Red Light, Green Light. He stood still during the chaos and walked to the finish line. After that, he looked at the prize money and still voted X to end the games. Even at the end of the games, he returned, got his food, and went upstairs to the second game, Sugar Honeycombs. He chose the Umbrella shape. 

Fate[]
Unfortunately for him, he later cracked his dalgona, and was therefore shot by a soldier.
