History
Before 2020
He was born in North Korea in an unknown year, but later made his way to South Korea. He lost some family member close to him because that person didn't receive a kidney for transplantation and eventually died. He started working for the Squid Games and became the leader of their secret organ trafficking ring. Around 2017 he met Kang No-eul and eventually got her hired for the squid games as a soldier number 11. He (presumably) was a recruiter from 1997-2014 (possibly) who used to recruit people before The Salesman but he used to recruit people to be either as a player or as a guard. At some point he befriended Park Yeong-gil and the other Fishing Group Members and went fishing with them.

2020
The Officer was a member of the Fishing Group and a secret leader of Organ Trafficking ring. He told Kang No-eul, "Last time, we had a player do the surgeries, in exchange for information about the games they were going to play." the player mentioned by him being Byeong-gi from the 33rd Squid Game. This conversation implies that The Officer was somehow involved in the Games during 2020. However, it is not yet confirmed whether he was already an Officer at that time or if he was promoted later for some unknown reason.

2024
     Masked Officer orders all the managers to guard the stairs to the control room.  
The Officer is employed possibly before 37th Squid Game but definitely after 33rd Squid Game (possibly getting his rank as the officer in 34th Squid Game) and carries out many of duties displayed by the Front Man during the games in 2020, mainly supervising and commanding the other guards. His hiring date is unknown nor who is behind the mask.     The Officer oversees and monitors the screens closely during the rebellion of the players.  
He serves as the acting senior-most individual in charge of the games after the first game is concluded, whilst the Front Man is covertly playing the game in order to supervise Gi-hun.
He is a part of the underground organ harvesting operation and is frustrated when No-eul eliminates Kim Nam-du during red light green light that was meant to be kept alive. He has a discussion with No-eul where he advises her to not interrupt their operation again.
During the player revolt, he orders many soldiers to fight the players and stop them from reaching the control room, as well as covertly cooperating with the Front Man to organize his safe return into the control room to emerge as the Front Man to confront Gi-hun.

Fate
No-eul was seen fighting with Masked Officer in the elevator, after she said that she needed to take the evidence from his brains while No-eul held him at gunpoint to delete every trace of Park Gyeong-seok. They eventually took the fight to the Office, leaving the gun behind the elevator door. After a fight broke out in the office, the Masked Officer eventually escalated down, as No-eul was hit on the head with a broken glass bottle.
No-eul, barely alive, is seen crawling to the elevator door. The Masked Officer asks where she is going, until he realizes what she is looking for. As soon as he found out she was reaching for the gun, Masked Officer is shot repeatedly by Kang No-eul during a brawl in the Front Man's office. His body was incinerated when the island was blown up.

Personality
     The Officer orders the retreat of the remaining guards inside the main player room.  
The Officer is shown to be stern and strict, and is very passionate about the work done by the Guards being done to the best of their ability. He drops the stern personality during the revolution, where he is shown to be more panicked and paranoid due to the situation, but he does not cease his work and continues to command the guards regardless.  
With his stern personality and strictness, he does participate in the organ harvesting operation and cares greatly about the organization and process of it. The Masked Officer is not a completely evil individual, alongside his boss he has dealt with loss and transitioned it into power, controlling the Organ Trafficking Conspiracy. It is unknown what his motive is for supporting the operation or if the Front Man is fully aware of the operation. As seen in Season 1, the Front Man does not care whether or not his personnel harvest organs, as he only cares about the equality of the game.

Trivia
It's likely that he recruited Kang No-eul to be a pink soldier before 2024 because the rookie guards are most likely low-ranked workers who make their way up to higher ranks.
He is the only director of the games whose name is unknown as the host's name is Oh Il-nam and The Frontman's name is Hwang In-ho.
It is likely that he would have become the next Front Man if Gi-hun had killed Hwang In-ho the night before the final game with the knife given to him.
He is the last organ trafficker to die.
He shares some similarities with his boss Hwang In-ho as both of them lost their loved ones because they couldn't afford a organ transplant to save them and worked for the Squid Games after witnessing a painful loss. However the difference is that In-ho is uninvolved in the organ trafficking ring whereas the Officer himself is involved in the organ trafficking. He claims that they are doing it to save others who need a organ transplant and don't suffer the same loss like him.
He is the last non-player villain to die.
He is the only director of the squid game to be killed by his own former minion as Oh Il-nam died from a brain tumor and The Frontman is still alive.
Some fans intend that there will be American Masked Officer in Squid Game USA which is highly expected by fans to be released in an unknown year.
He has a lot of different liquors in his room, and often drinks them regardless of the situation. By this it can be highly implied that he is a liquor addict.
He, Kang No-eul and Kang-Sae-byeok are the only major characters to be born in North Korea.
Similar to Kang Sae-byeok who lost her father, her grandparents and her older brother in North Korea and Kang No-eul who lost her husband and daughter who was just an infant in North Korea, he also lost his loved one in North Korea but unlike Sae-byeok who joined squid game to build a better life for her and her younger brother and to bring her mother from North Korea and No-eul who joined the game as pink soldier to find and reunite with her daughter, he went to the dark path like his boss In-ho but with a more villainous trait.
It's very likely that he is running the organ trafficking conspiracy for years as this isn't the first time he started it, he was already the ringleader of this illegal business for a long time. He had stopped it only due to Byeong-gi's Death and Jung-ho's absence from 34th-36th Squid Games.
It's very likely that he uses the corpse that aren't shot in head or are killed in special game for organ trafficking like players who weren't shot in head in Red Light Green Light, Six-Legged Penthathlon and Mingle and the players that were killed in special game.
It's very likely that He had been working for the games long before Hwang In-ho even joined the game.
It's likely that during his time as a recruiter he recruited The Salesman to be a pink guard.
The Masked Officer is one of the seven characters confirmed to be a smoker. The others being Seong Gi-hun, Park Jung-bae, Cho Sang-woo, Thanos, Jang Deok-su and Han Mi-nyeo.
He is the only character killed by Kang No-eul that she wasn't neutral towards when killing, as she appeared shocked once he died.
