History[]
Kim Jung-ho was born on June 12, 1981. As an adult, he got a job as a professor at Eungeol University Hospital. He was married to woman named Park Eun-hee and had a son, Kim Tae-sin, and a daughter, Kim Si-eun.
Jung-ho became a staff member in the Squid Games in 2020. He became a part of the organization's medical staff the following year after competing training for it. He took a leave of absence from the games starting in 2022, and returned in time for the 37th Squid Game. He became involved in an organ trafficking ring led by the Masked Officer, who needed him to act as the group's surgeon.

37th Squid Game[]
So you're Number 011.  You know how much money we lost 'cause of the shit you pulled out there?
Kim Jung-ho to Kang No-eul before opening up Park Gyeong-seok in "Keys and Knives"
It is unknown if Jung-ho participated in quelling the revolt. After it ended, Jung-ho and the other guards received a coffin with Park Gyeong-seok in it. Jung-ho and the other organ traffickers thought it was a “gift” from No-eul. They opened the coffin and cut Gyeong-seok open a bit, which caused No-eul to fight the organ traffickers. Jung-ho was the only one to live. He begged for mercy, claiming that unlike the other organ traffickers, he was only harvesting the organs for money. He then said that Gyeong-seok needed a transfusion due to blood loss. No-eul said hers would work, so Jung-ho asked what her blood type is. She said her blood type is type O. No-eul held Jung-ho at gunpoint as he extracted her blood. Jung-ho transferred No-eul's blood to Gyeong-seok, but No-eul shot him in the head and dressed his body in Gyeong-seok's outfit.
