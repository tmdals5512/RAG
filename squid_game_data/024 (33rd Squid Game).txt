History[]
Player 024 was presumably in massive debt and decided to join the Squid Games for a chance to win a huge cash prize. He called the number on the recruiting card, and got into a mysterious car. He then woke up in a massive dormitory with 454 other players. Player 024 watched as many other players' debts were revealed in front of him. Later on, he listened to the manager explain the rules of the games, he signed a contract along with the other players, he took his photo in the photo booth, and he made his way up a flight of stairs to the first game.
Once Player 024 entered the arena of Red Light, Green Light, he looked around the massive room of the game. Player 024 stretched a little bit before the game began, as he listened to the instructions of Young-hee. A few moments into the game, a massive wave of panic and fear spreads throughout the entire arena as many players are gunned down and executed. Player 024, however, managed to keep his cool and stand still. He eventually makes it through the mass genocide and continues to progress through the game.

Fate[]
Not long after the mass genocide, Player 024 stumbled during a red light and lost his balance. In return, he was shot on the chest for moving.

Trivia[]
Player 024's recurring actor is the same as Player 187's actor, one of the most recurring minor players of Season 1.
Player 024's faulty icon is the same as Player 161's and Player 294's icon.
Player 024's 37th Squid Game counterpart is a recurring minor character in Season 2.
Mistakes[]
Another Player 024 is shown at the lobby before the first game begins.
Player 024 is shown during the Fly Me to the Moon scene, despite being shot before that scene.
Player 024 is seen on the finish line completing the game in an error.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 024 was portrayed by Na Jo-eun and three unknown extras.
