History[]
Player 197 was in heavy debt and chose to enter the Squid Games to win money, being picked up and sedated by Squid Game staff and taken to the island where the games take place. After waking up in a large room with 455 other players, he signed a contract and took his photo to enter the games.
When most players started to run, Player 197 was about to also run but was able to control himself from doing so, saving him. He managed to survive the first game, Red Light, Green Light, after most of the contestants had been wiped out. Afterwards, like everyone else, Player 197 was shaken by the deaths of the contestants, when the prize money was finally revealed. A vote was held if the game should continue, and the majority voted no. However, those who were willing to return could, and he returned, along with most of the other players. He would get his breakfast after returning, go back to sleep, and then get his breakfast then go to the second game, Sugar Honeycombs. Player 197 would pick circle, and attempt to carve his dalgona.

Fate[]
Player 197 would however fail and would crack his dalgona, the soldier would then up to him and shot him in the head, eliminating Player 197. His corpse was then seen and his icon would fade.
