Circle of Trust was the sixth and penultimate game of the First Season of Squid Game: The Challenge. This game is a modified version of Heads up, Seven up.
Players were led into the white room, and seated at desks, arranged in a circle, with a podium in the middle holding a mini Coffin. This game is played in 6 rounds. When a round begins, all players must put on their blindfold, and remain seated while a ball containing a player's number is randomly selected by the Masked Manager. Once a ball is selected, the guards walk around in a circle around the desks, before tapping the selected player on the shoulder.
The selected player removes their blindfold, and picks up the coffin, and places it on the desk of a player they wish to eliminate, before going back to their seat and reapplying their blindfold.
Afterwards, all players remove their blindfolds, and the player with the box on their desk stands up. The player must now guess who placed the box on their desk. If they guess correctly, the person who placed the coffin is eliminated, and the guesser returns to their seat. If they guess incorrectly, the guesser is eliminated, and then the falsely accused and the coffin placer remain in the game.
