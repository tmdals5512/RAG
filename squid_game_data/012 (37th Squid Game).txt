History[]
Player 012 decided to join the Squid Games to help with her large amount of debt. After signing the player consent form with 454 other players, she was officially signed up for the games. In Red Light, Green Light, Player 012 could be seen walking into the arena, appearing confused. Later on, she was able to survive by listening to Gi Hun, and stood still, leading her to eventually finish and pass the game. Afterwards, she could be seen looking up at the piggy bank in the dormitory and walking to the first floor. Due to the horror she had to experience, she voted "X" to terminate the games.
In the second game, Six-Legged Pentathlon, Player 012 teamed up with Players 062, 099, 114, and 243. She chose to play Jegi and was able to pass all of the mini-games with her team and survive off-screen. After the game, she was seen standing next to Player 111 and Player 134, listening and watching Player 043 and Player 394 complain about how the money isn't enough for them. Later in the voting, she voted to terminate the games for a second and final time.

Fate[]
During Mingle, Player 012 was shot and killed during the second round of Mingle for not finding a room in time.

Mistakes[]
In Mingle, when trying to find a room with Player 051, they attempted to get into the same room Park Gyeong-seok and Cho Hyun-ju were running to. However, because of Gyeong-seok and Hyun-ju getting into the room before them, 012 and 051 were shot and killed for not getting into a room in time. However, this is an error as she canonically died in Mingle round 2.

Behind the scenes[]
Player 012 was portrayed by Son Young-min.
