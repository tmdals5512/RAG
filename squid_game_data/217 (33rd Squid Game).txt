History[]
Player 217 was likely in heavy debt and decided to join the Squid Games to pay it off. He woke up along with 455 other players in the lobby, where he signed his contract and took his photo. After that, he was led to the first game, Red Light, Green Light. After the first two deaths, a mass scare was triggered after players realized the game's style of elimination. Player 217 was one of the many to run to the doors.

Fate[]
During the initial scare that occurred as a result of Player 324’s death. Player 217 was one of the people who panicked, getting shot in the chest by a sniper right before reaching the doors.

Trivia[]
Player 217 is similar to the 37th Squid Game counterpart. Both were men, both ran to the doors during the mass panic, and both were shot & eliminated.
Mistakes[]
Player 217’s icon is shared with Player 397, Player 097, and the erroneous picture for Player 455.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 217 was portrayed by an Seo Yang-hun and Tae Joon-ho in his icon, and a unknown extra.
