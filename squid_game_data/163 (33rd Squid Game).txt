History[]
Player 163 was in high debt for unknown causes, so she decided to enter the Squid Games. She woke up in a room with 455 others players. She then signed her contract, took her picture and was then taken to the 1st game. She managed to survived the initial chaos and was even able to make some progress into the game.

Fate[]
During Fly Me to the Moon scene, Player 163 moved through the Red Light, then was shot by a sniper. Her icon faded.

Behind the Scenes[]
Player 163 was portrayed by Jeong Sin-ho.

Trivia[]
Her icon was taken by Player 361 , which is her number backwards.
