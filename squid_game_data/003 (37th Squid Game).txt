History[]
Player 003 was presumably in massive debt and decided to join the Squid Games for a chance to win a huge cash prize. He called the number on the recruiting card, and got into a mysterious car. He then woke up in a giant room with 454 other players. He was likely confused and decided to make his way into the center of the room, where he watched as many other players debts were revealed in front of him. Later on, he listened to the manager explain the rules of the games, he signed a contract along with the other players, he took his photo in the photo booth, and he made his way up a flight of stairs to the first game. 
During Red Light, Green Light, he and the other players watched as a strange man began screaming that the game was dangerous. Player 003 and the other players ignored Gi-hun's warnings, as they thought he was crazy and wanted the money for himself. He and the other players then continued the game without any worry. However, after sounds of gunshots ran out, Player 003 started to panic and run towards the doors in an attempt to escape.

Fate[]
During his attempts to run, Player 003 is shot to death for violating the rules of the first game. This would result in his elimination.

Trivia[]
