History
Player 431 was in massive debt and decided to join the Squid Games for a chance to win a huge cash prize. He called the number on the recruiting card, and got into a mysterious car. He then woke up in a giant room with 454 other players. He then made his way to the center of the room and watched as many other players debts were revealed in front of him. Later on, he signed a contract and made his way up a flight of stairs to the first game.
During the first game, he was soon terrified as players were shot in front of him during the mass panic. He was able to control himself and stand still. He eventually completed the first game after listening to Gi-hun's commands. After returning to the dormitory, many players pleaded the guards to let them go. However, The guards instead showed everyone the massive increase in prize money that accumulated after the first game. They then explained the voting process to all of the players. Player 431 did not care about the money and decided to vote X during the first vote. Unfortunately for him and the other Xs, more players voted to continue the games.
Later during Six-Legged Pentathlon, Player 431 partnered up with Player 298, Player 397 and 2 other unknown players and managed to complete all 5 minigames within the 5 minute timer. After successfully completing Six-Legged Pentathlon, him and the other players held another vote. He decided vote X again along with many other X players.
Later on during Mingle, He managed to partner up with other players and passed all 5 rounds. After round 4 he can be seen exiting room 40 with Player 080, Player 113, Player 116, Player 259 and Player 334. After Mingle, him and the other remaining players returned to the dormitory and have a third vote. Player 431 decided to vote X again along with other players. This vote ends up in a tie, meaning they will vote again in the morning. Player 431 and the other players seemed disappointed and moved back to their beds. Later on, Player 431 was informed that there were 5 eliminations as a result of The bathroom fight. Him and the other Xs then had a headcount, which is when they figured out they had more members compared to the Os. Him and the other Xs were happy and excited after learning this. During this, the Os weren't happy and talked about killing some of the Xs during the night. Later when the lights turned off, Player 431 went to bed unaware about the Os plan. Suddenly, the Os charged into the Xs bunk beds and started killing as many Xs as possible.

Fate
During the Special Game, Player 431 was stabbed repeatedly in his neck with a broken glass bottle by Player 192. His corpse can later be seen laying in a bed during Player 067's death, meaning he was likely killed in his sleep.

Trivia
He is the only player with their player number in the 4XXs to die in the Special Game.
He is one of the 4 characters with glasses to survive the first 3 games. The other 3 being Park Yong-sik, Player 048, and Player 366.
Although he is very close friends with Player 297 and they are even seen teaming up before the Pentathlon, during the Pentathlon they are on different teams.
He is the only recurring player in the 400 range to pass Mingle.
He's the only 37th Squid Game player whose icon has an incorrect number.
He, Player 432 and Player 433 are the first ones to vote X three times after each other.
He has some similarities with Player 219:
Both are the only recurring players seen voting during the third vote, both are the last recurring male player in their respective ranges (4XX and 2XX), and both died during Special Game.
He is the third to last male player to press the X button, with Gi-hun being the last in the finals, and 401 being second to last in the third vote.
Mistakes
His icon does not show the number "431" before RLGL, but a different number.
Behind the scenes
Player 431 was portrayed by Park Kwang-jin.
