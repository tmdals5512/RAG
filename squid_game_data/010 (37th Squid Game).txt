History[]
Before the Games[]
Shortly before Halloween, a recruiter approached Player 010 and played a game of Ddakji with him. The recruiter invited him to the Squid Games, where he could earn more money, The Recruiter gave him a card.
On Halloween night, He's calls the card number, A van arrived with some pinkmen with masks in front and behind there were more people, without warning they put him to sleep. He woke up in a Dormitory with 454 other people were sedated and taken to a giant room on a remote island, where he signed his consent to live and had his photo taken to participate in the games.

"001"[]
He woke up in a strange room with more people, then he saw the pink men again who took him in the van, He saw more people who were asking questions to the pink men to which they began to reveal debts of some people who were not allowed to pay those debts to which understood that there were more people like him who were not allowed to pay debts, then the men in pink showed a piggy bank that would be filled with money if the people were eliminated. The pink men told them that they had to sign a consent in case they wanted to participate to win the money that would accumulate. They gave him a paper that had some clauses, having so many debts, signed the consent. Then he went to take a photo and went up to the first game
In the first game, "Red Light, Green Light" the goal of the game was for players to reach the other end of an enclosed field, where the doll was located, in five minutes. They could move when the doll shouted "green light," but if it shouted "red light," they had to stay still while the doll watched them. Any player caught moving was eliminated. Player 010 and the other players felt the sound of a bullet, it was a player who was shot dead. He did not move so that this would not happen to him after the chaos of the first players he and none of the players moved until Seong Gi-hun told them that they had to reach the goal to survive to which he told them to hide behind another Player. After Seong Gi-hun shouted at everyone to make a run for the finish line, Player 010 without hesitation did so, he ran, managing to complete the first game.
After returning to the dormitory, the vote was held in order to determine whether or not to continue the games. He decided to vote O.

"Six Legs"[]
After the votes they gave him and the players lunchbox food: It contains egg, sausage, kimchi, white rice and black beans. The lunch boxes come with plastic water bottles and are somewhat dry for him to prepare for tomorrow.
The next morning they send him and the other players up the stairs for the next game, it is announced that the second game is "Six-Legged Pentathlon" which consists of five children's games: Ddakji (game I had already played with the recruiter), Biseokchigi, Gonggi, Paengi and Jegi, I manage to team up with the Player 061, Player 275, Player 326 and Player 356.

"One More Game" and Fate[]
After the other players' turn, it was his and his team's turn. Unfortunately they did not make it through the game and he and his team were eliminated, the guards shot them until they were eliminated.

Behind the scenes[]
Player 010 was portrayed by Kim Dong-min.

Trivia[]
He and his season 1 counterpart both made it as far as the second game and died in it.
He is likely the oldest 37th Squid Game player, as his actor is over 80 years old.
