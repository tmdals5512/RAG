History[]
At some point before Squid Game, Player 053 gathered a lot of debt, leaving him desperate for money. He eventually met a man in a suit who offered to play ddjaki with him. The man then gave him a card with a phone number to join the Squid Games, He made the call and then went to an address given to him. Suddenly, a van showed up. When he went inside, sleeping gas was released, making him fall asleep.
Player 053 then woke up to classical music in a jumpsuit on a bunk bed, in a creepy warehouse called the "dormitory." Then the "Guards," came in, telling him the instructions and that they were in a competition. He then signed a contract and proceed to walk into a giant room with multiple staircases, with classical music following the instructions. He then took his picture, following the guards into the first game: Red Light, Green Light.
Player 053 could be seen confused and doubtful about the giant doll, and an announcer explained the game rules, and so, The game begun. He and many other players ran, but the player that was right in front of him, Player 324, ran too fast and couldn't stop in time, followed by a loud bang and him dropping to the ground. This death made Player 250 scared and try to run away in panic, before getting shot, with his blood landing on Player 306's face, which made her scream and also get shot. After witnessing the death of 3 players, a lot of players tried to run to the doors but were shot. Player 053 was one of them, but it was an error.
After witnessing the death of 3 players, A lot of players tried to run to the doors but were shot and eliminated from the game. Although Player 053, along with many other players, managed to survive the initial chaos, and so the game continued, and Player 053 stood still and passed the finish line.
During the voting, its unknown what Player 053 voted, since he wasn’t shown in this episode. But it can be assumed that he returned to the games after players voted to end the games since he is shown in the following episode.
After Player 053 came back into the games, he went into the same giant room with multiple staircases to get to the second game, Sugar Honeycombs. He walked into the second game with 186 other players. The announcer instructed the players to choose one of the four shapes on the wall and line up in front of it. The four shapes were, the circle, the triangle, the star, and the umbrella. He was thinking about what shape he was gonna choose until he decided to choose a unknown shape. He decided to position himself near the umbrella queue. When Player 369 died, he was seen shocked at his death.
