History[]
Player 022 had a large debt and decided to join the Squid Games. In Red Light, Green Light Player 022 follows Seong Gi-hun instructions and positions himself in one of the rows, he manages to reach the finish line. Player 022 motivated by his large debt decides to vote "O" to pay his debt and luckily for him, the Team O wins the voting.

Fate[]
In Six-Legged Pentathlon Player 022 allies with the Player 208, Player 037, Player 385 and Player 025. Unfortunately, Player 022's team fails to complete all 5 minigames within 5 minutes, so they are eliminated.

Mistakes[]
Player 022 can be seen running away during the mass panic, which started after the death of Lee Eun-ju.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 022 was portrayed by Cha Ji-ho.
