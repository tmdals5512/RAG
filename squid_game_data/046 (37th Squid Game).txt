History[]
Player 046 was in massive debt, Therefore he decided to join the Squid Games in hopes of paying off his debt. After being transported to the Island, He wakes up with 454 other players, he wakes up he listens to the guards explain the rules of the games. After signing the consent form and taking his picture, he heads up the Stairs to the first game. Player 046 did not believe Gi-huns warnings and he continued on as normal. After Kang Mi-na is shot he stays still and forms a conga line with other Players to pass the game. during the post game vote he votes O, for the 2nd game he teams up with Player 330, Player 234, Player 032 and Player 231.

Fate[]
Player 046 failed to get to the finish line in time, therefore he got eliminated.

Trivia[]
A player named Yang Hyeong-taek won the 15th Squid Games in 2002 with this number.
Mistakes[]
Another Player 046 can be seen during his death scene in Six-Legged Pentathlon.
