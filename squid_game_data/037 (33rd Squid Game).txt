History[]
After accumulating large amounts of debt, Player 037 decided to join the Squid Games in order to win money after playing a game of Ddakji with the Recruiter and being given a card with a phone number on it, which she could call if she wished to participate in even larger sets of Games, for even larger Rewards. She was picked up and sedated by Squid Game Staff after calling the number on the card and giving the person on the other end of the phone the information they requested before being transported to the Island where the games take place. 
She can be seen walking past Oh Il-nam in the Squid Game lobby as Seong Gi-hun talks to him after waking up, most likely confused as to where she was. After the situation arised between Seong Gi-hun, Jang Deok-su and Kang Sae-byeok, a Manager walked out and explained the motives of the Games as well as how everyone in the Dormitory had major debts that they could not hope to pay off; mentioning about how they're giving the Players an opportunity and a chance before revealing the Piggy bank where in which the Players' Prize Money was planned to be stored.
After signing the Player Consent Form, Player 037 swiftly made her way towards the Game Hall before taking her Icon Photo and walking up the stairs to the first game, which was announced to be Red Light, Green Light. Once the game started, she played well, stopping at the Red Light where Player 324 was eliminated, as well as stopping at the red light where in which Player 250 and Player 306 were eliminated as well before the mass panic. After this panic, the rules of the game were re-explained and she managed to stay still through this panic, and kept on playing the game normally while still most likely extremely frightened by what she just witnessed.
She managed to survive the first game, staying still at every red light and only moving on green light in each turn until she made it across the finish line and passed the game. After most of the contestants had been wiped out from the first game, she was most likely shaken by the deaths of the contestants after heading down the Stairs back to the Dormitory before a Manager came out, and announced that 255 Players were eliminated in Red Light, Green Light and that 201 Players had survived with one of them being her as the accumulated Prize Money from the first game's eliminations was revealed, dropping down into the Piggy bank from above.
When the prize money was finally revealed a vote was held after Cho Sang-woo mentioned Clause three of the consent form, which stated that if the majority of the Players  voted to leave, the games would be terminated. While the vote was a 100 - 100 split, due to Oh Il-nam voting to leave a majority had voted out of the games, and the games temporarily ended; though only after the Manager announced that if a majority decided to come back again - the games would resume.  After leaving the games, she most likely came to the conclusion that she thought she would be better off in the games than outside of them.
After leaving, she eventually returned along with 186 other Players before heading up the Stairs to the second game, which was announced to be Sugar Honeycombs, subsequently surviving it by carving out her Dalgona within the time limit without breaking it before returning to the Dormitory where the Piggy bank was once again lowered, revealing the Prize money accumulated from all the Players eliminated in Sugar Honeycombs after breaking their Dalgona. She most likely witnessed Player 271's death along with many other players where in which they discovered that killing each other adds to the Prize Pool.
This lead to the Special Game's chaos that occured night after the players found out they could kill each other, which she was able to survive before heading to the Game Hall and going up the stairs to the third game the next day. After reaching the third game's lobby, it was announced that the game would be played in teams of ten, so she managed to form a team with nine other players before entering the arena where it was announced that the game would be Tug of War. Once all the Players sat down it was announced that her Team would be one of the first two team's to play.

Fate[]
Player 037 was on Player 245's team, that lost to Jang Deok-su's team, causing them to fall, and have the rope cut leading to them to fall to their deaths resulting in their elimination from the game. After this her body was presumably incinerated by Workers after being placed into a coffin. After this, ₩100,000,000 would've been added to the Prize Pool as a result of her death.
