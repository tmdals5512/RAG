History[]
Player 009 had tremendous debt he couldn't pay off and chose to enter the Squid Games to win money. After waking up in a large room with 455 other players, he signed a contract and took his photo.
Later, everyone was taken to the first game: Red Light, Green Light. When the doll turned around, everyone ran forward, with Player 250 and Player 324 ahead of everyone. Player 324 was unable to stop in time when the doll turned back around, resulting in his elimination. Player 324 then gets shot.

Fate[]
When the doll turned around and called out "green light", Players 324 and 250 ran forward. When the doll called "red light", Player 324 was unable to stop in time and lost his balance, resulting in him being eliminated and shot dead. Upon realizing what had just happened, Player 250 attempted to flee before being shot himself, his blood splattering across Player 306's face. The horror of this caused Player 306 to place her hands on her face and shriek in panic, and made lots of players scared and run for the doors in an attempt to flee, whom among of those was Player 009.

Trivia[]
He is likely the first 00X player to die placing 347th.
Ironically, his counterpart from the 37th holds the second-highest-placing among single-digit players.
Going by numerical order, He is the first iconless player in the 33rd Squid Game.
Despite this, it's confirmed through specific patterns that Player 168 and Player 282's icon will be his icon.
It's possible his name was intended to be Kim Kwang-hyun (김광현 Gim Gwang-hyeon?) due to that being the name of the 9th winner on the winners list.
