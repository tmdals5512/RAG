History[]
Player 167 was in heavy debt and decided to join the Squid Games competition held in 2024 after being contacted by staff. After many players were eliminated in the first game, the accumulated money prize was shown, and the vote was held. Although he voted to end the games, the majority voted to continue them instead. 

Fate[]
During Six-Legged Pentathlon, the second game, Player 167 teamed with Players 004, 412, 204, and 437. He played Biseokchigi and successfully passed.
They made it to the fourth game, Paengi, before they ran out of time and were subsequently gunned down.

Behind the scenes[]
Player 167 was portrayed by Na Jun-seok.
