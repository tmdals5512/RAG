In-game description[]
Kara Kwon balances her two cultures like a taekwondo black belt, fitting for the daughter of a former world champion.
This Korean-Australian honor roll student channels her fighting spirit into keeping her family's business alive.
But when financial blows threaten to knock out the family's future, Kara steps into the arena to risk everything in the high-stakes Squid Game where it's winner-take-all.

Trivia[]
In the launch trailer, Kara is supposedly the winner of the games.
