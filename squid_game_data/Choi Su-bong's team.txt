The Thanos Team was a team formed in the Squid Game competition 37th Squid Game. Led by Thanos, a well-known rapper, the team was formed during the second game, Six-Legged Pentathlon, with five members consisting of Nam-gyu, Gyeong-su, Se-mi and Park Min-su.

Information[]
Thanos (Player 230) recognized Lee Myung-gi (Player 333) as MG Coin, a YouTuber who made crypto investments and convinced his followers to invest in a crypto that turned out to be a scam, resulting in losses of millions. Thanos befriended Nam-gyu (Player 124) after discovering they had both been victims of the scam and confronted Myung-gi. Thanos began to blame Myung-gi for causing him to lose millions and told him that he wanted him to pay back what he lost in that scam. Myung-gi told him that he never forced anyone to buy that crypto and that everyone was responsible for their own investments, something that he always said at the end of his videos. Myung-gi made Thanos look like a fool and Thanos decided to hit him, but Nam-gyu stopped him because there were people watching. Thanos then told Myung-gi that he'd better do well in the Squid Games because he planned to make him pay for what he lost and they left. Thanos noticed Kang Mi-na (Player 196) and he found her attractive.
While the Players were taking their pictures, Gyeong-su (Player 256) recognized Thanos and got thrilled to discover that he was a famous rapper. Gyeong-su introduced himself and told Thanos that he was a big fan of his, several players recognized Thanos and wanted to take a photo with him. Nam-gyu took control of the situation and and told the fans to take a group photo, Thanos offered Mi-na the chance to be in the photo, but she declined, the group got ready for the photo, but then a Manager approached and told them that the photos were individual, so Thanos said that he would take a photo with them after the games. On the Stairs, Thanos separated from Nam-gyu and Gyeong-su to talk to Mi-na.
Upon arriving at the first game's hall, Thanos began flirting with Mi-na. Thanos started rapping and told Mi-na he liked her, Mi-na got interested in talking to Thanos and told him to see Young-hee (the giant robotic doll). When it was announced that the first game was Red Light, Green Light, Seong Gi-hun (Player 456 and winner of the 33rd Squid Game) stood at the front and told the players that those who lost would be killed. The players questioned Gi-hun and began to think he was crazy, Mi-na thought that Gi-hun was drun, and Thanos told her that Gi-hun sounded lke his father when he came home drunk and said crazy things; he said that there was a bug in his head. When the game started, Gi-hun told the players when to stop, and they all managed to survive 7 red lights, but there were some who still doubted Gi-hun. Mi-na wondered if Gi-hun was on drugs, to which Thanos replied that he wasn't and that nobody is like that when they're on drugs, Mi-na asked Thanos if he had been drugged and Thanos asked her if she wanted to know. But then Mi-na felt something on her neck and Thanos told her she had a bee on her neck, Mi-na panicked and moved, when she calmed down, she realized she had moved and let out a laugh, but then she was shot in the head (splattering Thanos with her blood) and fell to the ground dead. Mi-na's death sparked panic and some players tried to flee, resulting in them being shot dead. Thanos was shocked by Mi-na's death and terrified that he might die too, so he took out his cross necklace, which contained drug pills and took a drug to avoid the fear. Having taken the drug, Thanos stopped being afraid and managed to complete the first game along with Nam-gyu and Gyeong-su.
Back in the Dormitory, Thanos began to suffer drug withdrawal symptoms and Nam-gyu asked him if he felt bad, to which Thanos replied that he was fine. A Voting was held to decide whether to continue or end the game and Thanos' team decided to vote "O" won more money. In the end, the vote ended 182-183 due to Hwang In-ho (Player 001 and the Front Man in charge of the Squid Games, who entered the games as "Oh Young-il") and the games continued. During the meal time, Thanos and Nam-gyu started bothering and hitting Myung-gi, but In-ho stopped them and beat the aggressors up.
During the second game, Six-Legged Pentathlon, Thanos invited Se-mi (Player 380) to join his team and she accepted after Thanos also accepted Park Min-su (Player 125). Back in the Dormitory, Nam-gyu began insulting the other surviving groups and asked Park Min-su to count them. He reluctantly agreed, but Se-mi stopped him. She openly insulted Nam-gyu, pointing out that the guards would soon tell them how many had died; Thanos interrupted the discussion by talking to Park Min-su and making her note that both he and Nam-gyu were 27 years old (1997), so he had no reason to follow their orders. Then he asked Gyeong-su his age and he replied that he is 26 (1998) and he also asked Se-mi that she lied to him saying that she was 28 years old.
During the third game, Mingle, the team fell apart. In the second round of the game, the number of players was 4, so Thanos kicked Gyeong-su out of the team and he ended up being killed by the Pink Guards. In the third round the number was 3, so Thanos and Nam-gyu made Min-su and Se-mi play rock, paper, scissors to choose who would come with them, Min-su ended up winning due to pressure and fear, so Thanos and Nam-gyu took him away and they left Se-mi alone, but fortunately she managed to team up with Players 400 and 418 and save herself, which relieved Min-su (who felt guilty for leaving Se-mi alone). In the fifth round the number was 2, so Thanos chose to team up with Nam-gyu and they left Min-su alone, but fortunately he managed to team up with Player 015 and save himself. 
Back at the Dormitory, Thanos and Nam-gyu told Min-su that they were happy he was alive and tried to convince him to play one more game. During the Voting, Se-mi decided to join the side of Seong Gi-hun (Player 456) and vote for "X", which upsets Thanos and Nam-gyu. When Min-su's turn comes, Thanos and Nam-gyu try to force him to vote for "O", but Min-su finally decides to do the right thing and votes for "X", joining Gi-hun's (and Se-mi's) side and protecting himself from the bullies, making Se-mi happy. The voting ends in a 50-50 tie and it is announced that there will be another voting the next day.
In the men's bathroom, Min-su is cornered by Thanos and Nam-gyu, who threaten him into joining them in the vote, fortunately Lee Myung-gi (Player 333) arrives and defends Min-su. Team X and Team O start arguing about that Thanos and Nam-gyu interfering with Min-su's vote (which is forbidden) and Thanos and Nam-gyu mock Kim Jun-hee (Player 222) for being pregnant, which infuriates Myung-gi and a fight breaks out, allowing Min-su to escape to the Dormitory. During the fight, Thanos tells Myung-gi that he will take his money, his life, and Jun-hee, but Myung-gi uses a fork to stab Thanos, causing him to bleed to death, freeing Myung-gi, Min-su, and Jun-hee forever and leaving Nam-gyu alone.
At bedtime, a riot breaks out with players killing each other. Min-su grabs a bottle to defend himself and hides in a high bunk bed. Nam-gyu corners Se-mi while Min-su watches them from above. Min-su throws the bottle to help Se-mi, the bottle falls to the floor and breaks, Min-su quickly hides and Nam-gyu turns to see what happened, Se-mi takes advantage that Nam-gyu is distracted to grab the broken bottle and fight, but Nam-gyu overpowers her and stabs her to death, much to Min-su's horror.
Before the fourth game began, Min-su and Nam-gyu were assigned to the Seekers team and were both given a knife to kill the Hiders. Nam-gyu approached Min-su, who was angry with him for killing Se-mi. Nam-gyu asked Min-su if he had ever killed anyone and told him that if he couldn't do it, then he should switch teams, mentioning that Min-su was very good at hiding and that he never found him during the riot. Nam-gyu started mocking Se-mi and found out that Min-su saw him kill her, Nam-gyu told Min-su that he was too cowardly to save her and that Thanos, Se-mi and Gyeong-su were dead, so they were the only members of the team left alive. Nam-gyu told Min-su that they would see each other again and left.
During the game, Nam-gyu dropped Thanos' necklace and Min-su found it, Min-su remembered Nam-gyu's teasing and he took a drug to gain the courage that he needed to pass the game and avenge Se-mi. After the game, Nam-gyu discovered he had lost the drugs and began to suffer withdrawal symptoms, causing him to panic and despair. Knowing that he would die without the drugs, Nam-gyu voted for "X" in the next voting and Min-su voted for "O" to exact his revenge.
During the fifth game, Jump Rope, Nam-gyu was scared by how high the platform they were on was and Min-su saw that there was 1 drug left in the necklace. Min-su approached Nam-gyu and asked if he was okay, to which Nam-gyu told her to leave. Min-su then revealed to Nam-gyu that he had Thanos' necklace and Nam-gyu tried to get it back. Desperately, Min-su told him not to touch it or he would throw the necklace into the cliff. In desperation, Nam-gyu apologized to Min-su for everything he did and begged her to give him the necklace. Min-su threw the necklace onto the bridge and told Nam-gyu to go get it. Nam-gyu started to cross the bridge, terrified of falling, and managed to grab the necklace, but when he opened it, he found it was empty. Nam-gyu froze when he realized that Min-su had tricked him and was hit by the rope, falling to his death and avenging Se-mi. Min-su looked at Nam-gyu's corpse at the bottom of the cliff and took the last drug to pass the game.

Trivia[]
Se-mi and Gyeong-su, being 26 at most, were the youngest members of the team. Thanos, who was 36, was its oldest member.
Techically, this team is the second team, formed by Thanos. The first one consisted of two members, Thanos himself and Kang Mi-na (Player 196), but it was disbanded shortly after it was formed due to Mi-na's death in Red Light Green Light.
All their deaths are due to each other (Gyeong-su by Thanos, Se-mi by Nam-gyu and Nam-gyu by Min-su) or by Myung-gi (Thanos and Min-su).
Min-su is the only member to join the team while being apart of the X team.
Thanos' team was the only main team that didn't have a direct conversation with Gi-hun or his team. The closest they come to having a direct conversation with them was when Su-bong and Nam-gyu fought In-ho and when Gi-hun's team entered the bathroom, witnessing Su-bong bullying Myung-gi and stopping him. The problem are that In-ho wasn't part of the team when he fought them both and when Gi-hun's team stopped Thanos, they didn't talk since Su-bong stopped, seeing In-ho knowing that he would injure him
They all appear in "⭘△☐" whether alive (Park Min-su), in hallucinations (Thanos, Se-mi and Nam-gyu) or files (Gyeong-su)
For some reason, Nam-gyu, Se-mi, and Gyeong-su were never credited with their surnames or even mentioned.
Min-su is the only member of Thanos' team to reach the final game.
Thanos and Min-su are only ones in their team to be killed by Myung-gi.
Each of the male members of the team survived a different number of the six games. Gyeong-su survived two then died in Mingle, Thanos survived the first three then died in the bathroom fight, Nam-gyu survived the first four then died in Jump Rope and Min-su survived the first five games, becoming a finalist but was then the first person to die in the sixth and final game.
The male members of the team die starting with the tallest person and then going progressively to the shortest dying last: Gyeong-su is the tallest, Thanos second tallest, Nam-gyu second shortest and Min-su the shortest. If Se-mi was taller than Nam-gyu then it would follow a perfect sequence of the players dying starting with the tallest and progressively going to the shortest dying last: in other words the shorter you are the longer you live.
If Kang Mi-na is counted as a member of the team then this team holds a strange and unique position in that it contains three of the players who were the first to die in one of the games [Mi-na in Red Light, Green Light, Nam-gyu in Jump Rope and Min-su in Sky Squid Game] and additionally Thanos was the first to die int he bathroom fight. Of the other three-Player 045 for Six-Legged Pentathlon, an unknown player for Mingle since we don't see every room at once and Player 310 for Hide and Seek-they are not known to be affiliated with each other.
Since Thanos said that his team-mates could choose which mini-games to play in the Six-Legged Pentathlon it is likely he was either fairly skilled in all five of them or being slightly overconfident.
Gyeong-su [and Kang Mi-na] are the only member [s] to be killed by a Pink Guard, Nam-gyu fell to his death [somewhat caused by Min-su], Thanos and Min-su were killed by Lee Myung-gi/MC Coin/The Amazing Myung-gi/Player 333 and Se-mi was killed by Nam-gyu.
If Thanos is equivalent to Marvel's purple-skinned Titan who wanted to kill half the universe Thanos this team is equivalent to the Black Order. Hence Nam-gyu could be Ebony Maw, due to being Thanos' closest lackey and both he and Ebony Maw have slightly deformed cheekbones, Se-mi could be Proxima Midnight due to her cool-headedness, effectiveness in combat and being the only main female member, Min-su could be Corvus Glaive due to his somewhat romance with Se-mi and both being fairly quiet in terms of emotion and dialogue and Gyeong-su could be Cull Obsidian due to his unquestioning following of Thanos. Kang Mi-na could be Lady Death.
