Player 161 was in a heavy debt and chose to enter a high risk competition to win money, After waking up in a large room with 455 other people, he signed a contract and took his photo. He then entered the first game, Red Light, Green Light.

Fate[]
Player 161 died in Red Light Green Light, because his icon disappeared.
