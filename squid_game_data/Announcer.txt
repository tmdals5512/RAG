You are witnessing the fate of those who refused to the democratic process of voting and instead attempted to stop the game by using violent means. We will not tolerate any irrational behavior which attempts to destroy the fair rules of this game, and such actions will be punished in accordance with our strict standards. The Announcer to the remaining players showcasing the human chandelier of the Rebellion volunteers before Hide and Seek in Keys and Knives.
The Announcer is the voice that makes announcements in the Squid Games, such as whether a player passes or gets eliminated. 
It is voiced by an unseen woman, who does announcements such as the games' rules to the contestants, guides them, and announces eliminations and those who pass. The announcer is operated by managers who press buttons in the control room to activate voice lines that are then spoken out loud.
Given the announcer's nature, it's possible that the woman that provides the voice for it is unaware of what they are really used for. The announcer may be a Text to Speech in the context of the show.
