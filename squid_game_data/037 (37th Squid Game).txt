Player 038







Player 037 was in heavy debt and chose to enter the Squid Games to win money. After waking up in a large room with 454 other people, signed a contract and took his photo.
In Red Light, Green Light Player 037 follows Seong Gi-hun instructions and positions himself in one of the rows, he manages to reach the finish line.
Player 037 motivated by his large debt decides to vote "O" to pay his debt and luckily for him, the Team O wins the voting.

Fate[]
In Six-Legged Pentathlon, Player 037 allies with the Player 022, Player 208, Player 385 and Player 025. Unfortunately, Player 037's team fails to complete all 5 minigames within 5 minutes, so they are eliminated.

Behind the scenes[]
Player 037 was portrayed by Kim Jin-man and an unknown extra.
