Personality[]
Kim Nam-du is a distrustful and skeptical person, because he didn't trust that they would give him money as soon as he got in, and he also didn't believe Gi-hun's words at the beginning of Red light, Green light, so he even yelled at him, thinking that he was cheating. He also quickly loses his temper because when Player 165 and Player 384 was killed near to him, he began to justify himself by saying that he stood and begged while waving his arms despite of rules game.

History[]
Backstory[]
By 2024, Nam-du had a debt of ₩2,020,000,000 and couldn't pay it back. The Recruiter approached Nam-du and offered him the chance to play Ddakji to win money, the Recruiter gave Nam-du an invitation card to the Squid Games to win more money to pay off his debts and Nam-du decided to join the Squid Games as Player 444, so Nam-du was picked up by a van on the night of October 31, 2024 and taken to The Island where the games were held.

"001"[]
Nam-du woke up in the Dormitory along with the other Players and began to check the place to see what was going on. Soon the Pink Guards arrived and the Manager welcomed the players to the Squid Games, telling them that they would participate in 6 different games over 6 days and those who won all 6 games would receive a Prize money.
Cho Hyun-ju (Player 120) questioned what the manager was saying since they had practically kidnapped them. The manager apologized and explained that it was to maintain the game's security. Lee Eun-ju (Player 283) asked why the masked men wore masks and if they should keep their identities private, Nam-du agreed, asking why they were covering their faces and if they were in an illegal casino, although Eun-ju said that even the dealers didn't wear masks in those places. The manager explained that it was policy not to reveal the faces or identities of the staff and asked them to understand. The manager put on the screen a video of several players playing Ddakji against the Recruiter and mentioned their debts, the manager mentioned Nam-du's debt and said that all the players were living on a cliff-edge with debts they couldn't pay. The manager told the players that they had been given the money they won playing Ddakji and that they had volunteered to play the game, so he asked them if they preferred to go back to living like worthless scum and running from their creditors, or take advantage of the opportunity that they was giving them. Nam-du signed a consent form, took his player photo and followed the staff to the hall of the first game.

Hey, you bastard! You're not fooling us!
Kim Nam-du scolding Gi-hun after his warning before Red Light, Green Light 
The first game was Red Light, Green Light and the players saw Young-hee (the giant robotic doll), everyone remained calm since they believed it was just a kids' game, but then Seong Gi-hun (Player 456 and winner of the 33rd Squid Game) stood at the front and asked everyone to listen to him. Gi-hun told the players that it wasn't just a kids' game and that the losers would be killed, some players thought Gi-hun was crazy, drunk, or on drugs, Player 066 even believed that he was trying to scare them to keep all the money for himself and the players questioned Gi-hun. Nam-du believed that Gi-hun was trying to tricked them and told him not to do that, to which Gi-hun said that what he was telling them it was true and that they should believe him.

I don't wanna die here.
Kim Nam-du telling Gi-hun during the last few seconds of Red Light, Green Light 
The game started and Gi-hun told the players when to stop, Gi-hun's strategy worked and all the players managed to survive 7 red lights. But then Kang Mi-na (Player 196) felt that she had something on her neck and Choi Su-bong/Thanos (Player 230) informed her that she had a bee on her neck, Mi-na panicked, moved and was detected moving, so she was shot in the head. Gi-hun told everyone to stay calm and not move, but Eun-ju screamed in horror and moved when she saw Mi-na's corpse, causing her to be shot in the heart. The players realized that anyone who moved was being shot at and some began to flee in panic, leading to them being shot dead. Nam-du managed to stay still, along with most of the players and they realized that Gi-hun was telling them the truth, so they stayed still for fear of dying. Gi-hun stood at the front and told the players that they had to reach to the finish line or they would die, he told them that Young-hee had motion sensors and that she wouldn't see them if they stood behind someone, so he told them to stand in rows from the tallest to the shortest. The strategy worked and most of the players managed to reach the finish line.

Fate[]
Thank you so much. Thank you so much...
Kim Nam-du thanking Seong Gi-hun and Cho Hyun-ju for saving him, moments before he gets shot to the head in "001"
Nam-du stood behind Players 165 and 384, near the finish line, 165 was detected moving and was shot in the head, his corpse fell backward and pushed 384, she was detected moving and was shot in the heart, her corpse fell backward and pushed Nam-du. Young-hee turned to look at Nam-du and he began to plead, declaring that he did not move and that 384 pushed him, but as he pleaded he was detected moving and Soldier 005 shot him in the right leg, eliminating him and leaving him alive.

I'll help. Now, listen. When it calls green light, get on your feet.
Gi-hun tells Nam-du to get up when it calls green light 
Gi-hun encouraged the players not to give up and told them that they were almost at the finish line, but then he heard Nam-du calling for help. Gi-hun saw that there were 30 seconds left and ran towards Nam-du to save him, Nam-du asked Gi-hun for help, Gi-hun told him that he would help and to get up at the next green light. Nam-du got up and Gi-hun carried him with Hyun-ju's help. With 5 seconds remaining, the 3 players ran at full speed while Park Jung-bae told them to hurry up and they managed to reach the finish line just in time, finishing the first game.
Having managed to reach the finish line in time, Gi-hun asked Nam-du if he was okay and Nam-du thanked Gi-hun and Hyun-ju for saving him, but then Kang No-eul shot him in the head, killing him instantly. The players were horrified to see Nam-du's end and 005 saw that Nam-du had been shot, so he asked No-eul if it was her and No-eul said that she only killed an eliminated player. Gi-hun and Hyun-ju felt sorry for Nam-du's death and exchanged pitying glances.

Legacy[]
An organ trafficking operation was being carried out by a group of corrupt guards (Soldier 005, Soldier 007, Kim Jung-ho (Soldier 016), Soldier 025 and the Masked Officer), in which Jung-ho (the doctor who performed the surgeries) extracted the good organs from the eliminated players to sell them on the black market. The operation was going badly because the players were dead; having been dead for a long time, their organs were damaged and Jung-ho only managed to save the eyes of 1 player, 007 got annoyed upon see the meager merchandise and Jung-ho told him that the players had been dead for a long time and he could only save their eyes, reminding him that he had told him to bring live players, 007 didn't understand what Jung-ho was talking about and said that he left some players alive. Soldiers 005 and 007 checked the players' corpses and saw that they had all been shot in the head or heart. Jung-ho told them that someone shot them again to make sure they died. Nam-du's corpse was among the dead players and Jung-ho mentioned that Nam-du received a shot in the leg and later a shot in the head. Soldier 005 recognized Nam-du and realized and discovered that it was No-eul who did it.

Trivia[]
Kim Nam-du, like Byeong-gi (Player 111), Kim Jun-hee (Player 222) and Lee Myung-gi (Player 333), is a character whose name is known and whose player number has 3 identical digits.
Nam-du was the first player with 3 identical digits to be eliminated in the 37th Squid Game.
His Season 1 counterpart was also the first player with 3 identical digits to die in the Games.
Nam-du would need to be among the last 21 players to be able to pay off his debt, so he would have to survive Jump Rope for his share of the prize to be enough.
Alongside Player 096 and Player 303 from the 33rd Squid Game, they are the only players who managed to complete a game and later die in the same game.
Although Nam-du was eliminated, but not killed, he passed the game. At the end of the game, though, he was shot to the head.
Aside from Seong Gi-hun, Nam-du is the only player in the 400s range to be named.
Interestingly, Gi-hun was his savior alongside Hyun-ju. Both named characters within the 400s range knew one another as an ally.
Like Oh Yeong-uk, Nam-du was also shot in the leg, only this time. However he was able to pass Red Light, Green Light with the help of Gi-hun and Hyun-ju, despite his elimination.
As aforementioned above, his Season 1 counterpart would be Oh Yeong-uk. Not in terms of how they ranked overall in their respective Squid Games, but how similar both were. Both of them were allies with Seong Gi-hun, both of them were shot to the leg and yet survived, and both of them died in Red Light, Green Light.
In contrast to Yeong-uk, Nam-du was closer to the finish line than Yeong-uk was, and he even passed the game.
His name is similar to Player 124's name, Nam-gyu.
He is the only 2024 player in the four hundreds to not have his 2020 counterpart be named.
It is curious that coincidentally, in the Squid Game organized by the youtuber Mr. Beast, his counterpart was the first to pass RLGL, while Nam-du was the last player to cross the finish line.
He is the only player in the 440-449s range to not make it to Six-Legged Pentathlon and Mingle.
