Choi Soon-young (최순영 Choe Sun-yeong?) is the actor who portrayed Player 207 in the dormitory in Season 1, Player 372 during all of his scenes in Season 2, and Player 421 during Mingle in Season 2 of the Netflix TV series Squid Game.
