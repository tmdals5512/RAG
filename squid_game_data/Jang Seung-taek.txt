History[]
Yang Hyung-taek was in tremendous debt and chose to enter the Squid Games to win money, After waking up in a large room called "The Dormitory" with 455 other players, he signed a contract and took his photo. He managed to survive Red Light, Green Light as many other contestants were wiped out after witnessing the death of Player 250 and 306. After the first game, a prize money was held for people to look at which accumulated ₩25.5B. The vote began, He voted O, Hyung-taek would be seen upset after the games were terminated and those who were willing to return could return, along with other 186 players he returned. After returning and getting his food, he would go to bed then wake up the next day to go to the next game, Sugar Honeycombs. Hyung-taek would enter the second arena and see 4 doors with shapes on it, triangle, circle, star and umbrella. Hyung-taek would pick triangle. 

Fate[]
In Sugar Honeycombs, he couldn't cut his triangle dalgona in time, causing him to be shot repeatedly by the soldiers.
