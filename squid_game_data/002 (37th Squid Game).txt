History[]
Player 002 was presumably in massive debt and decided to join the Squid Games for a chance to win a huge cash prize. He called the number on the recruiting card and got into a mysterious car. He then woke up in a giant room with 454 other players. He was likely confused and decided to make his way into the center of the room, where he watched as many other players' debts were revealed in front of him. Later on, he listened to the manager explain the rules of the games, he signed a contract along with the other players, he took his photo in the photo booth, and he made his way up a flight of stairs to the first game.
During Red Light, Green Light, Player 002 and the other players watched as a strange man started screaming about how dangerous the games were. Player 002 and the other players ignored Gi-hun's warnings, as they thought he was crazy and wanted the money for himself. Player 002 and the other players then continued the game without any worry. However, after the sounds of gunshots ran out, and players started getting gunned down, Player 002 made a run for the doors in fear for his life.

Fate[]
During his attempts to run towards the doors, Player 002 was shot dead by a sniper for breaking the rules of the game. This is confirmed by his icon disappearing in the panic, and we also see him getting shot. 

Trivia[]
Player 002 was the first person chosen by The Recruiter to join the game, because Hwang In-ho only joined after Red Light, Green Light.
His number is the lowest number, excluding Hwang In-ho's.
He was the first player with a single-digit player number to be eliminated in the 37th Squid Game.
He shares the same digits with Player 020, who dies in the same game as him.
Mistakes[]
After the initial chaos, we can see Player 002 alive crouching behind another player.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 002 was portrayed by Lee Kwang-ho and an unknown extra.
