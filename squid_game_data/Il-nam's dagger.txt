Title: Il-nam's dagger
URL: https://squid-game.fandom.com/wiki/Il-nam%27s_dagger
--------------------------------------------------
Oh Il-nam's dagger was a weapon Il-nam gave to Hwang In-ho during the 28th Squid Game. He used it to kill all the finalists in their sleep during the Final Dinner in order to win the games and the Prize money to help his unborn child and his wife.
During the 37th Squid Game, this weapon was given to Seong Gi-hun with the same goal: to kill the other finalists. However, Gi-hun did not do this. During Sky Squid Game, Gi-hun kept the weapon and used it to cut through his opponents' jackets and threaten Lee Seung-won (Player 336).
He later used the dagger in a fight to kill Kim Gi-min (Player 203) by stabbing him in the heart. After Lee Myung-gi and Gi-hun ran over to the circular tower, Myung-gi ordered Gi-hun to put down the dagger or he would drop the baby. Gi-hun obeyed, and Myung-gi picked up the weapon, whereupon he tried to stab Gi-hun. However, Gi-hun was able to deflect the stabs with his jacket, and the two fell from the circular tower, the dagger most likely falling to the ground. Afterwards, the dagger was either taken by Hwang In-ho or exploded along with the facility on the island.
