Club HDH is a nightclub in Seoul within the vicinity of Yeouido. It appears in "Halloween Party", when Seong Gi-hun goes there for a meeting with the Front Man.

History[]
The club advertises itself as being geared towards hip-hop and contains a noise basement and a bar.

Trivia[]
Club HDH's exterior and interior shots were likely filmed at nb2, a real-life nightclub in Mapo-gu, Seoul.
Club HDH may be named after Squid Game's director, Hwang Dong-hyuk.
