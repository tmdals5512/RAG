Personality[]
Player 024 seemed a malleable and greedy individual. Although he voted X in the first vote, he ended up changing his mind during the second vote and he joined Team O. He shows no empathy for the X voters despite being a former member of Team X, as during the Special Game he murders Player 370 for money with no hesitation.

History[]
Player 024 was in massive debt and decided to join The Squid Games for a chance to win a huge cash prize. He called the number on the recruiting card and got into a mysterious car. He then woke up in a giant room with 454 other players (excluding 001). He then made his way to the center of the room and watched as the debts of many other players were revealed before him. Later on, he signed a contract and made his way up a flight of stairs to the first game.
During the first game, he was soon terrified as players were shot in front of him during the mass panic. He was able to control himself and stand still. He eventually completed the first game after listening to Gi-hun's commands. After returning to the dormitory, many players pleaded with the guards to let them go. However, the guards instead showed everyone the massive increase in prize money that accumulated after the first game. They then explained the voting process to all of the players. Player 024 did not care about the money and decided to vote X during the first Vote. Unfortunately for him and the other Xs, More players voted to continue the games.
Later during the Six-Legged Pentathlon, Player 024 partnered up with An Dong-uk, Player 378, Player 048, and Player 019. He and his team managed to complete all 5 minigames within the 5-minute time limit. After completing the Six-Legged Pentathlon, he and the other players held another vote. Player 024 changed his mind and decided to vote O instead of X, along with some other players.
Later on during Mingle, he managed to partner up with other players and passed all 5 rounds. During Round III, Player 024 tried to break a group of players and went in Room 08 with two other players. After Mingle, he and the other remaining players returned to the dormitory and had a third vote. Player 024 decided to vote O again, along with many other O players. This vote ends up in a tie, meaning they would vote again in the morning. Player 024 and the other players seem disappointed and move back to their beds. After the events of the bathroom fight, he and the other Os have a headcount and figure out that they have fewer players compared to the Xs. He and the other Os then decide to ambush the Xs during the night to put them ahead. When the lights finally go out, Player 024 and some other Os are seen sneaking over to Team X's side of the room. Eventually, Player 024 and the other Os charged into the bunks and started killing as many Xs as they could. He can be seen pinning Player 370 down and repeatedly stabbing her with a fork at the start of the Special Game. After the Special Game. He decided not to participate in Gi-hun's Rebellion and instead stayed in the lobby with all of the other players who also decided not to join. In the next round of voting, he voted O again and can be seen celebrating when they won the vote.
Later during Hide and Seek, Player 024 draws a blue ball from the gumball machine, which confirms his role as a hider. The manager later explains that he and his team would either have to survive the 30-minute timer or find an exit door to escape the maze. Later, when the guards allowed them to switch teams, Player 024 tried to switch with Player 080, but Player 080 declined the offer. He also tried to switch with Player 276. After the timer to switch teams ends. Player 024 enters the maze with his fellow Hiders and is given time to hide before the Seekers come after them. He then ran off into the maze and tried to find a hiding spot.

Fate[]
After Player 024 ran off into the maze, he was found and stabbed to death by an unknown seeker. His corpse can be seen when Seon-nyeo showed Im Jeong-dae how she found the exit door, and his blood was used to make an arrow.

Trivia[]
His ally is Player 048, which happens to be exactly double his number. Coincidentally, both are Team O's members and died in Hide-and-Seek.
Their roles are polar opposite however, as 024 is a Hider while 048 is a Seeker.
Not only that, but 024 had killed someone while 048 never managed to.
Funnily enough, his vote in the third round of voting left the O's with 48 votes.
He is the only recurring player in season three to have voted for both sides.
Additionally, his victim was also a recurring player who voted for both sides, with his and her S2 votes being flipped.
He was counted tenth in the Team O headcount.
He was the 175th to vote X in the first vote.
His actor claims that Player 442 was his killer based on info from Hwang Dong-hyuk himself.
He and his best friend were both last seen in the same place, being at a branched path in Hide and Seek. However, it was at different times.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 024 was portrayed by Jo Seong-jun and Min Da-eun.
When asked what Player 024's name was, Jo Seong-jun stated it to be Park Seo-woo.
