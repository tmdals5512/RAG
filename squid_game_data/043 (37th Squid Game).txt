History[]
Player 043 was in heavy debt, presumably higher than ₩78.8 million, so he joined the Squid Games in order to win money. Before Red Light green light, he was seen looking at Kang Mi-na and Kim Yeong-sam when they‘re debts we‘re call out. When they sign the papers, he told Jang Geum-ja and Park Young-sik to hurry up.
He survived Red Light, Green Light. Then Seong Gi-hun walked over to him and shaked him, and said he could be the next to die if he didn‘t leave now, but he still voted O.  
During the Six-Legged Pentathlon, he teamed up with four other players and managed to win the game. He supports Im Jeong-dae by saying 78 Million Won was not enough. He voted O again.    

Hold on, what? That's not even 100 million. Man, that's not even 80.
Player 043 to the Manager about the sum being too small in "One More Game"
During Mingle he was shot by Soldiers for failing to enter a room in the first round. He was left alive after being shot, but he was taken by Organ traffickers and killed by them.  

Fate[]
In Mingle, he was shot in the first round for not finding a room in time. He survived the shot, and Kang No-eul walked away after looking at Number 005 and Number 007, his shooters. His organs were then removed by Kim Jung-ho.

Trivia[]
In the first vote, Gi-hun told him that he could be the next one to die. If he had said this during the second vote instead, he would be correct.
He is one of five players who came to the game with necklaces. The other four are Thanos, Se-mi, Nam-gyu and Player 400.
He has the same number as Strip "The King" Weathers, a major character in Cars.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 043 was portrayed by Choi Hee-do and an unknown extra.
