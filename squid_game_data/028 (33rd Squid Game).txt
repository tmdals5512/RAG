History
Player 028 was in heavy debt, so he decided to enter the Squid Games. He woke up in a room with 455 others players. He then signed his contract, took his photo and was then taken to the 1st game. He managed to pass Red Light, Green Light alongside 200 others Players. 
During the voting, it is unknown what he voted. Nevertheless, everyone was released. 
He then returned alongside 186 other Players, and then he managed to pass the 2nd game, Sugar Honeycombs. 
In the Special Game, the bed he was sleeping on collapsed and he fell to the floor, but he still survived. He also managed to survive Tug of War.
For the 4th game, he partnered up with Jung Min-tae and played a unknown game with him to try and win all of his marbles.

Fate
He lost to Jung Min-tae, causing him to be eliminated and shot in the head.

Trivia
In the third squid game in 1990, a player named Park Pil-Sam won the games with this number.
In both seasons Player 028 died in the 4th game. they are also possible have the same positions as both were 29-26th place.
Player 028 is the highest placing contestant in Season 1 to be portrayed by an unknown actor.
Mistakes
During Red Light, Green Light, another Player 028 is seen.
In the Dormitory after Tug of War, he was portrayed by Player 014's actor.
Ironically, his number, 028 is double of 014.
In the vote, the person to vote after Player 029 was Player 027 despite he clearly surviving Red Light, Green Light.
Behind the scenes
Player 028 was portrayed by Shim Sang-min, Park Tae-gang, Yeong-gyu, Han Dae-won and five unknown extras.
