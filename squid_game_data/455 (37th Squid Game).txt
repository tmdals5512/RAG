History[]
Before the Games[]
Player 455 began to accumulate many debts that he could not pay after that. Shortly before Halloween, a recruiter approached him and played Ddakji with him. The recruiter invited him to the Squid Games, where he could win more money, The Recruiter gave him a card.
On Halloween night, he calls the card number, a van arrived with some pink men with masks in front and behind there were more people, and without warning he was put to sleep. He woke up in a dormitory with 454 other people, was sedated and to a giant room on a remote island, where he signed his life consent and had his photo taken to participate in the games.

"001"[]
He woke up in a strange room with more people, then he saw the pink men again who took him in the van, He saw more people who were asking questions to the pink men to which they began to reveal debts of some people who were not allowed to pay those debts to which understood that there were more people like him who were not allowed to pay debts, then the men in pink showed a piggy bank that would be filled with money if the people were eliminated. The pink men told them that they had to sign a consent in case they wanted to participate to win the money that would accumulate. They gave him a paper that had some clauses, having so many debts, signed the consent. Then he went to take a photo and went up to the first game.
In the first game, "Red Light, Green Light" The goal of the game was for players to reach the other end of an enclosed field, where the doll was located, within five minutes. They could move when the doll shouted "green light," but if it shouted "red light," they had to remain still while the doll watched them.

Fate[]
Player 455 and the other players felt the sound of a bullet, it was a player who was dead, Another player saw the dead player and started screaming, then Player 455 saw what happened, moving in the attempt, getting shot.

Trivia[]
Player 455's 33rd Squid Game counterpart, Kim In-hyeok, also died in the first game during their own mass panic.
However, Player 455 from Season 2 was more distanced from the doors whereas In-hyeok was significantly more closer to the doors when he died.
Player 455 is the first male player to die in the 37th Squid Game also is the first background character to die.
Player 455 is the first unnamed player to die in 37th Squid Game.
He is the last player and male player to be chosen by The Recruiter to join the game, because Seong Gi-hun wasn't recruited by him.
He is the only 45X to not pass the first game.
Mistakes[]
Player 455's icon is still lit after Round 3 of Mingle, despite the fact he was clearly seen dying after the death of Lee Eun-ju.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 455 was portrayed by Moon Min-chul. As shown via behind the scenes footage, Moon Min-chul is shown to have been part of a stunt team along with actors for Player 371 and Player 408.
