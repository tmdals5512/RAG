History[]
In her first look, she is seen looking at the piggy bank, surprised, and later she has put her life on continuing the games, just to get the money.
In her second appearance in the series, she is seen following the line for signature, moments later she is seen quite fine behind Thanos and Nam-gyu. As she is seen being polite and fine, when Thanos and Nam-gyu starts confronting Myung-gi, she's still seen quite confident for them. After the sudden chaos, she is still seemed careless.
In the first game, which is Red Light, Green Light, she is seen reacting clueless beside her fellow. And moments later, she was following Gi-hun's advice in order to survive the first game. When the game is near to end, she was seen, being scared along with 259 and 1 other player, and she ended up surviving the first game.
After the first game, players were seem afraid of the Guards due to them speaking the rules with kind words, but reckless. When the prize money increases, Player 035 was quite shock. Making her vote to continue the games. As she votes earlier, she cheers for Hwang In-ho to vote O. And he ended up making O voters satisfied.
In Six-Legs Pentathlon, which is the second game, she is seen finding teammates, making her alliance half complete as the timer dips below. After the time ends, she is along with her teammates, which includes, 339, 176, 052, and 405 as her teammates in the second game. After the second game she successfully survived along with her teammates who will die in the third game, including 035.
In Mingle, she is seen entering the arena, afraid and seem uncomfortable from the upcoming and settled game, which is the third game. During the 5th episode, Mingle was completely settled for the next surviving participants after the 2nd game.
When the platform was active and spinning around, Player 035 was seem scared again, thinking that her fate will be in early rounds. When the platform stops, Player 035 is seen forming an alliances along with players that are visible, who's likely 111, and 435. In round 2, Player 035 is seen getting abandoned by participants behind Thanos' Crew, leaving Gyeong-su and lead on his fate. This lead to Player 035 failing to find spare partners and a room in time, leading to her fate.
