History[]
Player 019 was presumably in massive debt and decided to join the Squid Games for a chance to win a huge cash prize. He called the number on the recruiting card, and got into a mysterious car. He then woke up in a giant room with 454 other players. He then made his way to the center of the room and watched as many other players debts were revealed in front of him. Later on, he signed a contract and made his way up a flight of stairs to the first game. 
During the first game, Player 019 heard Gi-hun screaming about how dangerous the games were. Player 019 and many other players ignored his warnings, and continued the game without any worry. However, Player 019 was soon terrified as players were shot in front of him during the mass panic. Player 019 luckily was able to control himself and stand still. He eventually completed the first game after listening to Gi-hun's commands. After returning to the dormitory with the other surviving players, Player 019 begged the guards to let him and the other players go. However, after a massive increase in the prize pool was revealed, Player 019 changed his mind and decided to vote O during the first vote. Him and the other Os then cheered after they won the vote. 
Later during Six-Legged Pentathlon, Player 019 partnered up with Player 024, Player 048, An Dong-uk (Player 247), and Player 378. Him and his team then managed to complete all 5 minigames within the 5 minute time limit . After successfully completing Six-Legged Pentathlon, him and the other players held another vote. Player 019 voted O again along with other greedy players. 
Later on during Mingle, Player 019 managed to partner up with other players and pass the first round.

Fate[]
Player 019 couldn't find a room or find enough players in the second round so he was eliminated.
