History[]
Player 047 was in heavy debt and chose to enter the Squid Games to win money. After waking up in a large room with 455 other people, he would watch Jang Deok-su harass and attack Sae-byeok, after Sae-byeok is dropped, Player 047 would point at them and begin walking over to them, likely to try and assist Sae-byeok. But Player 234 would hold his arm to try and keep him from getting in the fight, after that, he would sign a contract and took his photo.

Fate[]
After the first three players were eliminated in Red Light, Green Light, Player 047 panicked and ran towards the doors, which caused him to be shot. The corpse was seen, and his icon faded out. Although he later appears after the first game and disappears after the Special Game.

Trivia[]
He has the same number as a relevant character in season 2.
He is seen pointing at Deok-su and Sae-byeok and is then seen walking closer to them with a angry expression, he is however held back by another Player, Player 234.
