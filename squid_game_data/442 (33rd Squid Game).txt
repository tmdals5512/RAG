Player 443







Player 442 was in heavy debt and chose to enter the Squid Games to win money. After waking up in a large room with 455 other people, he signed a contract and took his photo.

Fate[]
In the first game, he witnessed a mass panic that made him die.

Mistakes[]
Another Player 442 is seen in the lobby before Red Light Green Light.
Another Player 442 is seen in Sugar Honeycombs.
Another Player 442 is seen during the Fly Me to the Moon scene in Red Light, Green Light.
Player 442’s icon shows as a woman despite being a man.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 442 is portrayed by Yi Kyung-mi, Lee Jin and two unknown extras.
On a viral Youtube video comparing his Sugar Honeycombs actor to a popular Korean streamer, Kane, a man named Lee Se-hoon claimed that he had portrayed Player 442 in Sugar Honeycombs. However, he later revealed that he had lied.
