History[]
Player 192 was in heavy debt and decided to join the Squid Games. When she woke up in a large room with 455 other players, she signed a contract form, took a photo and proceeded to the first game alongside with others. She would be able to survive the panic, and make it past the finish line with barely any time left. She would return to the games after the voting and would choose a unknown shape.

Fate[]
Player 192's place of death is unknown, she appears as a corpse in Sugar Honeycombs and her icon fades in Sugar Honeycombs also. But she doesn't have any known appearances in Red Light, Green Light, or the Voting. So she was either killed for getting detected by Young-hee, and being shot by a sniper, or because she cracked her dalgona and was shot for doing so.
