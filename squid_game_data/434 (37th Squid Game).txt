History[]
Player 434 was in massive debt before 2024. In September he got recruited by The Recruiter. He woke up signed he contract and played the Squid Games. He manages to make it through the first game, Red Light, Green Light, afterwards, he votes O to continue the games.
Later, during Six-Legged Pentathlon, he partnered up with 4 other unknown players and managed to complete all 5 minigames within the 5 minute timer. After successfully completing Six-Legged Pentathlon, him and the other players held another vote, he votes to continue the games again. 

Fate[]
In Mingle, he was shot by a soldier for not entering a room in the first round.

Trivia[]
The O button has been pressed 434 times. Which ironically is his number.
Player 434 was also portrayed by Meng Oan-ho who played Player 234.
Both 234 and 434 ends with the number 34.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 434 was portrayed by Kim Tae-gong, Meng Oan-ho, Park Chun-yeong and an unknown extra.
