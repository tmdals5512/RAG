Seong Ga-yeong (성가영 Seong Ga-yeong?) is the daughter and only child of Seong Gi-hun and Kang Eun-ji. She was at the center of the dispute between her parents. Her mother took her away from her father after he went into big debts to Loan sharks company.

History[]
Ga-yeong was born on June 8, 2011, to Seong Gi-hun and Kang Eun-ji their only child. Her mother who had collapsed in labour before taking herself to the hospital where Ga-yeong was born. Albeit without the presence of her deadbeat spouse, Seong Gi-hun, who was holding up in Dragon Motors during a violent strike around that time.  
By 2017 (three years before Gi-hun entered the 33rd Squid Game), her parents settled for divorce and she was taken under the care of her mother and could visit her biological father as Gi-hun was not in a good position to support and look after her. 
After an unknown amount of time, her mother then remarried to Ga-yeong's stepfather who provides for them and is more stable then Gi-hun she has a stepbrother living with her, they are now all residing in USA Los Angeles.  
On October 30, 2024, Gi-hun decided to call Ga-yeong to hear her out one last time before returning to the Squid Games. Ga-yeong answered the call and asked who it was, speaking in English, but Gi-hun couldn't say a word, then Ga-yeong thought it was her father and asked if it was him, speaking in Korean, Eun-ji told Ga-yeong it was time for dinner and Ga-yeong hung up the phone thinking it was a prank call.

Is that box from my dad? Did he send you here so he could see how I've been doing? Know what you could tell him? That I stopped waiting to hear from him a long time ago. I'll pretend my dad doesn't exist, and he can forget about me too.
Seong Ga-yeong to Hwang In-ho in "Humans Are..."
Six months later, a man claiming to be an acquaintance of Gi-hun comes in front of her house carrying a box. Ga-yeong initially denies the box, as she didn't want to be in any contact with Gi-hun. The man reveals that Gi-hun has died and the box is carrying his belongings. She takes the box and opens it to see a bloody tracksuit with the numbers "456" and a small letter containing a credit card with his name, presumably containing the remainder of the money her father had won from the Squid Games. She runs out only to see the black car driving away.

Personality[]
These were your father's belongings. Mr. Seong Gi-hun has passed away. Before he died, he left these things for you, Ga-yeong.
Hwang In-ho to Seong Ga-yeong as he hands her father'stracksuit and credit card to her in "Humans Are..."Ga-yeong is empathetic and caring, much like her father. She worries about her biological father's well-being, concerned about his tendencies to get into fights, gambling addiction, his smoking habits. She seems to care a lot about her family, and her family seemingly tries to not make her aware of any strife amongst them. She makes it her effort to be grateful and humble, especially when her birthday doesn't go as planned she is still happy to spend time with her father showing that despite Gi-hun's issues they had a close relationship. However by the 37th Squid Game, it's noticeable that Ga-yeong has since developed a grudge against Gi-hun as he has not contacted or visited her implying that she was deeply hurt when Gi-hun cancelled his trip to see her. When In-ho comes to deliver the box she initially denies it because of his lack of contact. When In-ho tells her that Gi-hun has passed away she was noticeably saddened to hear the news showing she still cared about him giving her some closure as to her father's fate and was surprised with the bloodied tracksuit and credit card containing Gi-hun's leftoever ₩45,600,000,000.

Trivia[]
Ga-yeong and Hwang Jun-ho are the only known characters to possess the golden Squid Game credit card without having participated in the games.
Ga-yeong's grandmother Oh Mal-soon mentioned that Ga-yeong loves fried chicken. Her father Seong Gi-hun once opened a chicken restaurant named after her which possibly explains why she loves chicken, as it reminds her of happier times when her parents were together.
Not long after Season 3 released, fake trailers were made for the American spin-off where Ga-yeong would be the 456th player for it, inheriting her dead father's player number.
This is heavily unlikely as it is doubtful that the games would let a teenager play. However, as the games let in a newborn baby (against their will) and let people presumably 19 or slightly older to be managers (thus giving the implication that they have been working in the games since they were in their mid to late teens), this can be debated. Another theme these fake trailers presumably have is where Ga-yeong tries to find out what happened to her father; this is also heavily doubtful, as she says that she forgot about him and has moved on, assuming she stays true to her word.
However, given that she loved her father very much, and especially after seeing how much money her father had given her, it is likely that she will not keep her word. Despite her resentment, she is still likely to love her father, and therefore she will be curious about his fate. Even if she does not become a player, it is possible that she may somehow disguise herself as a security guard.
If she returns to the American spin-off, even though her father is dead, it is possible that he will return to interact with her. However, in the fake trailers, Gi-hun is alive and trying to get her out of the game. Additionally, there are rumors online from fans that Gi-hun may be alive if the debris shielded him from the explosion. But this is unlikely, because it is highly likely that he would have been crushed by the debris, and since the director has already confirmed in an interview that Gi-hun is truly dead, it is unlikely that he will ever decide to "resurrect" him. Therefore, if an American spin-off or another future project involves their relationship, it is more likely that Gi-hun will return through her dreams or hallucinations.
And even if the director ever "revived" Gi-hun, he wouldn't be able to visit his daughter in America because he wouldn't have any money, as he was eliminated in the 37th games and the remaining winnings from the 33rd games were transferred to his daughter.
