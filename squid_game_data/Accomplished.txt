Trivia[]
It is a remix and reprisal of Way Back then (and by extension Way Forward), with the title referring to that fact that Gi-hun’s goal of ending the games had been accomplished.
The song is meant to be a sadder version of Way Back then, reflecting on Gi-hun’s death and the games coming to a stop.
