Player 437







Player 436 was in tremendous debt, so he decided to enter the Squid Game. He woke up in a room with 455 other Contestants, After watching the fight between Player 067 and 101 and listening to the Guard's speech, he signed his contract, took his photo, and was then taken to the 1st game. He managed to survived the initial chaos.

Fate[]
After Player 436 witnessed the death of 3 players, he panicked and ran to the doors, resulting in his death.

Trivia[]
A player named Lee Young-hoo won the 26th Squid Games in 2013 with this number.
If you divide this player's number with 2, it will be 218, which is the number of one of the main characters, Cho Sang-woo.
