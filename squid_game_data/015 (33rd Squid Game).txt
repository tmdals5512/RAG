History[]
Player 015 was in heavy debt and chose to enter a high-risk competition to win money. She was knocked unconscious with a gas and taken to an island, where she woke up in a large room with 455 other people. She signed a contract and got her photo taken. 
After 3 people died, it caused chaos. She managed to survive the initial chaos of Red Light and survived RLGL. After most of the contestants had been wiped out, Afterwards, like everyone else, she was shaken by the deaths of the contestants.
When the prize money was finally revealed, a vote was held on if the game should continue or discontinue. She voted to end the games like the majority and was sent back. However, those who were willing to return could, and she returned along with 186 other players. And went to the second game Sugar Honeycombs, presumably she picked star. After successfully craving her cookie out, she would make her way back to the Dormitory.

Fate[]
After getting her food and watching Player 271 die by Jang Deok-su, Player 015 would go to bed in fear. She would be attacked by an unknown player later during the Special Game and get stabbed to death off-screen, resulting in her elimination in The Special Game as her Icon blanks off and her corpse is seen
