Circle of Trust is the sixth game of the second season of Squid Game: The Challenge.
11 players participated in this game. 5 players were eliminated and 6 players progressed to the next round. At this point in the competition, 450 players in total were eliminated.

Game information[]
Players are seated at desks in a circle with a podium in the middle with a gift box, and then blindfolded. When tapped on the shoulder, the player secretly removes their blindfold, picks up the gift box, and places it on the desk of a player whom they wish to eliminate. Afterwards, the player whose shoulder was tapped returns to their desk and replaces their blindfold. All players then remove their blindfolds and the player who received the box must guess who placed the box on their desk. If they guess correctly, the gifter is eliminated; if they guess incorrectly, they are eliminated instead. This game is an altered version of Heads Up, Seven Up.
