History[]
Player 033 was in tremendous debt when she was offered the opportunity to join the Squid Games. After waking up in a dormitory with 455 other players, she signed a consent form, had her photograph taken, and was transported to the first game, Red Light, Green Light.
During the chaos of the game, Player 033 managed to cross the finish line and survive the round. Following the result of the prize money, she voted X in favor of ending the games. Although the games were temporarily halted, she would later return and participate in the second game, Sugar Honeycombs, and choose a unknown shape.

Fate[]
Player 033’s fate remains unknown. She was seen panicking during the initial phase of Red Light, Green Light, after which her icon disappears. However, she reappears later following the game’s conclusion and is confirmed to participate up to Sugar Honeycombs. She is not shown during the dormitory segment before the Special Game, leaving her status uncertain and suggesting she may have died in the first game or second game, or she is supervised.

Behind the scenes[]
Player 033 was portrayed by Min Seo-yeon, Lee Young-hee in her icon and 2 unknown extras.

Trivia[]
A player named Bae Dong-il won the 25th Squid Games in 2012 with this number.
