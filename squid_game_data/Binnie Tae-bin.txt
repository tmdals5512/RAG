Han Tae-bin (한태빈 Han Tae-bin?), also known as Player 355, is a man from South Korea who competed in the Squid Games.

In-game description[]
Han 'Binnie' Tae-bin dazzles in designer threads, trying to live a champagne lifestyle on a soju budget. At Seoul's most exclusive clubs and glamorous parties, Binnie spends big to maintain a glossy facade.
But when his credit runs dry and the loan sharks come calling, Binnie turns to the Squid Game to keep his carefully crafted image - and possibly his kneecaps - from getting shattered.
