Personality[]
You, are you sure he went left first?
Player 021 asking Player 453 if she is sure that Player 062 chose the left tile first during Glass Stepping Stones in VIPS
Player 021 seems to be a genuine person but is easily shaken up in stressful conditions. This is very apparent when he witnesses the deaths of players in Glass Stepping Stones, as he shows the most shocked reactions. Player 021 also is shown to be reliant and trustworthy on others such as Player 453 when he was unable to remember the correct glass order in the same game.

History[]
Player 021 was in heavy debt and chose to enter the Squid Games to win money. After waking up in a large room with 455 other people, he signed a contract, and got his photo. He managed to survive the first game, Red Light Green Light, after most of the contestants had been wiped out. Afterwards, like everyone else, he was shaken by the deaths of the contestants, when the prize money was finally revealed. A vote was held if the game should continue, but the majority, including 021, voted no. However, those who were willing to return could, and he returned, along with most of the other players.
He managed to survive the second game, Sugar Honeycombs, and the third game, Tug of War, joining a team including players like Player 062, and beat his partner, Player 276, in the fourth game Marbles, and survived until the fifth game, Glass Stepping Stones. For the game, he chose the number 4, which meant he would be the fourth to go. 

Fate[]
When it was his turn, he could not remember the pattern that Player 062 took, and so he asked the person behind him, Player 453, for help, but she also could not remember either. The 2 players behind her, Player 244 and Player 151 argued whether it was left or right. He decided to go left, and fell off the bridge.

Trivia[]
He was the 4th person to be eliminated in the 5th game.
He got 13th place in the 33rd Squid Game. Since this is out of 456 players this means he made it to the top 3 percent of players.
His scream when falling to his death is identical to Player 360's and similar to Jung Min-tae's scream.
He had a similar death to Player 377 from Season 2/3. They were both eliminated in the fifth game because they followed another player's instructions. They placed only 4 spaces apart in the game with Player 377 placing 17th.
He is likely one of the youngest players to reach the Glass Stepping Stones game [maybe around 30 years of age], with only Kang Sae-byeok and possibly Player 062 being younger than him.
Mistakes[]
Another Player 021 is seen at the Voting where Player 045 was hugging him or holding his hand.
The other one is seen at the start of Red Light, Green Light.
Another is shown during Player 271’s death.
Player 021 was seen grabbing the Number 03 vest and Player 062 was grabbing the Number 04 vest before Glass Bridge. During the game their vests switched.
He is seen upset that the games got terminated despite voting X.
His icon on the portrait grid and the icon in the VIP room have a little difference.
Behind the scenes[]
There is a continuity error where in Red Light, Green Light, another player can be seen wearing the 021 tag. And another Player 021 was portrayed by Jo Soo-yeon, Another known actor for Player 021 is Kang Yeon-hong who played Player 021 scanned. The actual Player 021 was portrayed by Moon Byung-Joo.
