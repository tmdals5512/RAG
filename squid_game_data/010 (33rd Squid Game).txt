History
Player 010 was in tremendous debt, so he decided to enter the Squid Games. After waking up in a large room with 455 other players, he signed a contract, took his photo, and entered the first game's arena.

Fate
After the first three deaths, a panic broke out, and Player 010 was one of the many players to run to the doors, resulting in him getting shot by one of the snipers. 

Trivia
It's possible his name was intended to be Kim Young-chul (김영철 Gim Yeong-cheol?) due to that being the name of the 10th winner on the winners list.
