History[]
Player 010 was in tremendous debt, so he decided to enter the Squid Games. He woke up in a room with 455 other players. He then signed his contract, got his photo taken, and then was promptly taken to the first game of Red Light, Green Light. He managed to survive the first game. A vote was later held on whether the games should continue or not, and the majority voted to end them. However, Player 010 and the majority of the players returned to the games after being given the opportunity. He then proceeded to the second game.

Fate[]
During Sugar Honeycombs, he broke his dalgona from stress by the other players getting shot, leading to him being shot by a soldier and eliminated.

Trivia[]
He and his Season 2 counterpart both made it as far as the second game and died in it.
This also applies to both Player 148s from both seasons.
It's possible his name was intended to be Kim Young-chul (김영철 Gim Yeong-cheol?) due to that being the name of the 10th winner on the winners list.
