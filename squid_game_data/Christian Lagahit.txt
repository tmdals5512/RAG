Christian Lagahit (크리스찬 라가히트 Keuriseuchan Lagahiteu?) is the Filipino actor who portrayed Player 276 in Season 1 of the Netflix TV series Squid Game. He originally auditioned for the role of Ali Abdul, but that role went to Anupam Tripathi, as Director Hwang Dong-hyuk feared that his Korean was not good enough.
