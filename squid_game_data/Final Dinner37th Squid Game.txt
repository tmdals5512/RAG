There were no eliminations.

Trivia[]
This is the only game/interlude game where no player was eliminated.
However, Im Jeong-dae almost got stabbed and eliminated by Seong Gi-hun.
Although 9 players attended this, only 1 actually participated.
The purpose of the game was a test made by the Front Man to see if Gi-hun would saw himself as if he had every right to kill the finalists after everything he was put through and also for the sake of the baby. Gi-hun ultimately managed to pass the test by refusing to did what the Front Man did in the past, which causing him to doubt about his belief that humanity is rotten and depraved to the core.
