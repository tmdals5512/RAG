History[]
Player 444 was in massive debt that couldn't be paid off, and decided to enter the Squid Games. After waking up in a large room with 455 other players, he signed a contract and took his photo.
He managed to survive the first game, Red Light, Green Light, after most of the contestants had been wiped out. A vote was then held to end the games. Despite the fact that he picked O, he was initially sent home since the slight majority voted to terminate the games. He and 186 other players decided to come back to win the prize money. In Sugar Honeycombs, he chose the circle and managed to cut it out.

Fate[]
During the Special Game, he was killed by an unknown player by being stabbed repeatedly in the neck region.

Trivia[]
Player 444 is the last person to get food before the Special Game.
He shares his numbers with one of the supporting characters of Season 2.
Both him and his Season 2 counterpart were the first players with repeating digit numbers to die in their respective Squid Games.
Mistakes[]
The other male Player 444 is seen leaving after having his photo taken.
The female Player 444 is also seen leaving after having her photo taken.
In the original version, His elimination was mistakenly announced as Player 198.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 444 was portrayed by Lee Sang-gyu and Kim Jeong-hee.
