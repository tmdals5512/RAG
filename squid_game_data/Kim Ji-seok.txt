Kim Ji-seok (김지석 Gim Ji-seog?) is a motorcyclist who was pulled over by Hwang Jun-ho. He appears in "Bread and Lottery" as a minor character of Season 2.

History[]
On June 21, 2024, he was riding a motorcycle with his girlfriend when he was stopped by Hwang Jun-ho and his partner for not wearing a helmet. He accused them of entrapment, describing him and his girlfriend as 'broke citizens'. He was forced to hand his license over and became exasperated when his girlfriend got off and flirted and took selfies with Jun-ho.
After Jun-ho presented him with a ₩20,000 fine, he drove off without his girlfriend. When she asked him where he was going, he told her to ask Jun-ho for a ride, calling him a "stupid pig", leaving her chasing after him as he drove away without her. It is unknown where he went afterwards.

Trivia[]
According to his traffic ticket, Kim Ji-seok was pulled over at 1:30pm on Jueundae-ro in Jongno District, Seoul. He lives at 132-637 Nonron-ro in the Gangnam District of Seoul, and his license plate registration is "서울 한서 가 9912".
Kim Ji-seok shares the same actor name trait with Na Jo-eun, Park Jae-cheol, and Choi Woong-seok.
