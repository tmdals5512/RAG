Catch is the second episode in Season 2 of the Netflix series competition TV series Squid Game: The Challenge and the 12th episode of the series overall.

Synopsis[]
The players face a moral dilemma about their weakest links. Before dinner, they're given a tasty snack, but in this game, there's always a catch.

Summary[]
The Six Legged Pentathlon concludes and the results show that 114 players out of the starting 226 players had been eliminated and that 112 players remained. the first vote was hosted where Players were to vote X if they wanted to keep the slowest players per minigame in the game or press o if they wanted to eliminate them.
