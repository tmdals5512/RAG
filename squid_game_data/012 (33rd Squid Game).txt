History[]
Player 012 was in heavy debt and chose to enter the Squid Games to win money. After waking up in a large room with 455 other players, he signed a contract and took his photo. He managed to survive the first game, Red Light, Green Light, after most of the contestants had been wiped out. He was likely upset from how the organization kills players for being eliminated. While he voted to stay in the games, he was initially sent home since a slight majority voted to terminate the games. So after the games were resumed, he and 186 other players decided to come back to win the prize money. After coming back into the games, Player 012 decided to befriend Player 190. In Sugar Honeycombs, he decided to choose the triangle Dalgona and he successfully carved out his Dalgona. In the Special Game, he witnessed the death of Player 271, and discussed his death to Player 205. After this, he went to bed and a fight broke out in the night and unfortunately for Player 012, he was caught right in the middle of it.

Fate[]
Player 012's fate would be unknown as he's icon blanks off after the Special Game ends but he's seen unharmed after it.

Trivia[]
His file shows that something happens to him on October 12, 2010.
It's possible his name was intended to be Oh Jeong-nam (오정남 O Jeong-nam?) due to that being the name of the 12th winner on the winners list.
