History[]
Player 027 joined the Squid Games to help pay off her heavy debts. After waking up in a large room with 454 other players and having the rules of the game explained to her, she signed a contract, took her photo, and proceeded to the arena of the first game called Red Light, Green Light, which she managed to survive and get through. Back in the dormitory, she was seen begging for her life, and she voted X in the first voting session. However, the games were forced to continue because of Hwang In-ho's deciding vote of O.
In the Six-Legged Pentathlon, she teamed up with four men, players 265, 291, 342, and 433. She played Jegi for her team, as seen by their seating arrangement. She was seen cheering for Cho Hyun-ju's team after they managed to pass Jegi. When it was time for Player 027's team to complete the game, she and her teammates managed to complete all five minigames within the time limit. In the second voting session, she changed her vote to O.

Fate[]
During Mingle, Player 027 managed to pass the first round, as she is seen on the carousel before the second round began. She couldn't find a room and was shot to death. She could also be seen before the fourth round, but this is an error

Trivia[]
She is the first to put O with more votes than X in the second vote, leaving it 8 O to 7 X.
