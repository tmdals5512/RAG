Ace Tran, also known as Player 077, is a gambler from the United States who competed in the Squid Games.

In-game description[]
For Ace Tran, every roll of the dice is just another chance to win big. From Hanoi to Vegas, there aren't many card tables that Ace hasn't left a few chips behind. There's probably been more losses than wins in Ace's life but he isn't keeping score.
Unfortunately, some people are and Ace owes them a lot of money, And these aren't the kind of people who will wait for Ace to hit a hot streak.
So he's all-in and hoping his luck is about to turn around in the Squid Game.
