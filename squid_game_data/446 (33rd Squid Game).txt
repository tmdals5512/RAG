Player 447







Like any other Player, Player 446 was in heavy debt and chose to enter the Squid Games to win money. After waking up in a large room with 455 other players, he signed a contract and took his photo. He managed to survive the first game, Red Light, Green Light, after more than half of the contestants were eliminated. 
Afterwards, like everyone else, he was shaken by the deaths of the other contestants, when the prize money was finally revealed. A vote was held if the game would continue, and while he voted to stay, the majority voted no. However, those who were willing to return could, and he returned, along with most of the other players.

Fate[]
In the second game, Sugar Honeycombs, he failed cutting his dalgona, causing him to be shot by one of the soldiers in the right shoulder and killed.

Mistakes[]
Player 446 is the 9th player to vote and the screen showed that he voted X however he is seen onscreen later on voting O.
Another Player 446, who also portrays Player 014, is seen running towards the doors in Red Light, Green Light.
A female Player 446 could be seen in Sugar Honeycombs.
Player 446's icon is the same as Player 194's faulty icon.
