Chae Kyoung-sun (채경순 Chae kyoung-sun?) is an award winning production designer and art director best known for her work on Netflix’s hit series Squid Game. She was a pivotal role in the visuals of the show.

Trivia[]
She described that for Hide and Seek, she let her son write and draw on the walls in the starting area.
