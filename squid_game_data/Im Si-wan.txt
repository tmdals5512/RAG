Im Si-wan  (임시완 Im Si-wan) born Im Woong-jae (임웅재 Im Ung-jae) on December 1, 1988 is a singer and actor who played Lee Myung-gi/Player 333 in Season 2 and Season 3 of the Netflix TV series Squid Game.[1] He is a member of the South Korean boy band ZE:A and its sub-group ZE:A Five. As an actor, he is best known for starring in the films The Attorney (2013), and the cable series Misaeng: Incomplete Life (2014), which were both successful at the box office and in the ratings. In addition, he was selected by the Korean Film Council as one of the 200 Korean actors that best represent the present and future of Korea's movie scene.
