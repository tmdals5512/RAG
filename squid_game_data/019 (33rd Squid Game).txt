History[]
Player 019 was in heavy debt and chose to enter a high-risk competition to win money. After waking up in a large room with 455 other people, he signed a contract, and got his photo taken. He went to the 1st game which was Red Light, Green Light. He managed to not move and/or get pushed during the initial panic and made it across the finish line before the timer ran out. 
After Red Light, Green Light, the voting was held. It is unknown what he voted but the games were terminated nonetheless and he briefly returned home. He was one of the 187 who decided to return to the games due to how hellish life outside was. 019 made it through Dalgona, although his shape is unknown, and he survived the Special Game that night. 

Fate[]
During Tug of War, Player 019 was part of Team Five, which consisted entirely of men. His team faced off against Team 4. They were the second teams to compete in the game. Initially, Team 4, though weaker in appearance, managed to gain an advantage using the strategic advice from Oh Il-nam. Team 5 struggled as Team 4 nearly won the match. However, Player 194 was able to rally Team Five, giving them enough strength to begin overpowering Team 4. Just as Team 5 was on the verge of pulling Team 4 off the platform, Cho Sang-woo's quick thinking led to a last-minute plan of stepping forward. This move caused Player 194 and the rest of Team 5 including Player 019 to fall and die.
