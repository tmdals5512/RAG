History[]
Player 025 was in heavy debt and chose to enter the Squid Games to win money. After waking up in a large room with 455 other players, he signed a contract, took his photo, and was taken to the arena of the first game, known as Red Light, Green Light. He makes little progress offscreen before Player 324 dies, thats when he started to feel unease of hearing a real gunshot.

Fate[]
Player 025 was terrified after seeing three people die, he attempted to escape through the doors leading to his death, being shot by a sniper.

Trivia[]
Player 025 shares his Red Light, Green Light actress with Player 449.
Mistakes[]
Player 025 has a total of five actors/actresses which are:
A female who portrayed Player 449 and Player 105 during Red Light, Green Light.
An older male extra in the first dormitory scene.
A male extra waiting to take his photo.
Another male extra during the initial chaos.
And finally a female extra on his Icon.
His icon erroneously appears in "Stick to the Team".
His icon also faded here too.
