History[]

Chronology





Previous player
Next player




Player 450
Player 452







Player 451 was in high debt. He was approached by a man and he offered to play Ddakji with him, whoever wins gets money. He possibly failed many times at the game but eventually won. He then saw an open van at the same location he was at. He went in the van and saw other people, unconscious. Due to the sleeping gas, he was knocked unconscious, too. While being unconscious, he was transported to The Island where the annual 33rd Squid Game and the Squid Games would be hosted. He was changed in his outfit with his number "451". He then woke up in a room with 455 other players. Visibly confused, he saw the players complain and saw their debts revealed, along with a video of them getting slapped. He listened to the guards and the guards clearing the players doubts. He then signed his contract and went up the Stairs. He then listened to the instructions to take his picture, and he took his picture, which would be his icon. He was then taken to the 1st game. 
When Player 451 saw the arena, he was confused as to what the game is. He realized it was Red Light, Green Light, and he proceeded on the first green light. He saw Player 324 getting shot but he didn't realize it yet, on the next green light Player 250 ran up to Player 324 and told him to "stop with the act", but when Player 324 spat blood out Player 250 realized he got shot and he ran then got shot too. Player 306 got his blood splattered on her face, and she screamed as she realized it was blood, eliminating her. This caused a mass panic with 255 players dying. However, Player 451 survived the mass panic and finished the game. It is unknown if he died here, but his icon faded. He voted X in the first round of voting, where the X outnumbered the O. However, he was taken again to the island. He then proceeded to the next game, Sugar Honeycombs. 
We can see him waiting in line for his dalgona, the umbrella, but even though the dalgona was hard to carve out, he still survived the game. When he got his food, he watched in fear as Jang Deok-su killed Player 271 by kicking him repeatedly. He then finished his food and went to sleep. 
In the Special Game, he can be seen trying to push over a bunkbed with someone tugging on his back, he panicked after and tried to hide, but he failed and was killed by an unknown player, though this is not confirmed yet. 

Fate[]
Its unknown what is Player 451's true fate as his corpse is seen after the Special Game ends even though his icon doesn't turn off making his fate subjective.
