John, also known as VIP John or VIP 3, is a VIP that attended the 37th Squid Game hosted by Hwang In-ho. John is the only VIP of the 37th Squid Game who didn't participate as a Soldier in Hide and Seek, because his chopper arrived late, though he could've also fell asleep after getting drunk. John has an interesting relationship with VIP Jennifer, since John finds Jennifer attractive, but he prefers it when she doesn't say anything (since Jennifer usually says negative things about John).

Personality[]
VIP John is usually more relaxed and less serious than the other VIPs, which is evident from the fact that he often gets drunk, which caused him to choose the wrong number when betting on a player. Despite showing clear signs of a villain, John has proven to be a better person than the other VIPs, since despite having bet on Kim Jun-hee (Player 222); who had an injured ankle and a newborn baby, John decides to support Jun-hee and her baby, even when the odds are against them. In Jump Rope, when Seong Gi-hun (Player 456 and winner of the 33rd Squid Game) carries the baby across the bridge, the VIPs think that he's crazy, but John decides to support Gi-hun since he helps Jun-hee.

History[]
Before the fifth game begins, the VIP's turn their attention to Kim Jun-hee (Player 222), since she seems to be at a huge disadvantage and they think that no one in their right mind would bet on Jun-hee. But it turns out that VIP John bet on Jun-hee (since he got drunk and pressed the wrong number), but VIP John refuses to give up and thinks that Jun-hee can still do it, because a mother does anything for her children.
When the VIPs discover that the next game is Jump Rope, VIP John gets frustrated since Jun-hee has a hurt ankle, so it seems like she won't make it, but then the others VIPs suggest he make that the baby play with her mom. VIP John questions how that will help him, but after the others remind him that a mother will do anything for her children and declare that it will be fun, VIP John agrees (thinking he's done for anyway). When the game starts, Seong Gi-hun (Player 456 and winner of the 33rd Squid Game) helps Jun-hee and her baby cross the bridge and VIP John cheers on Player 222, VIP Jennifer tells him that she thought that he gave up, but VIP John tells her that it's not over yet.  
Gi-hun carries the baby and tells Jun-hee that he will take the baby across and come back for her. While Gi-hun prepares to cross, the VIPs think that he's crazy for crossing with the baby and returning to the game after winning in 2020, but VIP John supports Gi-hun for helping Jun-hee and shouts to Player 456 that he supports him. Gi-hun manages to cross the bridge with the baby, makes VIP John happy and he tells the other VIPs that he knew that 456 would make it, the other VIPs congratulate him because the baby managed to cross and tell him that he already won the half of his bet.
VIP John was especially shocked when Lee Myung-gi (Player 333) revealed that he was the baby's father during Sky Squid Game.

Trivia[]
He's the only VIP in the 37th Squid Game that was never shown unmasked.
He’s the only known VIP to win a bet.
