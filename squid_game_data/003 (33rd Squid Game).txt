History[]
Park Pil-sam, at some point in his life, gathered a lot of debt, leaving him desperate for money. He eventually met a man in a suit who offered to play Ddakji with him. He managed to beat him at the game, and the man then gave him a card with a phone number to join the Squid Games, along with 100,000 won. Still desperate with no hope, He made the call and gave the game his name and date of birth. He then went to an address given to him. Suddenly, a van showed up; He got in through a password, "Red Light, Green Light." The driver was shown in a pink jumpsuit, but he had a gas mask. Suddenly, sleeping gas was released, making him fall asleep, and then the van would drive off.  
He then woke up to classical music in a jumpsuit on a bunk bed, confused and scared in a creepy warehouse called the "dormitory." Then, the same mysterious drivers, now known as "Guards," came in, telling him the instructions and that they were in a competition. Scared and desperate, the guards said she could win a "handsome cash prize." He was still scared; some players were asking questions. and the Guards answered, A guard with a square symbol on their mask tells them that the players are all living on the edge with debts that they simply cannot pay, so it made him earn the guards' trust. He then talked with his best friend, Na Jo-eun, signed a contract, and proceed to walk into a giant room with multiple staircases, with classical music following the instructions. He then took his picture, following the guards into the first game: Red Light, Green Light.
Park Pil-sam could be seen confused and doubtful about the giant doll, and an announcer explained the game rules, and so, the game begun. He and many other players ran, though a player, Player 324, ran too fast and couldn't stop in time, so his elimination was announced, followed by a loud bang and him dropping to the ground. His best friend, Player 250, who was just right behind 324, went over and checked on 324, thinking 324 was playing a trick on him, but 324 coughed up blood, which made 250 scared and try to run away in panic, before getting shot in the back, with his blood landing on Player 306's face, which made her scream and get shot in the head.
Witnessing the death of 3 players, A lot of players tried to run to the door but were shot and eliminated from the game. Although Pil-sam, along with many other players, managed to survive the initial chaos, and so the game continued, Pil-sam stood still and could be seen running with his best friend, Jo-eun, during some red lights and during 220's death. Pil-sam would then be able to pass the finish line, but other players weren't fast enough and were killed for not making it to the finish line in time.
Upon returning to the dormitory, Park Pil-sam was scared and shaken up from witnessing so many deaths, until the guards barged into the room, which made Pil-sam back away in fear. Some players started to protest about the game until a guard grabbed his gun and sent out a warning shot in the air to stop the ruckus, which made Pil-sam flop down to the floor, startled. The same guard then reminded the players about the first two clauses of the contract they signed, though another player, Player 218 (Cho Sang-woo), argued back about the third clause, which was that the players could vote whether to leave or stay in the game, and the guards accepted the request to begin a vote, however, the guards first showed the prize money accumulated in the first game, which made lots of players start questioning their choices, and so, the vote begun.
During the voting, Park Pil-sam can be seen beside his closest friend, Na Jo-eun, after watching multiple people vote, the vote was leading more to the side to continue the games, so a player, Player 119 (No Sang-hun) questioned the "O" voters, which made another player, Player 322 (Jung Min-tae), strike back with the argument that the games were his last chance in life, which led to 119 getting mad and trying to attack 322, though the guards interrupted them and the voting continued. Park Pil-sam was the 3rd to last to vote, and he decided to vote O, like his friend, Jo-eun, wanting the games to continue, making the counter 99-100, staying in favor of the Xs. After Player 002 (Mun Jang-ho) voted, the counter went into a tie. So the last player, Player 001 (Oh Il-nam), was the one to make the final decision, and decided to ultimately end the games, which left Pil-sam disappointed.
He was then brought back to the outside world, where he was dropped off on a roadside while tied up and barely clothed, alongside another unknown player. they then presumably helped each other get untied, and after getting clothed, they presumably went back to their respective homes, where after a few days, Park Pil-sam received another Squid Game business card, inviting him to return to the games, which he decided to do, so he went to the same location where he was previously dropped off, was picked up and was knocked out by gas.
Upon waking up, he found himself back in the dormitory, and after getting up, he and Jo-eun befriended Player 034 and Jang Seung-jong, and they talked and then went to the second game, where Pil-sam chose triangle, and successfully carves out his shape, upon returning to the dorms Pil-sam witnessed Player 271's death and went to bed in fear.

Fate[]
In the Special Game, Park Pil-sam was killed by an unknown player, as that was where he was last seen along with his icon disappearing. 

Trivia[]
Park Pil-sam dies in the Special Game alongside Choi Woo-seong (Player 006), and interestingly, Woo-seong's number is double the number of Pil-sam's.
Interestingly, The Pre-Special Game Pil-sam, who was portrayed by Kim Young-hee, only had a single scene portraying Park Pil-sam.
He is one of the few minor players with multiple actors who are all named. With two sharing the same name, Young-hee.
Park Pil-sam shares his name with the winner of the 3rd Squid Game, Player 028.
Mistakes[]
Before Red Light Green Light and before Special Game, the woman who portrays his icon is seen portraying Park Pil-sam.
Additionally, Park Pil-sam shares his icon with Player 171 and Player 272 icon.
In Red Light, Green Light, another Player 003 is seen.
Another female Player 003 is seen after the second game.
Behind the scenes[]
Park Pil-sam was portrayed by four different actors; two male and two female. During Red Light, Green Light he was portrayed by Kwon Gu-nam. During the pre and post Red Light, Green Light lobby scenes, as well as in Sugar Honeycombs, he was portrayed by Kwak Jang-ryun. During the picture taking and Special Game, Lee Young-hee acted as Park Pil-sam. and before the Special Game, he was portrayed by Kim Young-hee.
