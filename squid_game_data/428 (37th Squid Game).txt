History[]
Player 428 was in massive debt and decided to join the Squid Games for a chance to win a huge cash prize. He called the number on the recruiting card, and got into a mysterious car. He then woke up in a giant room with 454 other players. He then made his way to the center of the room and watched as many other players debts were revealed in front of him. Later on, he signed a contract and made his way up a flight of stairs to the first game.
During the first game, he was soon terrified as players were shot in front of him during the mass panic. He was able to control himself and stand still. He eventually completed the first game after listening to Gi-hun's commands. After returning to the dormitory, many players pleaded the guards to let them go. However, after a massive increase in the prize pool was revealed, Player 428 changed his mind and decided to vote O during the first vote. Him and the other Os then cheered after they won the vote.
After getting his food the following morning, he was among the group of players led by Player 100 and Player 226, who approached Gi-hun for knowledge on the second game, but left shortly after due to Gi-hun not agreeing to keep quiet about it.
When he stepped into the arena for the second game, it was announced that the players were to form teams. Realizing that they were not going to play Dalgona despite Gi-hun's claims, Player 428 and 285 followed 100 and 226 to join their team. He later greeted 023 when introduced, and afterwards decided to play Biseokchigi. He and his team successfully passed together, and stuck with eachother in the dormitory.
In the dormitory, when Seong Gi-hun's team came back, he, along with his team, were disappointed that more people passed the second game, barely increasing the prize money. The Manager later brought out the piggy bank and revealed that 110 players were eliminated in Six-Legged Penthathlon, which accumulated ₩20.1 billion in the piggy bank with each player's share ₩78,823,530. A couple players complained about the low prize increase, and 428 was shown to be clearly frustrated.
During the next vote, after it was clear O was gaining the majority, Hwang In-ho began to advise them to stop the game to save their lives, scolding Player 015 in the process. This lead to 226, 285 and 428 interfering. 226 claimed that the games were childish and easy, and that they should play one more, which 428 supported. He later chants with the other Os for one more game. After the voting was concluded and it was determined the games were gonna continue, The guards gave the players their food (being a bread and a carton of milk). 428 and his team were seen waiting in line.
The next day, players were woken up and taken to the third game, which was revealed to be Mingle. Player 428 and his team stuck together on the platform and waited for the first round to commence. From here, Player 428 was not seen until the 4th round. In Round 4 rooms of 6 are announced. Dae-ho's team makes it to Room 02 but discovers that 100, 226, 285, 428, 376 and 023 are inside. Player 428 and his group retaliated by shutting the door in their face and locking it. After Round 4 concluded, Player 428 made his way back onto the carousel and prepared for the finale round.

Fate[]
During round 5, Player 428 teamed up with an unknown player and attempted to secure a room, but they were unable to, and were shot by the soldiers.

Behind the scenes[]
Player 428 was portrayed by Lee Jong-moo.

Trivia[]
He was the last elimination announced in Mingle.
He is the only one of his pentathlon team who doesn't have a voting scene. Additionally, he is the only one of his penthathlon team who has no audible dialogue.
He is the last to die In the 420's. Interestingly, none of the 420s made it into the top 100, the only ten with whom this is shared is the 280s.
He was also allies with the final player of the 280s.
