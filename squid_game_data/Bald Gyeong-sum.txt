Personality[]
I agree! Look dude, all we got are these keys! That’s not very fair is it?!
Player 192 complaining that being a Hider is unfair in Keys and Knives
Player 192 is a greedy and remorseless man who is not above any ruthless means to win the money. He even took part in a special game and unfortunately managed to kill Player 431. He ignores Geum-ja when she tearfully pleads him to stop (meaning vote to end the games), despite him looking guilty while listening to her. He is also a liar, backstabber, someone who thanked other people for his survival, and even holding on to anyone for survival, even his ally.

History[]
Player 192 got into debt, presumably higher than ₩1.724 billion, through unknown means before being in the game. While unknown, it is implied the reason was because of gambling or other shady methods.
He decided to join the Squid Games for a chance to win a huge cash prize. He called the number on the recruiting card, and got into a car. He then woke up in a giant room with 454 other players. Later on, he signed a contract and made his way up a flight of stairs to the first game.
During the first game, he was soon terrified as players were shot in front of him during the mass panic. He was able to control himself and stand still. He eventually completed the first game after listening to Gi-hun's commands. After returning to the dormitory, many players pleaded the guards to let them go. However, after a massive increase in the prize pool was revealed, Player 192 changed his mind and decided to vote O during the first vote. Him and the other Os then cheered after they won the vote.
Later during Six-Legged Pentathlon, 192 partnered up with 4 players. He and his team managed to complete all 5 minigames within the 5 minute timer. After successfully completing Six-Legged Pentathlon, him and the other players held another vote. He decided to vote O again along with many other O players.
Later on during Mingle, He managed to partner up with other players and passed all 5 rounds. After Mingle, him and the other remaining players returned to the dormitory and have a third vote. 192 decided to vote O again along with 49 other O players. This vote ends up in a tie, meaning they would vote again in the morning. 192 and the other players seem disappointed and move back to their beds. Later on in the bathroom. 192 overhears Lee Mung-gi and Thanos arguing. He accidentally finds himself isolated from the other O players and behind the X players. He then listened to them argue even more and watched as Player 348 and Player 442 got involved. Suddenly, a fight ensued after Thanos punched Myung-gi in the face. During the fight, 192 tried to grab both Player 401 and Player 348, but failed. He was then overpowered and lifted into the air by Player 324. In the chaos. he then got into physical confrontations with Players 072, 206, and 324, eventually being pinned and stomped by 206 and 324, while Player 145 punched Player 442 nearby. After the bathroom fight ends, 192 and the other surviving players involved walk out of the bathroom. 192 and the other Os then start accusing the Xs of starting the fight. A giant argument then ensues between the 2 sides. However Im Jeong-dae breaks up the altercation by suggesting a headcount to see what team lost more players. 192 and the other Os agree and they all line up to start counting. When they eventually figure out that they have less players compared to the Xs, 192 and the other Os then decide to ambush the Xs during the night to put them ahead. When the lights finally go out, 192 and some other Os are seen sneaking over to Team X's side of the room. Eventually, 192 and the other Os charged into the bunks and start killing as many Xs as possible. During the Special Game, Player 192 managed to kill Player 431 via stabbing him to death with a broken glass bottle. After the Special Game. He decided not to participate in Gi-hun's Rebellion and instead stay in the lobby with all of the other players that also decided not to join. In the next round of voting he voted O again and can be seen celebrating and mocking Gi-hun when they won the vote. It was unknown that he witnessed Seon-nyeo getting strangled by Gi-hun for mocking his dead friends, shocking the rest of Team O.

Later during Hide and Seek, 192 drawled a blue ball from the gumball machine, which confirmed his role as a hider. The manager later explained that him and his team would either have to survive for the 30 minute timer, or find a exit door to escape the maze. 192 was visibly disappointed by this and yelled at the guards about how unfair the game was for him and his team. Later when the guards allow them to switch teams, Player 192 grabbed Player 440 and asked her to switch teams with him. Player 440 refused his request and instead walked off. However, 192 didn't take no for an answer and kept grabbing and persuading her into switching with him. Eventually, Player 440 gave in and handed him the knife and red vest. Player 192 then thanked her and walked off with his new vest and knife. If we switch, I promise I won't kill you.
Player 192  promising Player 440 that he won't kill her. in "Keys and Knives"Much appreciated!
Player 192 thanking Player 440 for switching with him in "Keys and Knives" Because 192 switched to a Seeker, he would now have to kill at least 1 Hider in order to pass the game. 192 and the other seekers then wait for the hiders to go into the maze and hide. After the timer to hide finishes, Player 192 and the others Seekers make their way into the maze. 192 later managed to pass the game by killing Player 440 about half way in the game. After Hide and Seek concludes, 192 and the other surviving players make their way back to the dormitory, where they have another vote. When it was his turn to vote, Jang Geum-ja stopped him and pleaded with him and the other Os to end the games. However, 192 did not care and decided to vote O anyway along with 15 other players. They end up winning the vote again and continuing the games further.
Later on, him and the remaining players make their way to Jump Rope. Upon entering Jump Rope, 192 and the other remaining players looked in fear at the bridge they had to cross. Later on, after witnessing the death of Nam-gyu and watching Gi-hun successfully cross the bridge, 192 and the other players get motivation to start crossing. 192 started crossing the bridge right behind Player 442, However, him and the other players then got stuck on the bridge after Player 096 starts pushing other players off. 192 then pleads with Player 442 to move forward after his legs start to give out. Player 442 then tells him that he can't because of Player 096 blocking the path. 192 and Player 442 then hear Im Jeong-dae suggest a plan of helping him push the other players off the bridge if Player 096 lets him on, and give him half of his share. 192 and Player 442 are angered by this and Player 442 threatens to kill Im Jeong-dae for suggesting it.

Fate[]
I can't jump anymore, keep going! (...) Just shove your way through!
Player 192's final words in "222" 
After Player 442 threatened to kill Im Jeong-dae, Player 192 lost his balance and started to slip off behind him. In a state of panic, 192 grabbed onto Player 442's shoulder. This stunned Player 442 and caused them both to start panicking. The rope then came back around and swept both of them off of the bridge, which caused them both to plummet to the ground and hit the flower painted floor, killing them both instantly. 192 and Player 442 were then shown as mangled corpses on the ground later in the episode. Their corpses were presumably incinerated after Jump Rope concluded. He placed 18th in the competition.

Trivia[]
He was the only supporting character to speak any English, as he said “Thank you” to Player 440 when trading vests with her.
He and Thanos both spoke English in any audio languages like Korean.
He, Player 344, Player 443 and Kim Yun-tae are the only O voters to not laugh at Dae-ho.
Kim Young-mi, the actor for Player 440, stated that Jang Tae-min improvised approaching her and said the words 'if we switch I promise I won't kill you.' This didn't work out however, as he did kill her.
It is possible that he didn’t plan on killing her and was actually going to live up to his promise but only broke it due to the time running out and he still hadn’t gotten a kill and she happened to be the closest target for him.
Player 192 was attacked by the most amount of players from the opposing team in the Bathroom Fight, which was 1 against 3.
Despite this, he survived the Bathroom fight, which makes him a strong minor character despite his small frame, as he survived being attacked by 3 X players at once
Additionally, Player 192 was likely skilled in combat as he somehow injured his 3 attackers all by himself, as seen on the bloodstains on them (072, 206, 324) after the Bathroom Fight.
A BTS picture of the maze from the 4th game revealed that Player 192 was given a triangle key. Based on the info that 192 was originally not supposed to swap teams and survive the 4th game, it’s likely that he was supposed to take Player 232’s place and die when Im Jeong-dae ditches him to save his own skin.
All the players he killed and indirectly killed had a player number with at least 1 digit being 4.
Excluding Jun-hee, he and Player 442 were the last players to die whose player numbers end with 2.
He is the last player in the 1X2 category to be eliminated.
He is the last player in the 19X range to be eliminated in the 37th squid game.
Since Player 192 placed 18th in the competition, his 33rd Squid Game counterpart is Oh Il-nam.
Coincidentally, both players were almost bald.
His thanks to 440 could also be taken as the actor speaking it, as it meant he could be in the show more.
He was a very memorable minor character as a lot of people noticed him on a first time watch due to his distinct and notable appearance, even casual fans.
He is the second player in the 100s range with most votes in O, only surpassed by Jeong-dae.
Just like Deok-su with Mi-nyeo in season 1, 192 and 442 are the only players who die at the same time in the fifth game.
He was counted 39th in the Team O headcount.
He also shares some similarities and parallels with Jang Do-yeong [Player 198]. -Both players have their player number in the 190s range. -Both players are part of the 0 team and are fairly outspoken, Jang Do-yeong to the pink guards and Player 192 when intimidating the X voters. -Both indirectly cause the death of another player or players, Do-yeong to the rest of his Six-Legged Pentathlon team by failing several times at the Flying Stone mini-game, 192 by grabbing Player 442 in panic in Jump Rope and knocking them both off. -Both have little or thinning hair while having a certain more amount of facial hair.  They also have differences/parallels: -Do-yeong is one of the most nervous and least violent O voters, the opposite of Player 192 who apparently participated in the Special Game and killed Player 431  there, as well as participating in the bathroom fight and intimidating the Xs on several occasions. -Jang Do-yeong dies in the second game of the contest while Player 192 dies in the fifth game or the second last.
