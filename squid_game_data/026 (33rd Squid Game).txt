History[]
Player 026 was in heavy debt and chose to enter the Squid Games to win money. After waking up in a large room with 455 other players, he signed a contract, and got his photo, proceeding to enter the first game, Red Light, Green Light.

Fate[]
He was unable to stop on a red light and afterwards got shot by a sniper.

Behind the scenes[]
Player 026 is portrayed by Jin Kyo-man, who also portrayed Yang Ho-jun in Season 2, as well as three unknown extras.

Mistakes[]
Player 026's icon is the same as Player 296's icon.
Player 026's icon is disappeared during the mass panic.
Another Player 026 appears after the real Player 026's death, and is also shot for moving.
