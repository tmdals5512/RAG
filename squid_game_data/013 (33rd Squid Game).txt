History[]
Player 013 was in crippling debt, and most likely a gambling victim. Later him was given the offer to enter the Squid Games to win large amounts of money. He accepted this offer. He was picked up and sedated by Squid Game staff and transported to the island where the games take place. After waking up, Player 013 could be seen sitting up in his bed and looking around the Squid Game lobby near Seong Gi-hun and Oh Il-nam as they talked. He later witnessed the fight that occurred between Jang Deok-su and Kang Sae-byeok before the games began.
After the fight is broken up, the guards explain the rules of the game to him and the other players. Later, he signed a contract, took his photo, and proceeded to the first game called Red Light, Green Light. He managed to survive the first game. A vote was then held to decide whether the games should continue, and the majority voted to end them. However, Player 013 and most of the other players returned to the games after being given the opportunity. He then participated in the second game.

Fate[]
During Sugar Honeycombs, his dalgona tore due to the stress of shooting the other players, which led to him being shot by a soldier and eliminated. 

Trivia[]
Going by numerical order, He is the second iconless player in the 33rd Squid Game.
It's possible his name was intended to be Yoon Ji-eun (윤지은 Yun Ji-eun?) due to that being the name of the 13th winner on the winners list.
Mistakes[]
Two female Player 013 corpses were seen in Red Light, Green Light.
Player 167's and Player 286's icons are the same as Player 013's icon.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 013 was portrayed by Kwon Oh-jin, Kim Soo-young and an unknown extra.
