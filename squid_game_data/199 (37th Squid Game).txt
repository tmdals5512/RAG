History[]
Player 199 was presumably in massive debt and decided to join the Squid Games for a chance to win a huge cash prize. He called the number on the recruiting card, and got into a mysterious car. He then woke up in a giant room with 454 other players. He then made his way to the center of the room and watched as many other players debts were revealed in front of him. Later on, he signed a contract and made his way up a flight of stairs to the first game.
During the first game, He heard Gi-hun screaming about how dangerous the games were. Player 199 and many other players ignored his warnings, and continued the game without any worry. However, He was soon terrified as players were shot in front of him during the mass panic. Player 199 luckily was able to control himself and stand still. He eventually completed the first game after listening to Gi-hun's commands. After returning to the dormitory with the other surviving players, Player 199 begged the guards to let him and the other players go. However, The guards instead showed everyone the massive increase in prize money that accumulated after the first game. They then explained the voting process to all of the players. Player 199 did not care about the money and decided to Vote X during the first Vote. Unfortunately for him and the other Xs, More players voted to continue the games. Which means they would have to continue the games.
Later during Six-Legged Pentathlon, Player 199 decides to partner up with 4 unknown players. He then watched as many other teams failed and passed the game.

Fate[]
Player 199 isn't seen after Six-Legged Pentathlon, which means him and his team were eliminated at some point during the game.

Trivia[]
Player 199 has the same player number as one of the main characters from Season 1.
They both voted X out of the games after the first game
His number (199) is a Prime Number.
He is the only one in X99 range who have voted X. The other ones voted O.
