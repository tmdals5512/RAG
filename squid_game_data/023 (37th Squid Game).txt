History
Player 023 was in massive debt and decided to join the Squid Games for a chance to win a huge cash prize. He called the number on the recruiting card, and got into a mysterious car. He then woke up in a giant room with 454 other players. He then made his way to the center of the room and watched as many other players debts were revealed in front of him. Later on, he signed a contract and made his way up a flight of stairs to the first game.
During the first game, he was soon terrified as players were shot in front of him during the mass panic. He was able to control himself and stand still. He eventually completed the first game after listening to Gi-hun's commands. After returning to the dormitory, many players pleaded the guards to let them go. However, after a massive increase in the prize pool was revealed, Player 023 changed his mind and decided to vote O during the first vote. Him and the other Os then cheered after they won the vote.
When he stepped into the arena for the second game, it was announced that the players were to form teams. While team-searching by himself, he was approached by Kim Yeong-sam, and was lead to the rest of the team, where they all greeted each other. Player 023 chose to play Ddakji. He and his team successfully passed together, and stuck with each other in the dormitory.
In the dormitory, when Seong Gi-hun's team came back, he, along with his team, were disappointed that more people passed the second game, barely increasing the prize money. The Manager later brought out the piggy bank and revealed that 110 players were eliminated in Six-Legged Pentathlon, which accumulated ₩20.1 billion in the piggy bank with each player's share ₩78,823,530. A couple players complained about the low prize increase, and 285 was shown to be frustrated, talking with Player 287.
During the next vote, Player 023 voted to continue the games once more. After it was clear O was gaining the majority, Hwang In-ho began to advise them to stop the game to save their lives. To this, Im Jeong-dae and Player 023 quickly arrived, complaining how the accumulated prize money wasn't enough. Later, 226 claimed that the games were childish and easy, and that they should play one more, which 023 supported. He later chants with the other Os for one more game. After the voting was concluded and it was determined the games were gonna continue, The guards gave the players their food (being a bread and a carton of milk). 023 and his team were seen waiting in line.
The next day, players were woken up and taken to the third game, which was revealed to be Mingle. Player 023 and his team stuck together on the platform and waited for the first round to commence. From here, Player 023 was not seen until the 4th round. In Round 4 rooms of 6 are announced. Dae-ho's team makes it to Room 02 but discovers that 100, 226, 285, 428, 376 and 023 are inside. Player 023 and his group retaliated by shutting the door in their face and locking it. After Round 4 concluded, Player 023 made his way back onto the carousel and prepared for the finale round.

Fate
During round 5, Player 023 teamed up with an unknown player and attempted to secure a room, but they were unable to, and were shot by the soldiers.

Mistakes
He is seen with a X patch twice in Six-Legged Pentathlon, and once more in BTS footage.
Player 023's icon is seen blacked out after round 3 of Mingle.
Behind the scenes
Player 023 was portrayed by Kim Joon-hwan.
