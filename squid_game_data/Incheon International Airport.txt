Incheon International Airport is an international airport that serves Seoul.
In 2022, Seong Gi-hun traveled to this airport to catch a flight to the United States to see his daughter. After encountering a man playing ddakji against The Recruiter, he contacted the Squid Game staff and turned back when he realized he was being monitored.
