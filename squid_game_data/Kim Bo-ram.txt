Kim Bo-ram (최종국 Gim Bo-ram?) is the actor, who portrayed Jang Seung Jong (dormitory and voting), Player 092 (Tug of War) and Player 115 in the Dormitory before Marbles and Player 228 during the Tug of War lobby in Season 1 of the Netflix TV series Squid Game.
