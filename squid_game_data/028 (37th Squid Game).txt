History[]
Not much known is about Player 028's life prior the games except she was presumably in massive debt and decided to join the Squid Games for a chance to win a huge cash prize and hoping to solve her financial issues. She called the number on the recruiting card and got into a mysterious car. 

Season 2[]
"001"[]
She then woke up in a giant room with 454 other players. She then made his way to the center of the room and watched as many other players debts were revealed in front of her. She eventually signed a contract, took her phot and made her way up a flight of stairs to the first game.
Before Red Light, Green Light, Player 028 listened to Gi-hun's alerting about the game's deadly nature although she and many other players were sceptical and ignored his warnings, so they continued the game without any worry. However, Player 028 was soon terrified as players were shot in front of her during the mass panic, luckily, she was able to control herself and stood still. She eventually completed the first game after listening to Gi-hun's commands. After returning to the dormitory with the other surviving players, Player 028 begged the guards to let her and the other players go. However, after a massive increase in the prize pool was revealed, Player 028 changed her mind and decided to vote O during the first vote. Her and the other Os then cheered after they won the vote.

"Six Legs" & "One More Game"[]
In Six-Legged Pentathlon, Player 028 decided to partner up with players 013, 134, 361, and 175. She played Jegi for her team, and they then managed to complete all 5 minigames within the 5 minute timer. After successfully completing Six-Legged Pentathlon, her and the other players held another vote. Player 028 decided to vote O again along with many other O players.

"O X"[]
Later on, during Mingle, In the first round she's seen getting left without a group while the timer drops but manages to find 9 players off-screen and survive at the end of it, Player 028 meets players 116, 119, and 310 and got with them for the 2nd round while struggling with other players, during the 3rd round she struggles with multiple players but manages to survive it and get a room. Before the fourth round starts, in the plataform, we can see Player 028 along with players 116, 119 and 310, who they are speculated to went with player 028 in fourth round. During fifth round, she is grabbed by Player 011 but by a posibblye anger reason to see Player 011's parch vote (X), she abandoned Player 011 and went with Player 219 instead, managing to pass the third game and made her way back to the Dormitory with Player 119 and later she and the rest of the players had a third vote. Player 028 decided to vote O again along with many other O players and she celebrates with Player 119 when Player 009 votes O again. This vote ends up in a tie, meaning they will vote again in the morning. Player 028 and the other players seem disappointed and they took a break before the third food. 
During the third meal, player 028 sat next to Player 310 and possibly told her about her life, but when Yong-sik tried to convince Team O to change their vote and then Kang Dae-ho and Yeong-sam discussing, Player 028 and Player 310 are seen mocking Dae-ho with the rest of their team, making the argument escalated causing that the O voter players get angry and discuss with the players from X side in the dorm, players 028 and 310 decided to stay quiet and mocking to them in their place. 

"Friend or Foe"[]
When the female announcer's voice announced the 5 eliminations, player 028 became worried while sitting next to players 116 and 310, not understanding what had happened. When Nam-gyu and some of the Team O players returned from the bathroom with blood soaked in their suits, and some Team X players returned from the other side quite injured, Player 028 stood alongside players 006 (Park Mi-wha), 116, 306 and 310 believing without question about Nam-gyu's word, who lied about how the players from Team X attacked them and killed his friend. After the events of the bathroom fight,  her and the other Os have a headcount, Player 028 remained silent alongside Player 119 and figure out that they have less players compared to the Xs. The other Osdecided to hide with multiple players as Team O ambushed Xs during the night to put them ahead. After the Special Game, Player 028 along Player 334 chose not to participate in Gi-hun's rebellion against the guards, so she remains in the dormitory.

Season 3[]
"Keys and Knives", "The Starry Night" & "It's Not Your Fault"[]
Following Gi-hun's failed Rebellion, Player 028 and other players noticed the pink guards carrying a coffin, containing Gi-hun's unconscious body. 
When Gi-hun woke up, Player 028 gave her bad opinion about Gi-hun alongside Player 024. Gi-hun went berserk against the guards and player 028 remained silent and watched him with possible disgust. 
In the re-vote, when the money won in the special game and the bathroom fight was shown in the piggy bank, player 028 watched with admiration alongside players 024, 119, and 310, possibly gaining ego by believing that she could win the next game, she remained voting O and due to a number of players in Team X were killed from the Special Game and the Rebellion, Team O won the vote and continue the games further. 
During Hide and Seek, Player 028 drew a red ball from the gumball machine, which confirmed her role as a Seeker. Later when the guards allowed them to switch teams, she decided not to switch with Player 394 who was trying to convince both her and 119 to switch teams. After the timer to hide finished, Player 028 and the other Seekers made their way into the maze.
Inside, we can see Player 028 confused and when everyone moves to kill the players from blue team, we can see that player 028 goes alone in the maze. We don't know why she didn't decided to go with Player 154 and what she did inside the maze during all the 30 minutes to kill somebody.

Fate[]
Player 028 did not kill any hiders in time, meaning she was shot to death by the Soldiers. She placed 33rd-26th along with the other failed Seekers who were killed off-screen.

Trivia[]
A man named Park Pil-sam participated in and won the 3rd Squid Game in 1990 with this number. Pil-sam also had the smallest player number ever to achieve victory in the Korean squid games.
Player 028 died in the fourth game during both the 37th and 33rd Squid Games.
She is the last player in the 02X range to be eliminated in the 37th games.
Player 028 is the player with the longest hair as seen in the first vote along with Player 164.
She is the last female player between 001-100 to die.
She was counted second in the Team O headcount.
She is seen running to a room with Player 011 erroneously in the final round.
She is the only one on her pentathlon team whose player number does not contain the number 1.
Along with Player 163, she is the only Female Player On Team O to witness Seong Gi-hun Strangle Seon-nyeo except for Park Mi-hwa.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 028 was portrayed by Ryu Jeong.
