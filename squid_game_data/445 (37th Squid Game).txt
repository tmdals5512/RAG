History[]
Player 445 joined the Squid Games while struggling with debt. He woke up in the dormitory with the other participants, signed the contract, and had his photo taken. Player 445 survived Red Light, Green Light and voted X in the first vote. He also survived Six-Legged Pentathlon and voted X again in the second vote.

Fate[]
Player 445 died in Mingle after failing to secure a room.

Behind the scenes[]
Player 445 was portrayed by an unknown extra.
