Kwon Byeong-su (권병수 Gwon Byeong-su?), also known as Player 047, was a participant in the 37th Squid Game. He was the leader of Team X and a part of Gi-hun's revolt.

Personality[]
We can't do anything sitting here! Let's follow them upstairs!
Kwon Byeong-su telling Oh Young-il to regroup with Gi-hun and Jung-bae during the Revolt in "Friend or Foe"
Byeong-su is portrayed as a brave and trustworthy person and usually stands up for Team X against Team O. He values life over money as he thinks that Team O is heartless and blinded by money. Despite him presumably being in heavy debt, he still chooses not to be blinded by money and fights for what is morally right. Although he is very friendly to the Xs, he presents extreme aggression towards the Os, calling out things they say, and yelling at them that it's their fault people have died.

History[]
Kwon Byeong-su faced a grave amount of debt, unable to pay it off. He was then approached by The Recruiter, who offered him to play Ddakji. And if he won, he would win 100k won. Byeong-su accepts the offer, and engages in a match with him. He likely wins the match without getting slapped, due to the fact that he passed first-try in the Six-Legged Pentathlon. Afterwards, he was given a strange business card with a phone number, a location, and the date to call it. When it was time, he dials the number at the specific location, and was picked up by a white van. Unknowingly being abducted to The Island, Byeong-su woke up on his bed in a large dormitory. Confused, he looked around from afar before the Pink Guards introduce themselves to the players and reveal the footage of the players being slapped on TV. Byeong-su then looked as the piggy bank descends into the dormitory to reveal itself, and he was convinced that he would actually get the money he needed to pay all his debt off to live a normal life. He signs a contract, takes his photo, and then walks up a bunch of stairs to the arena of Red Light, Green Light. Byeong-su looked around at the massive arena, confused. Once the first game was revealed to him, he understood it clearly. But that's when Seong Gi-hun warned everyone, including Byeong-su, that they will get killed if they move on red light. It's unknown if Byeong-su doubted this or not, but most likely he did as he maintained his composure when the initial chaos breaks out. Offscreen, Byeong-su successfully completes Red Light, Green Light, making it within the time limit. Horrified from the 91 deaths in the 1st game, Byeong-su votes X. But as Hwang In-ho votes O to continue the games, Byeong-su is forced to stay.We should've left! Now we're all gonna die! We're gonna die because half of you said wanted to keep doing this!
Kwon Byeong-su to O voters in "Six Legs"The 2nd day begins, Byeong-su receives his short meal and shortly afterwards, he proceeds to the arena for the Six Legged-Pentathlon, During this game, he formed a team with Player 308, Player 073, Player 328 and Player 401. After the eliminations of Team 1 and Team 2, Byeong-su and Player 014 stood up and yelled that was why they had to leave and that they would all die for all those who voted for "O". A player that closely resembles him was shown playing Ddakji, though his number was not clearly visible. Byeong-su survives the second game offscreen. After that, he votes for "X" for the second time. Again, he was forced to stay as the O's dominate the voting. On the 3rd day, he recieves his short meal again and shortly after, he goes to the 3rd game's arena. Which is revealed to be Mingle. Byeong-su teams up with unknown players for the first 4 rounds, and successfully finds a room with his unknown partner during the final round. He passes the third game offscreen as well. When the third vote was held after Mingle, Byeong-su voted for "X" for third time; the vote ended up in a tie, with 50 players voting for "X" and 50 players voting for "O". When the guards announced that there will be another voting after the 3rd one, Byeong-su asked them when it will take place. Is that true? Hey! What the fuck, man? That's cheating.
Kwon Byeong-su to Thanos and Nam-gyu in "O X"Shortly after the vote, a confrontation between Team X and Team O started in the bathroom. After he was informed that Thanos (Player 230) was trying to make someone switch votes, Byeong-su quickly joins in to protect their fellow ally, Min-su (Player 125). The confrontation quickly escalating into a fight. Byeong-su was seen fighting other players, such as: Kicking Player 358, and helping Player 094 attack Player 348. He survives the event and walks out the bathroom, exhausted and sweaty from all the physical activity. It is highly possible he has caused one of the three casualties for the O voters, which would be entirely justified considering they attacked first.  
That's fucking bullshit! You guys are the ones who started it. Fuck you. These bastards started threatening people on our side! They started the fight so they could win tomorrow's vote!
Kwon Byeong-su making assumptions to Team O in Friend or FoeEveryone! All the "X"s, come down! Group up so we can get a count.
Kwon Byeong-su telling Team X to assemble for a group count in Friend or FoeAfter the fight, the survivors came back to the Dormitory. Nam-gyu (Player 124) started making up false information and stated that Team X tried to kill Team O and mentioned the death of Thanos (Player 230), who was his best friend. But Byeong-su revealed that the Nam-gyu was lying and that Team O was the one who started the fight against Team X to win the next day's vote. Both teams started blaming each other for starting the fight until Im Jeong-dae (Player 100) stopped them and asked which team had more people, resulting in everyone in the "O" team to organize around him and be counted by Jeong-dae. Seeing that, Byeong-su did the same, raising his hand up and asking everyone from the "X" team to gather around to be counted. 
42, 43, 44, 45, 46, 47, 48. Our side lost two guys.
Kwon Byeong-su telling Team X about the players eliminated during the Bathroom fight in "Friend or Foe"
After the headcount, Byeong-su found out that Team X had 48 players, 1 more than Team O which had 47. Upon this, Park Jung-bae (Player 390) says that if they do not change their minds, they will win by 1 vote during the next vote. Byeong-su chips in and tries to convince the "X" team players to not change their votes and leave the games after the next vote.
A few minutes before lights out, some players including Byeong-su and his close friend, Player 145, gathered around. Gi-hun claimed that members from the other team would try to kill members of the "X" team, because if they did, they would be able to increase the prize money and win the vote.  Upon being asked what to do, Young-il suggested attacking the "O" players first. Byeong-su agreed with him, saying that since the "X" team has more women and elderly on their side, they would be at a disadvantage if they were attacked. Attacking the "O" team first would give them a better chance. However, Gi-hun convinced them to not kill, as he believes that is what the Front Man and the VIPs would want the players to do. He tells them not get involved in the fight, not even to protect the others, and tells them his plan to attack the soldiers whilst they were dealing with the dead bodies from the fight, to steal their guns and ammo and use these to shoot down as many of the Squid Game's staff as possible. Jung-bae, Hyun-ju, Young-il and a few other players, including Byeong-su, decide to found a team and join the rebellion.

Out of my way! Don't you see what they did? Just look around. They're not even human anymore. You have no right to stop me from killing these greedy assholes!
Kwon Byeong-su telling Seong Gi-hun to let him kill Team O in "Friend or Foe"
After successfully attacking the soldiers and stealing their guns and ammo, Byeong-su gets furious and attempts to use the gun to kill players from the "O" team. He claims that they are inhumane, and that they are "greedy assholes blinded by money". Gi-hun stops him and says that if he kills them, he would be no different from the guards and the VIPs. Hearing this, Byeong-su reluctantly points his gun away in frustration.

Here! Don't waste your shots. If you shoot blindly, you'll be empty soon.
Kwon Byeong-su telling Kang Dae-ho not waste his bullets during the Revolt in "Friend or Foe"
During the rebellion, Byeong-su manages to kill multiple guards. Seeing that Kang Dae-ho was shooting his gun recklessly, he advises against his actions and as Dae-ho would waste his ammo as throws him a magazine.

Fate[]
     In-ho betrays and shoots Byeong-su in the back.  
Later, after Gi-hun and Jung-bae find their way to the control rooms, Hyun-ju advises Young-il to collect the magazines from the dead soldiers along the path leading to the control room. Young-il brings Byeong-su and Player 015 after the two volunteered to go with him. Along the way, while Player 015 and Byeong-su were both standing in front of Young-il flanking his left and right, Byeong-su was betrayed and was shot in the back by Young-il, who was actually Hwang In-ho. Player 015 is shocked to see his ally get shot, but In-ho shoots him in the chest before he could do anything about it.
Byeong-su briefly appears in Season 3. His body was forcefully hanged along with those of Player 015, Player 072, Player 145, Player 206, Player 324, and Park Jung-bae, along with the manager taken hostage by the revolt team (wearing the Player 001 tracksuit) to show the consequences of attempting to take down the games.

Trivia[]
Similar to several living players, Byeong-su's hair seems to appear different when his body is seen hanging.
It's highly possible he may have killed one of the three O-casualties in the bathroom. It is absolutely justified since he was attacked.
His family name, 'Kwon,' is not only unique to his character, but can also mean 'authority' or 'punch,' two things embedded into his character, as he is a co-leader of the Xs, and is incredibly violent towards the Os.
His Pentathlon team was the only one shown in the montage that was not seen dying or crossing the finish line, but is shown directly when they leave the arena.
Kwon Byeong-su stands at 165 cm, tying with Player 306 as the shortest male contestant in the 37th Squid Game.
He seemed to have decent fighting skills for a person in their mid-50s, as he was able to pull Nam-gyu off Myung-gi (before getting shoved aside as he wasn’t expecting to be attacked), and assisted 094 and 307 against 348 and 358 respectively.
Three of the players Gi-hun prevented Byeong-su from shooting (096, Gi-min, and Seung-won) would later try to kill Gi-hun, showing utter ungratefulness and would even try to take Jun-hee's baby for a murder.
He is the only heroic player to have his name revealed through the credits, as Byeong-gi, Gi-min, Seung-won, Yun-tae, and the VIPs are all antagonists.
He gave team O the most insulting aliases, due to his extreme hatred for them.
Of the episodes where he appears (not counting Keys and Knives), One More Game is the only one where he doesn't speak.
Behind the scenes[]
Kwon Byeong-su was portrayed by Baek Seung-chul. His name was revealed through the credits.
