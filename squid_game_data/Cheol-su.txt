Cheol-su (철수 Cheol-su?) is a large male animatronic doll that is used in the Squid Games. It is used during the "Jump Rope" game during the 37th Squid Game.

Overview[]
Based on the male counterpart from the same Korean textbook series, Cheol-su resembles a young boy with short, neatly trimmed black hair styled in a bowl cut, topped with a teal-colored baseball cap. He wears a dark green and teal horizontal-striped polo shirt with a light grey collar, paired with brown shorts. His attire is completed with white crew socks and black-and-white sneakers. 

Behind the scenes[]
Like Young-hee, he is named after the South Korean book character of the same name.[2][3]and is voiced by Zander Chin in the English dub.

Trivia[]
Cheol-su's and Young-hee's motion-sensing eyes are not used during Jump Rope.
Cheol-su's house is the same house used in the Red Light, Green Light arena.
