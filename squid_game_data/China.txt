China, officially the People's Republic of China (中华人民共和国 Zhōnghuá Rénmín Gònghéguó), is a country in East Asia. With a population exceeding 1.4 billion, it is the second-most populous country after India, representing 17.4% of the world population. It borders several countries, with North Korea and Russia to the East, Mongolia to the north, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan, and Afghanistan in the west, and Pakistan, India, Nepal, Bhutan, Myanmar, Laos, Vietnam, Hong Kong and Macau to the south. 
At the very end of the third season, in "Humans Are...," Kang No‑eul boards a plane to China, a move initiated by the news that her long-lost daughter might have been there, as suggested by Park Man-cheol, a broker that she hired.
