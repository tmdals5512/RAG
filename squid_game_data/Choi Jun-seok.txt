Choi Jun-seok (최준석 Choe Jun-seog?) is the actor who portrayed Player 184 in Tug of War, Player 366 in Season 1, David Park, Number 029, and Player 406 in Season 2 and Player 080 in Season 3 of the Netflix TV series Squid Game.
