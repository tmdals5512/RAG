This unnamed man is the current husband of Kang Eun-ji. He is the stepfather to Seong Ga-yeong and father to her unnamed stepbrother.

History[]
He and Kang Eun-ji meet and married some point after she and Seong Gi-hun divorced. Becoming a blended family with his son from a previous relationship or marriage he provides for the family. He appeared to strongly dislike Gi-hun as well his visits to see Ga-yeong as he was upset when Gi-hun was in his house. In 2020, he announced his intention to move Ga-yeong and her mother to the United States as he got a new job there, and attempted to bribe Gi-hun with money to stop seeing Ga-yeong after he visited his apartment to ask for money for his mother's surgery. Gi-hun responded to this by punching him in the face, but regretted this action after Ga-yeong witnessed it. 
By 2021, a year after Gi-hun won the 33rd Squid Game, the stepfather and his family had all moved to the US in Los Angeles.

Trivia[]
If he had a wife, he most likely divorced her before remarrying Kang Eun-ji.
It is unknown if Seong Gi-hun and him were ever on good terms at some point before Gi-hun's continuous search for The Recruiter for three long years, within which the family is already living in the United States, and Gi-hun's re-entry to the Squid Games in 2024.
Behind the scenes[]
Seong Ga-yeong's stepfather was played by Jang Jae-kwon.
