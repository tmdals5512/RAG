History[]
Player 022 was in heavy debt and chose to enter the Squid Games to win money. After waking up in a large room with 455 other people, he signed a contract and took his photo. He managed to survive the first game, Red Light, Green Light, after most of the contestants had been wiped out. He was likely upset from how the organization kills players for being eliminated. While he voted to stay in the games, he was initially sent home since a slight majority voted to terminate the games. So after the games were resumed, he and 186 other players decided to come back to win the prize money. 

Fate[]
In Sugar Honeycombs, he was shot for breaking his dalgona.
