Player 043







Player 042 was in a lot of debt. One day, The Recruiter played a game of Ddakji with him and got an invitation card. Later, he entered a van and slept. He then woke up in a dormitory with 454 other players. He then signed the consent form. We see him in Red Light, Green Light, and he manages to survive it. He managed to survive Six-Legged Pentathlon, and then changed his vote from O to X. However the majority voted O which caused him to continue the games. He then made his way to Mingle.

Fate[]
He managed to successfully survive the first round of Mingle but could not get into a room in time during the 2nd round, which caused him to be eliminated.

Behind the scenes[]
Player 042 was portrayed by Kang Shin-myeong.
When asked what Player 042's name was, Kang Shin-myeong stated it to be Kim Jin-soo.
