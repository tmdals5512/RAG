The man is a minor character in the Squid Games series, appearing only once.

History[]
He was part of a "game" between Gi-hun and Oh Il-nam, which was meant to test whether people could be trusted. If they rescued him before he died, Gi-hun would win, and the old man would have to admit not all humans are the same. But, if Il-nam won, he would lose his entire fortune.
