History[]
Player 011 was in tremendous debt that he couldn't pay off and chose to enter the Squid Games to win a huge cash prize. After waking up in a large room with 455 other players, he signed a contract and took his photo.
Later, everyone was taken to an open area with a creepy robot doll at the front. It was revealed that the game was Red Light, Green Light. When the doll turned around, everyone ran forward, with Player 250 and Player 324 ahead of everyone, but 324 wasn't able to stop in time before the doll turned around, causing him to be eliminated. 324 then slumped over.
Player 250, thinking Player 324 was putting up an act, ran to him and asked him to stop faking, until he noticed blood gushing from 324's mouth, causing him to recoil and try to run away, but the doll, who was still looking, detected him and shot him, splattering his blood all over Player 306, causing her to scream and be shot.

Fate[]
It's unknown where he died, his icon faded in Red Light, Green Light, but appeared multiple times after. He could've died in the special game, as his corpse was seen there, and it's where he last appeared. 

Trivia[]
His actor's name, Shin Seok-young, is similar to the name of the 11th Squid Game winner, Sin Seong-yong.
