History[]
Player 438 was likely in heavy debt and decided to join the games to pay it off, after she woke up in a room with 454 other players, she signed a contract and took her photo, after that, she was led to the first game, Red Light, Green Light. After a couple of turns, also following the elimination of Kang Mi-na, a mass scare caused players to run back, Player 438 was one of the many to get eliminated during this part.

Fate[]
Player 438 likely got eliminated by being pushed over by another player, or running along with the others who also were eliminated.
