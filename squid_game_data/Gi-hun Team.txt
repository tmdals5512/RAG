Seong Gi-hun (Player 456) formed a team with several other players during the 37th Squid Game. During the Six-Legged Pentathlon, his team, then made up of Gi-hun, Park Jung-bae (Player 390), Hwang In-ho (Player 001), Kang Dae-ho (Player 388) and Kim Jun-hee (Player 222), were Team 73.
Most of the team consists of members of Team 73 and Team 4 (Cho Hyun-ju/Player 120, Park Yong-sik/Player 007, Jang Geum-ja/Player 149 and Kim Young-mi/Player 095) which combined during Mingle. Gi-hun's team accepted Park Gyeong-seok (Player 246) after saving Hyun-ju and Young-mi in the third round of Mingle.
The group eventually made outside alliances as well, unfortunately, some of Gi-hun's allies also took part in the Revolt against the games, which lead to many of their deaths. All members were dead by the time the 37th Squid Game concluded, with only Hwang In-ho (Player 001) and Park Gyeong-seok (Player 246) surviving.
