History[]
Player 020 was presumably in massive debt and decided to join the Squid Games for a chance to win a huge cash prize. He called the number on the recruiting card, and got into a mysterious car. He then woke up in a giant room with 454 other players. After waking up, he made his way to the front of the room with all of the other players and confronted the guards about the situation. The guards then explained to him and the other players why they were there and the conditions of the games. He later watched as Lee Myung-gi's identity as a MG Coin was revealed. He looked visibly shocked at this and stared at Myung-gi. He then watched as a giant piggy bank appeared above him with the promise of it filling with prize money after every game. Player 020 was excited by this. He later signed a contract, took his photo, and walked up a flight of stairs to the arena of Red Light, Green Light.  
During the 1st game, Player 020 ignored Gi-Hun's warnings and continued the game without any worry. However, after witnessing many other players get gunned down, Player 020 was horrified and ran towards the doors in fear. He can be seen panicking behind Thanos. 

Fate[]
After making his way to the doors and attempting to push them open, Player 020 was shot in the back by a sniper. His dead body can be seen at the doors when Player 066 dies.

Trivia[]
Player 020 was mostly recognized by viewers because he stood behind Lee Myung-gi throughout his argument with the Pink Guards, in a several camera views. He was also visibly shocked to know Myung-gi's real identity as a former youtuber, MG Coin.
Player 020 is similar to his season 1 counterpart. They both were seen in the dormitory, panicked, and died in Red Light, Green Light. Coincidentally, both Player 020 from Season 1 and Season 2's corpses were seen right at the doors.
However, Player 020 from Season 1 was only seen from afar at the dormitory, while Player 020 from Season 2 was seen at the front.
Additionally, both Player 020s died to the doors but Player 020 from the 33rd Squid Game died facing the ground while the Player 020 from the 37th Squid Game died facing upwards.
He and Player 420 are the only X20s to not be in the top 100.
He shares the same digits with Player 002, who dies in the same game as him.
He is the only X20 to not be on Team X, due to the fact he was never involved in a vote.
Mistakes[]
In the BTS Footage of the Special Game, we see another Player 020.

Player 020 was portrayed by Kim Su-jin and Choi Jae-ok.
