History[]
Player 187 was in debt, so he joined the Squid Games. After he woke up, he saw 454 other players in the dormitory with him. he got up from his bed and listened to the guards explain the rules of the games. He would afterwards walk up the stairs and take his photo. He went into the first game, Red Light, Green Light. After listening to a crazy man explain the deaths, he would make his way to the finish line, Kang Mi-na would be shot and multiple players would panic while he stood still then later made it to the finish line. He voted to discontinue the games after listening to the players beg and after he saw the prize money. He would go into the second game, Six-Legged Pentathlon, and he would team up with Players 179, 119, 164. and 346, and later complete all minigames off-screen. Player 187 would then vote to discontinue the games again, and would be disappointed upon the results of the second voting. Player 187 would then wake up and make his way to Mingle. Player 187 would be confuzzled upon the carousel starting to move, he would be able to survive round 1 and round 2 off-screen, and would get on the carousel for round 3. Player 187 would be seen this time after the round started, being seen grappling with unknown players, and being dragged in a room by unknown players. He would survive the round with barely any time left, and make his way to the carousel for round 4.

Fate[]
Player 187 couldn't find a room in time during Round 4 of Mingle. Therefore, he was shot to death by a soldier.

Behind the scenes[]
Player 187 was portrayed by an unknown extra.

Trivia[]
In the fifth squid game in 1992, a player named Jo Su-jin won the games with this number.
