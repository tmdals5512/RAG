Personality[]
Player 009 is a quiet yet greedy individual. She votes O each opportunity she gets, yet she also seems to be a coward, as she was seen with scared facial expressions during the Six-Legged Pentathlon when other players were getting shot. She also cheers excitedly when Park Mi-hwa changes her vote from X to O showing her remorseless side that she prioritises money over other people's lives. After the tiebreaker vote, she also smirks as Park Yong-sik tries to convince members of Team O to switch to X showing her arrogant nature. During Hide and Seek, she is desperate to survive and win the whole competition, so she and Player 447 follow Seon-nyeo. 

History[]
Player 009, like the other contestants, joined the Squid Games due to being in significant debt, presumably higher than ₩660 million. She, along with 364 other contestants, made it past the first game. During the vote on whether to end or continue the games, she voted to continue. 
For the second game, it was announced that they needed to form teams of five. She and Player 197 quickly found each other and the two of them then later found Park Gyeong-seok, Sim Jae-seok, and Player 140. The game was announced to be a  Six-Legged Pentathlon. She chose to play Biseokchigi, and her and her team succeeded and survived the second game. She voted "O" to continue in the second round, splitting off from her team. 
She managed to survive Mingle as well, though off screen, She voted "O" to continue in third round, and made it through the Special Game, making alliance with Player 394.
During the re-vote, Player 009 remained voting "O." Before the forth game, she drew a blue ball, placing her as a Hider for Hide and Seek. She, along with Player 447, joined the cult of Seon-nyeo (Player 044) and followed her for the majority of the 4th game. When Seon-nyeo unlocked a door and ran away, Lee Myung-gi (Player 333) and Nam-gyu (Player 124) ambushed them. Along with Player 306, she managed to escape. Following the deaths of Mi-hwa and 447, she and Player 306 managed to escape as they weren't targeted by Nam-gyu and Myung-gi.  

Fate[]
Despite escaping, she soon died afterwards. Her killer is unknown, but her corpse was seen by Park Yong-sik (Player 007) as he noticed her on the ground as he walked past, whimpering. Seon-nyeo also discovered her corpse, trying to see if her key was triangle or square, but finds her key to be useless as she already had the circle. She does however use blood to make an arrow to show the way out. Her corpse is on the stairs and her arm is twisted the wrong way, meaning the seeker possibly killed her at the edge of the stairs, and her corpse fell down.

Trivia[]
Before being a part of Seon-nyeo's team, she was a member of Gyeong-seok's team.
She seems to have a close relationship with Player 447 as the two are seen holding onto each other throughout Hide and Seek.
She is the second to last player with a single digit number to be eliminated, only beaten by Park Yong-sik (Player 007).
When Yi-eun was asked who had killed her character, she simply replied that "nobody" had killed her.
She died at  12:00  between 10:00 minute
She only correctly appears in even games.
She lasted way longer than her 33rd Squid Game counterpart, as he died in the first game.
She is one of only two minor characters in season two to appear in every vote, and one game (Pentathlon), the other being Park Mi-hwa.
Player 009's icon was replaced by Player 084's icon in Red Light, Green Light.
She is the only female Hider to have an unknown killer during Hide and Seek
She is the only single digit player in season three to be unnamed.
Her player number added to the number of her closest friend, Player 447 is 456, Seong Gi-hun's player number and the total number of players in the Squid Games annually
Additionally, their numbers were the third lowest (006-007-009) and the third highest (447-448-456) of Season 3.
She was the only player in the singles division who voted four times for one team.
She was counted 27th in the Team O headcount.
She was originally meant to vote X in the 3rd vote and be the one killed by Nam gyu at the beginning of the special game.
The killer is unknown; it's probably Nam-gyu and Lee Myung-gi, because after Seon-nyeo's betrayal, they likely sought out player 009.
She is played by a different actress in RLGL.
She is the last female player confirmed to have played Biseokchigi for the pentathlon.
Out of all of the last female players that are confirmed to have played each minigame, she is the only one who is not a main character.
Mistakes[]
In the 2nd vote, Player 009 is shown voting O during the vote. However, she is later seen receiving an O patch, which usually indicates a player who changed their vote to O after previously voting X. Since Player 009 had already voted O before, she should not have received an O patch. This may be a continuity mistake or editing error in the episode.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 009 was portrayed by Kang Yi-eun. In a private message with an unknown person on Instagram, she unofficially stated that she thinks Player 009's name would be Ok-ja.
