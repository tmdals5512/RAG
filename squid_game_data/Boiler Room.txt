Between Pentathlon and Catch, the door to the Boiler Room was intentionally left unlocked for players to find. Once inside, a vending machine was discovered with a coin nearby. This coin was used to buy cookies, which were distributed to friends of the players. The vending machine contained an option to input 10 coins, to eliminate a player of choice.

Between Catch and Mingle and after opening the doll, Player 075, Natalie, received a 10 value coin. She later went to the Boiler Room, inserted the coin into the machine and typed in the number of who she wanted eliminated. She uses this to eliminate Player 080, Ashley. At this point in the competition, 383 players in total were eliminated.
