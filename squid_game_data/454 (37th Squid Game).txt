History[]
Player 454 was presumably in massive debt and decided to join the Squid Games for a chance to win a huge cash prize. She called the number on the recruiting card, and got into a mysterious car. She then woke up in a giant room with 454 other players. She then made her way to the center of the room and watched as many other players debts were revealed in front of her. Later on, she signed a contract and made his way up a flight of stairs to the first game.
During the first game, Player 454 heard Gi-hun screaming about how dangerous the games were. Player 454 and many other players ignored his warnings, and continued the game without any worry. However, she was soon terrified as players were shot in front of her during the mass panic. Player 454 luckily was able to control herself and stood still. She eventually completed the first game after listening to Gi-hun's commands. After returning to the dormitory with the other surviving players, Player 454 begged the guards to let her and the other players go. However, after a massive increase in the prize pool was revealed, she changed her mind and decided to vote O during the first vote. Her and the other Os then cheered after they won the vote.
Later during Six-Legged Pentathlon, Player 454 partnered up with players 394, 192 and 400. They later managed to pass all 5 games within the 5 minute timer. After successfully completing Six-Legged Pentathlon, her and the other players held another vote. However, instead of voting O for a second time, Player 454 voted X after reaching the amount of money she owed. 
Later on during Mingle, Player 454 made her way onto a giant carousel and prepared for round 1.

Fate[]
During the first round of Mingle, Player 454 was shot after failing to find a room with the correct amount of players in first round.

Trivia[]
She is the first ever player to join Team O in 37th Squid Game.
She has the highest number for a female.
She is also the last female who got recruited by the Recruiter.
Out of all of the players in the 400s seen voting in the first vote, she is the only one to never canonically appear in any game.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 454 was portrayed by Jeong Eun-kyung and 2 unknown extras.
