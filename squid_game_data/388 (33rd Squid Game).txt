History[]
Player 388 was in heavy debt and chose to enter a high-risk competition to win money. After waking up in a large room with 455 other people, he signed a contract and took his photo. He was taken to the first game, Red Light, Green Light.  

Fate[]
Player 388 panicked and got shot by one of the snipers for moving on a red light.

Mistakes[]
Player 388's icon and Player 394's icon are the same. But the person in these icons is the actor of Mun Jang-ho and Player 183.
