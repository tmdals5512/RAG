History[]
Player 017 was in large debt and decided to join the Squid Games. He woke up in a Dormitory along with 454 other players, he then proceeded sign a contrast and take his photo. He survived the first game, Red Light, Green Light, and was shocked from the deaths of the players, which resulted in him voting for X. He managed to survive the 2nd game, Six-Legged Pentathlon, and voted for X like he did before.  

Fate[]
Unfortunately, he wasn't able to find a room in Mingle, and was killed for it.

Player 017 was portrayed by Kim Yong-kyun.
