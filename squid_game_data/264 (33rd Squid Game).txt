History[]
Player 264 was in heavy debt and chose to enter the Squid Games to win money. After waking up in a dormitory filled with 455 other players, she signed a contract and took her photo. She then entered the first game, Red Light, Green Light. 

Fate[]
A mass panic occurred when everyone realized that they will be shot when they move. She ran towards the doors during the initial chaos, causing her to be detected and shot by one of the snipers.
