Jo Hyun-woo (조현우 Jo Hyeon-u?) also known by his birth name Cho Seok-hyeon (조석현 Jo Seog-hyeon?), is the actor who portrayed Kim Nam-du/Player 444 in Season 2 of the Netflix TV series Squid Game.

Trivia[]
Jo Hyun-woo also portrayed Lee Jae-yun's father in Teach You a Lesson Episode 7.
