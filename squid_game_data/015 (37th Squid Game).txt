Player 015 was a participant in the 37th Squid Game who joined out of desperation as a result of his business failing. He eventually became a part of Seong Gi-hun's faction, and joined the rebellion with him and his team in an attempt to escape.

Personality[]
"I'll fight them with you."
Player 015 volunteering to join the revolt in "Friend or Foe"
Player 015 is a kind, empathetic, loyal, and courageous man. He is willing to put himself at risk for the sake of his family,  voting O in the hope of paying off his debts and securing a better future for them.
He is also shown to have great empathy, as he was the only O voter who explained himself and shared his reasons with Young-mi while she was crying, trying to comfort her, saying he needed at least half of what he owed to get out. He showed deep remorse for having voted O, as he did not shout “one more game” and changed his vote back to X in the very next voting despite his share of the prize not being overly enough but was half of his debt, keeping his word to Young-mi.
He also proved to be loyal and brave by volunteering for the revolt in the hope of putting an end to the games.

History[]
Player 015 accumulated a debt of more than ₩500 million after his business failed. This put him and his family in a desperate state, which led him to enter the Squid Games in 2024.
Player 015 appeared in "001" as an icon, and then appeared as an error in the second game and survived it off-screen. He first physically appeared changing his vote from "X" to "O" in the second round. Hearing Kim Young-mi begging the players to vote "X", he explained the reason he has to continue playing, though he seemed to be genuinely sympathetic towards her.
In "O X", Player 015 managed to reach the fifth round of Mingle, in which the number was 2. Everyone around him formed team and he was left alone while Park Min-su was left alone after Thanos teamed up with Nam-gyu, fortunately Player 015 and Min-su saw each other, then quickly formed grasped each other and ran, he and Min-su managed to reach a room and save themselves from elimination.
In the third vote, Player 015 changed his vote back to "X" and survived the Special Game even after being attacked by Player 048 and Player 344. He later volunteered to join Gi-hun's revolt team.

Fate[]
After Gi-hun and Jung-bae found their way to the control rooms, he advised Hwang In-ho to collect the magazines from the dead soldiers along the path leading to the control room. In-ho brought him and Kwon Byeong-su after the two volunteered to go with him. Along the way, while Player 015 and Byeong-su were both standing in front of Young-il flanking his left and right, he was betrayed and shot in the chest by Oh Young-il, who was actually Hwang In-ho using an alias. He survived the shot, which gave In-ho the opportunity to use his choking sounds to fake his own death. He then shoots Player 015 in the chest again, killing him for good, which made Gi-hun think that In-ho was the one who had gotten killed. In-ho then told the guards to "wrap things up", throwing the walkie-talkie on Player 015's corpse. His body was then hung along with the other rebels, and the manager taken hostage by the Revolt team (wearing the Player 001 tracksuit) to show the consequences of attempting to take down the games.

Trivia[]
Both him and his 33rd Squid Games counterpart were eliminated during an interlude by another player, with the latter being killed in her respective game's Special Game.
During the second vote, he was told by Hwang In-ho that continuing playing might lead to his death, which won't help his family. Curiously, he was killed by In-ho after voting to end the game.
This would make sense if one counts the Revolt as a deliberate part of the game, as it counts as part of the interlude. So technically, 015 was "continuing to play" the game, which lead to his death.
Aside from Cho Hyun-ju, Park Jung-bae, Se-mi, Player 054 and Park Min-su, Player 015 was the only player who had voted O that did not chant one more game during the second vote.
He is shown to be both sympathetic and pragmatic, as he voted O in the second vote for money, but also genuinely felt bad when Young-mi began to cry due to the O's winning the vote.
Out of the four additional rebellion volunteers (072, 206, 324), he is the only one who did not participate in the bathroom fight.
Player 015 was actually trying to warn Gi-hun over the radio when In-ho fakes his death.
He was the only minor character in the rebellion that did not participate in the bathroom fight.
He is the only rebel, who wasn't a main character, to make an appearance using his canon actor in Mingle.
He is the last player is the 01X range to be eliminated in the 37th Squid Game.
If we count the events and errors, all players in the X15 died in each game of season 2. Player 115 died in Red Light, Green Light. Then, Player 415 was eliminated in Six-Legged Pentathlon after that Player 215 was eliminated in Mingle. Player 315 died in Red Light, Green Light, but later appeared in the Special Game as an error, and lastly, Player 015 died in the rebellion.
This makes him the last player in the X15's range to be eliminated.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 015 was primarily portrayed by Lee Ha-nee, and Han Dong-ui in the Six-Legged Pentathlon. He is credited as "선한남" (seonhannam), a title that literally translates into English as "good man" or "kind man".[3] This refers to his kind gestures and characteristics in the series, such as telling Young-mi why he voted O and volunteering in the Revolt.
