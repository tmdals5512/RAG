Lee Han-sol (born March 22, 1988) is the actor who portrayed Player 324 in Squid Game (TV series). He has social networks and most of time in summers, he goes to the beach. He portrayed many minor characters in movies. He acted as a Sodo Member in Hellbound and a dormitory staff in Move to Heaven. He is also known as supervising some action parts in movies.
