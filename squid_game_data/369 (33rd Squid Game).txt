Park Ju-un (박주운 Bag Ju-un?), also known as Player 369, was a participant in the 33rd Squid Game. He survived until the second game, where he became the first person to be eliminated in Sugar Honeycombs.

History[]
Park Ju-un was ₩900,000,000 in debt and chose to enter the Squid Games to win money, being picked up and sedated by Squid Game staff and taken to the island where the games take place. When the staff projected footage of players playing ddakji and being recruited to the games, he was among those whose name and debt amount were read aloud to the crowd.
Ju-un managed to survive the first game, Red Light, Green Light, while many of the other contestants were wiped out. Afterwards, like everyone else, he was shaken by deaths of the contestants and confronted the Squid Game staff for killing the other players, stating that killing people cannot be called a game. When a vote was held to decide if the game should continue, he voted no, telling Jung Min-tae that they should go home when he said that they should continue the game. However, when the games resumed after being terminated, Ju-un returned, along with most of the other Players.

Fate[]
Wait... Give me one... Please, just one more--
Park Ju-un's last words in "The Man with the Umbrella"
During the second game, Sugar Honeycombs, Ju-un chose an umbrella at the start of the game and had to cut the shape out of a piece of honeycomb. He was eliminated when he broke his piece of dalgona and begged for mercy, asking for one last chance. He was shot in the head by a guard instantly killing him as he fell backwards from the top of the slide, leaving a trail of blood behind him. His death set off a wave of other eliminations that occurred shortly after.

Trivia[]
He was the first player eliminated in Sugar Honeycombs.
When he breaks his umbrella, the camera is positioned in a way where the viewer can initially mistake him to be Gi-hun, which is likely intentional.
He is one of few players of season one to have an icon consistent with the character.
Ju-un would've had to survive Tug of War and be among the last 40 players in order for his debt to be paid off.
He is one of the only players to have a correct icon that wasn't in the later part of the games.
In the Czech Dub, Manager mistookly says 'Player 169' instead of 'Player 369'
Mistakes[]
Another Player 369 was seen dead in the Red Light, Green Light game. You can see his body near the doors. Even though he dies in Dalgona, the second game.
Behind the scenes[]
Park Ju-un was portrayed by Yoon Seung-hoon and an unknown extra. In the Czech dub, he was voiced by Rudolf Kubík.
