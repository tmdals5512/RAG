Biseokchigi, also known as Flying Stone, is a traditional Korean game featured as the second mini-game in the Six-Legged Pentathlon in Squid Game: The Challenge Season 2.

Game Information[]
Objective: The player in charge of this mini-game must use a stone given by the guard to hit a small tombstone marked with three shapes by throwing it. Once the tombstone is knocked, the team passes this mini-game. 
The tombstone is placed 3-4 meters away from the player, and player must not cross the marked line. If the player misses the throw or cross the line, the team will fail and have to retrieve the stone back, losing precious time.
The rule about stepping over the line has been removed.
