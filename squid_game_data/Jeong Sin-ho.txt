Jeong Sin-ho (정신호 Jeong Sin-ho?) is the actress, who portrayed Player 361 during Dalgona, Food queue and Special Game, Player 222 during Food queue, Player 332 during Red Light, Green Light and icons of: Player 051, Player 148, Player 163, Player 291, Player 342 and Player 378  in Season 1 of the Netflix TV series Squid Game.
