Baek Seong-hyeong (백성형 Baeg Seong-hyeong?) is the name of one of the men who participated in the search for The recruiter.
He attended a briefing at the Sunshine Capital headquarters where Choi Woo-seok and Kim Jeong-rae told all the men about the salesman.   
On July 8, 2024, he and his partner Seo Jae-won searched Hangangjin Station for the recruiter, but were unsuccessful in doing so.
