History[]
Player 222 was in heavy debt, so she decided to enter the Squid Games. She woke up in a large room with 455 other players. She then signed her contract, took her picture, and was taken to the first game called Red Light, Green Light. She managed to pass the first game, and like everyone else, she was shocked by the deaths of the other players. During the voting, she voted to continue the games, but the majority voted to end them. Nevertheless, she returned alongside 186 other players. She managed to pass the second game, called Sugar Honeycombs. She would choose an unknown shape and be able to carve it out. Once back at the dormitory, she would watch Jang Deok-su beat Player 271 to death, she would go to bed in fear and would wake up from the sounds of screaming by Player 198.

Fate[]
Player 222 would then be either beaten or stabbed to death by a unknown player, her elimination was announced after the Special Game, Despite last appearing physically before Sugar Honeycombs. 

Trivia[]
She shares her number with Kim Jun-hee, one of the protagonists of Seasons 2 - 3.
That number is also shared with her baby, the winner of the 37th Squid Game.
Coincidentally, all of them happen to be females. Player 222 in the 1st Season happens to be more elderly than Jun-hee.
Despite being announced as a special game death, she last appeared before Sugar Honeycombs, in the food queue.
Mistakes[]
A male Player 222 is seen watching Jang Deok-su and Kang Sae-byeok in the lobby.
Another Player 222 is seen in the food queue before Sugar Honeycombs.
Her faulty icon is the same as Player 396's faulty icon.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 222 was portrayed by Jeon Yang-min, Jeong Sin-ho, Lee Eun-suk and two unknown extras.
