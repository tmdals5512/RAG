Jo Yu-ri (조유리 Jo Yu-ri?), anglicized as Jo Yuri, is a solo-singer and actress who portrayed Kim Jun-hee/Player 222 in Season 2 and Season 3 of the Netflix TV series Squid Game.[1]

Background[]
Jo Yu-ri was known as a former member of the South Korean–Japanese girl group IZ*ONE. She first gained prominence after participating in Mnet's reality competition series Produce 48 in 2018, where she finished in third place and debuted with Iz*One on October 29, 2018. 
Following IZ*ONE's disbandment in April 2021, Yu-ri embarked on a successful solo career, releasing her debut single album GLASSY in October 2021, which garnered her several nominations for Rookie of the Year at major music awards. In addition to her music career, Yu-ri has ventured into acting, making her debut in the web series Mimicus (2022). 
Around 2025, Jo Yu-ri has gained public backlash for attending the W Korea event that was supposedly about spreading awareness of breast cancer.
