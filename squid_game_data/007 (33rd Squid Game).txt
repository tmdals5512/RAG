History[]
Player 007 was presumably in heavy debt and chose to enter the Squid Games after being approached by The Recruiter and playing Ddakji with him. After waking up in a bed in the Dormitory, she was most likely confused at first like the other 455 Players, but she most likely was willing to play once she saw the Piggy bank where her Prize money would be stored shown off by the Manager, leading to her signing the Player consent form before she took her Icon photo, headed up the Stairs and to the Red Light, Green Light game arena.
In the game, she managed to stay still, as other players panicked and ran to the doors. After the mass panic that ensued, she managed to keep her balance and pass the game, stopping on all the red lights without moving, and approaching the red line on every green light without running out of time.
After the first game, she, as most of the Players was most likely very down, knowing the risk that the Games brought. Though, most Players are relieved as Cho Sang-woo recalls the third clause of the consent form which allows the Players to partake in a vote to continue, or to stop the Games
After the Prize money accumulated from the eliminations in Red Light, Green Light is shown inside of the Piggy bank, the voting commenced. She (after having seen the Prize Money voted to continue the games as one of the last Players to vote, willing to risk her life to pay off her debts. After a few more votes, it only came down to one man, Oh Il-nam he voted to end the games, putting a stop to the games. Though, despite voting to continue, Player 007 was happy about his decision.
A certain amount of days after the Games had been terminated, she received an invitation to join once again after returning to the outside world, and after most likely considering it for some time, she decided to rejoin, calling the number on the card once again.
After returning, she woke up in the Dormitory and ate her Food provided by the Guards she walked into the second game arena, Sugar Honeycombs, where she chose her shape, and after witnessing the death of Park Ju-un, she started to panic, but like the other Players in the arena, attempted to stay as calm as possible while carving out her Dalgona.

Fate[]
It is unknown when she died. Her icon fades in  icon disappears in Red Light, Green Light but there is no corpse to be seen and she is seen at the finish line, she is last seen in before the Special Game talking to Na Jo-eun, Player 201 and Player 307.

Trivia[]
Player 007 has the same number as one of the main characters in Season 2 and 3.
She and Jo Su-jin (Player 005), are the only females in the 33rd Squid Game with single-digit numbers.
