Kim Cha-i (김차이 Gim Cha-i?) is the stuntwoman who portrayed Player 087,  Player 153 and Player 215 during Red Light, Green Light in Season 1, Player 292 during the Special Game and Number 010 during the Rebellion in Season 2, and Kang No-eul's stunt-double in Season 3 of the Netflix show Squid Game.
