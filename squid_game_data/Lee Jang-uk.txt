Lee Jang-uk (이장욱 I Jang-ug?) is a man who was recruited for the search for the Salesman as part of Sunshine Capital. A selfie of him appears in "Bread and Lottery".
In 2024, Jang-uk attended a meeting at Sunshine Capital's headquarters, where Mr. Kim and Choi Woo-seok briefed them on the details of the search, which covered the entirety of the Seoul Metropolitan Subway. Per its guidelines, he took a photo of himself and his partner at Yongdu Station on June 22, which was received by Seong Gi-hun.
