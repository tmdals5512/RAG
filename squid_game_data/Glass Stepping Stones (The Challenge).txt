Glass Stepping Stones was the fifth game played in The First Season of Squid Game: The Challenge. It is a non-lethal version of the version played in the 33rd Squid Game.
The game featured a glass bridge containing 17 pairs of glass tiles. Each pair of tiles has one of two types of glass: one type was safe to stand on, the other was not, and would drop the player - indicating elimination. Each player received and wore a vest with a unique number ranging from 1 to 20, deciding the order they cross the bridge.
Players must cross the bridge in order within the 30 minute time limit. Any player which guesses a wrong tile, will be eliminated. Any player that remains on the bridge after the time limit elapses, will be eliminated.

Order assignment[]
Players were led into the White Room and instructed to select a teddy bear from a claw machine. Each bear was associated with a hidden specific number, which they then needed to assign to another player, establishing the sequence in which the contestants would participate in the game.
