Audrey Huynh (오드리 후인 Odeuli Hu-in?) is the Korean, Chinese, Vietnamese, and European voice actor, who dubbed Kim Jun-hee in the English dub in Season 2 and Season 3 of the Netflix TV series Squid Game.
