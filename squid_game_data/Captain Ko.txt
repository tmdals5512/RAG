History[]
And that's not all. You know Captain Ko from the other boat? He's supposedly this Moojin native, right? That's what Park told everyone. So I started asking around, and no one knows him either. My friend's from here, and he said he's never heard of the guy. It's like he doesn't even exist.
Choi Woo-seok to Hwang Jun-ho about Captain Ko in "It's Not Your Fault"
There is practically no information on Ko prior to him joining the search team. He was likely a member of The Pink Guards who eventually got promoted to Manager, as Captain Park was too. He was however part of The Fishing Group where he went fishing with Captain Park which included other Squid Game Staff members like The Recruiter and The Front Man.

Ko is recruited as the leader of Team 2 in the group led by Hwang Jun-ho which try to find where the Squid Games are located. Captain Ko (commanding a boat known as New World) appears to have known Captain Park for quite a while and is a good friend of his, Park describes Ko as a local that knows the area around the Squid Game Island "inside out". There's not much focus on Team 2 during the series so we don't exactly know what happens on his boat but it's implied that like Captain Park, Ko was misleading Team 2 the whole time.Captain Ko: This is Boat Two. Everyone's taken care of.
In-ho: And Boat One?

Captain Ko: It seems like... they got Captain Park.
Captain Ko to Hwang In-ho after killing Boat Two Mercenaries in "222"During Choi Woo-seok's investigation of Captain Park he discovers that his claim of Ko being a local was false as nobody in the area was even familiar with his name. Eventually Woo-seok is arrested by the police who inform Park that he broke into his house. Park now realizes that they've been exposed and he radios Ko, telling him they need to kill everyone on both boats. Ko's attack (while offscreen) was successful and he presumably took out all the mercenaries on Boat 2. Once he is done he goes back to the radio and tries to communicate with Park, but gets no response since Park was killed by Jun-ho. Realizing what has happened he contacts In-ho and informs him of the situation. That is the last which is seen of Ko, and his current status is unknown.
It is presumed that he fled and there has been no further news of him since he killed Team 2.
