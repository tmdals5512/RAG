Kim Hee-jin (김희진 Gim Hui-jin?) is the actress who portrayed Player 104 in Season 2 of the Netflix TV series Squid Game.

Trivia[]
She is married to Player 366's actor, Han Bong-soo.
They have notable scenes together during the filming of Season 2.
