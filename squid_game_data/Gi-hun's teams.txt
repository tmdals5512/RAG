Seong Gi-hun (Player 456) formed several teams with several other players during the 33rd and 37th Squid Games. Most of the team consists of members in the Tug of War, Six-Legged Pentathlon, Mingle, and Rebellion. He also formed a team with his loan sharks and mercs.

Pre Dalgona


Seong Gi-hun
Cho Sang-woo
Ali Abdul

During the first game, Gi-hun immediately spotted his childhood friend, Cho Sang-woo. During the game, Sang-woo helped Gi-hun by telling him Young-hee was a motion sensor, and to get behind a bigger player. At the end of the game, Ali Abdul prevented Gi-hun's death by grabbing onto his collar. The three of them are seen together in the dormitory in the silence.


Dalgona


Seong Gi-hun
Cho Sang-woo
Ali Abdul
Oh Il-nam
Kang Sae-byeok

After the games were temporarily halted and the players had returned, the three of them found each other. On top of that, the old man, Oh Il-nam, asked if he could join. He was accepted. The four of them briefly split up for dalgona, with the four of them taking one of the four shapes. Sang-woo took triangle, Ali took circle, Il-nam took star, and Gi-hun took umbrella.  Following Sang-woo, Kang Sae-byeok also chose triangle, after he got her assistance for figuring out the game. After the game, they reunited and all sat together during mealtime. Once it was clear the special game would occur. Gi-hun asked the loner, Sae-byeok, if she wanted to join them, as Jang Deok-su had it out for her. She agreed, and post special game, the five of them joined forces.

When instructed to form teams of ten, Sang-woo instructed them to all go find one person. First, Ali brought back the Filipino man, player 276. Next, Gi-hun asked the highly religious man, player 244, to join his team. Next, player 196 approached Sang-woo after Sang-woo rejected 069 and 070. Sang-woo agreed to him joining. Next Sae-byeok went up to Ji-yeong, asking her to join. As the nine of them sat down, Il-nam apoligised for not bringing back someone. Immediately after, Han Mi-nyeo barged in and joined them after being tossed out of Deok-su's team. The weak team managed only to win through the strategies of Il-nam and Sang-woo.


Post Tug-of-war


Seong Gi-hun
Cho Sang-woo
Kang Sae-byeok

Back in the dorms, the team continuously bickered, all having different views on what there team should do for the night. In the end, they settled for defense. When getting into duos for the fourth game, the team essentially fell apart. 244 and 196 split off, 276 went to find someone from another team, Mi-nyeo was essentially ignored, and the other six turned into duos. Once the game was over, Ali, Ji-yeong, Il-nam, 196, and 276, were all gone. Gi-hun's team was mostly just himself, and Sang-woo and Sae-byeok, though Gi-hun isolated himself from Sang-woo after he killed Jung-soo. Gi-hun tried to team up with Sae-byeok for the final game, but she was killed by Sang-woo. Gi-hun was then left with nobody after Sang-woo's death.


Gi-hun's Sharks and Mercs


Seong Gi-hun

In an attempt to find the recruiter, Gi-hun achieved alliance with Kim Jeong-rae, the loan shark who was threatening to kill him three years ago. Due to his immense amount of money, Jeong-rae, his right hand man, Woo-seok, and dozens of other sharks did everything he said. In the end, Jeong-rae and Woo-seok did find the recruiter, but he ended up killing the boss. When Hwang Jun-ho tried to arrest Gi-hun for the suspected murder of the recruiter, Woo-seok bashed him over the head with a fire extinguisher. Once Jun-ho and Gi-hun, and Woo-seok explained their situations, the three teamed up to find the island and get their individual vengeances. They three hired mercenary Kim, along with several other mercs. They also had the aid of Captains Park and Ko, two boat operators who were undercover, as they worked for the games. By the end of the show, team two had been completely massacred by Captain Ko. Team 1 was also almost entirely killed, with a soul survivor of Kim, only saved when Jun-ho put a spear in Caption Park's heart. Jun-ho and Kim discovered an injured Gyeong-seok, who told them where the island is. Kim then called the coast gaurd, leading to the island's explosion.


Pre Pentathlon


Seong Gi-hun
Park Jung-bae
Hwang In-ho
Kang Dae-ho

Once reinstated in the games, Gi-hun was soon discovered by Park Jung-bae, his old friend. Gi-hun aided him, along with all other players during the first game, and after the game, Gi-hun found himself part of a new large group, team X. Unfortunately, his team did not win the vote. During mealtime, Hwang In-ho, the front man of the games, attempted to put himself into Gi-hun's team, despite him being enraged at him. Kang Dae-ho also inserts himself into the team without being asked or recognized. During night time, In-ho had a fake heart to heart with Gi-hun, making Gi-hun alright with him joining. Before the second game, the players were instructed to make teams of five. With four of them already formed, they just needed one more.

For the first round of mingle, a group of ten players was required. Gi-hun turned to Cho Hyun-ju, who stated she had a team of four. After grabbing Seon-nyeo, the ten of them fled to room 44.
For the next round, a group of four players was required. Gi-hun tried to tell his team to go together, but In-ho beat him to it. Gi-hun went off with Jung-bae, Dae-ho, and Jun-hee, while In-ho teamed with three unknown players.
For the next round, a group of three players was required. Gi-hun and In-ho instructed the others to get a room together, with the two of them grabbing Jang Geum-ja.
For the next round, a group of six players was required. As Gi-hun and Hyun-ju's teams had merged, Gi-hun instructed all four women, and two men to get a room. Yong-sik stuck with his mother and Dae-ho volunteered. Unfortunately, in the commotion, Hyun-ju's best friend, Kim Young-mi did not make it. After the round is over, Gi-hun, In-ho, Jung-bae, and Hyun-ju's friend, Gyeong-seok managed to find a room with players 350 and 328.
For the final round, a group of two was required. Dae-ho grabbed Gi-hun, In-ho grabbed Jung-bae, Myung-gi grabbed Jun-hee, Gyeong-seok grabbed Hyun-ju, and Yong-sik grabbed Geum-ja. All players survived and the two teams formed a big group.

After the ambushers took over the dormitory, they asked for volunteers to take over. While most players stayed silent, Player 015, Player 206 and players 072 and 324 volunteered to fight. Yong-sik also tried to stand up, but Geum-ja stopped him. Once the fighting began, it didn't take long for 072 to be shot down. After that, Player 206 was surprised and shot multiple times in the chest. Gi-hun and Jung-bae went ahead into management, soon followed by In-ho, Byeong-su and Player 015, who took to the back. Running low on ammo, Dae-ho said he would go back to the dorms to get more, but once he did not come back, Hyun-ju went instead, leaving Gyeong-seok, 145, and 324. Byeong-su and Player 015 were about to shoot the managers from the back, but In-ho gunned Byeong-su down, and shot Player 015 in the chest. Using Player 015's bloody gurgles through the walkie-talkies, In-ho faked his death. Hyun-ju found that Dae-ho had cowered in the corner, so she set out, but was forced to duck down as guards came into the dorms. 324 tried to keep the soldiers away, but was shot in the shoulder. Knowing there was no way out for him, 324 blew his head off. 145 suggested he and Gyeong-seok surrender. 145 put his gun down, but was shot anyway. Gyeong-seok met the same fate, but lived (but that's another story). With no choice, Gi-hun and Jung-bae surrendered, with the front man shooting Jung-bae in the chest as an example for Gi-hun.

Jump Rope


Seong Gi-hun
Kim Jun-hee's baby

After the death of Nam-gyu, Gi-hun secured the baby to his chest using his and Jun-hee's jackets, and made his way across the bridge, promising to come back once the baby was safe. While he did make it across, due to 096's anticks, there was not enough time to go back for Jun-hee. Her fate was sealed, as she chose to fall from the platform. In the final game, both managed to outlive all the other finalists, but with the button not pushed, Gi-hun sacrificed himself, leaving the baby the soul survivor.
