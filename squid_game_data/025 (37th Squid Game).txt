History[]
Player 025 was presumably in massive debt and decided to join the Squid Games for a chance to win a huge cash prize. He called the number on the recruiting card, and got into a mysterious car. He then woke up in a giant room with 454 other players. He then made his way to the center of the room and watched as many other players debts were revealed in front of him. Later on, he signed a contract and made his way up a flight of stairs to the first game. 
During the first game, He heard Gi-hun screaming about how dangerous the games were. Player 025 and many other players ignored his warnings, and continued the game without any worry. However, He was soon terrified as players were shot in front of him during the mass panic. Player 025 luckily was able to control himself and stand still. He eventually completed the first game after listening to Gi-hun's commands. After returning to the dormitory with the other surviving players, Player 025 begged the guards to let him and the other players go. However, after a massive increase in the prize pool was revealed, Player 025 changed his mind and decided to vote O during the first vote. Him and the other Os then cheered after they won the vote.
Later during Six-Legged Pentathlon, Player 025 decided to partner up with players 022, 037, 208, and 385. He then watched as many teams passed and failed in front of him.

Fate[]
Player 025's icon disappears after Six-Legged Pentathlon, which means him and his team were eliminated in the game.

Mistakes[]
He finished the first game and there was no blood on his shoulder, but when he approached Gi-hun, there was blood on his shoulder. Most likely, this is a mistake.
