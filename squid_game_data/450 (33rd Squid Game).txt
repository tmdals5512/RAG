Player 451







Player 450 was in heavy debt and chose to enter the Squid Games to win money. After waking up in the dormitory with 455 other players, He signed a contract and took his photo and entered the first game called Red Light, Green Light. After hearing the game rules and moving during the first "green light," the deaths of players 324, 250, and 306 caused mass panic, yet player 450 remained still and then ran toward the finish line. Even while looking at the piggy bank, he voted "X" to end the games. After returning and having breakfast, he chose the Sugar honeycomb.

Fate[]
After hearing the rules of the game, Player 450 chose the star shape, but unfortunately broke his dalgona and was eliminated.
