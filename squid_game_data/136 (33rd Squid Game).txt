History[]
Before "Red Light, Green Light"[]
We know nothing about his life before joining the Squid Game. He most likely was in debt and joined after playing ddakji with The Recruiter.

"Red Light, Green Light" and Fate[]
Player 136 was taken by a van and transported to the island.  He never made a physical appearance. We only see his icon during the Red Light, Green Light panic, which disappears off-screen at some point in the first game, meaning that he died there.

Trivia[]
He never appeared in Squid Game.
