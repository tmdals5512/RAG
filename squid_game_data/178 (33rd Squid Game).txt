Player 179







Player 178 was in high debt and entered the squid games because of this. He woke up in a bed and was confused at 1st but once he saw the piggy bank he wanted to play. He signed his contract, took his photo and went to where red light, green light happens. He was shaken after seeing 3 people die but he did not run to the doors. He made progress through the whole round.

Death[]
Around the end of red light, green light, Player 178 accidentally moved and he got shot, killing him. The proof is that we see his icon has already disappeared.
