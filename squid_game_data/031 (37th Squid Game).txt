History[]
Not much is known about Player 031's early life, but she was a dedicated fan of Thanos. At some point in her life, she faced serious financial problems. Because of this, she made the decision to join the 37th Squid Game in hopes of winning the prize money. 
Player 031 is first seen standing next to Lee Myung-gi (Player 333) when the contestants wake up inside the Dormitory. Later scene she was seen looking up at the piggy bank as it was being lowered, before eventually signing the consent form. Once all contestants were brought out of the initial room, Player 031 was one of the 10 contestants who tried to take a photo with Thanos, even though it was against the rules. Thanos promised her a photo once the games were over and their phones were returned, which made her excited. She eventually had her photo taken and then headed to the first game.
Player 031 was seen walking into the first game and later having a conversation with Player 188. During the first game, Red Light, Green Light, Player 031 manages to survive by most likely following Seong Gi-hun's advice of hiding behind larger players to reduce her chances of being detected by Young-hee. 
After the first game ended, Player 031 was most likely shocked and shaken by the deaths she witnessed. Later the players were then shown the prize money that started filling up the piggy bank above them, following that a vote was held to determine if the players would like to continue or end the games. When given the chance to vote on whether to continue or end the games, Player 031 voted to end the games. Unfortunately, the majority of players decided to continue leaving her trapped in the games. Since she voted to end the games she became a member of Team X. 
In the second game, Six-Legged Pentathlon, Player 031 joined a team with Player 144, Player 420, Player 432, and Player 439. Her team is placed against Cho Hyun-ju's team. She plays Ddakji for the team and successfully passed the game, they were seen cheering at the finish line. However, they are not seen again.

Fate[]
Despite her team passing the second game, she was not seen again meaning that she and her teammates' passing was retconned, so they were actually shot to death instead for not completing or surviving the game in the time. 

Trivia[]
Player 031 was one of the two Thanos's fans were eliminated during Six-Legged Pentathlon alongside Player 004.
Her team was the only one to have a production error passing the second game due to their actors weren't present after their characters were supposedly to survive.
She was the second female Thanos fan and the fourth overall Thanos fan to be eliminated.
She is one of the players that noticed Gi-hun was trying to remove his tooth.
She was one of the first women to successfully complete Ddakji in the Six-Legged Pentathlon, the other being Kim Young-mi.
Additionally she is also the first female on Team X to be eliminated on the right track in Six-Legged Pentathlon.
Similar to Player 310, her hair is up when she is taking her photo, but in her actual icon, it is down. 310 is the reverse of this however.
