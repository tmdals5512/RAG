This unnamed man is the greedy and exploitative owner of a factory where Ali Abdul was employed at. He does not pay his employees, despite their months of hard work.

History[]
One day, when he is in his office on his computer playing a game, one of his employees, Ali Abdul, barges in, desperately telling him to pay him for the six months of hard work he did. The factory owner refuses to pay him, saying that the business is not doing well. However, Ali notices the money that he is carrying in his pocket so he rushes into him, and the factory owner refuses. The dispute becomes physical, and one thing leads to another. Soon, the factory owner's right hand gets stuck and shredded in a machine, crushing his fingers. As he screams in agony, this allows Ali the opportunity to steal the money and run away. He either bled out or was saved by his employees, even though he is most likely alive. The factory owner is never seen again. 

Behind the scenes[]
The unnamed factory owner was portrayed by actor Kim Seo-ha.
