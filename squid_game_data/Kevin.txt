Personality[]
Kevin is excited to be a VIP for the games, as shown by his attitude while eliminating the Hide and Seek players with the other VIPs. Like all the other VIPs, Kevin clearly views the game solely as entertainment and does not take the players' lives seriously. For example, during the vote after Hide and Seek, Kevin mocked Jang Geum-ja's speech to the O voters by saying it almost made him cry.
