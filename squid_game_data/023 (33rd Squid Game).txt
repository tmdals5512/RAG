History[]
Player 023 was in a heavy debt and chose to enter the Squid Games to win money. After waking up in a large room with 455 other players, he signed a contract and got his photo taken. After the death of Player 324, 250 and Player 306, it caused chaos, he managed to hold still and survived the initial chaos. He even survived Red Light, Green Light. After the first game, Like everyone else, he was shaken by the deaths of the contestants. When the prize money was finally revealed, a vote was held if the game should continue. He possibly voted to discontinue the games but the majority voted X. However, those who were willing to return could, he risked by returning to the games along with 186 players. He then proceeded to Sugar Honeycombs. 

Fate[]
Player 023 broke his piece of dalgona which made him eliminated, likely because people were getting killed around him, leading him to panic and break the dalgona.
