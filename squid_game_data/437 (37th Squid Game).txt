History[]
Player 437 was a man who was in debt and decided to join the Squid Games to pay it off. He woke up in a dormitory with 454 other contestants and signed a form and then made his way to red light green light. He managed to stay still during the panic and crossed the finish line thanks to Gi-hun's advice. He voted X in the first vote hoping to terminate the games but the Os won the vote and he made his way to the second game.

Fate[]
He and his team were shot for failing to complete all 5 minigames and crossing the finish line in time.
