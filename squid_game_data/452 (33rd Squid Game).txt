History[]
Player 452 was in heavy debt and decided to enter a high-risk competition to win money so they can pay off those debts. After waking up in the dormitory along with 455 other people, they signed a contract, took their photo, and was taken to the arena of the first game of Red Light, Green Light.  
They would survive after over half the contestants were eliminated. Like everyone else, they were shaken by the deaths of the majority. After the prize money in the piggy bank, a vote was held on whatever the games should continue or not. They voted to continue the games with 100 other players, but the majority voted to leave by one extra player, so they were sent back along with everyone else. They eventually returned when offered the chance. After eating their food, they would make their way to the second game and choose an unknown shape.
