Cho Ah-ra (조아라 Jo A-la?) is the actress and model and stuntwoman, who portrayed Player 429 in Six-Legged Pentathlon, Player 163 in Jump Rope and unknown guard in Season 2 and Season 3 of the Netflix TV series Squid Game.
