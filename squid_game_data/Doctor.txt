History[]
When Oh Mal-soon passed out, she was brought to the hospital where the doctor worked, and her son Seong Gi-hun was called in. The doctor diagnosed her with type 2 diabetes, and stated that she must have been in much pain. He advised that she stay in the hospital and said that in a worse case scenario, she would have to have her leg amputated. However, due to money issues, Mal-soon decided to leave the hospital, in fear of having more debt.

Trivia[]
The doctor claimed that Mal-soon was in pain due to her diabetes. This could be an inaccuracy, as the diabetes would have damaged her nerves, thus preventing her from feeling pain in her leg.
