History
Player 124 was in heavy debt and decided to join the Squid Games to win money. He woke up in a room with 455 other players, after being introduced, he signed a contract and took his player photo. After that, he was led to the first game, Red Light, Green Light.

Fate
Following the eliminations of 3 other players, a mass scare kicked off and players started running back to the doors, Player 124 along with Player 333 was also running to the doors and was shot.

Trivia
He has the same player number as one of the main characters from Season 2 and 3.
Mistakes
He's portrayed by a man Played with black hair in the lobby, but the man with grey hair is much more prominent.
