Kang Sae-byeok to Seong Gi-hun in "Front Man"The Final Dinner was an interlude game that took place between Glass Stepping Stones and Squid Game taking place in the dormitory.
3 players participated in this game. 1 player (around 33.3%) was eliminated and 2 players (around 66.7%) progressed to the next round. At this point in the competition, 454 players in total were eliminated.
The only elimination was Kang Sae-byeok.
This game took place between June 29-30, 2020.

Trivia[]
Seong Gi-hun initially was going to kill Cho Sang-woo, but Kang Sae-byeok prevented him by using her words.
Kang Sae-byeok is the only female player who participated and died in this game.
