History[]
Player 439 was most likely in huge debt and joined the Squid Games to pay it off. He woke up in a room with 454 other players and made his way to the first game, Red Light, Green Light. He survived the game thanks to Seong Gi-hun's advice and voted X in the first vote out of fear. However the Os won the vote and he made his way to the next game.

Fate[]
Even though he and his team were seen cheering because they passed the second game, they were still eliminated for not crossing the finish line in time.

Behind the scenes[]
Player 439 was portrayed by Jang Chang-kuk and Park Seok-hyun.
