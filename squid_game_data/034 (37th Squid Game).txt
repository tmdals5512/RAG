History[]
Player 034 was in heavy debt and chose to enter the Squid Games to win money. After waking up in a large room with 454 other players, signed a contract and took his photo.
Later, everyone was taken to an open area with a creepy robot doll at the front. It was revealed that the game was Red Light, Green Light. When the doll turned around, everyone ran forward, There was a bee on Kang Mi-na, Thanos pointed it out and she died, which caused a massive killing spree but Player 034 survived. He managed to pass Six-Legged Pentathlon along with Players 069, 132, 146 and 157.

Fate[]
For the third game, Mingle, he was shot because he couldn't get into the room in time at the 2nd round.

Mistakes[]
Despite dying in Round 2, he is mistakenly seen after Round 4.
Player 034 is announced eliminated after Round 3.
Behind the scenes[]
