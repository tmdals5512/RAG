History[]
Nothing is known about his background, except that he had two unknown jobs in his working career and that something happened involving him on October 2, 1994. He was likely in tremendous debt and decided to join the Squid Games to win the cash prize. After waking up in a large room with 455 other players, he signed a contract, took his photo, and entered the first game's arena.

Fate[]
After the first three deaths, a panic broke out, and Player 008 was one of the many players to run to the doors, resulting in him getting shot by one of the snipers. 

Trivia[]
He is the second 00X player to die placing 333rd.
On his player file, it looks like he has a family member on the third row of the Family part of his file so, there would be 2 unnamed family members of him.
Mistakes[]
He is seen multiple times after Red Light, Green Light.
Another Player 008 is seen after taking his photo.
His icon is the same as Player 281.
Another Player 008 was seen before the Special Game.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 008 was portrayed by Kang Hak-soo, Shin Seok-yong, Lee Jeong-hoon, and 4 unknown extras.
