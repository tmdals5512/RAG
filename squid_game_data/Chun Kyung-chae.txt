Chun Kyung-chae(천경채 Cheon Gyeong-chae?) is the actor and stuntman who portrayed Park Tae-jun (Player 229), Player 399, Player 077 in the dormitory before Red Light, Green Light, Player 373 in Red Light, Green Light, Player 074 in Sugar Honeycombs, Player 230 in Tug of War, Player 062 after Tug of War, Player 085 before Marbles, Player 413 in Marbles, Player 183 after Sugar Honeycombs and the icons of Player 076 and Player 354 in Season 1 of the Netflix TV series Squid Game.
