History[]
Player 005 was presumably in massive debt and decided to join the Squid Games for a chance to win a huge cash prize. She called the number on the recruiting card and got into a mysterious car. She then woke up in a giant room with 454 other players. She was likely confused and decided to stay in her bed, where she watched as many other players' debts were revealed in front of her. Later on, she listened to the manager explain the rules of the games, she signed a contract along with the other players, she took her photo in the photo booth, and she made her way up a flight of stairs to the first game.
During Red Light, Green Light, Player 005 and the other players watched as a strange man started screaming about how dangerous the games were. Player 005 and the other players ignored Gi-Hun's warnings, as they thought he was crazy and wanted the money for himself. Player 005 and the other players then continued the game without any worry. However, after the sounds of gunshots ran out, and players started getting gunned down, Player 005 made a run for the doors in fear for her life.

Fate[]
During her attempts to run towards the doors, Player 005 was shot dead by a sniper for breaking the rules of the game.

Behind the scenes[]
Player 005 was portrayed by 2 unknown extras.

Trivia[]
Player 005 was the first female to join the 37th Squid Game, just like her 33rd Squid Game counterpart.
She is the first female player with a single digit in her number to be eliminated from the 37th Squid Game.
