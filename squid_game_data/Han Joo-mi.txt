Han Ju-mi  (한주미 Han Joo-mi?) is the actress who portrayed Player 184 in Season 2 of the Netflix TV series Squid Game.
Ju-Mi, born January 1, 1998, is a multilingual singer, songwriter, and guitarist who speaks Korean, English, and Chinese. Best known by her English name, Nicole E., she shares her music across various platforms, including TikTok, Instagram, and Spotify. As a dedicated songwriter, she is known for spending long hours — sometimes staying up late as mentioned on her Instagram — to create original songs. In addition to her own compositions, she frequently performs guitar covers of popular tracks.

Trivia[]
Her most popular song on Spotify is titled, "Pumpkin Heart" featured in one of her single albums.
