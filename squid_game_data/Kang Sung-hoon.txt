Kang Sung-hoon (강성훈 Gang Seong-hun?), also known as Kang Poong (강풍 Gang Pung?), is the South Korean stuntman who portrayed Player 154 running to the doors in the episode "Red Light, Green Light" in Squid Game Season 1, and also portrayed Player 273 during Six Legs in Squid Game Season 2.
