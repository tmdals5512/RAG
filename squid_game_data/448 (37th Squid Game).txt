Personality[]
Player 448 was a timid and cowardly woman. Despite voting X in the first three votes she unwillingly changed her vote O after the Special Game and failed rebellion. Out of fear of Team O, since most of Team X had been severely outnumbered by the O Players while feeling intimidated by Kim Yeong-sam when she voted. She was seen with a sad and disgusted expression on her face after Hide and Seek, clearly feeling guilty when Jang Geum-ja pleaded Team O to stop the games, as she turned her face away. Showing that she knew that she did morally the wrong thing changing her vote especially since Kim Jun-hee's baby was now at risk in the games.     
This shows that she never wanted to vote O and always wanted to leave the games, proven by the fact she voted X in all the other votes, showing she valued life over money. Despite all this she still lets her fear overpower her when she unwillingly chose to vote O for a second and final time instead of making her own decision and finding the courage to stick with her X vote. As just like Player 039 she likely didn't think through or realize giving in to fear and voting for O unwillingly didn't make a difference. Since aligning with them didn't mean protection from its most violent and greedy members. Something that she faced in Jump Rope when Player 096 blocked the bridge, planning on letting all Players die, which tragically led to her death.   

History[]
Player 448 was a timid young woman in heavy debt, and decided to join the Squid Games. After waking up in a room with 454 other players, she signed the player consent form and took her photo, officially signing her up for the games.
After listening to Gi-Hun's advice of staying still, she was able to cross the finish line and survive the first game. Afterwards, she could be seen be seen begging for forgiveness from the guards in the dormitory, wanting to leave. Later on in the voting, she voted "X" to terminate the games, wanting to go back to her normal, but broke life. However, because of the O team winning the vote, she was forced to stay.
In Six-Legged Pentathlon, Player 448 was able to form her team consisting of 4 unknown players. Her team the managed to complete all mini-games in the required time. In the voting, she voted "X" again,
Player 448 was also able to survive all five rounds of Mingle, despite the difficulty and horror in the game. Afterwards, she decided to vote to terminate the games for a third time. However, due to the tie, she was forced to stay on the island once again. 
After the Bathroom fight incident, each team formed their own headcount to see how many players died on their sides. Player 448, hearing that the X team was in the lead, appeared relieved after hearing that she had a chance to leave the games. However, due to Gi-Hun not telling most of the X team about Lights Out, many players of the X team were murdered for their vote. However, she manages to survive. In the re-vote, Player 448 voted first because Gi-hun didn't vote. 448 switched her vote from X to O most likely because of her fear from being attacked by the O’s again or, knowing that O will win the vote anyways she also likely didn't realise voting O unwillingly wasn't going to mean she would be safe from Team O.  
In the fourth game, Hide and Seek. Player 448 got a blue ball from the Gumball Machine placing her on the blue team. After all the players recieved their boxes, she watched in fear as Nam-gyu revealed the Seekers get daggers sharp enough to easily slice his finger tip while Hiders get a key on a chain. She made her way into the game arena and runs off into the maze. After some time she encounters Player 202  attacking Cho Hyun-ju. Player 448 immediately runs away upon seeing this. She can be heard screaming when Park Yong-sik finds Player 009’s body since she was being chased by a Seeker, presumably Player 442.  She gets away from him and successfully survives the rest of the game, passing with no visual injuries. She then is seen entering the Dormitory, during Jang Geum-ja's pleas to stop playing during the vote. She is seen looking away, showing that she feels guilty and bad for voting O, but her fear overpowers her once again and she votes to continue the games unwillingly for the second and final time.   

Fate[]
When in Jump Rope, stands near Player 260 and Player 349, looking in fear at the game watching as Player 096 blocks the bridge trapping the players. After Gi-hun gets Player 096 to move out the way. She goes onto the bridge as part of the final group. Once 096 picks up his interruptions, player 448 is forced to not move, leading to her losing her balance on the edge of the first sector of the bridge. She attempts to catch herself to no avail, being hit by the rope, screaming and falling to her death. Her body was presumably incinerated after the game.

Trivia[]
She was the first woman to vote X in Season 2 and the first woman to vote O in Season 3
She was the last woman to vote O in Season 3 and also the last woman to vote in the whole competition.
Excluding Jun-hee and her baby, she is the highest placing female in the 37th Squid Game.
She was the last female member of Team O to be eliminated in 37th Squid Game she was however an unwilling O voter. (The last willing female O voter was Player 163.)
Taking into account that she never wanted to vote O she was the second to last female X voter to die.
She is the highest placing contestant in the 37th Squid Game whose player number includes the number 8, and the only O voter in Jump Rope to include that same number.
A board from a behind the scenes photo that shows players who were supposed to die in Jump Rope, lacks the number 448, which possibly means that Player 448 was originally not supposed to make it to the fifth game, and was to be killed in the fourth.
If we slow down the second vote, her vote is an O, which is most likely a mistake because she’s seen with a X patch later.
She is most likely left handed.
She is the second-highest player number to survive Season 2, behind 456, and the second-highest surviving number in the competition overall.
She is also the last player to have their number in the 400s range, excluding Gi-hun.
Her number is also the closest to Gi-hun in the end of Season 2 and Season 3.
She made it the furthest out of any other female on the Jump Rope bridge, dying just before the gap.
She was the only female member of Team O albeit unwilling to survive Hide and Seek as a member of the Hiders team so all willing female O voters died in Hide and Seek. (Except Player 163 who was a Seeker.)
She is the only female hider to survive who was a minor player.
Judging by the blood splattered on her face and clothes after the Special Game, she was either injured by an O or an X died in front of her, causing their blood to splatter all over her face or she tried to fight back against an O and manage to attack them or she assisted in the kill of an O.
She is the last background female character to die
Player 448 voted O in fear of being attacked or killed by an O twice. Ironically like other unwilling O voters who were part of Team X Players 039, 172 and 191 it was for nothing. In Jump Rope she and the other players were betrayed by Player 096 who planned on killing all remaining players by blocking the bridge anyway which lead to her death.
After the revote when 448 changed her vote in fear. It seemed to have made her distant from her Team X allies as a consequence as none of Team X are seen again with her after the revote including Player 349 who she appeared to have a close friendship with, which left her mostly on her own as Team O were more dangerous to align with.
Remaining members of Team X were likely disappointed in her cowardness when she changed her vote along with other unwilling O voters and didn't want to be associated with them anymore, judging by their expressions which included Yong-sik and Hyun-ju as well as minor X voters such as Players 359 and 209.
Player 448 was the only unwilling female O voter.
She is the last player in the 44X range to be eliminated in the 37th Squid Game.
If we count extras, she is the only recurrent character that survived Hide and Seek.
She is the only woman in the 400s range to survive Hide and Seek, as both Player 400 and 440 died.
Since Player 448 placed 13th in the competition, her 33rd Squid Game counterpart is Player 021
Her voting pattern is completely unique to her.
If she hadn't given into fear, she would be the player in the 400 range with the most votes in X.
She is the last minor player in the 400 range and the last female player overall to have their vote shown.
Out of all female players, Player 448 has the most voting scenes. With 4 voting scenes, she has more voting scenes than most of the main characters.
In the aftermath of Hide and Seek, if Player 448 along with Player 039 another unwilling O voter who survived Hide and Seek. Along with Players 260 and 435 who judging by their expressions also didn't want to play anymore all had the courage to change their votes. This would've been the result 12 (X) and 12 (O) another tie and if Gi-hun voted it would've been 13 (X) and 12 (O). This did suggest if players had made their own decisions and not give into fear Team X could've won the 4th round of voting.
