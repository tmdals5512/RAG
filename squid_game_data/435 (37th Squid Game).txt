Personality[]
During Season 2, he was presumably very greedy and seemed to have no problem voting for O and is even shown to have a possibly greedy smirk. While voting O in the first vote as well as the next two votes. Although despite this, he is however not cruel or violent like many of his allies and is generally shown to be averse to violence as he refuses to partake in the Special Game. In the aftermath of the Special Game and Rebellion he appears fearful and likely felt slightly sympathetic towards Gi-hun on some level as he didn't mock him like many other members of Team O.  
His aversion to violence is also proven by how he only kills someone when he had to in Hide and Seek. Afterwards he looked at Team X, likely feeling remorseful for what Team O put them through especially since Jun-hee's baby was now at risk. This strongly did imply that 435 was one of the few members of Team O who didn't want to keep playing anymore, however because of the Special Game where many X voters were killed, he was likely too scared to switch his vote to X and only voted O again in fear. He also looks at Kim Gi-min in shock and fear after he killed Player 349 for no reason. This would mean that he knew his supposed allies were greedy, selfish killers and felt that he had to act like he was still on the O's side in order to avoid being targeted by them. 

History[]
Player 435 likely went into debt either by borrowing from loan sharks or spending on gambling. He played Ddakji with the Recruiter, and Player 435 eventually joined the Squid Games. He woke up in the dormitory along with the other 454 players and later saw footage, including his own, getting slapped by the recruiter after losing several rounds of ddakji.
He signed the consent form and climbed the stairs to the first game, which was Red Light, Green Light. He listened to the rules and witnessed the death of Kang Mi-na. He was one of those who remained still during the panic caused by the other players.
Thanks to Seong Gi-hun’s strategy, he managed to survive the first game and rested for a while in the dormitory. Then he realized that the money accumulated from the 91 players who died in the first game wasn’t enough for him. A vote was initiated, and he voted to continue the games, due to not having enough money. The majority voted to continue as well. After receiving food, he went to sleep, awaiting the second game the next day.
He woke up and climbed the stairs to the second game: Six-Legged Pentathlon. He partnered up with Park Mi-hwa, Player 331, Player 056, and Player 443. Him and his team then managed to successfully completed the game. He voted O once more and completed the third game successfully.  
He votes O again during the third vote, and is seen cheering as Mi-hwa switches her vote to O. The vote ends in a tie. During the bathroom fight, he wasn't involved in the brawl between the X players and the O players, however in what is presumably an error he is later seen coming out of the bathroom with the rest of the O players, tired after the fight ended. After the events of the bathroom fight, him and the other Os had a headcount and figure out that they have less players compared to the Xs. The other Os then decide to ambush the Xs during the night to put them ahead. However, Player 435 decided to stay out of the fight and hide instead. Later on after the fight and Gi-hun's failed Rebellion, Player 435 voted O again during the morning vote. They end up winning the vote and continue the games further. 
In Hide and Seek, he was assigned to be a seeker, and kills Player 334 off-screen. The announcement of him passing is heard as Jeong-dae talks with Seon-nyeo. He votes O for the fifth time in a row and moves on to Jump Rope. 
During jump rope, him and a few other players stare at Kim Gi-min after he pushed off Player 349. Gi-min notices this and shouts at them, asserting he did everyone a favor.  
Before Player 096 attacks Gi-hun from behind; him, Kim Yun-tae, Player 039 and Gi-min began crossing the bridge as soon as Im Jeong-dae and Lee Seung-won passed, while the other players were knocked off.

Trivia[]
Player 435 and 448 are the only minor players in Jump Rope who had physically appeared in 001.
He is the second-to-last male player in the 400's range to be eliminated in the 37th Squid Game, just behind Gi-hun.
At the end of the fifth vote, Player 435 and Player 260  were the only O players to look at the X players, as if they felt sorry for them, suggesting that they were the only O players who didn't want to continue with the games and only voted O again in fear.
Take this into account with the 4th round of voting. If players 039, 260, 435 and 448 all had the courage to change their votes. This would've been the result 12 (X) and 12 (O) another tie and if Gi-hun voted it would've been 13 (X) and 12. (O) This did suggest if players had made their own decisions and not give into fear Team X could've won the 4th round of voting. Even if one of them [likely Player 039] lacked the courage to do this, if Park Min-su [Player 125] had voted X along with 260, 435, 448 and Gi-hun the games would still be terminated.
Even though he had been a fairly enthusiastic O voter and even cheered at the end of the second vote and when the third vote was a tie [meaning there was a chance the games could continue] it is possible that his look of sympathy and wanting to leave was because his debt was filled [with some to spare] after Hide and Seek and he was also growing uncomfortable with the shrinking amount of players meaning he could be targeted easier in the next game. It should be noted he voted when the 0s already had the majority to win [Player 353/Kim Yun-tae was the 13th to vote 0 meaning that the fifth game would take place no matter how the five remaining players after him voted] so he may have voted 0 to try and protect himself, for the same reasons as Player 039 and Player 448.
He is the last player in the 43X range to be eliminated in the 37th squid game.
Since Player 435 placed 14th in the competition, his 33rd Squid Game counterpart is Player 062.
Player 182's body was thought to be him until it was revealed that he would be in season three.
He was counted 31st in the Team O headcount.
Mistakes[]
He is shown leaving the bathroom fight with blood despite never actually appearing. While some believe he hid in a stall, that doesn't explain how he had blood on him so it's most likely the whole thing is just an error.
In his death scene, you can clearly see that he jumps to the side off the bridge, even before the rope hits his ankles. Matter of a fact, the rope doesn't seem to hit his ankles physically at all, making it seem like he just jumped off the side even more. This was also explicitly shown in the Season 3 BTS.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 435 was portrayed by Park Jong-young, Chae Dong-hee and an unknown extra.
