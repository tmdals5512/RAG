Do Jeong-soo's team, also known as Team 8, was a player group formed during Tug of War in the 33rd Squid Game. All of the players here are eliminated in either the fourth and fifth game of the competition.

Trivia[]
In a major continuity error, none of the players in the team except for Player 453 appear in Tug of War. They likely replaced Team Eight for the fourth game, as we can see a different 453 in Team Eight in Tug of War.
Player 413 was the first member of the team to die.
The five members who won the Marbles game probably won in this order: Player 017/Do Jeong-soo, Player 096, Player 021, Player 453 and Player 322 [Jung Min-tae] if the list of eliminated players called out is accurate. Those five then die in that order in the Glass Bridge game with the exception of Jeong-soo dying last of the group.
It is the second highest-placing Tug Of War team in the series, only behind Gi-hun's team.
It is the only winning team of Tug of War without any member dying during an interlude/between games.
Player 453 was the only female member of the team as well as likely the oldest member.
It is possible they used the same strategy to win Tug of War as Gi-hun's team if they had a good enough view of it.
