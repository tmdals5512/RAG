Kim Geum-sun (김금순 Gim Geum-sun) is the actress who portrayed Player 349 in Season 2 and Season 3 of the Netflix TV series Squid Game. She is also seen playing an survivor in Train to Busan.

Trivia[]
She stars in the same K-drama as Wi Ha Joon, called Siren Kiss. She also starred in a zombie horror film set on a train, where she played a survivor alongside actors Choi Gwi-Hwa (Kim Gi-min) and Gong Yoo.
