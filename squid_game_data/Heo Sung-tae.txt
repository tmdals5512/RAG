Heo Sung-tae (허성태 Heo Seong-tae?) is the actor who portrayed Jang Deok-su in Season 1 of the Netflix TV series Squid Game. He is prominent for portraying fictional characters who are just as antagonistic.

Trivia[]
Heo Sung-tae loves cats and often posts photos of them on Instagram.
He received a lot of hate for his role in Squid Game, like Im Si-wan.
He also gained 17 pounds just for his role of the gangster, which had a negative impact on health and caused health struggles.
He is actually younger than Kwak Ja Hyoung, Kim Joo-ryoung and Yoo Sung-joo.
Despite playing mainly antagonists, he is known to be very kind and friendly in real life.
He is the only person who played a main character who wasn't present in the Squid Game Parade Finale Fan Event, although fans speculate that he was too busy working on other movies like Good Boy.
