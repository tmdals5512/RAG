Personality[]
Get up. What are you doing? You're praying right now? Hey, just get up and stop praying, okay? You idiot, it's your turn! Everybody will die, you know!
Player 151 attempting to get Player 244 to stop praying during Glass Stepping Stones in VIPS
Player 151 is anxious person, as he sees the glass being placed on the bridge, he gets very confused. He becomes more paranoid as he crosses the bridge. Later, he became increasingly aggressive after seeing Player 244 pray and became hostile towards him, and was convinced enough he had to push him after being threatened by Player 407 and Deok-su. 

History[]
Player 151 was in heavy debt and chose to enter the Squid Games to win money. After waking up in a large room with 455 other players, he signed a contract and took his photo. He managed to survive the first game, Red Light Green Light, after most of the contestants had been wiped out. Afterwards, like everyone else, he was shaken by the deaths of the contestants, when the prize money was finally revealed. A vote was held if the game should continue, but the majority voted no. However, those who were willing to return could, and he returned, along with most of the other players. He was also seen stacking beds in the lobby for protection.  
He managed to survive the second game, Sugar Honeycombs, and the third game, Tug of War, and beat his opponent, Player 068 in the fourth game Marbles, and survived until the fifth game, Glass Stepping Stones. For the game, he chose the number 7, which meant he would be the seventh to go. 

Fate[]
Get going, you idiot. Step forward. Come on, damn it! You'll kill us all!
Player 151's last words to Player 244 before getting pushed to his death in "VIPS"
When it was Player 021's turn, he asked Player 453 for help, only for her to forget the tiles Player 062 stepped on. Player 151 told him to go left while the person in front of 151, Player 244, told him to go right. 453 believed what 151 said, and 021 decided to go left fell to his death. When it was 244's turn he started praying. 151 then tried to push him off (after being threatened by 407 and 101), but failed and was pushed into a normal glass tile by 244 leading to his death.

Trivia[]
Player 151's scream as he falls to his death is identical to Player 062 and Player 096
He is the only semi-finalist whose icon was incorrect.
Player 151 was portrayed on-screen by four different extras and one supporting actor.
The first one is seen in the dormitory in "Red Light, Green Light", "Hell", and "The Man with the Umbrella".
The second one is seen in his icon.
The third one is seen in Red Light, Green Light. He is seen running to the doors.
The fourth one is seen in the dormitory in "A Fair World".
The fifth one is seen in "Gganbu" and "VIPS".
