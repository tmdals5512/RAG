History[]
Player 454 was in heavy debt and chose to enter the Squid Games because of it. After waking up in a bed, he was confused and skeptical at first but once he saw the Piggy bank he wanted to play. He signed a contract, took his photo, and went to the arena where Red Light, Green Light happens. He was shaken after seeing 3 people die but did not run to the doors and made progress through the round.

Fate[]
A lot of players crossed the finish line, however, Player 454, along with 17 other people didn't finish the game in time, which caused them to be shot dead.

Trivia[]
Although we can't see his file, we can still see through it, unfortunately, the only thing visible is his File Icon.
Mistakes[]
Player 454 is seen running towards the doors.
Despite dying in Red Light, Green Light, he is seen at the voting. Player 453 was the second one to vote after 456, making it clear that Player 454 did not manage to survive the first game. This is likely a continuity error made by Hwang Dong-hyuk.
Player 454 has the same icon as Player 371 and Player 109.
Despite Player 454 being a guy, his icon shows a woman.
Player 454 crosses the finish line in Behind the Scenes of Red Light Green Light, but the scene is later repeated, and Player 454 is shown running out of time.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 454 was portrayed by Song Kyung-hyeon, Yun Hyun-ju, Moon Yun-hwan and an unknown extra.
