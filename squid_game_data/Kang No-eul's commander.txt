Keys and Knives[]
Mercenary navigator[]
     
A Navigator at Park Yeong-gil's ship. He was hired by Choi Woo-seok in order to find the Squid Game Island. He navigates the ship along with Kim and dies to Park Yeong-gil, who kills all the mercenaries in Hwang In-ho's order.


Second drone pilot[]
     
A drone pilot that was hired to join the mercenaries looking for the Squid Games after their original drone pilot was killed. When he is introduced on the shoreline, he greets everyone and says it's nice to meet them all. He was later killed by Park Yeong-gil on the orders of Hwang In-ho.


Kang No-eul's commander[]
Before No-eul shoots Number 005 in the head, she says to him that her commander in North Korea was a lot like him, and that she shot him in the head, which she then does to 005.
No-eul's commander never made a physical appearance; he was only mentioned by her once in the series.

It's Not Your Fault[]
Police officer[]
     
A police officer arrested Choi Woo-seok after a neighbor of Park Yeong-gil reported that there was a break-in at Yeong-gil's house.
The police officer is portrayed by Lee Do-goon.


222[]
Detective[]
The detective is a colleague of Detective Song at the police station in Moojin. He was the detective who interrogated Woo-seok about breaking into Captain Park's house. Later him and his colleagues would chase Woo-seok down after the latter made a run for it with his phone to call Jun-ho.
The detective is portrayed by Song Boo-gun. He wears a name tag that is indiscernible, although his actor's name appears on his business card.

Humans Are...[]
Seong Ga-yeong's friend[]
Seong Ga-yeong had a friend who was at her house when In-ho approaches Ga-yeong with Gi-hun's belongings.
She does not make a physical appearance; only her voice is heard. She is voiced by Du Ga-ae.
