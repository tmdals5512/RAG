Dani Álvarez, also known as Player 002, is a skateboarder from Spain who competed in the Squid Games.

In-game description[]
Barcelona's fearless skater Dani Álvarez blazes through life shattering speed limits and social conventions.
Shredding every barrier in their path, Dani's street-savvy skills made them a natural for off-the-books courier work until a high-stakes delivery gets gnarly and Dani finds themselves owing a dangerous debt to a very serious client.
Now, Dani must pull off the biggest ride of their life and barrel through the Squid Game.
