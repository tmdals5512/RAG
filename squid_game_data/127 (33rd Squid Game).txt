History[]
Before "Red Light, Green Light"[]
We know nothing about her life before joining the Squid Game. She most likely was in debt and joined the games after playing ddakji with The Recruiter.

"Red Light, Green Light"[]
Player 127 was taken by a van and transported to the island. a contestant in Squid Game. After waking up with the other players, he can be seen listening to the manager's speech.
After taking the photo, he, along with all the other who were really close to the first three deaths[1], can be seen running towards the doors and getting shot. Player 127 was the fourth player to be eliminated from the 33rd Squid Game and the icon disappears off-screen too during the panic too.

"Hell"[]
Player 127 makes another appearance during the voting, where she voted to leave the games. Eventually, she was sent home as the majority had voted to leave the games.

"The Man with The Umbrella"[]
Player 127 returns to the games and can be seen carving her dalgona in Sugar Honeycombs.

"Stick to the Team"[]
After passing Sugar Honeycombs off-screen, Player 127 can be seen witnessing the fight between Player 101 and Player 271, where the second one died. She allies with Player 011 and Player 206 off-screen and can be seen talking to each other while Byeong-gi is trying to find a team to survive the Special Game. Her corpse is eventually seen after the midnight fight ends and Player 127 had never appeared ever since.

Fate[]
Despite seeing Player 127 dead, she doesn't have a true fate, as her death was not announced nor the icon disappeared. It is likely, that she died in Red Light, Green Light or Special Game for the reasons stated earlier.

Trivia[]
A player named Lee Jung-soo won the 22nd Squid Games in 2009 with this number.
Mistakes[]
Player 127 was portrayed by four diffrent extras.
The first one is seen in the dormitory.
The second one took her icon photo off-screen.
The third one is seen getting shot during Red Light, Green Light.
And the fourth one is seen in "Hell", "The Man with The Umbrella" and "Stick to the Team".
For some reason, she is seen voting before Player 129 despite her number being lower.
Player 127 shares her correct icon with Player 378.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 127 was portrayed by Kim Eun-jin, Jeong Sin-ho and two unknown extras.
