History[]
Player 043 was in heavy debt like most players, and at a train station he met The Recruiter and was given a card to join the Squid Games. He accepted due to his high debt. When Player 043 woke up, he found himself into his bed in the Dormitory With 455 other players afterwards, he signed a contract and took a photo. During Red Light, Green Light, he managed to stay still the entire time even during the mass panic. At some point, He managed to make it to the other side within the time limit, Making him pass the first game. 
As Sang-woo Brought up the 3rd Clause from the Contract, It was officially announced that voting would be taking place. It's unknown whether Player 043 Voted O or X. However, he left due to the majority voting X. But after sometime, he decided to return mostly likely because of his debt. It's unknown what shape 043 chose, but he managed to carve out his cookie within the time limit. Player 043 managed to survive the Special Game by either hiding or being able to defend himself against other players.
In the Lobby, Player 043 managed to get in Do Jeong-soo's team and managed to beat their opposing team, Team Two. Player 043 and his team decided to build an base around their team base in the dormitory so that other players couldn't attack them. In the morning, 043 and the others lined up and made their way to the 4th game. When the rules were announced that players needed a partner, Player 043 decided to partner with Player 407. Afterwards, Player 043 opposed against Player 407 and played an optional game of theirs. 

Fate[]
In Marbles, he lost to Player 407, causing him to be shot as we can hear his elimination being announced.
