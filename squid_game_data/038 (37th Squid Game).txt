History[]
Player 038, like many other contestants, joined the Squid Game to escape her debt. She is later seen in the Dormitory watching other players get slapped, before signing the consent form and having her photo taken.
During the Red Light, Green Light, as the intital chaos started, she stood still and listened to Seong Gi-hun, allowing her to successfully pass the first game. After surviving the game, she voted to continue the games and fortunately for her, many others made the same choice. She was relieved and happy as the games continued.
In the Six-Legged Pentathlon, she teamed up with Players 059, 269, 345, and 367. She played Biseokchigi for her team. When it's her team turn, her team managed to complete all the mini games within the time limit and survived. During the voting, she once again chose to continue the games.

Fate[]
In Mingle, she made it through the first round offscreen with 9 other players, as she was seen on the platform before the second round of Mingle, when the round started, she was seen trying to find a group, she was than seen with Player 164, Player 368, and Player 176. The group even in the midst of flashing lights, and mayhem, successfully secured a room in time. But in third round, unfortunately, she failed to find a room in time and was shot to death.
