Jo Soo-yeon (조수연 Jo Soo-yeon?) is the actor who portrayed Player 208 during Red Light, Green Light, Player 223 in Sugar Honeycombs and the recurring Player 337 and Player 021 after Tug of War in Season 1 of the Netflix TV series Squid Game.
