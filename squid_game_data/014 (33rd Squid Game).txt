History[]
Player 014 was in alot of debt and entered the Squid Games cause of this. He woke up in a bed and was confused at 1st but once he saw the piggy bank he wanted to play. He signed a contract, took his photo and went to where Red Light Green Light happens. He managed to survive the first game. A vote was then held to decide whether the games should continue, and the majority voted to end them. However, Player 013 and most of the other players returned to the games after being given the opportunity. He then participated in the second game.  

Fate[]
Out of fear and nervousness, he broke a cookie and was shot by the guard. 

Trivia[]
His actor portrays Player 028 after the special game.
Coincidentally, 28 is the double of 14.
Mistakes[]
Even though Player 014's icon is correct, he shares his icon with Player 287.
A different Player 014 appears during Red Light, Green Light.
He appears later even though he died in Dalgona.
