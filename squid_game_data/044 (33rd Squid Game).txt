Player 045







At some point during Player 044's life, he became burdened by financial debt. Eventually, he was approached by the Recruiter and given an invitiation to the Squid Game where he could make more money. Having accepted the invitation, he was picked up, gassed, and taken to the island where he was changed into his player uniform and stripped of his personal possessions. 
He woke up in a room with 455 other contestants, where many of them demanded to know where they were, either out of anger or anxiety. It was revealed all of the contestants were chosen because of their large debts. Following this, Player 044 signed his contract, took his photo, and was taken to the first game: Red Light, Green Light.  

Fate[]
It's unknown where he died, he was seen panicking and his icon faded in Red Light, Green Light, but was seen multiple times after the game.   

Trivia[]
He has the same player number as one of the supporting characters from Season 2 / 3.
