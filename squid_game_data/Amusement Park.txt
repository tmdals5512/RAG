Seoul Land is an amusement park located in the city of Gwacheon. Its employees included Kang No-eul and Park Gyeong-seok. It appears in the episodes "Halloween Party", and "Humans Are..."
The park employed numerous mascots and portrait painters, as well as at least one security guard. It also held a Mardi Gras parade.

Trivia[]
The park exists in real-life under the same name.
Seoul Land opened in 1988, making it as old as Squid Game itself, as it began the same year.
The costumes of the mascots are of the theme parks real-life mascots, some of which are used for the park's Mardi Gras parade.
The park's name is never mentioned, though it is seen on a vehicle in the Mardi Gras parade.
Director Hwang Dong-hyuk made a mini cameo in the last episode as a painter in the background, when Park Gyeong-seok, Kang No-eul and Park Na-yeon were interacting.
