History[]
Backstory[]
Almost nothing is known about Player 158's life before joining the Squid Games. At some point, he was in massive debts and joined after playing Ddakji with The Recruiter.

"Red Light, Green Light"[]
Player 158 was taken by a van and transported to the island after saying the password "Red Light, Green Light". After waking up with the other players in the dormitory, he can be seen watching the fight between Kang Sae-byeok and Jang Deok-su, listening to the manager's speech and signing a contract.
He made another appearance in the first game, where he can be seen when Seong Gi-hun and Oh Yeong-uk talk to each other. He seems to have survived the panic and can be later seen walking on a green light in front of Player 023. He made it to the finish line and watched as the failing players were shot down.

"Hell"[]
Player 158 can be seen in the Dormitory after Red Light, Green Light and later voting, where he voted to continue the games. He was sent home as the majority had voted to leave the games, and was unsatisfied with the voting results.

"Stick to the Team"[]
After passing Sugar Honeycombs off-screen by carving his unknown shape out, he allied with Player 188 and can be seen fighting in the Special Game against Player 129. Player 158 would depart from them and attack an unknown player on a bed; he would overpower and wrestle with them, but the player would escape his grasp. Player 158 would then be hit on the ground by Player 211, who he would then counter by tackling him. Player 211 would be able to barely escape and would run off. Player 158 would then go on the stairs and fight an unknown player, stomping them and throwing them away to get back to Player 129, his former attempted victim. Player 158 would throw Player 129 around and eventually be able to kill him by strangling and beating him to death. He would then run off on the other side of the dormitory and would stomp Player 328's skull out, and would be able to survive the rest of the Special Game.

"A Fair World"[]
After passing Tug of War off-screen, he can be seen building barricades from bed frames with his team in order to prevent a possible attack. He later woke up from the sirens and walked into the line along with the team.

"Gganbu" and Fate[]
After being checked by the pink guards, he went to the lobby. He can be seen with his team in the lobby, and later partnering with Player 308. He can be seen with her in Marbles. Unfortunately for him, Player 308 managed to take all of his marbles, and he was shot off-screen. His elimination is announced, and his icon would disappear.
