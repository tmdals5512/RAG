Personality
What are you waiting for? Go across! If you don't, we're all dead!
Player 442 rambling during Jump Rope in 222
Player 442, like most members of Team O, is ruthless, greedy, cocky and sadistic as he is shown to be hostile towards Team X as he took part in the Special Game, furthermore he is even shown to be ruthless towards anyone showing he was one of the most brutal members of Team O. Also, he mocked Dae-ho, who claimed to be a Marine earlier during mealtime. Violence didn't appear to disturb Player 442 after the failed rebellion he was shown mocking Gi-hun, when he was shown the bodies of the revolt members it didn't seem to bother him as much as other players, he managed to kill an unknown hider in Hide and Seek without any remorse and before Hide and Seek he was seen showing his knife with other Seekers possibly excited about the game eager to kill anyone. 
He voted O despite Jang Geum-ja's pleas coldly ignoring her and knowing about Kim Jun-hee having a baby showing how selfish he was. He was even willing to kill Im Jeong-dae and Player 096 in Jump Rope showing he was willing to kill his supposed allies though in fairness both of those players had shown they were willing to cause his own death. He is however shown to be a coward inside when Kwon Byeong-su was about to shoot him and the other Os he hid behind the bed, he appeared to be afraid of heights in Jump Rope being hesitant to cross and when Player 192 grabbed him for support he immediately panicked yelling at him to let go. Despite all this, he seems to have a genuine friendship with Player 192.

History
Player 442 was in tremendous debt and decided to join the Squid Games for a chance to win a huge cash prize. He called the number on the recruiting card, and got into a mysterious car. He then woke up in a giant room with 454 other Players. He then made his way to the center of the room and watched as many other players debts were revealed in front of him. Later on, he signed a contract and made his way up a flight of stairs to the first game.
During the first game, he was soon terrified as players were shot in front of him during the mass panic. He was able to control himself and stand still. He eventually completed the first game after listening to Seong Gi-hun's commands. After returning to the dormitory, many players pleaded the Guards to let them go. However, after a massive increase in the prize pool was revealed, Player 442 changed his mind and decided to vote O during the first vote. Him and the other Os then cheered after they won the vote.
Later during Six-Legged Pentathlon, Player 442 partnered up with players 158,202,307 and 419. He and his team managed to complete all 5 minigames within the 5 minute timer. After successfully completing the Six-Legged Pentathlon, him and the other players held another vote. He decided to vote O again along with many other O players.
Later on during Mingle, He managed to partner up with other players and survive. After Mingle, him and the other remaining players returned to the dormitory and have a third vote. Player 442 decided to vote O again along with many other O players. This vote ends up in a tie, meaning they would vote again in the morning. Player 442 and the other players seem disappointed and move back to their beds. Later on in the bathroom. Player 442 overhears Lee Myung-gi  and Thanos arguing. Player 442 gets angry and starts yelling at the Xs to just do their business and get out. However a fight later ensues after Thanos punches Myung-gi in the face. Player 442 then tackled Player 145 and started punching and kicking him. However, he was soon overpowered and kicked onto the ground by him. Player 145 and Player 324 are then seen kicking and stomping on Player 442 repeatedly. After the bathroom fight ends, Player 442 and the other surviving players involved walk out of the bathroom. Player 442 and the other Os then start accusing the Xs of starting the fight. A giant argument then ensues between the 2 sides. However Im Jeong-dae breaks up the altercation by suggesting a headcount to see what team lost more players. Player 442 and the other Os agree and they all line up to start counting. When they eventually figure out that they have less players compared to the Xs, Player 442 and the other Os then decide to ambush the Xs during the night to put them ahead. When the lights finally go out, Player 442 and some other Os are seen sneaking over to Team X's side of the room. Eventually, Player 442 and the other Os charged into the bunks and start killing as many Xs as possible. After the Special Game. He decided not to participate in Gi-hun's Rebellion and instead stay in the lobby with all of the other players that also decided not to join. In the next round of voting he voted O again and can be seen celebrating and mocking Gi-hun when they win the vote. It was unknown if Player 442 saw Gi-hun strangle Seon-nyeo who made fun of his dead friends, shocking the rest of Team O.
Later during Hide and Seek, Player 442 drew a red ball from the gumball machine, which confirms his role as a Seeker. Later when the guards allow them to switch teams, He decides not to switch with a hider and keeps his red vest. After the timer to hide finishes, Player 442 and the others Seekers make their way into the maze. He later manages to pass the game by killing a unknown hider at some point during the game. After Hide and Seek concludes, Player 442 and the other surviving players make their way back to the dormitory, where they have another vote. He decides to vote O again along with 15 other players. They end up winning the vote again and continuing the games further.
Later on, him and the remaining players make their way to Jump Rope. Upon entering Jump Rope, Player 442 and the other remaining players looked in fear at the bridge they had to cross. Later on, after witnessing the death of Nam-gyu, he almost threw up, and watched Gi-hun successfully cross the bridge, Player 442 and the other players get motivation to start crossing. Player 442 started crossing the bridge right behind Lee Seung-won and with Player 192 close behind him. However, him and the other players then got stuck on the bridge after Player 096 starts pushing other players off. Player 442 then yells at Im Jeong-dae to just push his way through instead of waiting. As Player 096 continues to block the bridge, Player 442 insults him and threatens to kill him.

Fate
Fucking asshole! I'll kill you! (...) What?!
Player 442 last words before being accidentally killed by Player 192 in "222"
After Player 442 threatens to kill Im Jeong-dae if he helped Player 096, Player 192 lost his balance behind him and grabbed onto his shoulder for support. This stunned Player 442 and caused them both to start panicking. The rope then came back around and swept both of them off of the bridge, which caused them both to plummet to the ground and hit the flower painted floor, killing them both instantly. Player 442 and Player 192 were then shown as mangled corpses on the ground later in the episode. Their bodies were presumably incinerated after Jump Rope concluded. He placed 19th in the competition.

Trivia
Player 442 is likely afraid of heights, as evidenced by his facial expressions when he and the other players enter the room in the Jump Rope game. Furthermore, the fact that he looks uncomfortable when he sees the rope, stays more lower to the ground and feels like he might vomit after watching Nam-gyu falling and before going up on the platform indicates this likely fear of heights.
Player 442 has a habit of scratching his nose, it can be an allergy.
Since a behind the scenes photo that shows players who were supposed to die in Jump Rope does not show Player 448 and a BTS Image shows Player 442 with Player 448, It is possible that Player 442 was originally going to kill Player 448 in Hide and Seek. This means when player 442 yells for someone to 'get back here,' he might have been chasing Player 448, further supported by the female scream heard directly afterwards.
This would also explain why he is seen several times in Maze despite never showing or announcing his victim.
If he did kill Player 448 he would have shown unwillingly voting O didn't make a difference to Player 442 or other members of Team O as it didn't mean protection from its most violent members like Player 442.
Player 442's voice sounded different before the bathroom fight, in English dub. he sounded tough than his voice in Season 3, but in Jump Rope, he sounded way different like a guy in his early or mid twenties.
Despite being a recurring character, with a significant number of lines and screen time, he’s the only one of Jeong-dae’s allies who isn’t credited as a supporting character. Instead, he’s credited alongside the extras and minor players on numerological order. Funnily enough, Player 296, who has no dialogue and less screentime, is listed as a supporting character.
He canonically found/went after three hiders, Player 448, Player 084, and the hider he actually killed, making him one of the unluckiest seekers that passed the game.
Additionally, if his victim was Player 418, then all three hiders he encountered had 8 and 4 in their numbers.
He was the first player to enter the gumball machine room, followed by 418, 202, Gi-min, and 096.
Since Player 442 placed 19th in the competition, his 33rd Squid Game counterpart is Ji-yeong.
He was the only player in the 400s range in the fifth vote who was still loyal to Team O making 442 one of the most ruthless members.
Player 448 never wanted to vote O.
Judging by 435's expression he probably didn't want to continue playing either.
He is the first to put O with more votes than X in the third vote, leaving it 3 O to 2 X.
He died in the same game as the person who did this in the first vote.
He is the most seen minor seeker during Hide and Seek .
Ironically, his victim is unknown.
He was counted eighth in the Team O headcount.
He is presumably the fifth or fourth last seeker to pass Hide and Seek.
As the hiders enter the arena, he can be seen in the background, pointing his knife at them and grinning.
He  Is the only main players on Im Jeong-Dae's team who is not seen running towards the bunks in the special game. He Is also not seen fighting during the special game but only when the guards entered to stop the fight.
If he had outlasted five more players in the Jump Rope game he would have placed 14th which means he would have been the 442nd player eliminated, the same as his player number.

This means 442 had have to have already killed someone.
