History[]
Player 021, like many other participants, joined the Squid Games because he was struggling with debt. Player 021 survived Red Light, Green Light thanks to the instructions of Gi-hun.
In the first vote, player 021 believed that the money was not enough to pay his debt so he decided to vote Team O.
In Six-Legged Pentathlon Player 021 and his team manage to cross the finish line in 5 minutes, in the second vote 021 votes X since the money was probably already enough to pay his debt.

Fate[]
Player 021 dies in Mingle for not being able to find a room.
