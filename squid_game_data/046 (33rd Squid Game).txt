History[]
Player 046 was in heavy debt and chose to enter the Squid Games to win money. After waking up in a large room with 455 other players, he signed a contract and took his photo, officially joining the Squid Games.

Fate[]
During Red Light, Green Light, Player 046 survived the initial chaos caused by players 324 and 250's deaths. However, he was caught moving by the doll at some point, and is shown getting shot in the chest.

Trivia[]
A player named Yang Hyeong-taek won the 15th Squid Games in 2002 with this number.
Player 046 was seen rolling past the finish line when Sae-byeok and Deok-su were crossing the finish line. This is most likely a continuity error.
Even though Player 046 died later, his icon was already gone during the initial chaos of Red Light Green Light.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 046 was portrayed by Kim Seon-an and 3 unknown extras.
