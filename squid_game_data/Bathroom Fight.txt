The Bathroom Fight was an unofficial game and interlude that was a violent brawl between 23 players during the 37th Squid Game, taking place in the men's bathroom after the third game Mingle.
The fight was triggered by an argument between Lee Myung-gi, Thanos, Nam-gyu, and Park Min-su. The fight revealed to the players that they could kill each other to increase the prize money, leading to the Special Game occurring that night. At this point in the competition, 5 players in total were eliminated. Casualties of this interlude includes Player 230, Player 268, Player 299, Player 331 and Player 401. 
This fight took place between the night of November 3, 2024 and the early morning of November 4, 2024.

Prelude[]
Min-su. I know you're upset with me. I'm sorry, boy! But still, you shouldn't have betrayed me. That really hurt. Open the door!
Thanos attempting to convince Park Min-su to let him into his bathroom stall in O X
In the men's bathroom, Thanos and Nam-gyu corner Park Min-su in a stall after he changed his vote to X in the vote. Thanos bangs his head against the door multiple times, trying to force it open while apologizing to him but also talking about his frustration with his betrayal. During this, Nam-gyu peeks over the wall of the stall to try and negotiate with Min-su before trying to hop over the wall and grab him. Min-su tries to flee the stall but is stopped by Thanos, who tells him about how he was going to commit suicide, until The Recruiter gave him the card to enter the games, reigniting his will to live.

Everyone on Team X! These guys are threatening one of us!

Player 348 loudly insulting Team X in O X
Thanos tells Min-su that the fact that he voted "X" seriously upset him. Lee Myung-gi arrives and protects Min-su. Myung-gi asks for help from Team X and informs them that Thanos and Nam-gyu are threatening Min-su to change his vote to "O", and members of Team X in the bathroom arrive to help and tells the pair that what they're doing is cheating. Thanos calls out to Team O for help, and he lies that the members of Team X are about to attack them, igniting a verbal conflict. Min-su hides among Team X and Myung-gi prevents Thanos from approaching him. Nam-gyu then mentions that his motivation behind protecting Min-su is because of Kim Jun-hee. He mocks her for being pregnant and Thanos mocks Myung-gi for "fooling around with a girl". Myung-gi gets furious and tells them not to mess with her.

Fight[]
Your money, your girl, your life, they're all mine. It all belongs to me, Thanos the great! You fucking... you son of a fucking bitch!
Thanos's last words as he beats Lee Myung-gi in O X
Members of Team X and Team O originally try to just break up the fight, but start attacking each other. Myung-gi and Thanos face off against each other, where Myung-gi charges at Thanos. Nam-gyu attempts to kick Myung-gi away but gets distracted by Kwon Byeong-su and pushes him. Player 145 attempts to grab Thanos in the head, but gets tackled by Player 442 after the latter was unintentionally pushed by Player 056. Nam-gyu, Player 348, and Player 418 attempts to fight Kwon Byeong-su, Player 072, and Player 094, but Player 197 grabs and pulls Player 418 from the back. Player 206 ran across the bathroom where he attacked Player 056 off-screen. Player 268 then can be seen dropping Player 331 to the ground, then Player 192 attempts to stabilize himself by grabbing onto Player 401 and 348, but then gets lifted by Player 324. Player 102 backed up in fear but was then thrown into a stall by Player 056. Min-su flees to the Dormitory. Thanos starts beating up Myung-gi and tells him that he will steal his money, his girl and his life. He starts to strangle him, depriving him of oxygen, but Myung-gi grabs the Fork that he received during dinner for his Kimbap and stabs Thanos in his submental triangle, severing his facial artery and vein and causing him to severely gush blood from his neck. He then stabs him once more in the neck, severing his carotid artery and jugular vein causing Thanos to fall to the ground, and bleed to death. Thanos grabs Myung-gi's ankle out of desperation, but Myung-gi frees himself from Thanos's grip and crawls into a cubicle. Myung-gi hides in the cubicle for the rest of the fight, shaking and shocked at having killed Thanos.
The fight grows brutal. Player 197 and Player 418 struggle to get the upperhand and Player 418 trips Player 197 so he falls to the floor. Player 358 is dropped by Player 307 and falls to the floor however he strikes back and shoves Player 307 into a urinal. Player 094 and Player 348 struggle to gain an upper hand before Kwon Byeong-su assists the former.
Player 145 and Player 324 kick Player 442 on the floor while Player 072 and Player 206 fight against Player 192. Player 197 gains control and begins to strangle Player 418. Player 067 attacks Player 268, However Player 268 grabs him and tries to choke him.  Player 067 strikes back and Player 268 grabs him to get control but Player 067 throws Player 268 through a urinal partition.

Player 067 continues to Beat Player 268 however Player 299 stumbles up and grabs Player 067 from behind. Player 067 doesn’t get a chance to fight back before Player 299 slams him into a urinal and chokes him. Player 067 gasps for air and they both end up in a scuffle. 

Motherfucker! Fucking X bastard!

Nam-gyu fights with Player 331 yelling insults at each other and Player 331 lands a punch that sends Nam-gyu to the floor. He sees Thanos's corpse beside a stall and tries to grab the cross that contains his drugs but Player 331 kicks him on the floor. 
In retaliation, Nam-gyu rips the fork out of Thanos's neck and stabs Player 331 in the leg. He then moves upwards and repeatedly stabs him in the chest and neck killing him. Meanwhile, Player 067 and Player 299 scuffle and Player 268 tries to get up but (presumably) dies from lung failure.

It is implied the guards outside the room, suppressed the fight and escorted them back to the dormitory, as seen in the Special Game
When the fight has stopped, it is announced that Player 230, Player 268, Player 299, Player 331 and Player 401 had been eliminated, much to the shock and confusion of others in the Dormitory. In total, 5 people were eliminated - 2 from Team X and 3 from Team O.

Player Techniques[]
Since this game had no rules, there were multiple ways people eliminated or attacked each other.

Lee Myung-gi brought a fork into the bathroom (not to use intentionally as a weapon), and stabbed and killed Thanos with it. Nam-gyu would then use Myung-gi's fork to kill Player 331.
Player 197 put Player 418 into a chokehold.
Player 067 used a urinal partition to beat Nam-gyu to near death.
Some Xs attacked in groups.
Player 358 pinned Player 307 into a urinal and choked him with his bare hands while doing so.
Player 299 slammed Player 401’s head in to a urinal repeatedly and it killed him.
Player 056 took advantage of Player 102's attempts to stop fights and punched him into a cubicle, keeping him in there for majority of the fight and not letting him out.

Trivia[]
When filming the scene where Thanos throws Lee Myung-gi to the ground, T.O.P (Thanos' actor) put his hand under Im Si-wan's (Myung-gi's actor) head to prevent him from getting hurt.
Park Min-su and Lee Myung-gi are the only participants in the brawl who made it to the final game.
They were also the only ones to spend most of the battle not fighting. Min-su exits at the very start, and Myung-gi hides in a cubicle after killing Thanos.
Since the survivors returned to the dormitory shortly before Choi Woo-seok woke up around 3 a.m., it's likely that the fight took place on November 4, 2024, since the fight must have been brief or many more players would have died.
Unlike Lights Out, it was possible to accidentally avoid participating in this fight.
This fight is the only interlude game where only male players participated.
Player 192 and Player 442 were the only members of Im Jeong-dae's team that participated in the fight. Additionally, Player 192 and 442 are tied for being the longest surviving Team O member who participated in the Bathroom Fight (Excluding 435 as his appearance may be erronous).
No players who participated in the Bathroom Fight were seen in the dormitory before Red Light, Green Light and none of their actors are shown in Red Light, Green Light (excluding Thanos, Myung-gi and Nam-gyu).
Many players involved in the fight were first seen during the second vote, or in the bathrooms after the second vote, such as 056, 067, 094, 307, 358, and 442.
The players that were involved in the fight who survived until Season 3's games are Nam-gyu, Park Min-su, Player 192, Player 197, Lee Myung-gi, Player 418, and Player 442.
If Myung-gi, Thanos and Nam-gyu weren't in the bathroom, the fight may have not started and no deaths would've occured.
It is implied the guards stopped the fighting to prevent excess deaths, hinting that the Bathroom fight may have been planned similar to the Special Game.
Due to the fight, during the headcount, several members of Team X can be seen as completely zoned out, including Myung-gi, 197, and 094.
Mistakes[]
Player 299 was seen stomping on Player 333's stuntmen after Thanos and Player 401's deaths.
Player 435 is seen existing the bathroom after the fight despite not being involved in it.
Player 094 is not seen existing the bathroom after the fight despite being involved in it.
There are certain players present during the fight who were known to have died during Red Light, Green Light. These players were wrongly counted in the series and these errors were from BTS footage and these were also stuntmen.
