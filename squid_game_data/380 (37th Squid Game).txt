Personality[]
Don't even go there. Once you put that crap in you, that's all you want. If you ever get out of here, don't get involved with those assholes, they'll only hurt you.
Se-mi telling Park Min-su not to take Thanos' pills and trusting him and Nam-gyu in "One More Game"
Se-mi seems to adjust to people depending on their personality and how they act. She acts out a tough exterior, as seen done with Nam-gyu. On the other hand, she tends to be more gentle toward weaker people, as seen done with Min-su.

History[]
Backstory[]
And you can stop with the "Noona." I was born in '98. Just don't tell pricks.
Se-mi telling Park Min-su her real age in "One More Game"Se-mi was born at some point in 1998 or afterwards. She decided to enter the Squid Games to win money to cover her debt, presumably ₩200 million.
2024[]
At some point before Halloween, Se-mi was approached by a The Recruiter and played Ddakji with him. The salesman then gave her an invite to the Squid Games, where she could earn more money.
On Halloween night, she and 454 other individuals were sedated and brought to a giant room on a remote island[3], where she signed her life consent form and took a photo in able to enter the games.

"Six Legs"[]
     Se-mi, Min-su, Nam-gyu and Thanos during the Six Legged Pentathlon game.  
Off-screen, Se-mi manages to survive Red Light, Green Light, and when they had their first vote, she voted to continue the games. While looking for teammates for the second challenge, she became interested in Park Min-su (Player 125) and asked him to join forces. During their discussion, Thanos (Player 230), Nam-gyu (Player 124) Gyeong-su (Player 256) approached her and asked Thanos her to join Thanos' team, Nam-gyu told Thanos that they didn't know they were playing and that he didn't want a woman on the team, but Thanos shut him up and told Se-mi that he would protect her. Se-mi considered the offer although she mentioned that she had already chosen someone, Thanos told her that person could join as well and Se-mi introduced them to Min-su, Nam-gyu stated that Min-su looked like an idiot, but Thanos shut him up and accepted Min-su into the ''Thanos World''.
After Teams 1 and 2 were eliminated, Nam-gyu asked Thanos for a drug to control himself, as his hands were shaking with fear. After taking the drug, Nam-gyu told Min-su, Se-mi and Gyeong-su not to be afraid to win, to which Se-mi told "Mr. Shaky Hands" to take care of himself, Nam-gyu asks Se-mi why she acts like that and tells Thanos that's why he didn't want women on the team, since Se-mi had a "bad attitude". Se-mi tells Nam-gyu that he's a prick who needs to take drugs to control his nerves, which annoys Nam-gyu and he calls her a fucking bitch.

"One More Game"[]
Se-mi played Flying Stone during the Six-Legged Pentathlon game, and her team passed successfully passed this game and waited at the dormitory.
Once again, she came to Min-su's aid by stopping him from counting the remaining players. She stated to Nam-gyu that it is pointless doing so as they will find out who is left each game. While discussing their age, Se-mi lied about her age and Thanos gave her the nickname Noona. During the second voting session, Se-mi once again votes to continue the games.
Min-su, during their meal time wondered if she was scared playing the games. She admitted to being scared but felt like this is her only option. To break the ice, she told Min-su her actual age and requested him to not tell Nam-gyu and Thanos.
The next morning, she pulled Min-su aside and warned him to stay away from the rest of their team when they escape the games, calling them idiots only helpful in this game scenario. Min-su asks why she wanted to team with him, and she states it is because he would not betray her, and warns him his passive nature is not good for survival. She tells him that when her share reached ₩200 million, she would vote "X" to leave the games.

"O X"[]
Min-su draws scissors on Se-mi
While playing the third game: "Mingle", she stayed close to their group for the most part. During the second round, the announced number is 4. Thanos kicks Gyeong-su off the team and yells at the others to run, Thanos and Nam-gyu run while Min-su worries about Gyeong-su, but Se-mi grabs his arm and they run. Thanos' team manages to reach Room 41 and survive.
In the third round, the announced number is 3. Thanos and Nam-gyu tell Se-mi and Min-su to play rock, paper, scissors and that the winner will come with them, Se-mi tells Min-su to come with her and extends her hand, but Min-su gets scared by the pressure and extends two fingers, making it look like scissors against paper, Thanos and Nam-gyu tell Min-su that he won and take him with them, leaving Se-mi alone while she and Min-su are shocked by what happened. Leaving the rooms, Min-su sees that Se-mi managed to survive by joining Players 418 and 400, she stares at him in disbelief, alongside the viewers. In the end, Se-mi does manage to survive the Mingle game with another Player 400 in last round Mingle, and they manage to get to a room in time. possibly but looks at Min-su in disappointment, leaving the latter feeling guilty.

"Friend or Foe" and Fate[]
Fuck you! You son of a bitch! Ahh!
Se-mi's last words to Nam-gyu before being stabbed to death by a glass bottle to the neck in Friend or Foe     Se-mi's corpse.  
After the Bathroom fight, a headcount was held to find out which team lost the most players. Of the 5 players killed in the fight, Team X lost 2 and Team O lost 3, Se-mi realized that Team X could win the next day's vote (to which Park Jung-bae advises that if no one on their team swaps, they'll win by one vote) and the team went to sleep. Se-mi was unaware of the Team O's impending attack and took no measures to protect herself. When the lights were off during bedtime, Nam-gyu attacked Se-mi with the same fork that Lee Myung-gi used to murder Thanos. As Se-mi fought for her life, Min-su threw a glass bottle at Nam-gyu from above to save Se-mi but missed. Se-mi used the glass shards to fight back, stunning Nam-gyu. However, 2 players bumped into Se-mi and Nam-gyu, which gave the latter an advantage, debilitating Se-mi with a stab to the neck using the broken glass bottle shards. Then he stabbed her a second time and threw her to the ground before finishing her off with repeated stabs to the neck, all while Min-su sobbed in distress in a bunk above.

Legacy[]
She is mentioned by Nam-gyu in Keys and Knives in order to aggravate Min-su.
Se-mi later appears as a drug-induced hallucination in Min-su's mind in third season episodes "The Starry Night" and "⭘△☐" as a manifestation of his guilt over letting her down in Mingle and being unable to rescue her from Nam-gyu. In the former episode, her corpse takes the place of Seon-nyeo's corpse after Min-su, under the influence of Thanos' drugs, hallucinates Nam-gyu in her place and kills "him" to avenge Se-mi's death. When Min-su sees the hallucination of Se-mi's corpse in Seon-nyeo's place, he auditorily hallucinates the Announcer announcing Se-mi's death ("Player 380 eliminated") instead of Seon-nyeo's ("Player 044 eliminated") as he freaks out in horror. In the latter episode, right as Min-su's fate is sealed in the Sky Squid Game, she reappears in Lee Myung-gi's place, once again asking him to come with her and extending her hand. This time, Min-su apologizes repeatedly to her and sobs as he holds her "hand" (in reality, the iron pole used by Myung-gi to push him off the platform). He is comforted by her smile as he falls to his death.

Trivia[]
It's possible that Se-mi has surrounded herself with drug addicts, or even tried drugs herself at some point. This could be the reason why she knows people like Thanos and Nam-gyu very well, and doesn't approve them of using drugs. She even warns Min-su not to. This could also explain her saying "right now, if I leave this place, I have to face something scarier".
She is one of five players who came to the game with necklaces. The other three are Thanos, Nam-gyu, Player 400 and Player 043.
Se-mi was the only major and named character to have died in the Special Game.
The numbers of two of her team-mates, Nam-gyu/Player 124 and Gyeong-su/Player 256, add up to her player number, 380.
Se-mi and Gyeong-su are the only members of Thanos' team who did not take Thanos' pills.
She's one of the characters whose motive for entering the games and/or the amount of her debt is unknown.
Se-mi is the only major player who does not appear in the last round of Mingle.
She is the only player in the second vote that hadn't voted yet (but did vote O) that didn't chant one more game.
The two Thanos fans, Player 400 and Player 418, both ended up interacting with her later in the games.
During the Special Game, she was cornered by Nam-gyu, and Min-su threw a glass bottle to save her. However, she managed to attack a distracted Nam-gyu and initially overpowered him. If the two players hadn't bumped into her, she would have killed him.
She was the most seen player in season three who was not actually apart of it (excluding Hwang In-ho, as he was alive)
Her warning to Min-su that he wouldn't last long inside the games is highly ironic, since not only she died two games before him but Min-su also became a finalist.
Her rivalry with Nam-gyu is similar to Kang Sae-byeok's rivalry with Jang Deok-su. Both were former allies of their rivals, and both were targeted by their rivals during the riot. However, Se-mi is killed by Nam-gyu while Sae-byeok escaped from Deok-su and later became a finalist.
She's the last seen and most major death of the Special Game.
Se-mi is the only female player in the Special Game known to have been able to subdue an O player, although she still ended up dying.
Se-mi is played by a replacement actress during Six Legged-Pentathlon. Same as Park Min-su. It's very likely that her actress, Won Ji-an, wasn't present during the filming of those scenes. Hence why an extra is there.
Se-mi is the only one on her team who didn't seem worried about Gyeong-su during second round of mingle.
Her and Player 276 have extremely similar last words.
Her death was indirectly caused by erroneous players.
When Nam-gyu said that she liked Min-su it is uncertain if he meant platonically or romantically especially since she has some features common to lesbians.
Behind the scenes[]
Se-mi was portrayed by Won Ji-an and an unknown temporary replacement extra during the Six-Legged Pentathlon. In the English dub, she is voiced by Francesca Calo, and in the French dub by Marie Bouvet.
