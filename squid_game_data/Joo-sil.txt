Lee Joo-sil (이주실 I Ju-sil?) was a South Korean film, television and theatre actress. She portrayed the supporting character of Park Mal-soon, who is Hwang In-ho's stepmother and Hwang Jun-ho's mother in Season 2 of the Netflix TV series Squid Game.[4]

Overview[]
Joo-sil was diagnosed with breast cancer in 1993, but she recovered 10 years later.[5] She died from stomach cancer at her family's home in Uijeongbu, on February 2, 2025, at the age of 80[6], having been diagnosed with the disease 3 months prior.[7] She was the first actress on the show to die.
