History[]
Player 441 was struggling with financial problems. So The Recruiter approached him and played Ddakji with him. He received an invitation card and decided to join the Squid Games. He woke up in his bed in the dormitory with 454 other players. He signed the player consent form, took his photo, and proceeded to the first game Red Light, Green Light. He managed to cross the finish line in time and pass with 364 other participants. He decided to vote to end the games; received a X patch and stuck it on his chest. He grabbed his meal; ate his lunch in his bed, slept in his bed, and proceed to the next game Six-Legged Pentathlon. He teamed up with Player 366, Player 304, Player 252 and Player 271. Him and his team managed to cross the finish line in time and passed with 255 other people. After that, he decided to vote to end the games again. He slept in his bed, and then proceed to the next game Mingle. He stepped on the platform and waited for it to stop spinning. He passed the first round of Mingle 

Fate[]
He didn't manage to find a room in time and thus died during the second round of the game.
