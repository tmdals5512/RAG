Title: American Squid Games
URL: https://squid-game.fandom.com/wiki/American_Squid_Games
--------------------------------------------------
The American Squid Games represent the United States of America’s iteration of the globally infamous Squid Game franchise. Little is known about the inner workings of this version, aside from the fact that the recruitment of participants occurs in Los Angeles, California, orchestrated by the American Recruiter. Despite the scarcity of information, it stands as the most prominent and, to date, the only officially confirmed installment of the Squid Game universe set outside of South Korea, hinting at the international expansion and enduring reach of the deadly competition. It was likely the first or one the first countries to have the Squid Games outside of South Korea, possibly starting in the late 1990s or in the late 2010s, with the firsts editions were already going in 2013 (according to the squid archives in 2013 VIPs were a thing and so also international squid games).
