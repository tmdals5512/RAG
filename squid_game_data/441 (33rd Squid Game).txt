History[]
Player 441 was in tremendous debt and chose to enter the Squid Games to win money. He was picked up by Squid Game staff and taken to the island where the games take place, where he woke up on a top bunk in a large room with 455 other people. He then signed a contract and had his photo taken before proceeding to the first game. He managed to survive the first game, Red Light Green Light, after most of the contestants had been wiped out. Afterwards, like everyone else, he was shaken by the deaths of the contestants, when the prize money was finally revealed. A vote was held if the game should continue. He voted O but the majority voted no, so they were sent back home. However, he returned, along with most of the other players when the oppurtunity arose a few weeks later. 
After surviving Red Light, Green Light, Player 441 was greatly shaken by the deaths of the other players, begging the Squid Game staff to let him go when they announced the results of the game. Afterwards, a vote was held. He voted to leave the games after a narrow majority of the players voted to terminate the games, however, Player 441 was sent home along with the rest of the players. He was one of many contestants that returned when the games later resumed.  

Fate[]
In Sugar Honeycombs, he broke his dalgona and was shot by a soldier.
