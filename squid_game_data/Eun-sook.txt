Jang Eun-sook (장은숙 Jang Eun-sug?) Is the actress who portrayed Player 338, Player 113, and Player 41X in Red Light, Green Light in Season 1, as well as Player 298 and the other Player 275 in Season 2 of the Netflix TV series Squid Game.
