Hong Ui-jeong[1] (홍의정 Hong Ui-jeong?) is the South Korean stuntman who portrayed Player 079 in Sugar Honeycombs, Player 027 during Tug of War in Season 1, an unknown soldier during the Rebellion in Season 2, and Player ??? during the 28th Squid Game flashback in Season 3 of the Netflix show Squid Game.
