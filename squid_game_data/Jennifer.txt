VIP Jennifer, also designated as VIP 2, is an elite spectator who attended the 37th Squid Game, orchestrated by Hwang In-ho.
Jennifer professes a predilection for hunting and exhibits remarkable swiftness and precision, as evidenced when she intervened to rescue VIP Richard from Player 296  within the game hall of Hide and Seek. Furthermore, she demonstrates linguistic proficiency in Chinese, as illustrated when she declined VIP John invitation to drink with her. John tells Jennifer that she is more attractive with her mouth closed because she often speaks badly of him.

Personality[]
Damn that was close, good thing I like to go hunting.
VIP Jennifer after saving VIP Richard from being killed by Player 296 
Akin to the rest of the VIPs, Jennifer lacks empathy, treating players like racehorses. She took an opportunity to disguise as a Soldier and eliminate Players who failed in Hide and Seek, greatly enjoying this experience as it gave her a thrill. Jennifer also enjoyed the idea of putting Kim Jun-hee's baby in the game having called it "genius".
Although, Jennifer is shown to have some sense of fairness, as she didn't support the idea of eliminating the baby as she crossed the finish line of Jump Rope, as it would not be fair to punish someone who completed a challenge; making John tell her that  for the first time, she looked attractive with her mouth open. Moreover, Jennifer did not believe that Jang Geum-ja (Player 149) was serious about her promise to give up all her prize money to the other players if they vote "X", implying her to be deeply sceptical of people. Jennifer was noticeably not surprised when the final members of Team O betrayed each other as she views packs as something that never lasts. Additionally, she lacked understanding of why Seong Gi-hun (Player 456) took the baby with him while crossing the bridge during Jump Rope.
She appears somewhat oriented in popular culture, as she corrects VIP Kevin by informing him that Wonder Woman is from DC, not Marvel.

Trivia[]
Although she can speak Mandarin in the play, it is not standard.
She is the only Female VIP shown in the show.
She is one of the two VIPs who is heard speaking a different language than English, namely Mandarin, the other being the Deer Mask VIP from Season 1.
She is the youngest VIP out of all VIP, Even Younger than Hwang In-ho himself.
She along with VIP Richard, VIP Jack, VIP Kevin and Hwang Jun-ho are the only characters to be shown as a temporary guard.
Her and Kang No-eul were the only female guards shown in the entire series, though Jennifer only took the role briefly.
She can be somewhat similar to VIP 4 from Squid Game Season 1 since when she said "I'd rather drink with my footstool rather then you" to VIP John, It can be highly implied she had a sex with her Footstool, a thing VIP 4 tried to do with Hwang Jun-ho in Season 1.
