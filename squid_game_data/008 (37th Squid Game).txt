History[]
Not much is known about Player 008, except for he was in large debt and decided to join the Squid Games. After managing to cross the finish line in Red Light, Green Light, he decided to vote "O" to continue the games. 
In the second game, Player 008 searched for partners to complete his team. He was later on able to find enough teammates; Player 116, Player 131, Player 154, and Player 177. He played Gonggi for his team, and passed along with his teammates. After the game, Player 008 switched his vote to "X". 

Fate[]
In Mingle he managed to pass the first round because he joined a group of people and survived, and his number was not announced as a casualty in the first round, but in the second round, his team abandoned him and he was shot by soldiers because he could not find a room and three other people, and in the third round, his icon was darkened.

Behind the scenes[]
Player 008 was portrayed by Roh In-cheol and Lee Seung-hwa.

Trivia[]
Roh In-cheol was one of the only recurring minor actors from Season 1 who returned in Season 2 playing this player.
He is the only player in the 00X range who is not seen voting during the second vote.
He was the only player in the 00X range who died in the Mingle.
He outlasted his 33rd Squid Game counterpart competition wise.
He was the first unnamed player to change his vote to X.
He is the only background (and least relevant) player in the 00X range to pass Red Light, Green Light.
In a conversation with a fan, Roh In-cheol revealed his vision of Player 008's backstory[2].
He is the only one on his pentathlon team whose player number does not contain the number 1.
