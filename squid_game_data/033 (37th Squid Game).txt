Player 034







Player 033 was in heavy debt and financial stress so he chosed to enter the Squid Games to win money after playing Ddakji with The Recruiter. After waking up in a large room with 454 other players, he signed a contract after the staff convinced him over the final prize money, he went to stairs to take his icon and entered the Red Light, Green Light arena.

Fate[]
He died in Red Light Green Light after being detected by Young-hee and shot by a sniper after the panic.

Trivia[]
A player named Bae Dong-il won the 25th Squid Games in 2012 with this number.
