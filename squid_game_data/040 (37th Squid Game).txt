History[]
Player 040 was in massive debt and decided to join the Squid Games for a chance to win a huge cash prize. He called the number on the recruiting card and got into a mysterious car. He then woke up in a giant room with 454 other players. He then made his way to the center of the room and watched as many other players debts were revealed in front of him. Later on, he signed a contract and made his way up a flight of stairs to the first game.
During the first game, he was soon terrified as players were shot in front of him during the mass panic. He was able to control himself and stand still. He eventually completed the first game after listening to Gi-hun's commands. After returning to the dormitory, many players pleaded the guards to let them go. However, after a massive increase in the prize pool was revealed, Player 040 changed his mind and decided to vote O during the first vote.
Later during Six-Legged Pentathlon, Player 040 partnered up with Player 127, Player 200, Player 213, and Player 314. He played Biseokchigi for his team and managed to complete it, along with his teammates within the 5 minute timer. After successfully completing Six-Legged Pentathlon, him and the other players held another vote. However, instead of voting O for a second time, Player 040 voted X after presumably reaching the amount of money he owed.
Later on during Mingle, Player 040 managed to partner up with other players and pass the first round without any trouble. After round 1, he made his way back onto the carousel and prepared for round 2.

Fate[]
He was shot by the soldiers for not entering a room in time in Round 2 of Mingle.

Trivia[]
Both him and his 33rd Squid Game counterpart has a line.
He is the only male 37th Squid Game player in the X40 part since Player 140, 240, 340 and Player 440 were females.
