History[]
Player 014, like the other contestants, joined the Squid Games due to being in significant debt. He, along with 364 other players, made it past the first game. During the vote on whether to end or continue the games, he voted to end the games. After being forced to stay in the games, he slept and went on to the next game arena the next day. In the Six-Legged Pentathlon, he joined a team.
After Kwon Byeong-su (047) starts screaming at the "O" players after Teams 1 and 2 get eliminated, 014 adds to 047's arguments, questioning the O's decision to continue.

Fate[]
However, he and his team didn't finish on time and got gunned down during Jegi.

Mistakes[]
When Player 014 died after failing the Six-Legged Pentathlon, he was portrayed by a woman named Ha Seul-gi.
Player 014 can be briefly seen as an older man in BTS.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 014 was portrayed primarily by Park Seok-hyun but was also portrayed by stuntwoman Ha Seul-gi in the character's death scene and an unknown extra in a bts.
