DJ van der Groove, also known as Player 174, is a DJ from the Netherlands who competed in the Squid Games.

In-game description[]
DJ van der Groove spins more delusions than records as he chases dreams of EDM stardom through the streets of Amsterdam. Dropping euros faster than beats, he sinks every cent into gold-plated turntables and VIP passes.
But after he breaks the bank chasing his big break, it's time for DJ to face the music as his creditors compose a requiem for his career.
The next party DJ attends, the beats aren't just sick–they're lethal.
