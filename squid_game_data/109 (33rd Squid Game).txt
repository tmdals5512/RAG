History[]
Player 109 was in heavy debt and chose to enter the Squid Games to win money. After waking up in a large room with 455 other players, he signed a contract and took his photo. He managed to survive the first game, Red Light, Green Light, after most of the contestants had been wiped out. Afterwards, like everyone else, he was shaken by the deaths of the contestants, when the prize money was finally revealed. A vote was held if the game should continue, and the majority voted no. However, those who were willing to return could, and she returned along with 186 other players, subsequently surviving Sugar Honeycombs, as well as the Special Game that occurred between games.

Fate[]
In Tug of War, Player 109 and his team got pulled off by Team 6, causing them to fall off to their deaths off-screen.
