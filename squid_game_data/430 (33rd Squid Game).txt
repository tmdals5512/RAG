History[]
Player 430 was in a heavy debt and chose to enter the Squid Games to win money, After waking up in a large room with 455 other players, he signed a contract and took his photo and entered the first game called Red Light, Green Light.

Fate[]
Player 430 was probably among the players eliminated during the initial chaos that occurred with Player 324’s death, because he isn’t seen after the initial chaos.

Trivia[]
IMDb listed that Player 430 was portrayed by Nick Tonez, though there isn’t proof there is. This is likely an error on IMDb’s part.
Mistakes[]
