Choi Jae-ok (최재옥 Choe Jae-og?) is the South Korean stuntman who portrayed Player 272 (Rlgl) Player 314 (Death scene) in Season 1, Player 020 (Stunt) Player 032 (Death scene) in Season 2 and Lee Myung-gi in Season 2 and 3 of Squid Game.
