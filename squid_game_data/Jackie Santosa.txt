Jakiah "Jackie" Santosa, also known as Player 066, is a criminal from Indonesia who competed in the Squid Games.

In-game description[]
Jakiah 'Jackie' Santosa has a devil-may-care attitude and couldn't care less what you call her - unless of course, you call her crazy. The last time somebody called her crazy, that ended with Jackie being incarcerated in Indonesia's toughest prison.
But you can't keep a free spirit like Jackie down - after escaping, she's been living a life on the run.
They say don't stare too long into the abyss and that same advice applies when you look into Jackie's mischievous eyes.
