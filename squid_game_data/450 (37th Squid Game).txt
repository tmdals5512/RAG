History[]
Player 450 was in massive debt and decided to join the Squid Games for a chance to win a huge cash prize. He called the number on the recruiting card, and got into a mysterious car. He then woke up in a giant room with 454 other players. He then made his way to the center of the room and watched as many other players debts were revealed in front of him. Later on, he signed a contract and made his way up a flight of stairs to the first game.
During the first game, He heard Gi-hun screaming about how dangerous the games were. Player 450 and many other players ignored his warnings, and continued the game without any worry. However, He was soon terrified as players were shot in front of him during the mass panic. Player 450 luckily was able to control himself and stand still. He eventually completed the first game after listening to Gi-hun's commands. After returning to the dormitory with the other surviving players, Player 450 begged the guards to let him and the other players go. However, after a massive increase in the prize pool was revealed, Player 450 changed his mind and decided to vote O during the first vote. Him and the other Os then cheered after they won the vote.
Later during the Six-Legged Pentathlon, Player 450 partnered up with Player 348, Player 072, Player 312, and Player 324. Him and his team then managed to pass all 5 games within the 5 minute time limit. During the second vote, he decided to vote O again along with many other O players.
Later on during Mingle, Player 450 made his way onto the carousel and prepared for round 1.

Fate[]
During round 1 of Mingle, Player 450 was shot by the soldiers after failing to enter a room with the correct amount of players.

Behind the scenes[]
Player 450 was portrayed by Lee Jong-hag.

Trivia[]
He is the only recurring player on his pentathlon team.
He is the first player to start a tie in first vote. (3 X to 3 O)
He is second last player that voted O in second vote and also the player with the highest number that chanted for One More Game
