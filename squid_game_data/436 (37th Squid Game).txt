History[]
Player 436 was in debt and decided to join the Squid Games. He woke up in the dormitory and listened the manager, who came to welcome new players. When the full name and debt amount of Player 100, Im Jeong-dae, were read aloud, and the footage of him playing ddakji has been played, Player 436 smiled. When Im Jeong-dae finished his speech on the protection of himself, Player 436 said something aloud, most likely to himself. Later, Player 436 smiled, seeing piggy bank lowering down and was smiling, seeing two players near him, Park Yong-sik and Jang Geum-ja, fighting.
Before the first game, Red Light, Green Light, he was standing behind Player 196, Kang Mi-na, and Player 230, Thanos. Player 456, Seong Gi-hun, tried to warn the others about the danger of the game, but most players didn't believe him. When Kang Mi-na repeated Gi-hun's words to Thanos, Player 436 heard them, smiled and rhetorically asked himself aloud if the players would be shot.
When the game started, he advanced behind Thanos. After seven rounds of the game, Player 436 heard yet another Gi-hun's warning and smiled. He continued smiling when Kang Mi-na and Thanos discussed if Gi-hun is high and when Thanos told Mi-na that she has a bee crawling on her neck. When Player 196 panicked, moved and got shot, Player 436 followed her collapse on the ground with his eyes, but didn't try to flee. He didn't move when Lee Eun-ju noticed the dead body of Kang Mi-na, screamed and moved, which ended with her death and mass panic, caused by it. However, he moved when Player 407, who was trying to flee, pushed him, and got killed by a sniper.
