History[]
Player 032 was in heavy debt and so chose to participate in the Squid Games. After waking up in a room with 454 other players, he signed a contract and took his photo. He managed to survive the first game, Red Light, Green Light. In the post-game poll, he voted for X.

Fate[]
During the Six-Legged Pentathlon, despite successfully completing all five mini-games, he and his four teammates were eliminated and shot dead when time expired just before the finish line.

Behind the scenes[]
Player 032 was portrayed by Jim Seong-Kim and Choi Jae-ok.
