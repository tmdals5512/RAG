Player 039 was a participant during the 37th Squid Game. He was one of the finalists of the competition.
A timid and generally passive person, Player 039 began voting to continue the games out of fear of Team O after the Special Game. He was a member of an alliance consisting of Kim Yun-tae, Kim Gi-min, and Im Jeong-dae.
During the final game, his teammates turned on him and beat him up, calling him Lunchbox and designating him as a sacrifice.

Personality[]
And, its mother's gone. Don't you think it feels right that we... just you know... ended its life now painlessly. Wouldn't you agree?
Player 039 telling Seong Gi-hun to let go of the baby in "⭘△☐"Player 039 was timid and cowardly in more of self-preservation rather than fear of the games, because after the Special Game he voted for O, out of fear even after Hide and Seek where he voted first. He chose to vote O unwillingly again out of fear instead of taking his chances, that his vote could influence people to change and stick with his X vote. Showing the extent of his cowardness despite a new-born baby now being in the game and being at risk showing he prioritized his own life. While it's unclear if he felt any guilt for unwillingly voting O or knew it was morally the wrong thing to do. During the democratic vote in Sky Squid Game it was clear that he was the most passive among all and was initially hesitant to go along with the other Os' plan when they were discussing about the final round beforehand. It's also noted that his cowardness is more for self-preservation.  
Despite being a coward, he still has elements of cruelty in his behavior, since he agreed to kill a baby with the other Os, although he likely thought it was his only way out as this action doesn't fit his cowardly nature. He was shown to be the least heinous out of Im Jeong-dae's Group during the final game. Unlike the other finalists, Player 039 never seems to want to continue playing and appears to just want a way out as soon as possible. He wasn't likely inclined to be against Gi-hun for the final game, just as he may not want to kill the baby or Min-su.  
Player 039 is shown to be strategically intelligent in some ways however, as he seems to show respect to anyone who kills someone like Gi-min or Hyun-ju, showing himself to be subdued and passive in order to avoid angering those who are clearly stronger than him. He still follows Jeong-dae's instructions because he believes that, given his personality, including voting O out of fear again after Hide and Seek, shows he believes he has no chance of surviving without strong allies, even though he didn't agree to such plan.  
Despite this however like all unwilling O voters he clearly didn't realize how dangerous it was to align with Im Jeong-dae's Group and Team O. As he didn't consider that because of their psychopathy and greed, they would be willing to attack and betray their own team members for their own benefit. As he was shocked when his supposed allies turned on him so suddenly without any regard for his life. After the rest of his former team was eliminated, he chose to have some self-respect by killing himself as he didn't want to be a "lunchbox". After the deaths of Players 203 and 353, he heard 333 say that his child and Gi-hun would each have 15.2 billion without Jeong-dae. He realized they still wanted to use him as a lunchbox to beat the game. After Player 100's death, he decided to throw himself down because he didn't want to be sacrificed and because of the excruciating pain, leaving the child of Players 333 and 456 alone. 

History[]
Player 039 was in heavy debt and decided to join the Squid Games. He woke up in a Dormitory along with 454 other players, then 039 proceeded to sign a contract and then took his photo. He made his way to Red Light, Green Light, where he manages to pass. He voted X. Later during the Six-Legged Pentathlon he partnered with four other players and his team passed all 5 games. He voted X again in the second vote. Later during Mingle he survived all 5 rounds and votes  X again. He survives the Special Game and doesn’t join the Rebellion.

For the re-vote, he voted unwillingly O out of fear. In Hide and Seek, he was placed on the Seekers team. He was briefly seen looking at Cho Hyun-ju killing Player 202, and decided to walk away. Later on, Player 039 managed to kill an unknown hider, and was able to pass the game. He was the first one to vote after Hide and Seek and voted to continue again likely out of fear of Team O. During Jump Rope, he was stuck on the bridge while Player 096 held up the line, however he was able to endure and pass the game along with 7 other players and the baby, making him a finalist.But we don't know exactly what the game will be yet, so... And besides, they told us more than three could go. 
Player 039 telling his team after the Final Dinner in "222"When the staff came in, they announced that there were 9 players. The group was confused, saying that there were 8. When he saw that the baby was another player, he advocated for the death of the baby alongside most of the other finalists in fear of getting singled out. He enjoyed the dinner, along with the other players. when it was his turn in the last round of Voting, Im Jeong-dae asked Player 039 if he really needed to vote considering that Team O already had a majority; Player 039 nervously said that he was already going to vote O and freely abstained, leaving his vote uncounted. He was a part of Im Jeong-dae's team for the final game.
Everyone, relax, okay? We said we would all stick together until the very end!
Player 039 to Im Jeong-dae's team during the second round of Sky Squid Game in "⭘△☐"
In Sky Squid Game, during the first round, he said it was better to throw out Player 222, as it was unfair that a baby also had the chance to get part of the prize without doing anything. In the end, Myung-gi dissuaded him and the others from this idea, and they instead chose on eliminating Min-su. When the second round started, after failed attempts to separate the Gi-hun and the baby, they decided to follow Myung-gi's new plan: the 6 of them connected themselves using their own jackets, and the player in the front, 336, would grab the baby from Gi-hun's arms, and 333 would push him off the tower. What they didn't expect is that Player 456 had a knife, given to him by the Frontman in the previous night. With it, he cut the jacket that was holding 336 together with the others, which caused them to fall back. Player 039, who was at the back, almost fell from the tower. Another surprise came when Myung-gi betrayed them all by pushing player 336 from the tower, instead of Gi-hun.
The remaining players were confused at first, but after learning he was the kid's father, they had to come up with a new plan. Suddenly, Gi-min started beating player 039, while the other two held him in place. While they kicked the man, who was now laying on the ground, player 100 explained he would be beaten to a pulp, then dragged to the next tower and thrown off, That way, everyone else would get their shares of the prize, and nobody would have to fight. 039 crawls on Gi-hun's direction, begging for help, only to be kicked in the nose by 203, who got annoyed by the man's persistence. He asked 353 to hold his left leg, and then broke it by stomping on it, causing 039 to scream in pain. Gi-min then slapped his face and deemed him a "Lunchbox". Gi-hun disagrees on the plan, telling everyone to draw lots, except for the baby, but while he's distracted, 203 lunges into him, and a fight breaks out as 353 also attacks 333.

Fate[]
I'm sorry, but I.... I really don't wanna be your lunchbox. You guys can talk to each other all you want. I'm done with this.
Player 039's final words before ending his own life during the second round of Sky Squid Game in "⭘△☐"
After Gi-min and Yun-tae are murdered by Gi-hun and Myung-gi, respectively, the latter then follows by pushing Jeong-dae to his death, right before the timer ends. Player 039, noticing he's now the lunchbox of Myung-gi and Gi-hun, crawls to the edge of the tower while they are distracted. When they notice, Myung-gi desperately tells him that they're not planning on doing that, and says "let's talk about it". Despite his attempts to de-escalate the situation, Player 039 voluntarily falls off the platform to his death. He placed 4th in the competition.

Trivia[]
According to Lee Myung-gi's plan, he gave up his jacket last to make a long safety rope, but Gi-hun took his jacket and cut it. This action foreshadowed Player 039's fate, as he was the one who was beaten half to death in that same round and was about to be used as a lunchbox.
Player 039 is the last surviving player and the only finalist, excluding, Jun-hee's baby whose name was unknown.
He is the only finalist who did not vote in last round of voting. (excluding Jun-hee's baby)
He and Jun-hee's baby are the only unnamed players that made it to the Sky Squid Game.
He was the last player in the 37th Squid Game to have two digits in their number, also he is the only player to have two digits as a finalist.
Player 039 is the only finalist that isn't heard speaking in the first five games.
He was the only O Voter albeit unwilling that committed suicide.
He was the penultimate O Voter to die, as well as the last player who voted O unwillingly.
He was the last person to be eliminated on the second platform in Sky Squid Game.
He seemed to be afraid of Kim Gi-min, and he would flinch when Kim Gi-min addressed him, and he would get scared when Kim Gi-min looked at him. Kim Gi-min noticed this and scared him, and he may have come up with the idea of using him because he was the weakest, and he told Jeong-dae and Yun-tae about it.
When he fell, his left leg, which had been broken by Kim Gi-min's heavy stomp, was bent and his thigh bone was exposed, making his death one of the most brutal and painful.
Player 039 seemed to have some friendship with Player 172, since in several scenes in the dormitory they are seen together, it is possible that apart from fear, he also voted O to not break his friendship with him.
This would make their friendship to appear to be the last remaining alliance to Team X they both would have. As all unwilling O voters appeared to have been distant from their remaining Team X allies as a consequence due to their disappointment in their cowardness. As none of Team X are seen with them again after the revote leaving them mostly on their own or with the dangerous alliance with Team O, further suggesting unwillingly voting O wasn't the right choice.
Additionally, his friend, Player 172 was killed by Player 203 during Hide and Seek, which could also explain why 039 is afraid of Player 203 [he would also know that Gi-min was the one who eliminated Player 172 because the announcer announces the Hider taken out and the Tagger who got them].
When all the other players were wanting to kill the baby for decreasing their shares which he also protested with it is likely he didn’t want to kill the baby and was only trying to fit in.
This is evident when he was the last one to shout his "desire" to kill the baby, showing he is  not genuine and more so trying to fit in.
During the dinner and final vote when Jeong-dae said that they should end the voting before Player 039 could vote he then asked if he needed to vote which 039 declined to stating that he "was gonna vote O anyway." It is likely that he was lying when he said that and actually wanted to vote X but still felt intimidated by Jeong-dae and his gang and by Kim Gi-min [Player 203] in particular, and also realised that with the 0s having a majority vote his vote for X would not be able to stop the sixth game from happening.
In the French version of Squid Game, Player 039's first line comes after Player 203 tells Player 100 to get on the jump rope bridge, saying they had to figure out who would go first on the bridge but in reality it was actually Player 435 who said it.
He is the only supporting character finalist to not have another actor in The Second Game, as Lee Seung-won, Kim Gi-min, Kim Yun-tae all had different actors.
Player 039 is the only player that voted X, O and to abstent.
He is the last player in the 03X range to be eliminated in the 37th games.
Player 039 was a former Team X voter who voted O unwillingly in fear. Ironically like other unwilling O voters 172, 191 and 448 it was for nothing. This Team O alliance leads to him being betrayed, badly beaten to near death and the plan to sacrifice him in the final round.
Player 039's decision to unwillingly vote O after Hide and Seek. Likely only influenced more people to vote O unwillingly as he was the first voter after hide and seek which decreased the chances of players voting for X which helped insure Team X wouldn't win the vote.
In the aftermath of Hide and Seek. If Player 039 along with Player 448 another unwilling O voter who survived Hide and Seek. Along with Players 260 and 435 who judging by their expressions also didn't want to play anymore all had the courage to change their votes. This would've been the result 12 (X) and 12 (O) another tie and if Gi-hun voted it would've been 13 (X) and 12. (O) This did suggest if players had made their own decisions and not give into fear Team X could've won the 4th round of voting.
Since Player 039 placed 4th in the competition, his 33rd Squid Game and 28th Squid Game counterparts was Do Jeong-soo and Player 3??.
If the lunchbox plan were to be used, another five people would've survived, the five being Jeong-dae, Gi-min, Myung-gi, Yun-tae and Gi-hun (in addition to the baby who survived anyway).
According to a list of uncertain origins he played Ddakji in the Six-Legged Pentathlon, making him the last living adult player who played Ddakji there.
Mistakes[]
Player 039 is shown with a completely clean knife when he encounters Cho Hyun-ju despite the fact every single hider with an unknown killer's corpse is seen before this scene. This either means one of the hiders was meant to die later or he was meant to have blood on his knife in the scene.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 039 was portrayed by Woo Jung-kook. He is credited as "도시락남 (dosiraknam)", which translates to "lunchbox man," which his fellow contestants called him before beating him up in the final game.
