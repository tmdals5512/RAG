Kwon Gu-nam (권구남 Gwon Gu-nam?, Kwŏn Kunam) is a South Korean actor who portrayed Park Pil-sam in Red Light, Green Light, Player 326 (error), Player 372 and Player 403 (Error) in Season 1 and Season 2 (Player 332) of the Netflix TV series Squid Game.

Trivia[]
Every player he plays has a 3 in their number.
