Yun Hyun-ju (윤현주 Yun Hyeon-ju?) is the South Korean actress who portrayed Player 380, Player 150 in Red Light, Green Light, Player 070 before Marbles, and the icons of Chae Gang-sun and Player 109, Player 454, and Player 371 in the Netflix television series Squid Game in Season 1. She has also portrayed Player 248 in Mingle of Season 2.
