History[]
Player 066 was in massive debt and decided to join the Squid Games happening in 2024 after being contacted by staff. In Red Light, Green Light, when Seong Gi-hun (Player 456) tried to tell the other players that if they moved they would be killed, the players thought Gi-hun was crazy and 066 said that he thought Gi-hun was trying to scare them so he could keep the money for himself. 

Fate[]
Player 066 was among the many that ran to the doors after panic spread through the back-right portion of the field following Lee Eun-ju's death. Player 066 realized those who weren't moving weren't being shot and he abruptly stopped, but he already broke the rules of the game and Soldier 11 shot him in the head when he stood still. 

Trivia[]
Both he and his counterpart from the first season had lines among the few supporting characters.
Ironically while his season 1 counterpart managed to survive the games by voting to leave and never coming back, he himself didn't even manage to make it to the first voting round.
Both him and his season 1 counterpart was deeply mistrustful of someone, Season 1's being the game organizers and Season 2's being Gi-hun.
A player named Sin Seong-yeong won the 11th Squid Games in 1998 with this number.
He’s the only known player to be killed by Kang No-eul during the panic.
He is the last player eliminated in the mass panic.
It is somewhat probably based on his attitude to Gi-hun about the money that he would have voted 0 if he had survived this game but this can not be said for certain.
