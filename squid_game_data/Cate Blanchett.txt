Cate Blanchett is an Australian actor and producer who played the American Recruiter in Season 3 of the Netflix TV series Squid Game. She has received numerous awards and is regarded as one of the best performers of her generation. She is known for her many roles in various movies and TV shows, including The Lord Of The Rings trilogy (2001-2003) and  Indiana Jones and the Kingdom of the Crystal Skull (2008).
