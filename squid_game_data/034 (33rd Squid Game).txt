Player 035







Player 034 was in heavy debt and chose to enter a high-risk competition to win money. After waking up in a large room with 455 other people, he witnessed the fight between Jang Deok-su and Kang Sae-byeok. After this, he signed a consent form and entered the first game. 

Fate[]
In Red Light, Green Light he ran to the doors in panic, causing him to be shot by a sniper.
