History[]
Player 452 was presumably in massive debt and decided to join the Squid Games for a chance to win a huge cash prize. He called the number on the recruiting card, and got into a mysterious car. He then woke up in a giant room with 454 other players. He then made his way to the center of the room and watched as many other players debts were revealed in front of him. Later on, he signed a contract and made his way up a flight of stairs to the first game. 
During the first game, he heard Gi-hun screaming about how dangerous the games were. Player 452 and many other players ignored his warnings, and continued the game without any worry. However, He was soon terrified as players were shot in front of him during the mass panic. Player 452 luckily was able to control himself and stand still. He eventually completed the first game after listening to Gi-hun's commands. After returning to the dormitory with the other surviving players, Player 452 begged the guards to let him and the other players go. However, the guards instead showed everyone the massive increase in prize money that accumulated after the first game. They then explained the voting process to all of the players. Player 452 did not care about the money and decided to Vote X during the first Vote. Unfortunately for him and the other Xs, more players voted to continue the games -- they would have to continue the games. 
Later during Six-Legged Pentathlon, Player 452 decided to Player 086, Player 041, Player 193, Player 387. Him and his team then watched as many teams failed and passed the game.

Fate[]
Player 452 wasn't seen after Six-Legged Pentathlon, which means he was killed at some point during the game.

Trivia[]
His 33rd Squid Game counterpart also made it to the second game and died in it.
He was the first one to vote after the one who voted the same as him.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 452 was portrayed by Seo Jun-geol, Yeo Myeong-hwa and 2 unknown extras.
