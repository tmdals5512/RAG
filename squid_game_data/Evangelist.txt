This unnamed man is an evangelist who religiously proselytizes in the street. In "One Lucky Day", he encounters Seong Gi-hun after he wins the Squid Games.

History[]
He finds Gi-hun in the street on a rainy night when he is dumped from a limo after winning Squid Game. He had posters that promoted Jesus is Lord. He said that to Gi-hun when he gained consciousness.
