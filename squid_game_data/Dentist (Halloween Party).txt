History[]
This unnamed man who is Dentist a doctor who fitted Seong Gi-hun with a fake tooth with a tracker so Hwang Jun-ho and Choi Woo-seok could track him when Gi-hun re-entered the Squid Games. The Dentist asked Gi-hun to open his mouth, he pulled out a tooth and fitted the false tooth with the tracker. After completing the work, Woo-seok handed the Dentist an envelope full of money in exchange for the latter keeping quiet about the appointment, to which the Dentist indicated he would.

Behind the Scenes[]
The Dentist was portrayed by Jung Jong-yul.
He looks a lot like the icon Player 311.
