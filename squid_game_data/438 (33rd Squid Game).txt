Player 439







Player 438 was in high debt, so he decided to enter the Squid Game. he woke up in a room with 455 other contestants; after watching the fight between Kang Sae-byeok and Jang Deok-su and listened to the Guard's speech and he signed a contract, him took the photo and was then taken to the first game. 

Fate[]
Player 438 panicked and ran towards the doors, then he was shot by a sniper. We weren't able to see his icon face however as it is too far down to see.

Mistakes[]
Despite dying in Red Light, Green Light, his icon is still lit way after red light, green light.
