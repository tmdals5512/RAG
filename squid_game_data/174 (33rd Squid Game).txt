History[]
Most of his history is unknown, but we do know that he attended an unknown school, has two unknown family members and had one job in his working career. He got into severe debt for 4 unknown reasons, and joined the Squid Games. After waking up, he signed a contract, took his photo, and headed up the stairs to the first game.

Fate[]
Park Do-seok tried to run after the death of Player 306, but was sniped down shortly after.

Trivia[]
His resident registration number is 880507 - 1XXXXXX.
In the 1st Squid Game in 1988, a player named No Hyun-woo won with this number.
He has the least screentime if you don't count the players whose icons were never displayed on show.
Mistakes[]
Park Do-seok's file was shown to the Frontman after the return of the games despite dying in Red Light, Green Light; it is unknown if this was intentional or not.
His file icon is the same as Players 064 and 352.
