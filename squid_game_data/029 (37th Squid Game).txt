History[]
Player 029 was presumably in massive debt and decided to join the Squid Games for a chance to win a huge cash prize. He called the number on the recruiting card, and got into a mysterious car. He then woke up in a giant room with 454 other players. He then made his way to the center of the room and watched as many other players debts were revealed in front of him. Later on, he signed a contract and made his way up a flight of stairs to the first game.
During the first game, Player 029 heard Gi-hun screaming about how dangerous the games were. Player 029 and many other players ignored his warnings, and continued the game without any worry. However, he was soon terrified as players were shot in front of him during the mass panic. Luckily, he was able to control himself and stand still. He eventually completed the first game after listening to Gi-hun's commands. After returning to the dormitory with the other surviving players, Player 029 begged the guards to let him and the other players go. However, after a massive increase in the prize pool was revealed, he changed his mind and decided to vote O during the first vote. Him and the other Os then cheered after they won the vote.
Later during Six-Legged Pentathlon, Player 029 decided to partner up with players 021, 147, 410, and 447. They then managed to complete all 5 minigames within the 5 minute timer. After successfully completing Six-Legged Pentathlon, him and the other players held another vote. However, instead of voting O for a second time, Player 029 voted X after reaching the amount of money he owed. Unfortunately for him and the other Xs, more players voted to continue. Which meant they would have to continue the games.
Later on during Mingle, Player 029 managed to partner up with other players and pass the first 2 rounds without any trouble. After the second round, he made his way back onto the carousel and prepared for the third round.

Fate[]
During the third round of Mingle, Player 029 was shot after failing to find a room with the correct amount of players.

Mistakes[]
In the first vote, his vote was counted for X, but he was actually an O voter during Round 1.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 029 was portrayed by Kim Yun-bong and Lee Tae-yeong.
