Kim Sung-yong (김성용 Gim Seong-yong?) is the actor, who portrayed Player 310, Player 243 in the Tug of War lobby, Player 417 in Tug Of War, and Player 068 before Marbles in Season 1 of the Netflix TV series Squid Game.
