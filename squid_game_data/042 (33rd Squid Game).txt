History[]
Player 042 was in debt, so he chose to enter the Squid Games after playing Ddakji with The Recruiter. He got transported to the island and the following morning, he woke up in a dormitory with 455 other people. Shortly after, he witnessed the fight between Players 067, 101 and later 456. The manager entered the dormitory and gave a speech to the players and after that, Player 042 and others have signed a contract, took their picture and entered the Red Light, Green Light game area. 
He managed to survive the first game, and likely was shaken by the deaths of the contestants. After the the prize money was revealed, the vote was held whether the games should be continued or not. It is unknown what he voted, as we don't see him in that scene, but he was sent home after the majority voted X.
Although, when opportunity arised, Player 042 returned to the games along with 186 other players. 2 days after making this decision, he was proceeded to the Sugar Honeycombs game area.

Fate[]
During the middle of the Sugar Honeycombs, he broke his dalgona. This caused him being shot dead by a soldier.

Mistakes[]
A female Player 042 can be seen behind Player 222 during waiting in line to sign a contract.
An elderly male Player 042 can be seen during Red Light, Green Light.
Another Player 042 can be seen getting shot during Sugar Honeycombs.
Player 042 shares an icon with Player 328.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 042 was portrayed by Moon Ki-hwan, Lee Hak-ryeol, and two unknown extras.
