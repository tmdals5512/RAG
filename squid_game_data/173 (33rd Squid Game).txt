History[]
Player 173 was in high debt, so he decided to enter the Squid Games. He woke in a large room with 455 other players. He then signed his contract, took his photo and was then taken to the first game, Red Light, Green Light.

Fate[]
Player 173 moved during the initial chaos of Red Light, Green Light, causing him to be detected and shot. The proof is that his icon disappears after Red Light, Green Light.
