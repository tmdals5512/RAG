History[]
Player 030 was presumably in massive debt and decided to join the Squid Games for a chance to win a huge cash prize. He called the number on the recruiting card, and got into a mysterious car. He then woke up in a giant room with 454 other players. He was likely confused and decided to make his way into the center of the room, where he watched as many other players debts were revealed in front of him. Later on, he listened to the manager explain the rules of the games, he signed a contract along with the other players, he took his photo in the photo booth, and he made his way up a flight of stairs to the first game.
During Red Light, Green Light, Player 030 and the other players watched as a strange man started screaming about how dangerous the games were. Player 030 and the other players ignored Gi-Hun's warnings, as they thought he was crazy and wanted the money for himself. Player 030 and the other players then continued the game without any worry. However, after sounds of gunshots ran out, and players started getting gunned down, Player 030 realized Gi-hun was serious about the danger. Luckily for him, he managed to stand still during the panic, and continued to follow Gi-hun's directions.

Trivia[]
Player 030 replaced Player 162 on his team during the start of Six-Legged Pentathlon.
He is the only player to die in Red Light, Green Light, but go onto erroneously appear in the Six-Legged Pentathlon, as a pose to the others, who appear in Mingle, Bathroom Fight BTS, and Special Game.
Mistakes[]
Player 030 is seen crossing the finish line, despite his corpse already being shown.
Player 030 is one of the last players to be eliminated in Red light, Green light.
Player 030 is also seen looking at the piggy bank, and even during the voting during Team X's side.
Player 030 is seen in the Six-Legged Pentathlon searching for teammates and sitting with his erroneous team. (consisting of players 171, 176, 191, and 279).
