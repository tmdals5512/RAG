This Unnamed Man also known as Deer Mask VIP was one of the third of the VIPs attending the 33rd Squid Game hosted by Oh Il-nam. During the fifth game, he complains that it's no fun, in reaction to the ability of Do Jung-soo (Player 017) to detect which glass panes are safe. He says few lines and hasn’t been seen since after the 33rd Squid Game in 2020 ever again.

Personality[]
While watching the final game, he recites a saying in Chinese "好雨知时节",that translates into "Good rain knows the best time to fall", originally coined by poet Du Fu (Chinese: 杜甫).

Trivia[]
Most fans thought Min-su was the Deer Mask VIP because his actor "David S. Lee" had a similar name to Min-su's actor, "Lee David" only for them to realise that they’re not the same person.
Behind the scenes[]
The VIP is portrayed by David S. Lee. He is credited as "VIP 3".
