Ball in a Cup is a game featured as the first mini-game in the Six-Legged Pentathlon in Squid Game: The Challenge Season 2.

Game Information[]
Objective: The player in charge of this mini-game must get the ball, attached by a string to the base of the cup, into the cup through a whipping motion to launch it over the brim. Once the player gets the ball into the cup, the team passes this mini-game.
The player is not allowed to touch the ball, or any part of the cup besides the handle.
