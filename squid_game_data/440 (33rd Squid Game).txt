Player 441







Player 440 was in heavy debt and chose to enter a high-risk competition to win money. After waking up in a large room with 455 other people, he signed a contract, took his photo and entered the Red Light, Green Light arena.

Fate[]
His true fate is unknown. In Red Light, Green Light, he panicked to the doors and was shot since his body was shown there. But he is also seen numerous times after this. Being seen in the voting, Sugar Honeycombs, and the Special Game.
