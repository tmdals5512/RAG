History[]
Player 032 was in a heavy debt and chose to enter the Squid Games to win money. After waking up in a large room with 455 other players, he signed a contract and took his photo. He managed to survive Red Light, Green Light. Player 032, like everyone else, was afraid that the players would die. Then the prize money was shown. During the vote, it was unkown how he voted but everyone was forced off the island. He opted to return to compete. He subsequently survived Sugar Honeycombs, the Special Game and Tug of War. He teamed up with Jang Deok-Su's team in the meantime. For the 4th game, he chose a partner but then it was revealed that the game was Marbles and that everybody would face their partners. 

Fate[]
Player 032 teamed up with Player 083, however, they either cheated or didn't manage to finish on time, getting them both killed.
