History[]
Player 093 entered the Squid Game due to her inability to pay off her debts. After waking up in a room with 455 other people, she signed a consent form, took her photo, and went upstairs to the arena of the first game called Red Light, Green Light, which she managed to pass. A vote was later held on whether the games should continue or not, and the majority voted to stop them. However, when the opportunity to return arose, she returned with 186 other players. She then went to the second game called Sugar Honeycombs. 

Fate[]
Her death would be unknown/debatable.
