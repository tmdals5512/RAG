This unnamed Homeless man is a man living in the United States of America.

History[]
Almost nothing is known about this man, except that After Hwang In-ho returns to America to bring Seong Gi-hun's tracksuit to his daughter, he can be seen playing Ddakji with the American recruiter. This probably mean that he was later given the card to play in games, but it's unknown if he accepted to call the number and play or continued to live in the streets.
