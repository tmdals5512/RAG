Player 042







Player 041 was in heavy debt and chose to enter the Squid Games to win money. After waking up in a large room with 454 other players, signed a contract and took his photo.
He walks up the stairs to get ready for the first game. He then hears Player 456 warning the others about how you get killed in these games. He and the 454 other players doesn't believe this and thinks he's insane. He plays the game well and survives the chaos when Player 196 gets shot and people starts running away. He ends up making it to the finish line with the other players. 
During the vote he picks "X" but unfortunately for him and the other X's the majority picked to continue the games. He then goes up the stairs to the second game Six-Legged Pentathlon. He finds a team and he is seen looking at the first 2 teams getting eliminated. When it was his team's turn to participate, they didn't finish all five minigames in time, resulting in all of the team being shot and eliminated off-screen.

Fate[]
He wasn't seen after Six-Legged Pentathlon, which means he and his team was eliminated in some point during the game.
