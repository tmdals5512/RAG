History[]
Player 449 was on financial ruin so she joined the Squid Games. She managed to pass the first game, Red Light, Green Light. After the first game, a vote was held on whether or not to terminate the games, Player 449 voted to continue the game, however, the majority voted to terminate the games, and when the games resumed, she returned along with 186 other players.

Fate[]
Player 449 proceeded to Sugar Honeycombs where she picked the umbrella shape and attempted to carve it out to pass the challenge. However she broke her Dalgona and this caused her to be shot by a soldier, killing her almost instantly. She was also seen during the Special Game as a man, but this is an error.

Mistakes[]
Another Player 449, played by the actor of Player 311, appears in Red Light, Green Light.
Other Player 449 appears during the umbrella queue in Sugar Honeycombs.
Another Player 449 is seen after the Special Game.
She shares an icon with Player 110.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 449 was portrayed by Hwang Gyu-yong, Song Dae-ok and 2 unknown extras.
Her actress also portrayed Player 025 in S1 Red Light Green Light and Player 106 in Mingle.
