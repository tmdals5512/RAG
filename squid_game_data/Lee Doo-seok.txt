Lee Doo-seok (이두석 I Du-seog, born May 10, 1985?) is the South Korean actor, who portrayed Player 062 in Season 1 of the Netflix TV series Squid Game. He graduated from Dongincheon High School and later obtained a bachelor's degree in theatre and film from Suwon University.
