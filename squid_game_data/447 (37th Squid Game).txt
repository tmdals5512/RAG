History[]
Player 447 was in tremendous debt and decided to join the Squid Games. He woke up in a dormitory along with 454 other players, he then proceeded sign a contract and take his photo. He survived the first game, Red Light, Green Light, and voted to continue the games. During the 2nd game , he teamed up with Player 021, Player 029, Player 147, and Player 410. He and his team managed to pass the 2nd game with his team. He then proceeded to voted O, like he did before. He also survived Mingle and voted O for the third time.He later participated in Special Game and is seen rushing at Team X.He voted "O" again during the re-vote so he plays Hide and Seek, he eventually joined Seon-nyeo (Player 044)'s cult before the game started. He followed her most of the time in the fourth game before she ran away in fear after opening a door, leading the eventful ambush by Nam-gyu and Lee Myung-gi.

Fate[]
After he got ambushed, Myung-gi pushes him to the ground and stabs him in the chest several times, resulting in his death.

Trivia[]
Both him and his Season 1 counterpart died being part of a team. Additionally, both were killed by another player (Gi-hun/Myung-gi) and these 2 players were placed 2nd and 3rd in Squid Game 37 respectively.
He was one of the few men of Team O who didn't attack any of the X voters during Special Game.
Many people say he looks like Tetsu from the Japanese show, "Alice in Borderland".
He was counted ninth in the Team O headcount.
During the pentathlon, him and all his teammates had O patches. After the second vote, he was the only one to not switch to X.
Player 447 is seen to get nervous very easily, as in a game like Hide and Seek where a sound can give your location away, he yells "Me too!"
Behind the scenes[]
Player 447 was portrayed by Choi Young-rok, Shin Sang-ok, Yun-yuk and 3 unknown extras.
