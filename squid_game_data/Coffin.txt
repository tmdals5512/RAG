Title: Coffin
URL: https://squid-game.fandom.com/wiki/Coffin
--------------------------------------------------
The coffins are used to hold eliminated Players corpses, and are typically placed into an incinerator afterwards in order to burn the Player's body. The coffins are modelled after gift boxes, like presents people would get on birthdays or on Christmas - almost symbolizing that it appears like it is a reward to be placed in one of them. Every Player that is eliminated and sent to the Organ trafficking ring will have some of their blood marked on their coffin in a cross, to signify to the corrupt Workers that these deceased Players do not need to be fully incinerated, as they are still partially alive, meaning their organs can be harvested. 
In the 37th Squid Game, Seong Gi-hun was placed into a Coffin after being knocked out following his failed Rebellion and brought back to the Dormitory while he was still alive. Every Player from both the 33rd Squid Game and the 37th Squid Game's body was incinerated by a Worker besides those who were eliminated in Sky Squid Game, Hwang In-ho, Park Gyeong-seok, Kim Jun-hee's baby, and the Supervised Players
