History[]
Backstory[]
Not much is known about Kang Mi-na's past and outside life, except that she was in ₩45,000,000 worth of debt, which led her to enter the Squid Games after playing Ddakji with The Recruiter.

Waking up in the Dormitory[]
Did you people strip me when you shoved me into this?
Kang Mi-na to a Manager in "001"
Kang Mi-na appears on screen waking up in the Dormitory. She can be briefly seen above the right shoulder of Seong Gi-hun (Player 456) during the scene with him throwing aside the counterpane and recognized by her braids. Later, she can be seen walking in the middle of the dormitory and looking around. 

     Mi-na complains about her uniform.  
When the Pink Guards arrive to introduce the new players with the games, Mi-na listens to other players complain and seems dissatisfied as well. She throws off her jacket and snatches a moment to complain that the guards took off her clothes and put her in the uniform. Thanos (Player 230) begins to speak and she waits, displeased, until he stops talking. Mi-na then continues to complain about the size and colour of her uniform and asks the guards if she can wear their outfit instead, mentioning that she likes the pink in their outfits. Having received a refusal, she stops talking.

     Kang Mi-na being slapped  
Mi-na is the second player whose name and debt amount are read aloud by the Squid Game staff. She appears uncomfortable when the staff play footage of her being slapped by the Recruiter. Later, Mi-na gives a brief look to Kim Yeong-sam (Player 226) when he jumps in front of her, seeking Im Jeong-dae (Player 100) in the crowd, and looks at him when he erupts in front of the players. When the guards reveal the massive piggy bank to the players, she looks up at it with surprise and smiles.

     Mi-na signs a consent form  
One of the first amongst the players, she signs a consent form and becomes a witness of the confrontation between Thanos and Lee Myung-gi (Player 333). When it's over, Mi-na stays at the same spot and notices that Thanos is staring at her, passing by. She seems dissatisfied with such attention. While the players take their photos, Mi-na sees several people swarming around Thanos in order to take a group photo with him, but shows no interest in joining them. Having received Thanos's personal offer to join him and his fans, she gives him a look of contempt and proudly retires. 

Before the first game[]
Is this you trying to flirt?
Kang Mi-na to Thanos in "001"
     Mi-na smiles at Thanos' compliments.  
Mi-na takes her photo and goes upstairs to play the first game, followed by Thanos who catches up with her at the entrance of the large room and makes up rap lyrics about her on the fly. Mi-na seems unimpressed by his lyrics and self-praise, putted in it, but smiles when he says that he likes her and does the finger heart gesture (Korean gesture, which symbolizes love and affection). Shortly after Mi-na notices Young-hee and changes the subject, leaving Thanos' advances without an approval or rejection.
Before the game Red Light, Green Light, having heard Seong Gi-hun (Player 456) warning players about the danger of being shot, Mi-na doesn't believe him, suggesting that he may be drunk. She repeats Gi-hun's words to Thanos, and he tells her about his father who behaves similarly to Gi-hun while being drunk. 

Fate[]
Oh, shit. I just moved, didn't I?
Kang Mi-na's last words, to Thanos in "001"
     Mi-na asks how would Thanos knows about drugs  
The game starts. Mi-na does well, advancing slightly ahead of Thanos. When Young-hee turns its head towards the players at the seventh time, Mi-na hears yet another one of Gi-hun's warnings and starts questioning if he is high. Thanos answers in the negative and tells her that people don't act like that when they're on drugs. With a smile, Mi-na asks how would he know, and Thanos, in turn, asks if she wanna try. Being distracted by a bee that flies over to her and lands on her neck, Mi-na doesn't answer the question. 
She follows the rules, doesn't move and asks what is it out of the corner of her mouth. Thanos gives her a compliment and an answer that a bee was on her. Suddenly, Mi-na starts to panic, scream and jump. Once she chases the bee away and calms down, she realizes that she moved and laughs nervously at the possibility of being eliminated.

     Kang Mi-na gets shot  
Mi-na doesn't expect to be killed and when she gets shot in the back of the head, she has a surprised expression on her face. She falls on the ground dead and lies there with her eyes open. The bee can be seen crawling on Mi-na's cheek, but soon it flies away, passing by Lee Eun-ju (Player 283), who turns her head, sees Mi-na's body and screams in horror, kick-starting a mass panic. Thanos looks at Mi-na's corpse for a long time, being scared and saddened by her death, swallows a pill in order to suppress his nerves and continues to advance towards the finish line.

Personality[]
Look at this. The size is all wrong, and I hate the colors you're using. Can I have the outfit you guys are wearing instead? The pink's cute as hell.
Kang Mi-na to a manager in "001"
     Kang Mi-na takes a photo  
Mi-na is a pretty and feminine girl who concerns about her appearance. She has pink manicured fingernails. Taking a photo for the game, Mi-na uses one of her clip-in braids to prink in front of the camera. Trying to look more attractive, she ties up her T-shirt and exposes her stomach with a navel piercing jewellery. Mi-na loves luxury. She wears jewellery and was dressed in fashionable clothes before the games as it is shown in the video of her playing ddakji with the Recruiter.

     Mi-na is asking for the pink suit  
Mi-na highly values comfort. She complains on her uniform and playfully asks the guards if she can wear theirs, since she prefers pink. She panics when she learns that it is a bee is crawling on her neck, afraid of being stinged. Kang Mi-na is susceptible to flattery. She is unconcerned about Thanos being a celebrity and dislikes his arrogance, but relents when he says that he likes her. She teams up with him and requests his assistance when she gets into the difficult situation due to the bee.

Trivia[]
Mi-na's name is a charactonym. From Sino-Korean 美 (mi) meaning "beautiful" combined with 娜 (na) meaning "elegant, graceful, delicate".
Mi-na's player number, 196, is
a square number (196 = 14²), a trait she shares with Player 324 (324 = 18²) from Season One. Coincidentally, both were the first to die in their respective Squid Games.
a Lychrel number (a number that can't form a palindrome through the iterative process of repeatedly reversing its digits and adding the resulting numbers). Similarly to how 196 can't form a symmetric number, Mi-na has problems with her uniform.
the standart atomic weight of the chemical element Au (gold). Mi-na wears gold jewellery.
She is the first named player to die in 37th Squid Game.
Kang Mi-na and Player 240 from season 1 share a trait that no other characters have - they both died smiling. The main difference, however, is that Ji-yeong knew she was about to die, whereas Kang Mi-na didn't.
Amongst all players whose debts are known, Kang Mi-na had the second lowest debt after Kim Jun-hee.
Her debt: ₩45,000,000 (which is approximately $32,580) is less than half the amount of the prize money earned for her death, ₩100,000,000.
Kang Mi-na would have to complete the first two games to pay off her debt.
Behind the scenes[]
Kang Mi-na is portrayed by Song Ji-woo. She is credited as "패션녀" in Korean, which translates to "fashion girl". In the English dub, she is voiced by Halley Kim. In the French dub, she is voiced by Julie Mouchel.
