Chu Jong-kyung (추종경 Chu Jong-gyeong?) is the actor, who portrayed the male Player 005 during Tug of War, Player 223's icon, Player 224's icon and in Sugar Honeycombs, Player 099 after Sugar Honeycombs, Player 331 in the Dormitory, and Player 332 during the Special Game in Season 1 of the Netflix TV series Squid Game.
