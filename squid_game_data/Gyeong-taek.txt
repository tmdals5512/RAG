Gyeong-taek (경택 Gyeong-taeg?) is a man who becomes friends with Choi Woo-seok in prison.
After committing unspecified crimes, Gyeong-taek was incarcerated at Moojin Penitentiary, where he met and befriended Choi Woo-seok, who was in prison for breaking into Park Yeong-gil's house. When they were both released from prison in May 2025, they promised to stay in touch before leaving.

Triva[]
He is not only in all three seasons, but interacts with two main characters and lives through the whole show.
