History[]
Not much known is about Player 013's life prior the Squid Games, although she fell into a large amount of debt, which led her to participate the competition after she was invited by The Recruiter, hoping she can solve her financial issues. 

"001"[]
Player 013 first appeared waking up in the dormitory, she then proceeded to sign a contract and having her photo taken which officially signing her up in the games. She was seen judging Seong Gi-hun for warning the players about the games' true nature before Red Light, Green Light, although she obeyed his instructions during the game and were one of the players who remained frozen during the panic. She eventually crossed the finishing line, making her pass the first game. After returning back in the dormitory, the guards stepped in to announce the amount of eliminated Players. Gi-hun mentioned the third clause of the contract, causing a vote between the games continuing or stopping to be held. However, a large piggy bank had lowered down to reveal a huge increase in prize money due to the eliminated players from the previous game. During the first round of voting, she voted "O" to continue and cheered after Hwang In-ho broke the tie by joining Team O, so they'll play the next game.

"Six Legs"[]
When the players were told to form in groups of 5, she teamed up with Players 028, 134, 175, and 361 before Six-Legged Pentathlon. Player 013 was spectating the first two teams playing the minigames and was frightened after both teams were gunned down for failing to complete in time.

"One More Game"[]
Player 013 played Ddakji for her team since they were next to play the minigames and successfully completed on which they eventually passed Six-Legged Pentathlon in time. In the second voting, she remained voting "O" again, feeling unsatisfied due to prize money barely increasing for having less players dying in the game.

Fate[]
Player 013 canonically died in the first round of Mingle as we hear her number being mentioned in the list of eliminated Players. Despite this, she also appears multiple times after her initial eliminination, with her last appearance being at the final round of Mingle where she's seen partnering up with with Player 215 and never seen again, however all her appearances after the first round were errors.

Trivia[]
Player 013 is the first player to be announced eliminated during the first round of Mingle due to her being the lowest number.
Her actress, Gong Se-young had portrayed another background character in the final episode of Season 3.
She and Player 060 can be heard shouting for 8 players during the first round of Mingle before being shot and killed.
Mistakes[]
Player 013 was already present on the O side despite her number hasn't been call out yet in round 1 when Jeong-dae going is up to vote.
Player 013's elimination was announced in the first round of Mingle, however, she was seen unharmed throughout the rest of the round until the final.
If it is basing on her elimination and icon dimmed, Player 013 has a continuity error.
This also applies if it is basing on her last appearance during round 5 that her "elimination" from round 1 and her icon dimmed is an error due to her frequent appearance in all of the rounds.
