Player 030 was in severe debt and chose to enter a high-risk competition to win money. After waking up in a large room with 455 other people, she signed a contract and got herphoto taken. She managed to survive the first game, Red Light Green Light, after most of the contestants had been wiped out. Afterwards, like everyone else, she was shaken by the deaths of the contestants, when the prize money was finally revealed. A vote was held if the game should continue, and the majority voted no. However, those who were willing to return could, and she returned, along with most of the other players.

Death[]
During Sugar Honeycombs, she broke her dalgona and was shot by a Solider.
