Hwang Jae-hun (황재훈 Hwang Jae-hun?) is a loan shark and member of Sunshine Capital. Like his colleagues, he was then recruited into the search for the Salesman. He appears in "Red Light, Green Light" and in "Bread and Lottery."
In season 1 he is one of Mr Kim's loan sharks and is seen with him when Mr Kim asks  Seong Gi-hun to repay his debts.
In 2024, Jae-hun attended a meeting at Sunshine Capital's headquarters, where Mr. Kim and Choi Woo-seok briefed the men present on the details of the search, which covered the entirety of the Seoul Metropolitan Subway. As part of search, he took a photo of himself and his partner at Oksu Station on June 22 and sent it to a group chat Seong Gi-hun was in.
