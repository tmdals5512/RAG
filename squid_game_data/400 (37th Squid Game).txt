Personality[]
While there's little information about Player 400's personality, it can be noted that she valued her life over the prize money. At the start of the games, she seems cheerful and carefree however she later becomes a little timid as the games progress. She also doesn't seem to hate O players like some other Xs, even forming small alliances with players like 394 or 418.

History[]
Backstory[]
Not much is known about Player 400 before entering the Squid Games, but it was clear that she was struggling with a significant amount of debt. As a result, this caused her to be unexpectedly met by The Recruiter who offers her in a match of Ddakji, and if she wins she would get 100k won as her prize. Eventually, she wins after countless attempts of failing and receives her cash. She was then given a strange business card with a location and specific date to contact it. Once it was time, she contacted the number and was picked up by a van. She enters the van, and gets knocked out by the unconscious gas released to knock out the other players inside that van. It is also noted that Player 400 was also a fan of the famous rapper Thanos and presumably attended most or all of his concerts.

Season 2[]
"001"[]
Really? Can't you make an exception? We can't take pictures since you guys took our phones, please?
Player 400 pleading at the manager for allowing her and the other fans take pictures with Thanos in "001"
Player 400 woke up in the dormitory with the other 454 players, she signed the player consent form and is heading to take her photo. She was first seen watching Gyeong-su talking to Thanos for recognizing him as a rapper and wanting him to take a photo, while she had a conversation with Player 418 that he and she joined in with the other Thanos' fans. However, a Manager stepped in warning the fans that they are breaking the rules and should be taking their photos one by one, Player 400 begged the manager by letting her and the others know that they've got their phones taken until the manager declined her reasoning, making her saddened. Despite this, Thanos made a promise to his fans that they'll take a photo together once they leave the games, making her and the others happy and excited before they go back in line.  In Red Light, Green Light, Player 400 and the other contestants were warned by Seong Gi-hun, telling them that the games are dangerous and if they were caught moving after Young-hee detects them, they'll be shot to death. After the game begins, she listens to Gi-hun's instructions and was one of the players who remained still during the panic caused by Lee Eun-ju so she successfully survives the first game. During the first round of voting, Player 400 voted "X" to terminate the games, but unfortunately she cannot leave the games because more players voted to stay.

"Six Legs" & "One More Game"[]
Player 400 can be spotted with players 394, 454, 192 and another unknown O member during the Six-Legged Pentathlon, but she still managed to win the game with her team. After surviving the second game, she voted "X" again though the Os win again due to the number of players voting to stay for the second time.  

"O X"[]
In third round of Mingle, Player 400 was seen teaming up with Player 418 and Se-mi (Player 380) after they leave a room In the fifth round of Mingle she stayed close to Player 418 and Se-mi and when the number two was called she ran with another player resulting in her survival. She was seen alive after the third game because she voted "X" in the third vote and was eating her food while she sits with Se-mi, she smiles at Yong Sik's comments towards the O's and when the X's reunite after the bathroom fight, she is the one to point out that they have the advantage now as they lost only two people during the fight, she is relieved at the possibility of winning the voting tomorrow and going out, so she goes to sleep with a smile.

"Friend or Foe"[]
During the Special Game, she was one of the Xs who weren't warned by Seong Gi-hun, she was attacked and grabbed but she was luckily able to fight off her attacker and counterattack and making them back off, so she fortunately survived the Special Game and chose not to participate in the rebellion.
Despite the horror and difficulty of every game, she survives the events of Season 2 and is alive. After the Special Game, she is seen standing behind Park Yong-sik. Next to Player 134 and Player 105.

Season 3[]
"Keys and Knives"[]
After the rebellion, she was seen standing with Player 197 and Player 377 before and after Seong Gi-hun wakes up. In the re-vote, Player 400 was one of the remaining Team X members who didn't change her vote even voting X first despite Team X being heavily outnumbered due to the Special Game and Seong Gi-hun's failed Rebellion. She and the others also stood by and watched Seon-nyeo nearly getting killed by Gi-hun for making fun of his dead friends. 
In the 4th game, it is revealed that the players will be playing Hide and Seek. Upon drawing the blue ball from the gumball machine, she is assigned to the Blue Team. As a result, her objective like the other hiders is to avoid being killed by the Red Team for 30 minutes or locate the exit and escape. Prior to the start of the 4th game, Players were given the option to switch teams if they felt incapable to complete the objective assigned to their original team. She is seen sitting in the corner, debating whether or not to make the switch while talking to one of her allies, Player 377, who's also placed on the Blue Team. After some consideration, she ultimately decides not to switch and remains on the Blue Team.

"The Starry Night"[]
Player 400 can be seen scared and nervous entering the maze alongside the other Hiders, granted a two minute head start to find a hiding spot. She starts panicking and rushes off to find somewhere to hide, running into a hallway on the same floor they began, followed by a desesperate Player 394, then she wandered around the maze alone trying to survive the game. 

Fate[]
Halfway through the game, Lee Myung-gi heard a series of screams, mistakenly believing it belonged to Kim Jun-hee. He runs towards the series of screams. Upon arriving, he witnesses Player 400 being stabbed repeatedly in the abdomen by Player 276. Before she starts screaming a stab can be heard then she screams in fear so it is presumed she was stabbed while running then a thud and her grunting is heard which is presumably Player 276 putting her against the wall to stab her even more, breathless she eventually dies from her wounds at 15:15 minutes left.

Behind the scenes[]
Player 400 was portrayed by Nam Ji-hyun MBC Academy, an acting school in Seoul, made an Instagram post[2]  celebrating Ji-hyun’s successful casting into Squid Game. It also stated her role to be "이대녀" (idaenyeo), a term that generally refers to a young woman in her twenties. She is also referred to as idaenyeo in Hider and Seek maze layout at the top.

Trivia[]
Player 400 and Player 418 are the highest-placing unnamed Thanos fans.
Their corpses were also very close, with only a wall between them.
She is one of six players who came to the game with necklaces. The others being Cho Hyun-ju, Thanos, Nam-gyu, Se-mi and Player 043.
During the third round of Mingle, not only did she team with Player 418 and Se-mi, but their icons coincidentally touched, forming a line.[3]
Additionally, from top to bottom is the order they were eliminated.
She, Se-mi, and Cho Hyun-ju were the only female players wearing necklaces.
Player 400 is the highest-placing female of Thanos' fans.
She's one of the three Thanos fans to outlive Thanos himself, the others being Player 308 and Player 418.
She is one of the only young women to survive the Special Game and remained voting X.
She was one out of the three women from Team X who survived the Special Game despite receiving no prior warnings about the incoming attack, the other two being Player 349 and Player 448.
She has a habit of often intertwining her hands when she's scared or nervous.
In the re-vote, as Gi-hun abstained and Player 448 voted for O unwillingly, Player 400 was the first player to vote X in the revote.
She's the only woman Se-mi has had a known interaction with.
If you include minor characters, she ranks 9th, putting her in the top 10 highest-placing female minor characters.
Player 400 was most likely meant to survive longer, as Player 394's actress was filming the death scene with Player 276's actor, and the scene briefly shows Player 394 being stabbed before it switches to Player 400. Meaning to say, Player 276 was originally meant to kill Player 394, and Player 400 survives longer.
She is the only Thanos fan to have her elimination announced.
She is the first female player to have her death depicted on screen during Hide and Seek.
Additionally, she is the fourth female player killed in this game, following Player 310 , Player 028 and Player 394.
She was killed in the same game with her friend, Player 197.
She died at 15:15 minutes left.
She was the 13th player eliminated in Hide and Seek.
Player 400 voted X 4 times and voted O 0 times, when explaining the rules of Hide and Seek she was standing in the 4th row, died in the 4th game, was the 4th hider to die on screen, was the 4th woman and also the 4th X voter to be eliminated in Hide and Seek.
She was the first female Team X voter to die in Hide and Seek.
She is the last player in the 40X range to be eliminated in the 37th squid game.
She is the only woman of the S3 players to have a special title (though she isn't directly credited with it, but it does appear in a map of Hide and Seek).
Player 400 and Gi-hun are the players in the 400 range with the most X votes.
Additionally, she was the last female player in the 400 range to vote X.
Her actress' first name is shared with Player 105's actor.
Also, her actress was the youngest member of the cast, being 21 years old at the time she started filming for season 2.
She, Player 394, Kang Dae-ho, Player 411 and Player 418 have consecutive numbers among the top 60 contestants. Coincidentally, all four were hiding-side participants in the hide-and-seek round, and there is an official behind-the-scenes group photo of them. Player 400 makes a brief cameo appearance nearby.
She is the first player in season two to be credited by number.
She is one of the few players with an especial title.
Mistakes[]
When Lee Myung-gi heard her screams and believing it to be Kim Jun-hee, he runs around the corner. At first, the woman he sees being stabbed in the distance appears to be Player 394. However, when the camera zooms in for a closer view, it is revealed that the person being stabbed was actually Player 400.
This shows that the decision to change Player 276's victim from 394 to 400 was possibly a fast decision which was approved of late.
She is played by another actress during Six Legged-Pentathlon.
