This unnamed man was a member of a gang in South Korea and Jang Deok-su's subordinate. He was killed due to him betraying Deok-su.

History[]
He is first seen meeting up with his boss. The two would drive to a bridge and discuss their plans on how to infiltrate the Squid Game Facility and get guns for Deok-su to steal the money and pay his debts. However, he mentions that Deok-su seems to be in hiding after stealing from the "big boss" and comments how he knows him gambling and losing a lot of money at a Filipino casino. He then reveals that the casino thugs tracked him back to South Korea. He threatened that when they capture him, they will take his organs for the money he owes and attempts to leave the car but not before Jang Deok-su stabs him to death. The casino thugs attempt to get and kill Deok-su but he successfully escapes by jumping down the bridge. It is unknown what happened to his corpse, but is likely it was recovered by either the thugs or the authorities. 

Personality[]
While the unnamed gang member only appears in a single scene, one thing that is immediately and unmistakably apparent is his level of arrogance to the point of stupidity. He didn't think of leaving the car before telling him that he betrayed Deok-su, nor did he consider him having a weapon like any well experienced gangster should always assume.
