Blair Arellano is a Filipino voice dubber who voices the young Seong Gi-hun, Cho Sang-woo, and the dormitory Masked Manager in the Tagalog Dub of the Netflix TV Series Squid Game. He is a well known voice dubber, gaining recognition for voicing famous characters in other works that are in the tagalog dub.

Trivia[]
Outside of his voice dubbing career, he is also an actor known for starring in filipino works.
Blair Arellano is well-known in the Philippines for voicing anime characters, cartoon characters, and more.
Excluding his career of acting in general, Blair owns a YouTube Channel where he uploads stories made up by him to share online. He titles his stories as "SirBlair Stories".
