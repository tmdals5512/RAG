Workers (일꾼들 ilkkundeul) (symbolized by a ⭘), also known as Circles (동그라미 dong-geulami), are the maintenance crew tasked with assisting game operations, general upkeep and clean-up detail of the site.

Role
Workers are the "civilians" of the Pink Guards. They cannot speak without permission from Managers (similar to Soldiers), move beyond their designated post or use firearms. Their role is simply reduced into menial labour: transporting and cremating deceased Players, maintaining the Game facilities and distributing food to the players. They may not show any empathy or favoritism towards the players and must strictly adhere to the rules and regulations of the Squid Game organization. Like other guards, they are armed with switchblades but do not possess firearms like Soldiers but instead they hold knives, although it's not very shown what knife they use.
