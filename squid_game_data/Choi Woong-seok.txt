History[]
Choi Woong-seok was presumably in massive debt and decided to join the Squid Games for a chance to win a huge cash prize. He called the number on the recruiting card, and got into a mysterious car. He then woke up in a giant room with 454 other players. He then made his way to the center of the room and watched as many other players debts were revealed in front of him. Later on, he signed a contract and made his way up a flight of stairs to the first game.
During the first game, He heard Gi-hun screaming about how dangerous the games were. Woong-seok and many other players ignored his warnings, and continued the game without any worry. However, He was soon terrified as players were shot in front of him during the mass panic. luckily, Woong-seok was able to control himself and stand still. He eventually completed the first game after listening to Gi-hun's commands. After returning to the dormitory with the other surviving players, Woong-seok begged the guards to let him and the other players go. However, The guards instead showed everyone the massive increase in prize money that accumulated after the first game. They then explained the voting process to all of the players. Woong-seok did not care about the money and decided to Vote X during the first Vote. Unfortunately for him and the other Xs, More players voted to continue the games. Which means they would have to continue the games.
Later during Six-Legged Pentathlon, Woong-seok partnered up with players 136, 173, 201, and 309. They then managed to complete all 5 minigames within the 5 minute timer. After successfully completing Six-Legged Pentathlon, him and the other players held another vote. Woong-seok decided to vote X again along with many other X players.  Unfortunately for him and the other Xs, more players voted to continue. Which meant they would have to continue the games.
Later on during Mingle, Woong-seok managed to partner up with other players and pass the first 2 rounds without any trouble. After the second round, he made his way back onto the carousel and prepared for the third round.

Fate[]
During the third round of Mingle, Woong-seok was shot after failing to find a room with the correct amount of players.

Trivia[]
Choi Woong-seok shares the same name with his actor.
His name is incredibly similar to Choi Woo-seong, another player whose name was revealed through the files.
