This unnamed man was a drone pilot and mercenary who was hired as part of a strike force to find and take down the Squid Games.

History[]
The pilot is part of Hwang Jun-ho's team, which consisted of mercenaries and other related operatives sent to find and infiltrate the Squid Game island. The pilot was tasked on using drones to search for the island and find any clues. He was also seen treating one of his comrade's wounds, showing his team medic skills. 
In the early morning of November 4, he couldn't sleep and felt sick from the boat shaking from the waves. He noticed Captain Park Yeong-gil tampering with his drone and started to question him. The captain then stabbed the pilot and threw him off the boat. 
By the morning, his deserted corpse washed up upon an island nearby.

Behind the scenes[]
The drone pilot was portrayed by Ji Nam-hyuk. He was credited as "Drone Knight (Mercenary)".
