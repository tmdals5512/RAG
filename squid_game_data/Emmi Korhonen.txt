Emmi Korhonen, also known as Player 219, is a woman from Finland who competed in the Squid Games.

In-game description[]
Finland's Aurora Borealis can't dazzle like Emmi Korhonen. She illuminates the darkest Nordic winters with a smile that melts permafrost.
But don't be fooled, Emmi's bubbly spirit springs from a cure of pure sisu. That spirit fuels her unwavering drive to help others but when her twin brother's rare illness forces her to pursue expensive treatment abroad, Emmi's inner fire faces the ultimate test.
In a land of long Winter nights, the brightest smile might conceal the hottest flame.
