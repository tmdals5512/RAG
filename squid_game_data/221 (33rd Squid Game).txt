History[]
Player 221 suffered a severe amount of debt, unable to pay it off. He was then approached by a man, who offered him to play Ddakji. And if he won, he would get a handsome cash prize of 100k won. He played Ddakji and possibly got slapped, but eventually wins and receives his money. After that, he was given a strange business card with a phone number on it with the specific date to contact it and a location. After dialing the number at the specified time and location, a van then picked him up. He entered the van, saw the rest of the unconscious players, and were knocked out by an unknown gas. He then woke up in a large dormitory with 455 other players. Confused, he woke up from his bed and witnessed the fight between Kang Sae-byeok and Jang Deok-su from a far. When the Pink Guards stepped in and stopped the fight, the TV shows multiple players being slapped. Convinced that Player 221 would receive the prize money, he signed the contract, had his photo taken and then proceeded to the first game; Red Light, Green Light. He was confused, and the arena was massive. But as the instructions were spoken by the announcer, he understood it clearly and made a bit of progress in the 1st game before chaos erupted.  

Fate[]
His true fate is unknown, his icon faded after the Special Game, but his corpse was only seen in the first game, Red Light, Green Light.
