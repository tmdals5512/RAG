Park Mi-hwa (박미화 Bag Mi-hwa?), also known as Player 006, was a participant in the 37th Squid Game. During the competition, she became a devoted follower of Seon-nyeo.

Personality
Let her go! Stop it! Let her go! Are you alright, Miss Seon-nyeo?
Park Mi-hwa telling Seong Gi-hun to stop strangling Seon-nyeo in "Keys and Knives"
Park Mi-hwa appears to be anxious and emotional during the first two games and votes X, despite the prize money not being nowhere near big enough to pay her debt showing she prioritised life over money. However in Mingle she became  Seon-nyeo's minion and devout follower. Changing her vote from X to O through Seon-nyeo's influence, possibly becoming delusional as her vote could've ended the games there. She also constantly follows Seon-nyeo faithfully and believes that she will be a reliable source of help in order to survive the games and pay off debts becoming protective of Seon-nyeo as she saved her from Seong Gi-hun (Player 456). This also suggests that she is desperate and naïve being somewhat intimidated to back away from Seon-nyeo as she doesn't want to be cursed.

History
Not much is known about Park Mi-hwa, except that she had 3.1 billion won worth of debt. Because of this, she decided to participate in the Squid Games after playing Ddakji with The Recruiter. She woke up in the Dormitory with the other 454 players, She signed the player consent form and took her icon photo before entering Red Light, Green Light.
She passed the first game, Red Light, Green Light, and then was one of the players to beg for her life after the game. When she voted, she voted 'X.' She passed the Six-Legged Pentathlon after teaming with Player 056, Player 331, Player 443, and Player 435.
In Mingle, she teamed up with Seon-nyeo and became her devoted follower afterwards. With Seon-nyeo's influence, Mi-hwa became the only player after the third game to change votes from 'X' to 'O', causing a split vote and the Special Game event to occur.
She can be seen with Seon-nyeo after Lights Out and during the re-vote, Mi-hwa remained voting O to play Hide and Seek. When Seon-nyeo mocks Gi-hun after the vote he grabs Seon-nyeo neck attempting to strangle her. Mi-hwa, and Player 306 immediately rush to save Seon-nyeo and get her out of Gi-hun's reach. She is on the Blue Team as she draws a blue ball in the gumball machine along with the rest of the cult.
During Hide and Seek, she followed Seon-nyeo, along with Player 009, Player 306 and Player 447, trusting in her abilities. She asked Seon-nyeo if she can really find the way out - somewhat sceptical of her shaman abilities. When Seon-nyeo opens a door, she turns around and runs, leaving Mi-hwa and the other cult members, confused she asks her where she is going before she got ambushed by Nam-gyu (Player 124) and Lee Myung-gi (Player 333). Both her and Player 447 were attacked, but Player 009 and 306 managed to run away.

Fate
Ma'am?
Mi-hwa's last words before her death in "The Starry Night"
After she was ambushed, Nam-gyu stabbed her in the neck whilst he grabbed her, killing her instantly. Her death was announced.


Trivia
She had the third highest known debt out of any players. Behind only Jeong-dae and Eun-ju, excluding the collective debt Myung-gi had caused.
Mi-hwa would have needed to survive Jump Rope and be a finalist in order for her share of the prize money to be enough.
Through her association with Seon-nyeo, she is responsible for the games not being terminated after Mingle. Her vote indirectly sets off a chain of events that result in The Bathroom fight, Special Game and Team X permanently losing the vote.
Mi-hwa is the only member of Seon-nyeo's group who voted X during the first and second round. She is the only player who changed her vote to O from X during third round after Mingle, resulting in the tie.
Mi-hwa and Yong-sik are two name characters with consecutive numbers who both died in Hide and Seek mainly because of the person they trusted the most (Mi-hwa being left to her fate by Seon-nyeo and Yong-sik being stabbed from behind by Geum-ja).
Mi-hwa only voted to keep the games going during the third round under Seon-nyeo's influence. It was a choice that would cost her she died in the next game Hide-and-Seek, along with Seon-nyeo as her vote could've ended the games before her death.
Both she and her Season 1 counterpart both have confirmed names.
Additionally, Both were also fatally stabbed by another player under circumstances in which players were allowed to kill each other.
She lasted longer than her 33rd Squid Game counterpart.
Also both had their eliminations announced on the intercom onscreen.
Interestingly, she and Player 009 have many similarities between each other.
Both her and Player 009 were allies, due to being part of Seon-nyeo's group.
Both of them are middle-aged women.
Both of them are in the 001-010 player number range, being only 3 numbers apart from each other.
They were both Hiders in Hide and Seek.
They were both killed in the 4th game.
Additionally, if 9 is flipped, it becomes 6.
She is the only player with a factorial Number that made it to Hide and Seek that did not get a circle key.
Nam-gyu is seen opening her eye when he stabbed her as he wanted to see her eyes and life fade.
She is one of only two minor characters in season two to appear in every vote, and one game (mingle), the other being Player 009.
She was counted 35th in the Team O headcount.
She was the last one to vote X in the first vote.
Mi-hwa is the only player introduced by the manager whose pentathlon team is unknown.
She is the only player seen voting in every vote she was present for (although it is only audibly for the revote).
She is the only confirmed victim of Nam-gyu whose number is below his.
Mistakes
During the fourth vote, you can see the icons for a short amount of time before the next scene. However, Park Mi-hwa's icon had already disappeared, despite the fourth game not beginning yet.
Another player is seen in the Six-Legged Pentathlon with the same number.

Park Mi-hwa was portrayed by Jo Si-nae.
