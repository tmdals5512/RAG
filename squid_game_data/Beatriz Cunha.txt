Beatriz Cunha, also known as Player 153, is a fisher from Brazil who competed in the Squid Games.

In-game description[]
Growing up along coastal Brazil, the sea always beckoned to Beatriz Cunha. A woman of impressive will and strength, she muscled her way into life aboard a rough-and-tumble sea trawler. And after years of struggle, she became the Captain of her own ship.
But when Beatriz stood up to a corrupt off-shore oil drilling company threatening her fishing waters, they destroyed her ship and left her adrift.
So she'll risk everything to get back to the freedom of the high seas.
