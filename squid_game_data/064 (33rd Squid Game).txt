History[]
Player 064 was in heavy debt and chose to enter the Squid Games to win money. After waking up in a large room with 455 other players, he signed a contract and took his photo and was taken to the first game called Red Light, Green Light.

Fate[]
His death would be unknown/debatable as he's seen being attacked in Special Game even though his icon doesn't blank neither corpse is seen.

Mistakes[]
Another Player 064 is seen in the lobby before Red Light, Green Light.
Another Player 064 is seen in Sugar Honeycombs.
Player 064's icon is the same as Player 352's erroneous icon.
Another Player 064 is seen after Sugar Honeycombs. He is seen being strangled by Player 103.
