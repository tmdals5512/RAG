Lee Eun-ju (이은주 I Eun-ju?) is the actress who portrayed Player 173 and Player 396 (Dormitory) in Season 2 of the Netflix TV series Squid Game.

Trivia[]
She shares her full name with Lee Eun-ju, Player 283 of the 37th Squid Game.
