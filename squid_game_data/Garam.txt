Ga-ram (가람 Ga-lam?) is the actress who portrayed Player 430 in Season 2 of the Netflix series Squid Game.

Trivia[]
Her son and husbands surname are Hong (홍 Hong?).
In Korea, women do not typically take their husbands surname, so hers is unknown.
