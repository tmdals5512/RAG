Kwon Hyuk-seok (권혁석 Gwon Hyeog-seog?) is the South Korean stuntman who portrayed Players 093, 106, and 275 in Red Light, Green Light. Player 337's icon in Season 1, and Player 382 during the character’s death scene in Squid Game Season 2.
