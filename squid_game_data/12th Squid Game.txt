Title: 12th Squid Game
URL: https://squid-game.fandom.com/wiki/12th_Squid_Game
--------------------------------------------------
The 12th Squid Game was the annual Squid Game competition that took place in 1999. Nothing is known about this particular event, except the first two players names, and that a player named Oh Jeong-nam won in the end. Player files for the game appear briefly in "A Fair World" and "Gganbu".

Known players[]
Player 001 - Lee Young-hoo
Player 002 - Bae Dong-il
Player 003
Player 005
Player 377 - Oh Jeong-nam (winner)
Trivia[]
The winner of this edition, Oh Jeong-nam, has the same number as a supporting character in the 37th Squid Game.
