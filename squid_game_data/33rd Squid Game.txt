    33rd Squid Game players  
The 33rd Squid Game was the Squid Games competition held in 2020.[1] The main span of the games started around June 25 to June 29,[2] while the first game occurred around June 9.[3] A total of 456 players participated in the competition, and the winner was Seong Gi-hun, also known as Player 456, who earned the total prize money of ₩45.6 billion (equivalent to $31.2 million USD).[4]
A total of six games were played in six days during the Squid Games: Red Light, Green Light, Sugar Honeycombs, Tug of War,  Marbles, Glass Stepping Stones, and Squid Game. Interlude games that occurred during the game as well include the Special Game and the Final Dinner.
After surviving the competition, Seong Gi-hun set out on a mission to shut down the Squid Games forever, which resulted in him being placed in the 37th Squid Game.

Game Overview[]
     
      
Round 1 Red Light, Green Light: Out of the 456 players who participated, 255 were eliminated, and a total of 201 players successfully completed the game. The total accumulated prize money at the end of the round was ₩25.5 billion.[1]
Voting: Out of the 201 players that participated, 101 voted to leave, thereby ending the game. When the games later resumed, 187 players returned, bringing the total accumulated prize money to ₩26.9 billion.
Round 2 Sugar Honeycombs: Out of the 187 players who participated, 79 were eliminated, and a total of 108 players successfully completed the game. The total accumulated prize money at the end of the round was ₩34.8 billion.[5]
Special Game: Out of the 107 players involved, 27 players were eliminated, with one player having been beaten to death beforehand.
Round 3 Tug of War: Out of the 80 players who participated, 40 were eliminated, and a total of 40 players successfully completed the game. The total accumulated prize money at the end of the round was ₩41.8 billion.[6]
Round 4  Marbles: Out of the 39 players who participated, 22 were eliminated, and a total of 17 players successfully completed the game. The total accumulated prize money at the end of the round was ₩43.9 billion.
Round 5 Glass Stepping Stones: Out of the 16 players who participated, 13 were eliminated, and a total of three players successfully completed the game. The total accumulated prize money at the end of the round was ₩45.3 billion.
Final Dinner: Out of the 3 players who participated, 1 was eliminated.
Round 6 Squid Game: Player 218 voluntarily eliminated himself and 456 was the final survivor and winner of the 33rd Squid Game, earning the final total accumulated prize money of ₩45.6 billion.[4]

Trivia[]
The 33rd Squid Game had two players make it to the final game which is presumably the lowest amount ever, those being Cho Sang-woo and Seong Gi-hun (218 and 456).
Presumably, this could also imply that Squid Game was generally unknown to the VIPs for this reason. In both the 28th and 37th Squid Games, there were at least 5 or more finalists, which just so happen to be just the right amount of finalists for Sky Squid Game.
Player 324, Park Ju-un, Player 241, Player 414, Player 096, and Cho Sang-woo were the first players to die in the first, second, third, fourth, fifth, and sixth games respectively.
Player 119 [No Sang-hun], Player 001 [Oh Il-nam], Player 017 [Do Jeong-soo] and Player 218 [Cho Sang-woo] were the final players to die in the second, fourth, fifth and sixth games respectively. Coincidentally two of them directly commited suicide while one of the others [Oh Il-nam] conceded the game to Player 456 [Seong Gi-hun]. There are many errors on who was the last player to die in Red Light, Green Light and the last twenty players to be eliminated in Tug of War are not seen.
The 33rd Squid Game marks the only time in Squid Game franchise where all the finalists have a name.
Aside from the Special Game and the Final Dinner, Player 271, Byeong-gi (Player 111), and Player 069 are the only players to die between games.
Players with their numbers starting with 16, (16X) 17, (17X), 25 (excluding 250) (25X), 26, (26X), 28, (28X) 29 (29X), 34 (34X), 35 (35X) (excluding 357), 38 (38X), 42 (42X) and 43 (43X) never had an appearance in person.
They are all considered Iconees.
Theoretically, 46 is the maximum number of players who could survive this competition:
In Red Light, Green Light, Sugar Honeycombs, the Special Game, and the Final Dinner, everyone could have a chance of survive.
After the voting, 14 players left for good, thus there are 440 players left.
In Tug-of-War and Marbles, because no more than 50% players could survive these two games, no more than 220 players could survive Tug-of-War and no more than 110 players could survive Marbles.
Assumed that all normal glass are shattered in Glass Stepping Stones, 92 is the minumum number of survived players.
In Squid Game, no more than 50% players could survive, so 46 is maximum numbers of survivors.
The numbers of survivors might increase if there are less normal glass being shattered in Glass Stepping Stones.
In Season 2, the games are incorrectly claimed to happen in 2021, specially by Gi-hun himself, who says that he won 3 years ago (as of 2024).
