The changing room was a location in Squid Game. It is where the workers change players' clothes before every Squid Game. It was presumably built in 1988, then destroyed on November 7, 2024

General[]
The changing room is a rectangular room with a grey floor and walls with a lot of lights and a big entrance in the bottom left corner, it has a lot of beds (presumably 456 beds) for all of the 456 players.
