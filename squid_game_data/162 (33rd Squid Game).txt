History[]
Player 162 was in heavy debt and chose to attend a life and death competition to earn money. After waking up in a dormitory with 455 players, she signed a contract and took her photo. She then went all the way up the stairs to the first game. Once she got there it was revealed the game was Red Light, Green Light. However during the game, Player 324 did not manage to stop in time which caused him to get shot. Player 250 thinking he was just playing dead, stood above him told him to stop faking. He then noticed blood coming out of 324's mouth, while the doll scanning the players, which caused him to panic and run back all the way to where Player 306 was and got shot in his chest. After Player 306 noticed his blood in her face she screamed in fear and causing many players to start panicking and running around. Luckily, Player 162 managed to stand still and survive all this chaos.

Fate[]
However, her lack didnt last long, as in one of the upcoming red lights she didnt manage to stop in time causing her to get detected and thus shot.
