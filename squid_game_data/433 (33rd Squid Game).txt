Player 433 was in high debt. He was approached by a man and he offered to play Ddakji with him, whoever wins gets money. He possibly failed many times at the game but eventually won. He then saw an open van at the same location he was at. He went in the van and saw other people, unconscious. Due to the sleeping gas, he was knocked unconscious, too. While being unconscious, he was transported to The Island where the annual 33rd Squid Game and the Squid Games would be hosted. he was changed in his outfit with his number "433". He then woke up in a room with 455 other players. Visibly confused, he saw the players complain and saw their debts revealed, along with a video of them getting slapped. He listened to the guards and the guards clearing the players doubts. He then signed his contract and went up the Stairs. He then listened to the instructions to take his picture, and he took his picture, which would be his icon. He was then taken to the 1st game.

Fate[]
When Player 324 was caught moving on a red light after betting with Player 250 as to whoever reaches the end first gets 1 million won, he got shot. Player 250 got confused and on the next green light he moved to Player 324's corpse and told him to "stop with the act". He then coughed up blood and Player 250 got scared, running away on the red light. He got shot in the chest and his blood splattered all over Player 306's face. Player 306 got scared and wondered what was on her face. She realized it was blood after rubbing her face with her hands and the hands getting blood too. She screamed and got shot in the head for moving. This caused a mass panic among a lot of players, and Player 433 was one of the players to panic. He was running to the doors and got shot at some point in the game.
