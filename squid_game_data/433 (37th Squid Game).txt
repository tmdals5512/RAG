History[]
Player 433 was most likely in debt so he joined the Squid Games. After waking up he saw 454 other players the Guards came in and explain and listed off debts later he signed the contract and took his picture. First game was Red light Green light after a bit Kang Mi-na got shot and people started to panic later he made it to the finish line. After the first game he was Shooken up and later a Vote was held he voted for X but O won the vote. Second game was The Six-Legged Pentathlon he got in his team and completed all 5 Minigames on time. He voted for X again but the O's won again. After the third game was Mingle he passed round 1, 2 and 3.

Fate[]
He would then most likely die in round 4 Mingle as he isn't seen afterwards. Being shot by a soldier for not entering a room in time.

Behind the scenes[]
Player 433 was portrayed by Meng Oan-ho, Kim Jung-sik, and Park Chun-yeong.
