History[]
Player 453 was in heavy debt and chose to enter the Squid Games to win money. After waking up in a large room with 455 other players, she signed a contract and took her photo. She managed to survive the first game, Red Light, Green Light, after most of the contestants had been wiped out. Afterwards, like everyone else, she was shaken by the deaths of the contestants, and went on her knee begging for the staff to spare her life. A vote was held if the game should continue, and she voted O after knowing how much money she could win, but the majority voted X. However, those who were willing to return could, and she returned, along with most of the other players.
She managed to survive the second game, Sugar Honeycombs. She was seen in Tug of War, as a member of Team 8. It is known that she was in Do Jung-soo's team. She passed that game. In Marbles, she beat her opponent, Player 085 in the fourth game Marbles, and survived until the fifth game, Glass Stepping Stones. For the game, she chose the number 5, which meant she would be the fifth to go. When the player in front of her, Player 021, asked what the order was, and she said she could not remember. However, while Player 244 told her the truth, Player 151 (unintentionally) told her a lie, which she believed and told 021, causing him to fall off.

Trivia[]
She is the highest placing unnamed player with glasses in the entire series.
She is also the only player with glasses in Glass Stepping Stones, and the last player of the 33rd Squid Game to die while wearing glasses.
She and Player 360 are the only people not shown falling to their deaths in Glass Stepping Stones.
She is the first player to vote O.
453 is the only background player in the penultimate game to not be portrayed by a random extra in the first episode.
She and Im Jeong-dae are the only people to have one of their organ revealed upon death. (excluding the players in the Organ trafficking ring)
Both of these players were elderly, meaning they broke easier.
Curiously, both are the first and last players to vote O in the entire series, respectively.
Also her player number subtracting his is the number of another finalist and one of Jeong-dae's allies in the 2024 Sky Squid Game, Player 353/Kim Yun-tae.
She is likely the oldest player to reach the Glass Stepping Stones game with the possible exception of Player 017 [Do Jeong-soo].
She was one of the few supporting players who appeared consistently; the actor shares this with the players. 014, 075, 187, 236, 311, 312, 313, 397, 447, and 450
Mistakes[]
In a continuity error, Player 085 is instead seen playing Marbles with Player 158.
In a mistake, in the sixth episode, another actress portrayed her in lobby, which is possibly her stunt double, due to similarities in appearance.
In the Tug of War, when she is in Team 8, she is portrayed by the same actor as the female Player 004.
A male Player 453 is seen in Red Light, Green Light BTS.
In a massive continuity error, her and her team do not appear in Tug of War.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 453 was portrayed by Yang Mal-bok. Player 453 in Episode Six was portrayed by an unknown extra which is possibly her stunt double due to similarities in appearance, and in Episode Four and Five, Player 453 was portrayed by Oh Tae-nam that portrayed the female Player 004 too.
Player 453 dies offscreen and is not seen falling from the glass bridge, as the bridge on the set, being 1.5 meters off the ground, was too high for the actress to fall from due to her old age. In fact, in a behind the scenes clip, she had to be helped getting across each stepping stone.
