History[]
Player 038 is deeply in debt and sees the high-stakes competition as a last chance to win a life-changing amount of money. Like many others in the game, he is driven by desperation to escape his financial burdens. After being recruited, he wakes up in a large, unfamiliar room with 455 other players. This disorienting experience sets the stage for the tension and fear that permeates the competition. Upon arrival, he witnesses a confrontation between two other players, Jang Deok-su and Kang Sae-byeok. This fight highlights the underlying tensions and desperation among participants, showcasing the hostile environment of the game. He signs a contract agreeing to the rules of the game and poses for a photo, officially entering the first game called Red Light, Green Light.
