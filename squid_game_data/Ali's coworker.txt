Red Light, Green Light[]
Man waiting to bet[]
     
A man at the racetrack who waits in line behind Seong Gi-hun. He gets impatient and repeatedly pesters Gi-hun to hurry up, causing him to scream at him in anger.
The man is portrayed by Kim Kyung-min.


Player 115's friend[]
     
A man gambling at horses alongside his friend Player 115. He was seen getting blamed for the loss and was hit by his friend.
He is portrayed by an unknown extra.


Lottery room kid[]
     
An unnamed boy who is skilled at arcade games and helps Seong Gi-hun win a prize for his daughter.
He is portayed by Jeong Min-gyu.


Hell[]
Fish shop customer[]
      
A woman who listens to stories about Cho Sang-woo from his mother while buying food from her. She attempts to set him up on a date with someone she knows and is present when detectives arrive looking for him.
The customer was portrayed by Jun Hyun-sook.


Detectives[]
     Detective #1  
     Detective #2  
Two men who were detectives in the Ssangmun-dong Police Department. They informed Cho Sang-woo's mother that her son has a warrant out for his arrest, and that they need Sang-woo to come to them immediately.
The detectives were portrayed by Kim Sung-gon and So Yun-ho.


Ali's coworker[]
      
This unnamed immigrant was the friend and coworker of Ali Abdul. As Ali returned to the factory they worked in after having disappeared for a few days, he tried to ask Ali where he had been, but was ignored. After Ali's fight with their boss, he ran away, as his coworker shouted for him to stop. He was played by Akash Chodda.


Filipino casino operators[]
      
They were mentioned antagonists by the Gang Member. They were to get back at Jang Deok-su for stealing from them and the Gang Member betrayed him to them so that they could get him back.
One of the members is portayed by Ko Chang-hee.


Gganbu[]
Ji-yeong's father[]
Ji-yeong's father is a minor antagonist. He constantly abused Ji-yeong and her mother, and asked God for forgiveness, not for his own actions, but for those of his family. Eventually, he murdered Ji-yeong's mother with a knife, whereupon Ji-yeong killed him in revenge.

Ji yeong's mother[]
She was abused and beaten by her husband. Later he killed her via stabbing her with a knife

One Lucky Day[]
Flower seller[]
      
At the beach, Gi-hun came across a flower seller, who he bought flowers from. There was also a note from Oh Il-nam to meet him at the seventh floor of the Sky Tower. She is most likely associated with Il-nam.
She was portrayed by Son Young-soon.


Hairstylist[]
      
A woman working at Minji Hair Salon who cuts and dyes Gi-hun's hair sometime after the death of Oh Il-nam. While working, she remarks on how dirty he is.
She is portrayed by Kim Min-che.
