History[]
Player 142 was in heavy debt and chose to enter a high-risk competition to win money. After waking up in a large room with 455 other people she signed a contract, took her photo and then made her way to the first game arena.

Fate[]
During the first game, Red Light, Green Light,  Player 306 started screaming. As the resuly, Player 142 panicked and ran towards the back doors, which caused her to get detected and be shot.
