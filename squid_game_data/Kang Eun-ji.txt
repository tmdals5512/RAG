Kang Eun-ji (강은지 Gang Eun-ji?) is Gi-hun's ex-wife and the mother of their daughter, Seong Ga-yeong. Eun-ji is also the stepmother of her new husband's son.

History[]
In "Red Light, Green Light", she allows Gi-hun to spend time with their daughter on her birthday though she is clearly unhappy about it. When he brings her back from an outing at a fried chicken place, she complains about how late it is and says she'd planned to drive to get her.Oh, come on, dude! If anything, my wife left 'cause of you. Every night, asking me to get drunk with you. And you're the one who took me to the horse races for the first time.
Seong Gi-hun to Park Jung-bae why he and Eun-ji divorced in "One More Game"In "Hell", Gi-hun begs her for financial assistance, but she gets upset at him and angrily berates him for all the grievances she held years prior. When he reveals that he needs the money in order to give treatment to his mother, who has a severe case of diabetes, while she appeared to feel genuine sympathy for Mal-soon, she tells him she gets all her money from her husband.
She currently resides in Los Angeles with her new husband and their children. It's unknown that she learned about her ex-husband's death in "Humans Are...".

Personality[]
Kang Eun-ji, as a mother, cares deeply about the well-being of her daughter and prefers a safer choice to taking an excessive risk. Her attitude towards Gi-hun highlights how she dislikes gambling. She is also capable of holding resentment, as she brings up many of Gi-huns past mistakes and no longer trusts him due to his inability to provide financially for their daughter and numerous financial mistakes. While this resentment wasn't passed on to his mother as Gi-hun mentioned that she was always really good to her. When she hears from Gi-hun she has severe diabetes and needing surgery she was genuinely sad to hear and told her husband about it which caused him to try bribing Gi-hun to not see their family anymore.  

Behind the scenes[]
Kang Eun-ji is portrayed by Kang Mal-geum. She is credited as "Ex-wife" (전처 Jeoncheo?) throughout the series. Her name is only revealed through Gi-hun's player file.
