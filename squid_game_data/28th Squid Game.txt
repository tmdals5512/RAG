Hwang In-ho (Player 132) after stabbing the other Finalists to death during the Final Dinner.  
The 28th Squid Game was the annual Squid Games competition that took place in August or September 2015. Hwang In-ho, also known as Player 132, ultimately won. Player records for the game briefly appears in "A Fair World" and several of the finalists briefly appear in a flashback in "⭘△☐".
The night after the fifth game, Hwang In-ho was gifted a knife by Oh Il-nam, which he used to murder at least five other finalists in their sleep. It is unclear if the 6th game was played after this, or if anyone was spared. If so, they would have died too as Hwang In-ho was the only winner.

Trivia[]
There are no mural drawings in the Dormitory that would indicate the games they were going to play, it neither shows a message about what it’s like being in Squid Games, which suggests the murals may have been added to the Dormitory for the 29th Squid Games, later, or only in the 33rd Squid Game to help Oh Il-nam to remember what games would he play.
It's probable that all the shown finalists, except Player 132, were part of the same alliance, just like in the 37th Squid Game. And that's why In-ho was gifted the knife to ensure his survival, just like Player 456. If that is true, it means that either the 2nd, the 3rd or the 4th game involved teams groups of about 10 players, like Tug of War, and of that 10 players of the team of Player ?53, 5 made it to the finals.
This may have possibly been the first game where the 6th and final game was not played unless a previous annual game had a similar situation where a player killed all the other players.
However, the list of Squid Game winners found by Hwang Jun-ho in the Squid Archives is titled "Round 6 Winners", referring to a game instead of the competition overall. This could mean that the sixth game did go on, but this is likely just a mistake as Seasons Two and Three were not planned yet during the making of Season One.
When In-ho was talking to Oh Il-nam, we see that his hair was wet. Perhaps this is a hint that the 5th game was related to some kind of swimming activity or a game related to water. But this is refuted by the fact that they would have dried out long ago and the other finalists' hair was quite dry. However, in a YouTube video interview of Lee Byung-hun, he says that they opted for that messed haircut because In-ho went through very difficult games before that scene. So most likely the 5th game was a physical game. This is further explained by the fact that all the finalists were males and not a single female was spotted.
Mistakes[]
When Hwang Jun-ho opens the player files of the 28th Squid Game held in 2015, Player 004's file appears. It is an error for Player 004's file to be in the 2015 player files, because the file clearly says it is a file from the 33rd Squid Game. You can also see that the file consists of the 2020 template, so it can only be the 33rd Squid Game.
