History[]
Jung Il-sik was born on June 14, 1952 in Seoul. He graduated from Sanbon High School and later attended Mihwan University. He got a job at Kishin Electricity after graduating before moving on to a job as a security guard at an Emart in Sanbon.

Fate[]
Though Jung Il-sik's fate is never confirmed, it can be assumed that he either died playing the games or left after a majority vote to terminate them.

Trivia[]
According to his player file, Jung Il-sik's resident registration number is 520614–1011232. He lives in 17-221 Giheung-gu, Yongin-si, Gyeonggi Province.
Jung Il-sik seems to share the exact same player icon as Player 008 and Player 281, players in the 33rd Squid Game.
The person in Jung Il-sik's photo plays Player 011 in the 33rd Squid Game.
Behind the scenes[]
Jung Il-sik was portrayed by Shin Seok-yong.
