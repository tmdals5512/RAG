Personality[]
Player 040 is one of the most ruthless players because he has no hesitation in taking people's lives for money, and he doesn't feel guilty about killing anyone or being friends with psychopathic players. Despite his evil nature, he is loyal to his gang as he gives up his place to sleep for Deok-su and enjoys having fun with his best friend Player 303. 

History[]
Player 040 entered the Squid Games due to heavy debt. Like many participants, he hoped to win the substantial cash prize to turn his life around. Player 040 woke up in a large room with 455 other players. He witnessed the chaotic atmosphere, including a fight between Jang Deok-su and Kang Sae-byeok, which set the tone for the intense dynamics among players. After the fight was broken up, he was welcomed by the staff. After that, he signs a contract agreeing to the rules of the game and has his photo taken.  
Player 040 survived Red Light, Green Light The trauma from the deaths around him deeply affected the players, leading to a vote on whether to continue the games. it’s unknown what he voted though, the narrowly majority decided to terminate the games and he along with other players were sent back to their hometowns, but he ultimately decides to return with 186 other players after some decide to continue. Upon returning, Player 040 aligned himself with Jang Deok-su’s team, which gives him a stronger position in the games. His choice to join a more aggressive team reflected his strategic approach to survival.  
During the Sugar Honeycombs challenge, Player 040 struggled to carve out his star. However, he notices Seong Gi-hun successfully licking his umbrella to make the process easier and copies his method. He manages to carve his shape just before time runs out, showcasing his adaptability under pressure. After that, he, Jang Deok-su, and his gang cut in the line and received meals twice. In the Special Game. He managed to get a hold on Kang Sae-byeok by suffocating her. She managed to escape from 040's hold. He and Jang Deok-su’s gang managed to survive the Special Game that occurred.  
Player 040 participated in the Tug of War challenge alongside Jang Deok-su's team. Jang Deok-su’s team managed to survive the third game by killing Team Seven. The preparation included playful moments, such as a lighthearted fight with Player 303 using metal bars, which contrasted with the otherwise grim setting. Jang Deok-su later told them to rest instead of preparing for an impending conflict, which reflected the camaraderie among the players. Player 040 was the first one keep watch for his teammates during night. Jang Deok-su couldn't sleep so he offered to watch for his teammates. Player 040 told his leader to rest up as he promised to keep them safe. Jang Deok-su thanked Player 040 for having their backs. 

I’ll keep everybody safe tonight .
Player 040 says to Jang Deok-su in "A Fair World"
During the night, an alarm woke all players, signaling a disturbance. Player 040 tells Deok-su that Byeong-gi had not returned from the bathroom. Before the 4th game, Player 040 teams up with Player 303.

Fate[]
For the Marbles game, Player 040 teams up with Player 303. After losing all 20 of his marbles, Player 040 is shot by one of the guards as per the rules of the game.

Trivia[]
Standing at a height of 6'1.5" (187 cm), he is one of the show's tallest characters.
His number "040" is the amount of people who survived Tug of war
Mistakes[]
In "Stick to the Team", his icon was seen as a different person. This was most likely a continuity error made by Hwang Dong-hyuk.
Even though Player 303 was his partner, he was later seen playing with Player 126. This is most likely a continuity error.
Player 040's icon is the same as Player 320's icon.
They both share their icon with Kim Yea-han, a player from the 28th Squid Game.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 040 was portrayed by Yoon Young-kyun, Kim Ju-sik, and Kim Seong-rak. Despite the fact that his player number is clearly visible, he is credited as "Deok-su's Gang Member 3".
