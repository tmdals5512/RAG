History[]
Player 036 was presumably in massive debt and decided to join the Squid Games for a chance to win a huge cash prize. He called the number on the recruiting card, and got into a mysterious car. He then woke up in a giant room with 454 other players. He was likely confused and decided to make his way into the center of the room, where he watched as many other players debts were revealed in front of him. Later on, he listened to the manager explain the rules of the games, he signed a contract along with the other players, he took his photo in the photo booth, and he made his way up a flight of stairs to the first game.
During Red Light, Green Light, Player 036 and the other players watched as a strange man started screaming about how dangerous the games were. Player 036 and the other players ignored Gi-Hun's warnings, as they thought he was crazy and wanted the money for himself. Player 036 and the other players then continued the game without any worry. However, after sounds of gunshots ran out, and players started getting gunned down, Player 036 made a run for the doors in fear for his life.

Fate[]
After listening to Gi-hun's orders, Player 036 managed to survive the panic but was shot off-screen for moving on a Red light.

Mistakes[]
Player 036 is seen running towards the doors, despite surviving the initial chaos and dying later on.
