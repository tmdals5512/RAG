History[]
Player 035 was in a heavy debt and chose to enter the Squid Games to win money. After waking up in a large room with 455 other players, he signed a contract and got his photo taken. He managed to stay still during the initial chaos when Player 324 was shot, and later managed to pass the first game. During the voting he voted for X, and the majority said no. However, those who were willing to return could, and he returned along with 186 other players. 

Fate[]
During Sugar Honeycombs, he attempted to carve his dalgona, but he unfortunately broke it, and was shot and killed.

Player 035 was portrayed by Park ?-?, Lee Jeong-hoon and four unknown extras.
