A Manager introducing the game to the players in 001 (episode).  
The 37th Squid Game was the Squid Games competition held in 2024, as well as the final one held in South Korea. It lasted from November 1 to November 7. A total of 456 Players initially entered the competition, but Kim Jun-hee's baby was later entered, making the total 457. The survivors include Hwang In-ho (001/Oh Young-il), Park Gyeong-seok (246) and Kim Jun-hee's baby, with the baby being declared victorious.
Round 1: Red Light, Green Light. Out of the 456 players who participated, 91 were eliminated, and a total of 365 players successfully completed the game. The total accumulated prize money at the end of the round was ₩9.1 billion.

Voting: Out of the 365 players that participated, 183 players voted to stay and 182 voted to leave, and the games continued.
Round 2: Six-Legged Pentathlon. Out of the 365 players who participated, 110 players were eliminated, and a total of 255 players successfully completed the game. The total accumulated prize money at the end of the round was ₩20.1 billion.

Voting: Out of the 255 players that participated, 139 players voted to stay and 116 voted to leave, continuing the games.    Voting scene  
Round 3: Mingle. A total of 100 players successfully completed the game, and 155 players were eliminated. The total accumulated prize money at the end of the round was ₩35.6 billion.

Voting: Out of the 100 players that participated, 50 players voted to stay and continue playing while the other 50 players voted to stop and leave, resulting a tie. A second vote was scheduled for the next day.
Bathroom fight: Thanos and Myung-gi started a fight in the men's bathroom. A total of 24 players participated in the fight and 5 were eliminated.
Special Game: Out of the 95 players involved, 25 players were eliminated.
Rebellion: An active attempt to take over the Squid Game complex and end the games occurred, led by Gi-hun. This rebellion ultimately failed, and 10 players were eliminated.
Revote: Out of the 60 players that participated, 41 players voted to stay and continue playing, while 18 players voted to stop and leave and 1 player abstained, continuing the games.
Round 4: Hide and Seek. A total of 25 players successfully completed the game, and 35 players were eliminated. The total accumulated prize money at the end of the round was ₩43.1 billion.

Voting: Out of the 25 players that participated, 16 players voted to stay and continue playing, while 8 players voted to stop and leave and 1 player abstained, continuing the games.
Round 5: Jump Rope. A total of 9 players successfully completed the game, and 15 players were eliminated. The total accumulated prize money at the end of the round was ₩44.7 billion.

Voting: Out of the 9 players that participated, 5 players voted to stay and continue playing, while 2 players voted to stop and leave 2 players abstained, continuing the games.
Final Dinner: Out of the 9 players involved, nobody was eliminated.
Round 6: Sky Squid Game. 8 players were eliminated, with Gi-hun voluntarily eliminating himself to allow Jun-hee's baby to win. As such, Player 222 was the final survivor and winner of the 37th Squid Game, earning the final total accumulated prize money of ₩45.6 billion.

Trivia
This year’s game is unique. Instead of there being only one winner, if a majority of the surviving players want to quit, everyone splits the prize money in the prize pool equally.
However, this was never seen in the 37th Squid Game as majority of the players voted to continue the games in every vote.
This Squid Game had two returning players: Seong Gi-hun, winner of the 33rd Game, and Hwang In-ho, winner of the 28th Game. These are presumably the only two winners to have ever returned for a second go at the games, and the only year in which this has occurred.
The final game, Sky Squid Game, presents the opportunity that the competition ends with multiple winners or even no winner depending on how the players tackle the game.
This is also presumably the first time that an eliminated player survives the games.
The winner is a baby of 2–3 days old.
This also means that Kim Jun-hee is the first posthumous winner of the Squid Game.
This Squid Game is seemingly the first to technically have more than 456 players, due to Kim Jun-hee's baby becoming the new Player 222 following the elimination of Kim Jun-hee herself.
This appears to be the final Squid Games competition held in Korea, as the island explodes soon after its conclusion.
Nine players made it to the final game, those being Lunchbox, Im Jeong-dae, Park Min-su, Kim Gi-min, Player 222, Lee Myung-gi, Lee Seung-won, Kim Yun-tae and Seong Gi-hun. (039, 100, 125, 203, 222, 333, 336, 353 and 456)
The 37th Squid Game had the inclusion of music in almost every game, Red Light, Green Light, Mingle, Hide and Seek and Jump Rope.
Player 196, Player 045, Player 454, Player 310, Player 124, and Player 125 are the first players to die in the first, second, third, fourth, fifth, and sixth games respectively.
Player 100, Player 333, and Player 456 are the only players who played all six games onscreen.
Conversly, this is the only Squid Game where not a single player successfully passed through all six games to win the competition.
Hwang In-ho and Kim Jun-hee's baby are the only players who did not sign the consent agreement.
The 37th Squid Game is the only Squid Game competition to have lasted 7 days instead of 6 days
It is probably the only edition with more than 456 players.
