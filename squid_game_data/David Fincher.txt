David Fincher is an American director who is currently developing a separate English-language Squid Game series on Netflix.[1] News of this American adaptation first emerged in October 2024 when it was confirmed that Fincher has been working on the project for several years now.[2]
He is best known for directing such movies as Fight Club (1999), Seven (1995) and other psychological thrillers.
