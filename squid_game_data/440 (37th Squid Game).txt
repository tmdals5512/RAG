Personality[]
Player 440 is a selfish and a greedy woman, she also kept on voting O many times and ignored other players' deaths, she and Player 394 also accused Gi-hun for lying to them when Gi-hun reveals himself as a former player, and mocked Dae-ho after claiming to be a Marine, which makes her very rude. While she didn't partake in the Special Game she didn't appear to show much remorse for anyone who was killed as in the revote she didn't appear fazed by the previous night's violence. Despite her rudeness and greed, she isn't completely heartless as she didn't believe she would be able to kill anyone herself and gave into 192's attempts to switch vests. Later, in Hide and Seek, she became timid because she knew that the seekers might arrive.

History[]
Player 440 was in large debt and decided to join the Squid Games. She woke up in a Dormitory along with 454 other players, she then proceeded sign a contract and take her photo. She survived the first game, Red Light, Green Light and voted O. She managed to survive the 2nd game, Six-Legged Pentathlon  and also survived Mingle. During the third vote, she continued voting O and was present in the headcount after the Bathroom Fight. She doesn't participate in the Special Game and Gi-hun's rebellion. Player 440 remained staying in Team O after the tie breaking vote and she went to the next game with the other Players. It was unknown she mocked Gi-hun after the rebellion failed.

Fate[]
In Hide and Seek, she was originally part of the Red Team. Before the game had started, Player 192 convinces her to switch vests with him. During the game, around 18 minutes and 30 seconds into the game, he betrays her and stabs her in the stomach, killing her.

Trivia[]
Player 440 was initially meant to survive until Jump Rope. However, Hwang Dong-hyuk spontaneously decided that she should swap places with Player 192.
However, her number can still be seen on a plaque in the fifth game while 192 is absent.
Her actress' name is the same as Player 095's name, both being called Kim Young-mi.
She disappears after the Special Game in Season 2 and reappears in Season 3 after the failed Rebellion.
She would have been the third female surviving seeker if it was not changed.
Originally , she was supposed to passed Hide and Seek and  killed Player 192, she would be the only player  with 5 votes in O.
She was counted 33rd in the Team O headcount.
As Hide and Seek, began, she stood next to Hyun-ju. This may be a nod to the fact her actor shares her name with Hyun-ju's best friend, who by that point was dead.
Out of all the female members on her team, she is the only one who's actor's name doesn't end in 'eun.'
Mistakes[]
When Lee Myung-gi draws his blue ball, Player 440 is already seen on the red side. But right after she is shown waiting to draw her ball.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 440 was portrayed by Kim Young-mi. 
Young-mi had asked to audition for the role an assistant director for the show, with whom she had previously worked with on the show "Badland Hunters". Around the same time, however, her brother had been diagnosed with a malignant brain tumor. Though she considered withdrawing from the show, both her brother, a fan of Squid Game, and the assistant director encouraged Young-mi to continue acting in the show. Her brother sadly passed away towards the end of filming.[2]
Kim Young-mi seems to be a lot more connected to her character, even giving her a name, Kim Mi-yeon, though this name is not canonical, rather just a fun name to give.
