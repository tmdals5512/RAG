Diego Cortés, also known as Player 278, is a former special operations soldier from Mexico who competed in the Squid Games.

In-game description[]
Diego Cortés' world changed the day he held his newborn son. Growing up in the roughest part of Mexico City, Diego did what it took to survive.
But he started a new life when he joined the Army, becoming a decorated special operations veteran and thriving on danger. Until he became a father and retired from a life of military adventures to raise a family.
But when Diego's grown son was jailed for a crime he didn't commit, Diego came out of retirement to do the thing he does best - protect his family.
