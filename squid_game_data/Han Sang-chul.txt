Han Sang-chul (한상철 Han Sang-cheol?) is the actor who portrayed Player 411 in Season 2 and Season 3 of the Netflix TV series Squid Game. He is also known for his role as the Archery Bus Driver in All Of Us Are Dead Season 1.
