History[]
Player 039 was in debt and chose to enter the Squid Games to win money. He then woke up in a strange big room with 455 other players, after witnessing a fight between Kang Sae-byeok and Jang Deok-su, he then signed a contract and had his photo taken before entering the first game. After the deaths of Player 324, Player 250 and Player 306, most players panicked and ran for the doors, but Player 039 remained motionless. Later, it is unknown what he voted for in the vote, but X won the vote and Player 039 was returned home with the other players. But he, along with many other players, returned and entered the Sugar Honeycombs game. 

Fate[]
In Sugar Honeycombs, he couldn't cut his dalgona in time, causing him to be shot repeatedly by the soldiers.

Trivia[]
He shares the same player number with one of the major characters in Season 3.
