Im Seung-muk (임승묵 Im Seung-muk?) is the South Korean stuntman who portrayed Player 370 in Red Light, Green Light mass panic, Player 337's and Player 367's death scene during Sugar Honeycombs, Park Tae-jun in the Special Game, and Player 156 in Tug of War in Season 1 in the Netflix TV series Squid Game.
