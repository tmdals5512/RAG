History[]
Player 432 wasn't able to pay his debt and joined the Squid Games. Player 432 woke up in a dormitory with 454 other players and signed a player consent form and then made his way to the first game, Red Light, Green Light. He made it to the finish line thanks to Gi-hun's advice and voted X in the first voting to terminate the games. How ever the games still continued and he made his way to the second game.

Fate[]
At some point during the game he and his teammates were eliminated for failing all 5 minigames and also for not reaching the finish line.

Behind the scenes[]
Player 432 was portrayed by Lee Dong-hoon and an unknown extra.
