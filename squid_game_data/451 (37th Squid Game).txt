History[]
Player 451 is in debt and chose to join Squid Games to earn money. After he woke up in a strange big room with 454 other players, he signed a contest and took a picture before entering the Red Light, Green Light game. He was lucky to survive the first game, after listening to Seong Gi-hun's advice. When it was his turn to vote he pressed the O button.  He was also lucky to survive the Six-Legged Pentathlon game. when it was his turn to vote he still pressed the O button.

Fate[]
In Mingle he couldn't find teammates, so at some point, he was killed during the game.

Trivia[]
He was friends with his number neighbor, 450.
He is by far the highest numbered player with his actor still unknown.
