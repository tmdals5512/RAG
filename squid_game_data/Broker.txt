Park Man-cheol (박만철 Bag Man-cheol?) is a broker based in Seoul, South Korea who helps North Korean defectors enter the country by facilitating their transport.

Personality[]
In Season 1, he was depicted as quite a greedy, selfish person, who demanded Sae-byeok a large amount of money to help her reconnect with her mother. His such attitude drives Sae-byeok mad and even causes her to threaten him. While he works, he drinks tea without serving any to his guests.
However, in Season 2, his personality is further explained, revealing that he is actually a rather decent person. He displays sympathy towards No-eul’s situation, while giving her realistic advice. He even refuses to receive her money on his ethical basis. This is further confirmed in Season 3 when he continues to work on Sae-byeok's and No-eul's cases.

History[]
Little is known about his early life prior to becoming a broker, but he later met Kang Sae-byeok when he started working with her to free her mother from North Korea.

Sae-byeok visited him in 2020 to discuss on how to let her mother escape from North Korea. He tells Sae-byeok that she would have to get 40 million won in order to at least get her mother to China. Furious, Sae-byeok splashes him with hot tea and threatens him with a knife at his neck. After scolding the broker, Sae-byeok leaves but then the broker realizes that she quickly cut through his jacket and stole money from him.Oh. Here she comes! We're over here! You must be Sae-byeok's mom. Welcome. I'm so happy to see you.
Park Man-cheol to Kang Cheol and Cho Sang-woo's mother in "Humans Are..."In 2024, he helps Seong Gi-hun with freeing Sae-byeok's mother from North Korea. He shows him a photo of her and says she was recently released from a concentration camp in Hamgyong. When Gi-hun asks about her health, the Broker replies there was a tuberculosis outbreak in the camp and she didn't receive quality treatment, so she may have difficulties to move around. He says he owes one to Sae-byeok and will bring her mother no matter what as soon as she recovers. Later, he accompanies Sae-byeok's brother Cheol and Sang-woo's mother at the airport, where Cheol is finally reunited with his mother. 
He's also been helping Kang No-eul to free her daughter from North Korea to no avail. When she arrives to his office, he isn't pleased to see her. He refuses the money she attempts to give him and tells her to forget about her daughter, because there's no way she could have survived in North Korea as a baby without her parents. About 6 months later, the broker discovers that No-eul that her daughter Han Song-i has possibly been spotted in China. He arranges everything for No-eul to arrive in China, and calls her about this while he is still walking Cheol and his mother out the airport.

Trivia[]
He is one of the eight characters that appear in all 3 seasons, the others being Seong Gi-hun, Hwang In-ho, Hwang Jun-ho, Sang-woo's mother, Kang Cheol, Kang Sae-byeok, Park Jung-bae, and Gyeong-taek.
Additionally, he is one of the five characters that appear in all three seasons and make it out alive, the others being Hwang In-ho, Hwang Jun-ho, Sang-woo's mother, and Kang Cheol.
Behind the scenes[]
Park Man-cheol was portrayed by Choi Jae-sup.
