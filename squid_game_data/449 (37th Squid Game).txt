History[]
Player 449 was presumably in massive debt and decided to join the Squid Games for a chance to win a huge cash prize. He called the number on the recruiting card, and got into a mysterious car. He then woke up in a giant room with 454 other players. He then made his way to the center of the room and watched as many other players debts were revealed in front of him. Later on, he signed a contract and made his way up a flight of stairs to the first game. 
During the first game, He heard Gi-hun screaming about how dangerous the games were. Player 449 and many other players ignored his warnings, and continued the game without any worry. However, He was soon terrified as players were shot in front of him during the mass panic. Player 449 luckily was able to control himself and stand still. He eventually completed the first game after listening to Gi-hun's commands. After returning to the dormitory with the other surviving players, Player 449 begged the guards to let him and the other players go. However, The guards instead showed everyone the massive increase in prize money that accumulated after the first game. They then explained the voting process to all of the players. Player 449 did not care about the money and decided to Vote X during the first Vote. Unfortunately for him and the other Xs, More players voted to continue the games. Which means they would have to continue the games. 
Later during Six-Legged Pentathlon, Player 449 partnered up with 4 unknown teammates. He later managed to pass all 5 games within the 5 minute timer with his 4 teammates. During the second vote, Player 449 changed his mind and decided to vote O instead of X along with some other players. 
Later on during Mingle, Player 449 managed to partner up with other players and pass the first 2 rounds without any trouble. After the second round, he made his way back onto the carousel and prepared for the third round.

Fate[]
He was shot by the soldiers for not entering a room in the third round of Mingle.
