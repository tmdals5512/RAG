History[]
Player 045 was in heavy debt and chose to enter the Squid Games to win money. After waking up in a large room with 455 other players, she signed a contract, had her photo taken, and went upstairs to the arena of the first game called Red Light, Green Light. She managed to survive the first game, Red Light, Green Light, after most of the contestants had been wiped out. Afterwards, like everyone else, she was shaken by the deaths of the contestants, when the prize money was finally revealed. A vote was held if the game should continue, and the majority voted no. However, those who were willing to return could, and she returned along with 186 other players.

Fate[]
In Sugar Honeycombs, she was shot by a soldier for breaking her circle dalgona.
