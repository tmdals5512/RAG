History[]
Player 041 was in a heavy debt and chose to enter a high risk competition to win money, After waking up in a large room with 455 other people, he witnessed the fight between Jang Deok-su and Kang Sae-byeok. After this, he signed a consent form, took a photo and entered the first game.

Death[]
During Red Light, Green Light, he moved, resulting him to get shot by one of the snipers. His icon disappeared as well.

Mistakes[]
