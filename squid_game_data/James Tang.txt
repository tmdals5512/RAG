James Tang is an American actor who was the English dub voice for Choi Woo-seok in Season 2 of the Netflix series Squid Game.[1] He is currently based in Los Angeles, California and considers himself a TCK, which stands for Third Culture Kid. This means he doesn’t fully identify with his parents’ cultures or the culture of the country he grew up in, but rather, a third culture that sort of exists between. Although he was born in the U.S. to Chinese parents, he grew up mostly in Thailand while attending an international school.[2]
