Player 444







Player 443 was in heavy debt and chose to enter the Squid Games in order to win large amounts of money. She was picked up and sedated by Squid Game staff and taken to the island where the games take place. She is one of several players shown being monitored in the Squid Game control room as they wake up. She went to the first game, Red Light Green Light. Player 443 would be confused upon the sight of the doll, and would survive the mass panic. Player 443 would be able to successfully pass the first game with barely any time left. Player 443 would then go back to the dorms and beg for mercy. She would, however, calm down upon the sight of the money. Player 443's vote was unknown but she would return to the games as she was still in need of money. Player 443 would eat her breakfast and make her way to Sugar Honeycombs, she would then pick the circle shape and successfully carve out the shape. She would then get in line for food, but would be disappointed upon seeing that there was no food left. She would go to bed in fear after watching Player 271 be beaten to death. 

Fate[]
Player 310 would be waken up by the noise of players fighting and killing each other, she would run around in panic and would get right in the middle of the fight, Player 310 would drop her and most likely kill her by beating her to death.
