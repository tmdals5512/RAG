Title: Fork
URL: https://squid-game.fandom.com/wiki/Fork
--------------------------------------------------
A fork is a pronged kitchen utensil used to grip food. It is typically made of plastic or metal, and in Squid Game, the forks handed out are metal.
During the Squid Game competition in the 37th Squid Game, forks were distributed along with food during mealtimes. Eventually it became known as a recognizable weapon for the Players as later on, after Lee Myung-gi infamously stabbed Thanos in his throat with a fork (killing him), it was used by players to kill one another, especially during the Bathroom fight and the Special Game, when Kim Gi-min stabbed Player 094 to death with one, along with many others.

Victims[]
Thanos (Player 230): Stabbed twice in the neck by Myung-Gi and bled to death.
Player 331 - Stabbed repeatedly in the chest by Nam-gyu.
Player 185 -Stabbed to death in the neck by Nam-gyu.
Player 182 - Stabbed in the back and abdomen then thrown off the bunk beds by Player 102.
Player 370 - Pinned against bunkbed and stabbed by Player 024.
Player 094 - Kim Gi-min (Player 203) landed 4 punches to the throat.
