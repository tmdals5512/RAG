History[]
Player 430 was in massive debt and decided to join the Squid Games for a chance of turning her life around. Even after Kang Mi-na is shot and players start running for their lives, Player 430 manages to stay still and survive the initial chaos. Eventually, she crosses the finish line, completing the first game. After returning to the dormitory, she votes O to continue the games. In Six Legs, Player 430 is seen cheering for other teams. She teamed up with Player 218, Player 316, Player 251 and Player 042. They were able to pass, as she can be spotted in the dormitory after it ends. There, she votes, once again, to keep the games going. Player 430 makes it through the first round of Mingle, and can be seen before the second one, which she also passed.

Fate[]
After the third round finishes and the doors are locked, Player 430 is seen laying on the Mingle platform, presumably after being pushed by someone else. She is then shot offscreen for not finding a room in time, and her number is announced in the list of eliminated players. It's also possible to see her corpse in the same spot, while the guards clean the arena for the next round.
