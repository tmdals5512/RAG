History[]
Player 045 was presumably in massive debt and decided to join the Squid Games for a chance to win a huge cash prize. He called the number on the recruiting card, and got into a mysterious car. He then woke up in a giant room with 454 other players. He then made his way to the center of the room and watched as many other players debts were revealed in front of him. Later on, he signed a contract and made his way up a flight of stairs to the first game. 
During the first game, He heard Gi-hun screaming about how dangerous the games were. Player 045 and many other players ignored his warnings, and continued the game without any worry. However, He was soon terrified as players were shot in front of him during the mass panic. Player 045 luckily was able to control himself and stand still. He eventually completed the first game after listening to Gi-hun's commands. After returning to the dormitory with the other surviving players, Player 045 begged the guards to let him and the other players go. However, The guards instead showed everyone the massive increase in prize money that accumulated after the first game. They then explained the voting process to all of the players. Player 045 did not care about the money and decided to Vote X during the first vote. Unfortunately for him and the other Xs, More players voted to continue the games. Which means they would have to continue the games. 
Later during Six-Legged Pentathlon, Player 045 formed a small team with players 286, 341, and 395. Player 045 and his team then started looking for a potential member 5th to complete the team. They started talking amongst each other when suddenly Cho Hyun-ju came up to them and asked them if she could be their 5th member. Player 045 and his team looked at her with disgust and sent her off to find a team elsewhere. Later on, Player 045 and his team equipped Player 254, as their 5th member, forming Team 2. When Six-Legged Pentathlon officially started. They were one of the 2 teams up first. They started off slow with Player 286 getting stuck on Ddakji, but later passed Team 1 after Player 045 successfully cleared Biseokchigi on his first try. However, they soon slowed down after Player 254 failed Gonggi a couple times. When Player 254 finally did complete Gonggi, Player 045 and his team tripped on each other and wasted a bunch of time trying to get back up. They moved on to Paengi Chigi where Player 395 failed the game on his first try. When he tried to retrieve the spinning top, he tripped on his teammates again and the whole team fell over. Later on, after Player 395 finally finishing Paengi Chigi, Player 045 and his team rushed towards the finale minigame in a hurry after looking at the timer.

Fate[]
After Player 045 and his team finally finished Paengi Chigi, they rushed towards the finale minigame in order to complete the round. However, they suddenly ran out of time and were repeatedly shot by a firing squad of soldiers for failing to complete all of the minigames within the time limit.

Trivia[]
Player 045 was the only player on his team to complete a minigame on his first try.
He was the first player to die in the second game. During the death scene, you can see how the first shot hits him in the heart.
He was the first Team X player to die in the 37th edition.
He was the first player in the 040s to be eliminated.
He is the only player on his team in the six-legged pentathlon who did not have his own stage in the voting. (Player 395 was between Player 401 (37th Squid Game) and Park Jung-bae).
It seems like he had a crush on Ms. Player 254 because he is seen frequently looking at her throughout the Pentathlon game, he shushes Player 395 when he yells at her when she tries Gong-gi the first time, he quickly hands her the missing Gong-gi pieces back after she fails it the first time, he places his hand on her shoulder during the Pentathlon and he hugs her close and tightly immediately after she successfully completes the Gong-gi game.
He bears some resemblance to Player 196 of the first season and both players die in a team game with an even number: both were also team-mates with a quite outspoken woman, Player 212/Han Mi-nyeo for 196 and Ms. Player 254 for Player 045.
Behind the scenes[]
Player 045 was portrayed by Kwon Jae-hwan and an Unknown extra.
