House of Cards is a game featured as the fourth mini-game in the Six-Legged Pentathlon in Squid Game: The Challenge Season 2.

Game Information[]
Objective: The player in charge of this mini-game must form a stable "House of Cards" structure utilizing 7 cards. Once the structure is formed, the team passes this mini-game.
If the player fails to construct a stable structure, or their House collapses, they must retry until completion. The player is not allowed to support the structure with their hands, only the cards can support each other.
