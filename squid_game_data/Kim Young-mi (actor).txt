Kim Young-mi (김영미 Gim Yeong-mi?) is the actress who portrayed Player 440 in Season 2 and Season 3 of the Netflix TV series Squid Game.
Young-mi wasn’t planning on acting in Squid Game, but auditioned for her role after her brother unfortunately passed away from cancer. Because he was a huge fan of Squid Game, Young-mi was motivated to audition in honor of her brother since that’s the show he enjoyed, even if it meant being a background character. Although because of her inspiring story, the director, Hwang Dong-hyuk allowed her to have an appearance in Season 3.
