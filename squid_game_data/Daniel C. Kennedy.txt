Daniel C. Kennedy is an American-South Korean actor who portrayed VIP 2 in the television series Squid Game.
In an interview, Kennedy said he was initially considered for VIP 4 and submitted a self-tape for that role[1]. However, in the end he was cast as VIP 2 while Geoffrey Giuliano played VIP 4.
