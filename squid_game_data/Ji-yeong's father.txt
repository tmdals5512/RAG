Ji-yeong's father was the husband of Ji-yeong's mother, and the father of Ji-yeong. He was a pastor, and was abusive toward his family. Ji-yeong recalls him physically abusing her during her childhood, and he would also beat her mother. He would constantly ask God for forgiveness after his actions.

Fate[]
One day, after returning from school, Ji-yeong found her mother dead as a result of her father stabbing her to death. He stood beside the corpse, with a knife covered with Ji-yeong's mother's blood on it. To avenge her mother, Ji-yeong stabbed him to death. She was incarcerated as a result.
