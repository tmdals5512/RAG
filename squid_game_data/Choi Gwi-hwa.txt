Choi Gwi-hwa (최귀화 Choe Gwi-hwa?) is the actor who portrayed Kim Gi-min/Player 203 in Season 2 and Season 3 of the Netflix TV series Squid Game.[1] He also portrays characters in other pieces of media, such as the homeless man in Train to Busan. And He also played a role alongside Lee Jung-jae in the television series Nice to Not Meet You.
