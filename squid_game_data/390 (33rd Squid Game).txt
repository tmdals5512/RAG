Player 390 was a participant in the 33rd Squid Game. 

History[]
Player 390 was in debt and then a man played Ddakji with him. He got a card and was invited to the Squid Games, where he called the number on the card. A van came to him and when he went in, he was knocked unconscious by gas. He was then transported to The Island. He woke up in the dormitory, confused. He then heard the guards tell the rules and saw the Players complaining about the game. He saw them get slapped on-screen with their debts revealed, and watched the fight between Kang Sae-byeok and Jang Deok-su. He then walked to the first game, Red Light, Green Light. He saw Player 324 die unknowingly, and saw Player 250 check on Player 324, panicked, and got shot. He then saw Player 306 get something on her face and screamed, and she got shot. A mass panic occurred, and Player 390 also panicked.

Fate[]
He died in the mass panic of Red Light, Green Light.
