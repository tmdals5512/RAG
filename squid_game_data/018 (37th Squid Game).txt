History[]
Player 018 was in heavy debt and decided to join the Squid Games in 2024. After waking up in the dormitory, she could be seen listening to the guards explain why they're there. Later on, in Red Light, Green Light, because of Lee Eun-ju screaming, panic quickly started. 

Fate[]
Player 018 was one of the contestants who ran in fear and was seen being encouraged to run by Player 033. Because of her breaking the rules, she was shot and killed before she could reach the doors.

Trivia[]
Player 018 is one of the most elderly female players to have participated in the 37th Squid Game.
Player 018 is the first player in the 01X place to have been eliminated.
Her actress re-appears as Player 397 during the first vote.
Behind the Scenes[]
Player 018 was portrayed by Choi Seung-chae and an Unknown extra.
