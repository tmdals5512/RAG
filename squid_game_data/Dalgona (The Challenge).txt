Dalgona was the second main game featured in The First Season of Squid Game: The Challenge, akin to its counterpart in the main series. 
119 players passed this game while 77 were eliminated.
All players were led into the white room, and were told to form 4 equal lines. It was then asked of the first player in all of the lines to enter the game hall by themselves, accompanied by the staff. It was then announced to them, and all players that the 4 people in the game hall would have to agree on a shape for the 4 of them, indicated by touching the door, and that the 4 people would be representing their lines. The shape the 4 agreed on would be given to their respective line. They had two minutes to decide, or they would be eliminated.
The first 4 players - 098, 166, 170 and 328 - could not decide on who should receive the umbrella, as it was the hardest shape, within the time limit.
After this, the next 4 players - 288, 321, 378 and 454 - were led into the game hall and given 2 minutes. Once again, they could not decide who should receive the umbrella and were subsequently eliminated.
After this, Players 299, 344, 432 and 452 were led into the game hall and given 2 minutes. 344, 432 and 452 decided to act fast and selected their shapes before 299. They then pressured him into selecting the Umbrella, but 299 demanded one condition - that the other players with easier shapes should help out those with umbrellas. The players agreed and each chose a figure, with 452 choosing the circle, 432 the triangle, 344 the star and 299 the umbrella. Unfortunately - once shapes had been selected it was revealed all shapes would compete separately from each other, and players which had completed their shape were not allowed to help others. 
One by one, the lines were led in. All players were given a tin containing one Dalgona, with their respective shapes. The aim of the game is to successfully carve out the shape stamped into the Dalgona, either with the provided needle or other strategies, within the 10 minute time limit. Any player who cracks their Dalgona, will be eliminated. Any player who does not remove their shape from the Honeycomb within the time limit, will be eliminated.
