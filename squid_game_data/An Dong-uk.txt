History
An Dong-uk was born on December 30, 1977. He attended Seoul Sungjin High School and then Osan University, married Seo Seon-hee and had two children; a daughter and then a son. Dong-uk then worked as a dry cleaner, however he later lost or quit his job and became a construction worker. Eventually, Dong-uk went into debt and needed to find money. To do so, he enters the Squid Games. He signs a contract and takes his photo before the first game, Red Light, Green Light. When Gi-hun warns them about shooters, he doubts him at first but was able to stay calm and passes the finish line. He then voted O in the first vote. During the Six-Legged Pentathlon Dong-uk Partnered up with Player 024, Player 048, Player 019 and Player 378. He and his team managed to pass within the five minute time limit. He then votes O again in the second vote before proceeding to Mingle.

Fate
After surviving the first three rounds, An Dong-uk makes it back to the center of Mingle. Unluckily for him, Dong-uk wasn't able to find enough partners or wasn't able to reach a room in time. This would result in him getting shot by the Soldiers after failing Round 4.

Behind the scenes
An Dong-uk was portrayed by Park Geon-woo, Park Byeong-gwan, Lee Kang-min and an unknown extra.

Trivia
His daughter's first name is the same as Jo Su-jin's first name.
He is the only named minor character in all of Squid Game who's name starts with the letter A, and is the only Korean character in the entire show who's name begins with an A.
His name may be a reference to Hwang Dong-hyuk, since their names are similar.
An Dong-uk's Six-Legged Pentathlon actor is the same actor who portrays Player 090 in Season 1.
