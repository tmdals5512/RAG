Trivia[]
Players who played an even mini game died without their corpses being shown, while the ones who played on odd game were shown.
Additionally, the evens were killed by Soldiers and the odds were killed by their fellow players, (Player 299, Player 158, and Hwang In-ho)
Additionally once more, evens died in Mingle, and the other died in the three interludes, Bathroom fight, Special Game, and Rebellion.
There was supposed to be more footage of them shown, but it was cut.
Player 401 is the only one who voted O in the first vote on the team.
Player 308 and Kwon Byeong-su were the only members from their team that were seen playing their respective minigame.
Mingle is the only game to claim members of this team, with all other members dying before Hide and Seek.
Aditionaly, the three that died before Hide and Seek all died in separate events.
It is the only team seen in the pentathlon montage to pass the game, with the other team passing as well.
It is the only team to have members that died in all three of the the main interludes.
Kwon Byeong-su was the longest living member of the team placing roughly 67th out of the 456 players.
